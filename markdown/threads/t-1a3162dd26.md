```
try this
FD UNI-CAT-FILE RECORD CONTAINS 333 characters
01	MY-RECORD.
	05	STANDARD-RECORD.
		10	STANDARD-DATA	PIC X(222).
		10	 VARIABLE-STUFF	PIC X(111).
	05	FUNNY-RECORD REDEFINES STANDARD-RECORD
		10	FUNNY-STANDARD-DATA	PIC X(222)
		10	FUNNY-DATA		PIC  X(32)
		10	FILLER				PIC  X(79)
	05	ODD-RECORD REDEFINES STANDARD-RECORD
		10 ODD-STANDARD-DATA		PIC X(222)
		10	ODD-DATA			PIC X(64)
		10	FILLER				PIC X(47)

It really doesn't matter that your record may be fewer characters as long as
the FD is set up for "worst case". Now your only challenge is to figure out
which "template" (REDEFINE) maps to the record you just read. We do that with a
special indicator character as part of the record key.
```
