Pick up a copy of Teach Yourself COBOL in 21 Days, MacMillan Computer
Publishing's best seller.

It's an excellent, easy to understand text, that doesn't make assumptions about
what a student knows, and gives lots of real life examples. The book can be
done with a PC based compiler - such as MicroFocus.
The book sold out in both at the publisher and at stores across the US last
month, but the new edition should be on the shelves this week.
