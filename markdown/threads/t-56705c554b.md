```
Computer Programmer

CODE: 086400

Pay Grade 21 ($11.38 - 15.44/hr.)

Value of State-paid Health and Dental Insurance = $141.60 biweekly
Value of State's share of Employee's Retirement = 11.53% of pay

The State of Maine has current and future vacancies statewide. This is
entry level technical services work in developing and maintaining
computer programs. Responsibilities focus on designing, testing,
debugging, and maintaining programs under the direction of seasoned
data processing personnel.


In order to qualify, you must have a Bachelors Degree in Computer
Science, Management Information Systems, or a directly related area
-OR- an unrelated Bachelors Degree which includes a minimum of 18
credits in the areas of information systems, automation technology, or
statistics -OR- one year of application system development experience.



YOU MUST COMPLETE AND SUBMIT THE REQUIRED SUPPLEMENTAL QUALIFICATIONS
FORM WITH THE REGULAR APPLICATION MATERIALS IN ORDER TO BE EVALUATED
PROPERLY.


EXAMINATION PROCESS: The information your provide with your
application and Supplemental Qualifications Form will be compared to
criteria developed by experts who are familiar with this job and its
requirements and converted to a numerical score.


Applications are accepted continuously for this classification.


Visit http://www.state.me.us/bhr/career/appinfo.htm for application
information.
```
