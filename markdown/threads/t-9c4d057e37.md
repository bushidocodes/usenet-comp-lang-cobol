```
To send any hex codes in almost any cobol

01 HEX-01 PIC 99 COMP VALUE 01. (some cobols store 00 01 hex

others 01 00....watch out)
01 ANOTHER REDEFINES HEX-01.
05 FIELD1 PIC X.
05 FIELD2 PIC X.

EITHER TRANSMIT field1 or field2 depending on storage mechanism.
probably field2.

JR


and stir with a Runcible spoon...
```
