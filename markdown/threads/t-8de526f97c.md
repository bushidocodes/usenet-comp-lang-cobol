It just shows you that a good sort utility takes care of these details.  I
tend not to waste too much time trying to tune a sort.

In OS390 and COBOL II, if I use the USING/GIVING structure instead of the
PROCEDURE structure, the compiler makes the program eligible for the FASTSRT
option.  But I have never tested this against the regular sort, and I don't
know what effect it would have on your UNIX box.  I would check the compiler
messages.

Jeff
