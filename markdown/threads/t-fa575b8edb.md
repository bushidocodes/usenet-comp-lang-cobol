Micro Focus COBOL (always on UNIX & sometimes on NT depending on functions
used) charges a run-time fee for deployed applications. you do NOT have to
buy additional run-times for development (i don't know who told you that,
but they are wrong).

there are two available models for the run-time (also called the
"Application Server"). a 10-user license (i think that's something like the
$800 you mention; so $80 each) and an unlimited-user license (this is the
$10K item) for transaction based server applications (such as web apps).
