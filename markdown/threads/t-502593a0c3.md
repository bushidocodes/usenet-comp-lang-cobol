Actually the old program DID beep, you just didn't hear it cause the
BEEP was busted. Way back in early MF they added a switch to change
the beep behavior. With 3.4 they changed the default state of this
beep to be on.

Try setting this in your environment area or the .CFG for your
mainline program.

SET COBSW=-P1

That'll make it behave like it did in the old COBOL. We recently hit
this as in the past, we had to put in 10 beeps to be heard. Now it's
rather annoying!!!
