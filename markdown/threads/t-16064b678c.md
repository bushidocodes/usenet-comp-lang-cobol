Quite true, Bill.

On the other hand, IBM Debug Tool, if ordered with the Utilities and 
Advanced Functions module, includes a copy of CCCA, so that you can 
convert your OS/VS COBOL and get it into the supported COBOL world.

Our shop had not bothered to license a conversion aid, so this was a 
real advantage.  Previously, if someone picked up a really bad OS/VS 
COBOL program, they would come by and beg me to convert it for them. 
I'd have to download the program to my OS/2 PC, where I could run the 
VisualAge COBOL port of CCCA and COBOL/SF.  (We don't have the latest 
version of VA COBOL, either.)
