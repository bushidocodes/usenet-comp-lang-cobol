No the merger didn't fail to happen - they became part of Merant and they lost
lots of good technicians. Now they are a separate entity again,
privately/corporately funded off shore (Bahamas or whatever).

But the merger itself *did* fail because it didn't function - as with many
mergers -  different corporate philosophies. (From memory the 'new' Merant
Board had only one Micro Focus member - and that appears to have been a 'thank
you' to the lady who handled the merger from the Micro Focus end). Although M/F
produces lots of support software - their official declaration is to strongly
support the continuance of COBOL.
