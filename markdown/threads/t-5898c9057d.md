Too bad you didn't ask last week.  I tossed one of those old IBM pads in the

Instead of paper I use a text editor (or a word processor with a fixed
font).  At the top of the screen I put:
         1         2         3 ...
123456789012345678901234567890 ...

Then I type, cut & paste untill the report template looks good.  This way I
don't kill nearly as many trees as I used to.

Some text editors will display the line/column number somewhere on the
screen.  I know the MicroFocus Edit/Animator does.
