PowerHouse (PH) is a 4GL that was developed some years ago on the HP-3000 MPE
platform. The company that developed it is now called COGNOS and is located in
Canada. PH has since been ported to a number of other plaforms including PC and
VMS. I am a died-in-the-wool COBOL programmer, but I absolutely love PH on my
HP-3000. It (the QUIZ reporting tool in particular) is easy to use and I've yet
to see something that couldn't be done in PowerHouse. The main downside is that
as a 4GL it is not a terribly efficient user of hardware resources and, in the
hands of the uninitiated user, it can eat your machine alive.

Good luck in your job search.
