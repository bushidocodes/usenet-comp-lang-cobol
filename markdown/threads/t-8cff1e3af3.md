Cross System Product (CSP) is one of IBM's SAA languages.  As the name
implies it is "Cross System", it will run on all of IBM's platforms
from the
'mighty' MVS to the lowly 'PC-DOS' and everything in the middle too.

It is COBOL like, very easy to learn.  I walked into a shop, did a CBT
on it
and was comfortable with it within the first two weeks.

Should you take the job?  I can't tell you that.  Personally, I'm
staying away from it.  In the states it is not very popular. 
Apparently in Europe, more specifically Germany, it is much more
popular.  It was coded mostly in Germany and mostly in C.

Here in Chicago I know that Allstate and Kemper insurance companies use
it extensively.  Motorola has (maybe had) a system or two in it.  Nike
in Portland uses it too.  There are a few other places around the
country that I have heard about too that use it.
