```
Does anyone have experience of using these routines??

We've tried using them and while the status returned is zero, nothing,
bitmpa-wise, appears on the printer. Actually that's not quite true
because sometimes a part of the BMP does appear, but the right hand
10% is not too useful!

Do they really work? In particular what are the units for the start
position and width and height of the bitmap: character positions or
what ? And what is the procedure - presumably load the BMP at the
beginning and free at the end but should the ...WRITE_BMP be issued
prior to every page written, or is it meant to issued just once before
the first page and the subsequent pages, until the free, are "loaded"
with the BMP automatically ? The Help describes the individual
routines reasonably well, although not completely clearly in some
cases, but not the overall usage.

For a variety of reasons it seemed a good idea at the time...

The alternatives looked to require effort especially if it meant
finding the correct sequences for a variety of printers for loading
graphic data to a page and then sending that sequence from the
program. Generating PDF also came to mind and this is looking more
attractive now!

graham 
Olympic Computers
```
