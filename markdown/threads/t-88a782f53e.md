There is an excellent journal now available about COBOL called
(appropriately enough) The COBOL Report. I recommend that anyone who
works with (or intends to work with) COBOL subscribe to it. If your
school teaches COBOL, they should subscribe as well. The rates are very
reasonable at $59 per year for individuals and $125 for companies. Take
a look at http://www.objectz.com/ for more information.

You will find a lot of answers to questions posted in this newsgroup in
back issues of this journal. You will also find articles about object
oriented COBOL and the tools and techniques you will need to remain
current in computing.
