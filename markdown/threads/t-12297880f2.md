Trondheim eh? Would love to visit you mid summer. As to your problem. There
are a lot of situations where you get misleading error codes for MF/COBOL. They
do have a lot of different error that they try to report on. Sometimes this
zeal to give you the most information sends you down a wrong path. Often the
problem is easy to recognize. For example the system says it is out of disk
space when it is not.

Your problem is not so easy. Last year I had a user report 9/018. Here is what
we discovered in his case. Yours could be different. The file he was getting
this error on was a sequential file. They were using Quarterdeck's QEMM386 on
MS/DOS 5.0. After they removed QEMM386, it ran fine. After that they upgraded
to MS/DOS 6.0 and used EMM386 and the problem never came up again.

I hope this helps. If not contact MF directly. While the company may not
officially help you, many, many of their employees go out of their way to help.
I have found the people of MicroFocus to be first rate.
