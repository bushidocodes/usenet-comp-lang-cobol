Try looking for any usenet newsgroup that ends with the word "jobs".

Such as, stl.jobs (St. Louis Jobs Usenet forum).

I don't know where you're from so I can't suggest the appropriate city
prefix for the usenet group. Not all cities have their own usenet groups.

Short of that, have you tried placement firms? They usually know who
uses what.

You could also try attending local user groups for any type of hardware
software platform. You'll usually find someone there that knows folks
who want a change, want to move or feel their underpaid. (Woops, we all
feel that...the jury will scratch that last remark.)
