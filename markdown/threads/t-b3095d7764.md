```
FYI, the following is what I submitted concerning the call for votes on
creating the new acucorp newsgroup,

  ***

"In the past I have expressed my belief that this was a mistake.  I still
vote NO - although I believe that this proposal is much better than any
previous "version" of the proposal.

HOWEVER, there is one statement within this document that is simply so FALSE
that I think it needs to be addressed specifically.  The document states,

"Acucobol contains significant extensions to the COBOL standard.
 Discussion of these extensions would be of little interest to
 comp.lang.cobol readers who are not Acucobol developers."

The proposer of this group has been repeatedly told that this statement
simply is not true.  There is ABSOLUTELY no evidence within the history of
comp.lang.cobol that there is anything other than SIGNIFICANT interest among
most readers and contributors in the extensions available in the AcuCOBOL
product - and all other COBOL implementations.  Furthermore, it is my belief
that this MISconception is what has caused this group to be proposed and
that even when it comes to the non-COBOL products and features of Acucorp,
these could be well processed and discussed in the existing newsgroup

Again, I vote NO - and really hope that - should this group be formed - that
the misconception of comp.lang.cobol and its participants be corrected as
quickly and thoroughly as possible."
```
