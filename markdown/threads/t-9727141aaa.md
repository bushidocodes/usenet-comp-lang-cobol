This seems less a question about 'MVS-Cobol' and more a question of ISPF 
Editor options.

The ISPF Editor allows for a variety of numbering schemes when dealing 
with data; the specifics can be found in Help (start editing a file, press 
PF1 or type 'help' (no ') <enter> Topic 7 (Sequence Numbering).

Researching this topic will give you a sense of what you might need to 
do as well as introduce you to classic IBM documentation.  As my first 
programming instructor taught:

'What is an IBM manual?  An IBM manual consists of twenty-four chapters, 
each of which assumes intimate familiarity with the other twenty-three.'
