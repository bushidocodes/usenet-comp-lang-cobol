COBOL is nothing like HTML. COBOL is a procedural programming
language designed for business oriented tasks. Math skills are almost
completely un-necessary to use the language, though basic arithmetic
will be needed by a lot of the applications, and an understanding of
hexadecimal is useful in some serious debugging and optimization
tasks. Imagine something like BASIC, with a requirement to pre-define
all of your data items, and most data items being hierarchical
(variable EMP-RECORD contains variables EMP-NUM, EMP-NAME,
EMP-ADDRESS; while EMP-ADDRESS contains EMP-STREET, EMP-CITY,
EMP-STATE, etc.).


Ed Stevens/TN
