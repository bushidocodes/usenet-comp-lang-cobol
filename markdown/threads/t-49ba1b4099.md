There are a number of Linkers available as shareware or 
freeware.  ALINK (for which source is available) is under
active development presently.  Also the old Digital Research
tools are being made available (CBASIC, PL/I &c) freely
for non-commercial purposes -- the CP/M-86 and the DOS
versions and they come with linkers.  The ValArrow package
has a linker.  Plus some of the last 16 bit linkers from MS are
(or at least were) available for download from the MS website.

I'd suggest you start with Simtel, you'll find plenty.
