my apologies to the group.

in my youth i was a terrible speller and new nothing of sentence structure.  i learned of these deficiencies as an adult and worked hard at overcoming them.  but now, thanks to several factors, i find myself struggling with the effort it takes to write, and the results speak for themselves.

my last posting contained several errors (spelling and structure).  the errors i would like to correct are the ones in the paragraph that contain a quote, for i really hate it when i mess up a quote.  the corrected paragraph follows... and i hope it is corrected.

sam walter foss was reading homer one day and one of homer's lines caught his eye, so
much so that sam wrote a poem about it.  my grandfather (on my mother's side) would
often recite this poem from memory, and even as a small boy i remember relating to the
words 'Let me live in a house by the side of the road and be a friend to man.'.  nice words.
nice thoughts.  but they really ought to be expressed in a different group.

an additional thought.  in my day we spent many hours discussing 'structured' vs 'inline'.  spending time in this group has prompted me to do some additional research into this matter using the bible as my referance, for on a subject as important as this one god must surely have an opinion.  and much to my surprise he does.  you can 'go to' heaven, you can 'go to' hell, and you can 'perform' penance.  so god votes 2 out of 3 in favor of inline.  christian, jewish, and arabic programmers, if they be true followers, must write inline code. 

what a great day.
