```
---------------------------

Use the LINK program from your DOS or a compiler (such as MASM).
I have tried various ones and they all work.

Use the following statement:

link progname,,,cobol650,,

Change 'progname' to the same name as your source (except no suffix).

The final two commas may or may not be needed, depending on the
version of link.
```
