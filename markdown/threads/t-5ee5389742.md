Be thankful it isn't reversed Polish... :-)

1. Go to the Google groups page... If it is in Polish (or anything else 
other than English) paste this link into your Browser address bar:

http://groups.google.com/grphp?hl=en

2. Click "preferences". If you are still seeing Polish, paste this link into 
your Browser address bar:

http://www.google.com/preferences?hl=en&prev=http://groups.google.com%2Fgrphp%3Fhl%3Den

3. Click "Languages"  (It doesn't look like a link but it actually is...)

4.  Untick Polish in the list of languages, and make sure English is ticked.

5. Save and exit.

Better?
