You have shown us the run-time (LE) options, but have not shown us your
compile time options.  There are (at least) 2 required compile-time options
(that you may NOT be using).  They are:

  "NODYNAM and PGMNAME(LONGMIXED)"

See:
  http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr/BOOKS/igy3pg00/3.4.3

I also seem to remeber that there MIGHT be problems with using spaces
instead of commas to separate "parameters" - even though it is documented as
valid to do so.  See:

 http://publib.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr/BOOKS/igy3lr00/4.2.3.1

You might want to try changing,

> 77  FILE-ENVIRONMENT-VARA             PIC X(43)

> 77  FILE-ENVIRONMENT-VARB             PIC X(43)

    to

will delimit your environment variable.  Note you need to change the

    05 FILLER                PIC X(43)
        VALUE Z"OUTPUTF=DSN(COGBD.WORK.COBOL(DYNAOUT)) SHR".
    05 FILLER                PIC X(01) VALUE LOW-VALUES.

For your reading enjoyment the bookmanger book Enterprise COBOL for
z/OS and OS/390 V3R1 Programming Guide, is pretty good.  But beware,
the examples for dynamic allocation do not reflect how the book
describes setting environment variables.  This case in point.
