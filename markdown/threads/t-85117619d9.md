```
It sounds like you are doing the move to a group field, ie:

      01   Com-3-field           PIC S9(11) COMP-3.
      01   Group-Field.
           03  Display-10-field   PIC 9(10).
           03  Display-1-field    PIC 9.

           MOVE Com-3-Field    TO Group-Field

will treat the move as an alphanumeric move, thus the first few
bytes will just be the packed data and the PIC 9 fields will
be invalid.  You need to do a num,eric move to unpack it.
```
