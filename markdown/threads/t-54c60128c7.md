Not sure what you intend doing here; are you going to use the cursor keys to
move along and between lines?
How about having this as your data field
01 DATAFIELD.
03 DATALINE OCCURS 6.
05 DATACHAR PIC X OCCURS 55.
and for your cursor and marker
01 CURPOS. 01 FIELDPOS PIC 999 COMP.
03 CURX PIC 99.
03 CURY PIC 99.
To move between lines increment or decrement CURX and add or subtract 55 from
FIELDPOS and increment/decrement both fields to move along a line; to insert
/delete a character "shuffle" the subsequent characters forwards or backwards
by one in DATAFIELD etc.
Redraw the 6 DATALINEs after each character press
