I have been able to use OO Coble and have found it easy to use
as far as OO programming goes. It depends heavily on the
concept of nested programming, which was made available in
Cobol85. There are a few new verbs (commands) and concepts to
learn and your original investment in COBOL programmers is
maintained. In my opinion, if you have a few years of COBOL
experience, it will be easy to learn and implement.
