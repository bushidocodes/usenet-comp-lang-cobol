          SENIOR EXERCISE REGIME

Pass this along to your senior friends, for those getting along in years...

Here is a little secret for building arm and shoulder muscles.

Three days a week works well.

Begin by standing outside behind the house, and with a 5-lb. potato sack in

each hand... extend your arms straight out to your sides and
hold them there as long as you can.

After a few weeks, move up to 10-lb. potato sacks and then 50-lb. potato 
sacks, and finally get to where you can lift a 100 lb. potato sack in each 
hand and hold your arms straight for more than a full minute.

Next...start putting a few potatoes in the sacks, but be careful not to 
overdo it.
