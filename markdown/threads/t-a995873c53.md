You appear to be searching backwards.  Your table is sorted
from lowest to highest, but you are searching based on a greater
than condition.  Since the first element is the smallest, the result
will always either be the first element, or out of the table.  I would
re-order the table from highest to lowest, or change the condition
                  WHEN SALARY >= TAX-AMT (TAX-INDEX)
to
                  WHEN SALARY is less than TAX-AMT (TAX-INDEX)
