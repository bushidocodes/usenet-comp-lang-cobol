```
Micro Focus has Sales and Support opportunities in your city. Work from
a home office. There is usually no need to relocate.

Sales Representative
--------------------

Responsibilities - As a sales representative you will direct-sell Micro
Focus Year 2000 professional software products and solutions targeted
primarily at MVS mainframe customers. You will develop new accounts and
grow existing accounts. You will also provide price quotes and product
descriptions upon customer request, as well as work with the Territory
Manager and Marketing department to implement sales strategies. You
will be expected to fulfill a specified quota.

Requirements - Qualified candidates will have a BS/BA or equivalent and
a minimum of three years of professional software sales experience into
MVS and CICS/IMS environments. Experience in top-down selling is
essential. Excellent presentation, verbal and written communication
skills are required, as are strong organizational skills and the ability
to establish priorities. Regional travel is required.

Sales Support Representative
----------------------------

Responsibilities - You will support all technical aspects of the selling
cycle, from pre-sales and demos through installation and account
management of Micro Focus Year 2000 products and solutions.

Requirements - Qualified candidates will have a BS degree or equivalent
and a minimum of five years of COBOL application development experience
under CICS, SQL, OS/2, JCL, PL/1, or DB2 as well as a working knowledge
of MVS mainframe applications and code. Experience with Micro Focus
products is preferred. Excellent verbal and presentation skills are
essential, as is the ability to travel within a geographical region, and
the ability to regularly transport a 10-20 lb computer.

Technical Consultant
--------------------

Responsibilities - Develop and implement Year 2000 solutions for large
customers acquiring Micro Focus products. Provide recommendations to
customers regarding Micro Focus products as well as companion products,
networks, hardware, etc. Assist customers to define and manage the
proper environment to enable large-scale implementation of Micro Focus
products. (Provide development and implementation plans, training,
source management, internal support plans, etc.) Provide product
support for Micro Focus products. Extensive (up to 75%) travel is
required.

Requirements - BS or equivalent and a minimum of five years consulting
or technical experience with mainframe, UNIX, or PC platforms, including
Micro Focus products. Ability to work independently and perform on-site
consulting assignments for varying lengths of time. Excellent
presentation and interpersonal skills. Ability to travel 75%.

Micro Focus offers excellent compensation and benefits packages.

Please Fax or Email your resume to;

Ron Davidson
Fax: 415-843-7248
Email: r.··.@mic··s.com
```
