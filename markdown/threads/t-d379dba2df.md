: That's the way the system appears externally.  How it works internally
: is a little different.  Banks to not post money to transaction
: accounts real-time.  If the merchant has an account at your bank, the
: debit clears to his account when nightly posting is run.  A hold is
: placed on your account to commit the funds until the debit is posted.
: If the merchant has no account at your bank, the debit must clear
: through the fed, and a hold is placed on your account to commit the
: funds until the debit arrives from the fed and is posted.  Banks used
: to require the merchant have an account at the bank to speed
: processing, but merchants simply refused to handle that bank's debit
: cards, so the requirement was dropped.  An item can take several days
: to clear through the fed.
