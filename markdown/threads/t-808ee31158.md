   Pushed by someone I can't remember whom on this forum, I read about  
the VALIDATE statement in the 2002 COBOL standard, both the syntax  
description, and the Annex E "Concepts" and I am quite impressed. That  
is the kind of program generators I like.

   VALIDATE checks a whole series of data-items and moves them based  
on clauses in the declaration of those data-items, specifying VALID  
values, and DESTINATIONs, also their format based on the PICTURE  
clause, plus relations between those items as specified with the  
VALIDATE statement itself.

   My question is if this facility is implemented in any compiler, and  
to the extent of the specification in the standard?

   If yes, in which compiler?
