Snow does have several product lines, the only line that I have worked with is
what is called "Snow Report Writer and Query Facility". In "my" version of 4.1
(and copies of it that I have installed at other user sites in the past,
includes the ability to "Define File Relationships" under "Special Menu
Selections". To do the actual linking does require knowledge of how the
relationship between the two files are established (key structures and type of
relationship).

As far as your support issue and whether or not they should do this for you
as part of the support is up to them.  For our customers, we would define the
typical lookups in advance.  If the user required something different, we
would either "walk" them thru it or do it for them.  Since these requests
were occasional and usually only one or two, we would do it as part of our
support contract with them.

My suggestion would be to talk to your support people, maybe they didn't give
you the correct Relationship file (RELFIL.DAT), or they may have an updated
version of it, or maybe they will do what you need.  Worst case, you could go
back to Snow and "Upgrade" your product to the full blown version that gives
you the capabilities that you need.

Good Luck!
