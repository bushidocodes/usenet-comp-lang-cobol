```
IBM watchers, COBOLers, or just those interested in "when to do compiler
upgrades",

you might want to look at a "semi-hidden" paragraph in today's IBM CICS
Announcement at:
  http://www.ibmlink.ibm.com/usalets&parms=H_201-060

Look for the paragraph that says,

"Support remains in CICS TS V2.1 for the earlier (pre-Language Environment)
compilers OS/VS COBOL (5740-CB1, 5740-LM1, and 5734-CB4), VS COBOL II
(5668-958, also 5688-023 and 5688-022), OS PL/I Version 2 (5668-910), and
SAA AD/Cycle� C/370T (5688-216). Translator support for these compilers is
withdrawn in CICS TS V2.2. It is planned that run-time support for load
modules created using these compilers, that do not use Language Environment,
will be withdrawn in the release following CICS TS V2.2."
```
