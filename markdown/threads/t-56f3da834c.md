```

To anyone who's ever been in the same room with a
Wang VS computer:


I don't know if I've made an official mention of this
before, but, there's an e-zine for Wang VS systems...

   www.HotVS.com

It's been around for about four months and will be 
filling the void left by the demise of Access to Wang.

Any old Wangers are invited to stop in and see that yes, 
Wang is still in business.  In fact, they're introducing 
a new processor -- the CP18 -- that blows the doors off of 
all prior Wang processors.  Realistically a 1000-user machine.

To see what else is out there try the portal address...

   www.hotvs.com/portal

for links to other Wang-related sites around the world.

Did you know that there's even a Web Server that runs
on a VS?  Not a PC connected to a VS... *on* a VS!

Give it a spin:  

   www.phoenix.net/~tjunker/server-test-203494.html 


In the next few months I'm going to be writing on ways
to tune your applications.... ways to write Cobol so that
your programs get the most out of every CPU and I/O cycle.
```
