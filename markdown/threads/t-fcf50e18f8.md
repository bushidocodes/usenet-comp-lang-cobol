In this case, I think the Usenet comp.lang.cobol newsgroup was a great
place to inexpensively get the word out about their product. I think
the delay that many have experienced in getting the CD in their hands is
due to the fact that so many people responded to the offer of free
software. They had to go back to their CD manufactuer and get some
more CD's made up.

You should just fill out the Web page they have, and then, after a month
or two of being patient, you'll see the offer is for real. That's my
personal feeling.

The explanation as to why Fujitsu is doing all this, I'd guess, is because
they want to go from having 0 % of the COBOL compiler market to become
a player in the game. That is, I really doubt version 2 of their product
will also be free.
