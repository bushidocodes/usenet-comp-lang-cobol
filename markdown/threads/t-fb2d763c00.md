Well....I love mine.   But, I'm not a college student!   I use it every
day.

If you're going to invest in the Palm, a couple of things you may want
to consider.

First, you'll probably be spending (if you're honest) some money for
after market software.  I've replaced my calendar and To Do programs
with more sophisticated ones.  I've also added a program called BugMe,
which allows you to write hand written (non-graffiti) notes to yourself
and set alarms, etc.   There are a couple of other pieces of software
that I've added over the years, but may not be useful to you.

Second......if you're thinking of a Palm Pilot, you may want to consider
the same sort of unit from Handspring (check www.handspring.com).  It
was started by the same folks as developed the Palm, but with some
interesting differences.   

Dave Bretz
