If you are specifically asking can I get reports from Fujitsu ISAM files - Yes.

Regardless of tool, COBOL Report Writer, Flexus SP2, or Crystal etc., - you have
to read in your ISAM records, (usually sequentially, or even against an
Alternate Key, but again sequentially) and fit the data into a template or
'mask', representing your print lines, in the appropriate tool.
