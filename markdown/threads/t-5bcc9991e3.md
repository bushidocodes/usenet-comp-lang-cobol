I have two books on the subject:

"Programs from Decision Tables", E Humby, Macdonald/American
Elsevier Computer Monographs. 1973,
ISBNs 0 356 04126 3 or 0 444 19569 6

"An introduction to Decision Tables", Herman McDaniel.
A Petrocelli book, 1978, ISBN 0-89433-092-6

I used decision tables in the early 70's on ICL 1900 series
and 2903 machines. I had some ideas about implementing my
own pre-processors in the early 80s, but these are no longer
needed as the table can be coded directly as an EVALUATE
statement.
