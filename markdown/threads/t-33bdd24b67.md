```

In your class exercise, is your "master file" a sequential file or an
indexed file? The way you can find the "number" of the last record varies
for these two types of files.

Another question, is what compiler are you using? (Depending on the answer
to the 1st question and to this question, you may want to see if your
compiler has a "READ PREVIOUS" - not just "READ NEXT" option. It might also
have a "START LESS THAN" feature.) Finally, in the "real world" for large
files that are on tape (not disk) you might want to look at the "OPEN
REVERSED" feature. I don't know of many NEW programs that use this feature,
but it was common in the days when most files were still on tape.

P.S. I don't know how to find a copy of the book you are asking about. I
understand that amazon.com might have a "search for hard to find books"
feature - but I have never used it personally.
```
