I also had a problem like this when our shop was
upgrading to a new compiler and sort release.
What was happening to my newly compiled program
was that sort was "falling through". The cause was
a missing "SECTION" on the OUTPUT-PROC.
Make sure your OUTPUT-PROC reads:

OUTPUT-PROC SECTION.
and not just
OUTPUT-PROC.

Just a thought.
