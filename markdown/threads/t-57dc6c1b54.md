```
RainCode's technology expands now to CSP.

After Cobol and PL/1, a CSP version of RainCode is now functional and
available.
As RainCode can be adapted to almost any kind of language, due to its
strong object structure and modularity, we are constantly creating new
versions of it, following the needs of the developers community.

If you're facing problems maintainig some huge amount of undecipherable
source code, don't hesitate to contact us and send us some samples of
your code: RainCode can help...

To have an overview of RainCode's technology, visit our website at
http://www.raincode.com
```
