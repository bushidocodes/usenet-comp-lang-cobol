Do you mean that the "/" extentions work in the tbox editor, but not
in the tbox animator? I've never experienced that situation.
Function keys won't work, of course, unless you happen to have a
termcap or xterm emulation set up specifically for working with Micro
Focus, but I've always been able to use the extention characters to
move around in the UNIX MF when I've had to. (Of course, I much prefer
doing my development on a PC and move only tested, debugged code to
the UNIX box.)

For the unfamiliar, Micro Focus uses function keys are used to
control various aspects and operations of their development
environment. For those with only a standard QWERTY keyboard and xterm
setup, they provide "extention" characters to take the place of those
function keys normally available in the PC world. A menu of those
extention characters and their definitions is displayed by entering
"/m".
