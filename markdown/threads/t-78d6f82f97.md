Yes, there is a minor difference. On MVS and CMS (I do NOT know about VSE),
there is a PTF/APAR that added support for

Accept data-name from DATE YYYYMMDD
and
Accept data-name from DAY YYYYDDD

(new/proposed ANSI syntax)

This syntax is NOT available with COBOL/370 - but is with COBOL for MVS & VM
as well as for COBOL for OS/390 & VM. (Of course it isn't available with VS
COBOL II - and VS COBOL II doesn't support any of the date intrinsic
functions that are useful in Y2K remediateion.)

There are ways of doing Y2K work with earlier compilers, but the newer the
compiler, the better the solutions (in my opinion).
