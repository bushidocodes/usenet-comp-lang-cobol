Unfortunately the registration software leaves behind a reminder.
To remove the reminder, edit your WIN.INI file and remove
the line that has the path to the REMIND.EXE or REMIND.SCH file.
This line is usually about the 4th or 5th line down and as always
whenever you modify the WIN.INI file, it is always prudent to make a
backup first.
