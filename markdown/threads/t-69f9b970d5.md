Hi,

Bill Klein's COBOL FAQ is a good place to look - 

http://www.cobolreport.com/faqs/cobolfaq.htm

It has a section on COBOL standards, currently in item 7.

A new standard, COBOL 2002, has just been issued and can be bought
from ISO or NCITS as can COBOL 85.  It looks as though NCITS will be a
lot less expensive than ISO.  While no implementor as yet has issued a
COBOL 2002 compiler, many are already part way there using some of the
facilities proposed during its development, though not always exactly
as per the finalised new standard.

Implementors don't have to implement the full standard, but you would
still find most of the new features in compilers eventually.

Robert
