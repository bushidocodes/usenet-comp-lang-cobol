```
I have been covered by MetLife and "on" Long Term Disability for over 5
years now.  I won't say they are perfect, but they do seem "reliable".  My
insurance started when I was a full-time employee for a company - so I don't
know if they do or do not offer such insurance for "contractors".

P.S.  Note to "full time employees" -
  If your company does include a Long Term Insurance benefit, make
ABSOLUTELY certain that "officially" you pay for it and not your company
(i.e.  have your company "pay you" and you "pay the insurance company").
This make an INCREDIBLE tax difference if/when you ever need to use the
insurance.  If you have paid for it, then what you get back is TAX FREE - if
the company pays for the insurance, you must pay taxes on any benefits you
receive.
```
