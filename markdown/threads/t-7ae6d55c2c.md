G'day,
with all the doom and gloom surrounding Y2K, I thought it
might be fun to inject some fun into the subject. To that end,
I am putting together a book of reasons why, "The world will
end on the 13th August 1999 and where you would like to be
when it happens."
Although we will receive many serious reasons from serious
people,we would like this to be as entertaining as possible,
so please think about some crazy reasons as well, (crazier the
better).
If your reason is selected to be published, then you will
receive a free copy of the book. We will give you recognition
by way of initials and city/state or you can remain anonomous,
it is up to you. If you send us your reason you are
automatically giving permission for your reason to be published.
Have some fun with this. Love to hear from you.
John.
