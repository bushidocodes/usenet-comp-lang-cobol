JOB TITLE: Project Manager

ROLES & RESPONSIBLITIES:
To establish a Project Management Office
- Establish and maintain a Master Plan of all work specifically to enable

impacts of any schedule or other change in order to manage and schedule

- Enhance our current systems inventory with additional information
- Establish a systems to maintain the relationship between systems and

- Establish a vehicle to manage project status at the master plan level

- Very strong knowledge of the systems development life cycle, with specific
knowledge to guide the development of the master plan identifying key
resource requirements such as test environment
- Knowledge of the technical environment that exists at the Board
- Deliverable focused approach in providing hands-on guidance and assistance
to the team towards meeting its objectives
- Proven record of successful project management experience
- Knowledge of the Navigator Project Management methodology is desirable but
not mandatory
- Solid foundation in project disciplines such as issue management, scope
management, status reporting, change management, project risks and quality
management
- Excellent facilitation, organization and communication skills
- Ability to develop professional presentations
- Proven record of establishing new business processes a definite asset
- Team building and conflict resolution skills
- Understanding of service quality principles and practices.
