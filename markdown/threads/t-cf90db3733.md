I don't know of any LinuxPPC, MkLinux or for that matter Linux68K port of a 
COBOL compiler so I'd guess the original poster is out of luck. The only 
MacCOBOL I know of is a very old port of Micro Focus Level II COBOL from about 
1985 and I haven't seen that around for years. It's probably not 32bit clean 
for a start.

emulation and the many PC compilers or through UAE and run the Amiga's NRCOBOL.
