I'm not sure about how to do this in Power Cobol, but in
general, you can send a message to the listview that will
register a callback function to do your sorts--this gives you
absolute control over the row ordering.

A lesser, but simpler alternative, is to format the date such
that it alphanumerically sorts as well.  (ie, "yyyy/mm/dd")
