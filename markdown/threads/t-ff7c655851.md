```
I have been considering a Master's degree in the C.S. field, either
e-commerce, or straight C.S.  In your opinion, is an advanced degree a
worthwhile pursuit?

I am currently a programmer with a B.A. and additional schooling through
certificate programs, which my company paid for.

My boss feels that advanced C.S. degrees are worthless, and that an M.B.A.
is much more beneficial in the I.T. area, as it gives the student practical
business knowledge.  He feels that most University C.S. programs are a joke,
and that everything in this business can be learned on the job.

Thoughts?

Advice from people in positions where they actually *HIRE* people is greatly
appreciated.  What do you look for in a candidate, and do advanced degrees
give a prospective employee an advantage over a similarly qualified
non-master's candidate?

Thanks-
```
