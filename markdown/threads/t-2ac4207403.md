```
A PDF version of the "latest and greatest" draft of the next Standard (i.e.
CD 1.8) is now available from a link on the J4 external web-site at:
  http://merant.microfocus.com/Standards/

I am looking into whether or not there will be any method for PURCHASING a
hard-copy of this draft.  (Free hard-copies will not - as far as I know - be
available for non-committee members.) I will keep comp.lang.cobol informed of
the status of this.

NOTE WELL:
   This draft will be used for an *internal* "mini quality review."  The
purpose for the "mini quality review" is for the committee to clean up the
document so that it can be
sent for the next public review."  Hence, general (external) comments are not
being solicited.  If you find any specific ERRORS (not requests for
"enhancements" or "deletions"), then please feel free to pass them on to me,
and I will try and get them to the committee.
```
