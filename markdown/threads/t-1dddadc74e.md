DESCRIPTION
To gather and analyze information for developing and modifying data processing
systems and programs, prepare system and program specifications and record
layouts. To design and code the most complex programs.

Principal Responsibilities:
1. Define requirements for improving or replacing systems.
2. Perform interviews and other data gathering.
3. Document current systems operations.
4. Prepare specifications for systems improvements.
5. Develop systems testing and conversion plans.
6. Develop accurate and efficient computer programs.
7. Modify existing programs.
8. Test Programs thoroughly.
9. Provide guidance to and assist in training less
   experienced Information Systems personnel.

Bachelors degree Computer Science or related.
Minimum 5 years experience programming in COBOL, CICS, JCL, and DB2.

Training in systems analysis with special emphasis in user relations, data
gathering techniques, written and oral communications, and management
information systems.

Ability to work effectively under deadlines and extreme workloads. Competence
in fulfilling any additional responsibilities which may be assigned.
