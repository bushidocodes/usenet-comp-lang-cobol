>
> And, How Can I use asambler with Cobol?

That's a function of the specific COBOL you are using. In _general_, you may
link or call dynamically modules written in any source language, provided
you respect the calling conventions. Check your RM manual for specifics for
the RM compiler.

Can't help you on the ACCEPT question, but that sure looks
compiler-specific. In your RM manual, you might want to look under
SPECIAL-NAMES and/or SCREEN SECTION to see if RM requires something special
to handle extended keys via the ACCEPT verb.
