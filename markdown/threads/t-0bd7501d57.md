Guiseppe:

What do you exactly mean with DL/I?

DL/1 or DL/I (same strange name as PL/1/I) has two separate meanings in
the big blue world:
1) The DOS/VSE variant of IMS.
2) The "language" (big word for a bunch of calls) within IMS to access
the database.

Anyway, the differences between IMS on VS/MVS/ESA and on DOS/VS/VSE are
not that big. Getting a book about one can help with the other. (There
are some nasty details in the differences however, but thats for you to
figure out whenever it comes that far).
