Luis:

You must explicitly LINK the intrinsic function support with your
application. Your LINK statement should be of the (general) form:

LINK myprog+cobintfn.obj,,,

where the intrinsic function support resides in the MF supplied module
COBINTFN.OBJ. You may also have to experiment with the libraries to
resolve externals used by COBINTFN.OBJ...
