Are Your Business Applications Ready for the 21st Century?

If you're like the majority of IS shops, the answer is "probably not."

Why? Most business applications that rely on date calculations simply
aren't equipped to handle a date containing two zeroes.

o How many applications will that effect at your shop?
o Where are they?
o How do you make the right changes?
o What will it cost?

Defuse The Time Bomb in Your Systems
