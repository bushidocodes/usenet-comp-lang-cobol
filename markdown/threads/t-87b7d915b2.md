Midwest Area Micro Focus Users Group

1995 - A New Year!

Welcome! our many new and old friends of Micro Focus products to the
1995 vintage of the Midwest User Group. My name is Jeffrey Benner, and
I am president of the User Group.

For those of you who may be new to us, the Midwest Area Micro Focus
Users Group serves the users of the Micro Focus family of software
development tools in the mid-western United States. Benefits of
membership include:

 Attendance at our quarterly meetings. The all-day meetings give
the Micro Focus user a chance to meet directly with Micro Focus
representatives and other users of the Micro Focus products. SIGs
allow you in-depth discussion of your area of interest. Speakers
offer presentations on more general topics of interest.

 Membership list. You will be given information on how to contact
the other Micro Focus users in your area.

 A general purpose network of Micro Focus users to help you enhance
your productivity and understanding of the product.

This is our fifth year. User group tends to vary in size between 120 and
300 people.

We will probably use the Holiday Inn at O'Hare for all meetings this year.
