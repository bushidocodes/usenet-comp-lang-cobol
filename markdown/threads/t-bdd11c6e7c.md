Hi, All -

I am at a client site in Indianapolis which *may* (but not certainly)
have need of some additional Honeywell Bull expertise for maintaining
a legacy manufacturing system. It's the usual: GCOS, TSS, COBOL-74,
IDS/II data base (only a little of that, and maybe none at all.)
There's a little bit of programming, but actually a fair amount of
utility work just doing data maintenance on the system against indexed
sequential files.

Project is almost certainly through December, 2012 (the deadline for
the new replacement system), but if past experience holds true, that
replacement system deadline will slip and so will your engagement, to
probably two years or more.

Rate - uhhh, very modest, say, $40-$45/hour 1099 or corp-to-corp, W-2
adjusted downward accordingly.

HOWEVER, I don't believe there will be many takers at that rate when
the client goes outside for people, so FEEL FREE TO TELL ME in all
sincerity AT WHAT RATE you would seriously consider this opportunity.
Be fair to yourself without giving the appearance of taking advantage
of this client, who I like. Per diem is doubtful, so include it in
your rate.

Public CLC posts welcome of course, but so also are private e-mails to
me at klshafer<<at>>att.net.

Thanks,

basis of "keywords".

organizations do it by Keyword because HR jealously guards their province of
