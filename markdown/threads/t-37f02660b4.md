Quick Update...

Cobol MVS orders are starting to pile up, clients are CRYING cause
they're not getting any resumes. Recruiters are starting to laugh out
loud..."You want HOW MANY Cobol programmers?"

Job orders are being marked "Urgent", "Top priority". Management (in
there infinite wisdom) is mandating overtime for recruiters -
Saturdays, Sundays... The Unix server w/ the candidate database is
starting to freak out we've done some many searches for Cobol
programmers.

Bottom line... There aren't any more Cobol Crankers under the Christmas
Tree. If you want one, you gotta pay.

Regards,
Steve the Recruiter - "An error doesn't become a mistake until you
refuse to correct it."
