I am afraid that I must say "wrong, Randall".

The *SEMANTICS* of the ASSIGN clause are totally implementor-defined.  The
SYNTAX is not at all implementor-defined.  A conforming implementation
ABSOLUTELY  POSITIVELY must support both a literal and a system-name.  (If
you want to see how the Standard documents a feature where the implementor
is allowed to support only one of multiple options, read the RERUN clause
syntax rules.)
