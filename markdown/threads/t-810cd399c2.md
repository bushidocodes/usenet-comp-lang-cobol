Environment variables are in the form:

     name=value

where the 'name' is something that the program will look for.  I don't
think that the compiler or run-time will be looking for 'PCOBOL32',
but will be looking for 'LIB' or 'PATH'

You seem to have just made up the 'names' but you need to find out
what they actually should be.
