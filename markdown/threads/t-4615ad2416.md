I am certain that others will comment on this, but I think your 3 best bets
are:

 -  IBM VisualAge COBOL (most similar to CURRENT IBM mainframe COBOL - with
and without OO - fairly pricey)
 -  Micro Focus COBOL (either NetExpress or MainframeExpress if you want to
spend a lot of money - or Personal COBOL if you don't need to product
"production" code)
 -  Fujitsu COBOL (V3.0 can be downloaded for free, V4 is available for
purchase)
 - (I tend to "ignore" AcuCOBOL for this type of use - but others may
recommend it instead)

Several of these are available on CD's with some of the current COBOL books.
I would strongly suggest that you get one of the cheap/stripped-down
versions first - to see if it meets your desires/needs.
