$30-35 per hour contracting. I believe salaries for FTEs and salaried
contractors are be in the &45-$55k range. Or at least that is what the
consulting companies are paying W2'ed employees. The exact rates
they're charging clients are much more.
I've been contracting non-stop in Phoenix since I moved here 3 yrs ago.
I am on my third contract. As with most cities, there is not enough
COBOL programmers to supply the demand. Expect rates to begin to rise,
especially as Year2000 projects ramp up. Don't take any long term
contracts. And monitor az.jobs and this newsgroup, there are some good
resources here.
