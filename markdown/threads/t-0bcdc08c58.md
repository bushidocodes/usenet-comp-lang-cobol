```
Short Story:
How do I get a "Removal Key" so I can take NetExpress off one computer and
put it on another?

Long Story:
I am an independent developer and I use NetExpress for a couple clients
(they started out on MicroFocus COBOL years ago and I have been supporting
them).

I have used a notebook computer as my main system for a couple of years but
I now have a new desktop system that I would like to use for development.  I
tried for a couple months to get a second key for my desktop but I have been
unable to get Merant to see things my way.  I would like to develop on the
new system, but keep NetExpress on the notebook for when I visit clients, so
I can make last minute changes if needed.  All other software vendors have
given me the second key, but Merant won't do it.

Now no one answers me at all.  I think I have been 'black-balled' for
complaining for 2 months.  I have tried to find out how much a second
license would cost, but I haven't been able to get that answer.

Then I decided to remove NetExpress off my notebook, but I don't know how to
get a 'removal key' (whatever that is).  (I decided I could just dial in to
my main computer when I was on the road to do any last minute changes).  No
phone or email message has been returned.  So I am stuck.  I have a great
new machine and the 30 day use of NetExpress ended almost 2 months ago.

WHY IN THE WORLD WON'T MERANT LET ME RUN THEIR SOFTWARE ON TWO COMPUTERS?
WHY WON'T THEY TELL ME HOW MUCH A SECOND LICENSE COSTS?  WHY WON'T THEY GIVE
ME THE SUPPORT TO MOVE THE SOFTWARE TO A DIFFERENT
COMPUTER??????????????????????????????

Annual Renewal Information:
I received my renewal for NetExpress.  It is $650 and there is no mention of
any runtime fees.  I don't know if that means there are no fees, or if they
will surprise me with the additional charges after I pay the $650.
```
