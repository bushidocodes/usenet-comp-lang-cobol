I'm a Brit COBOL programmer working in the US. I would say you get better
rates working as a contractor through an agency in the UK assuming that you
will be working in London or the south-east of England. However that would
need to be weighed against the higher cost of living in that part of
Britain, plus the cost and hassle of commuting. You also need to think
about the kind of lifestyle you would be prepared to put up with e.g
travelling with the masses twice a day on a long commute. A little
searching on the web should get you to a few sites that provide average
contract rates in different parts of the US and Britain and cost of living
indices. Good luck!
