It is extremely unlikely that Fujitsu NetCobol 7 for Linux would be
suitable.  It has no SCREEN SECTION or ADIS capability.  It does do
ANSI ACCEPT/DISPLAY but does not implement ACCEPT/DISPLAY AT and
requires that some other user interfacing be used, such as SP/2, CGI,
or perhaps some form of CICS.

Also be aware that these may be supported on RedHat 7.2 but may not be
on 8.0 - I haven't checked recently.
