Hi Greg, you seem to have two problems here when I try this with a DOS
compiler. Firstly, transform is shown in the Language Reference as an
COBOL extension from the OSVS dialect of COBOL, so you will have to use
OSVS as a compiler directive. Folk sometimes set this as a default (more
usually under DOS, Windows or OS/2 rather than Unix) but to be sure use
$set OSVS at the front of the program.

Without this "transform" is a paragraph label rather than a reserved word
and hence your error. Dunno why you get that error message, I get:
transform j '1' to '0'.
* 9-S**
** '.' missing

But even with the right directive, you have missed out a reserved word:
transform j '1' to '0'.
* 83-S********
** Reserved word missing - FROM expected.

Hope this helps.
