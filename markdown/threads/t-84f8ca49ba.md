Is there any way the company you work for will give you an entry-level
position to start? Sometimes, the best way to get your foot in the door
is to keep bugging the right people to give you a chance. It seems that
employers don't do this as much as they did years ago, and I think that
it is unfortunate. That's how I got started. A lot of the people in
the ITS department I work in started that way and are some of the most
dedicated and experienced people we have. Maybe you should discuss the
possibility, if you already haven't, with someone in your organization.
