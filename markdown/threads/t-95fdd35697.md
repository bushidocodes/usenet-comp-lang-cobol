Your told your application to use "Windows Standard" colors and they
probably look swell on your machine.

The user, however, can specify whatever shades he wants for
WindowBackground, ButtonFace, etc. These choices, often done while drunk,
are often bizarre or incompatible.

Your alternatives are two: 1) Use your own colors (Standard Colors) or
2)Leave things alone under the theory that if the user wants goofy colors,
that's his problem.

You may test your program under various renditions of windows 'standard'
colorizations to point out any wild combinations.
