Do as much screen editing as you can. Assume your operators will
enter something dumb, because eventually they will. It's difficult
and boring to write, but worth the effort to get your data entered
cleanly. It's far better to force your op. to enter something right
than to get them to go back and correct it later.

When an error is encountered, display a suggested alternative.

For validation codes that get changed often, use tables that can be
updated online.

Consider security features such as only allowing a supervisor to
delete a record.

That's a start. Hope it helps.
