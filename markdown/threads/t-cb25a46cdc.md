Susan A. Allen gave some very good advice.

If you do not have a degree, jobs are harder to find. Many companies insist
on a degree, and will not hire people without one.

If you decide not to take a class, you will need a COBOL compiler with
associated documentation and a COBOL tutorial. The machine and operating
system you use will partly determine your choice of compiler and
documentation. Thus, you should post an email to comp.lang.cobol asking for
advice about which COBOL compiler to get for your computer and operating
system.
