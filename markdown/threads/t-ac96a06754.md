- **From:** scm@bolo.freeserve.co.uk (Shaun C. Murray)
- **Date:** 1998-11-13T00:00:00
- **Newsgroups:** comp.lang.cobol
- **Message-ID:** `<364c5a4a.85833281@news.freeserve.net>`

```
Came across an interesting date windowing problem recently.

A client was using date windowing in their software as a quick fix for
Y2K. So they'd changed their software to say dates before '93' were
treated as 2093 and after were 1994 which was when their software
first went in. They tested everything, worked perfectly and so shipped
it.

Then users in Saudi Arabia started complaining. It's 1491 currently in
Saudi!

So, be careful with your date windowing routines people.
```
