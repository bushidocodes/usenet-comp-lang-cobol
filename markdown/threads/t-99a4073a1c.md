Microsoft no longer sell their PC COBOL compiler. They stopped about 2-3
years ago.
At that time support for the product reverted to Micro Focus (MS-COBOL was a
rebadged
version of Micro Focus COBOL anyway). Micro Focus ran an upgrade scheme for
Customers at the time as the MS COBOL product was 1 or 2 releases behind our
own current product.

I suspect it would be illegal for you to download a copy of Microsoft COBOL
as you would
be breaking the licence agreement with Microsoft and Micro Focus - beware!

Micro Focus still market a number of PC based COBOL products which you can
choose from:

Personal COBOL - low-cost educational product. Not designed for production
use.
COBOL 3.4 - 16 bit product for DOS, Windows and OS/2.
COBOL 4.0 - 32 bit product for Windows 95/NT and OS/2.
NetExpress 2.0 - 32 bit product for Windows 95/NT.

If you would like further information then I would suggest you contact your
local
Micro Focus Sales office.
