Sr. Business Analyst (Subject Matter Expert)
This position is a Multiple Year Contract position and is located at
Baltimore, MD

QUALIFICATIONS
Our client is in a need of a Senior Level Business Analyst with Cobol
Atleast 8+ years of  technical experience in the field directly
related to the required area of expertise.
The candidate must have hands on experience with:
Knowledge of COBOL, CICS, JCL, VSAM, DB2 environment
Job Description:
Client is looking for a Business Analyst to assist the client with
ICD-10 remediation project. Candidate will assist team of 15 technical
analysts, programmers, and testers in developing and implementing a
solution for the legacy mainframe application.
• Knowledge of COBOL, CICS, JCL, VSAM, DB2 environment.
• Elicit requirements using interviews, document analysis,
requirements workshops, surveys, site visits, business process
descriptions, use cases, scenarios, business analysis, task and
workflow analysis.
• Critically evaluate information gathered from multiple sources,
reconcile conflicts, decompose high-level information into details,
abstract up from low-level information to a general understanding, and
distinguish user requests from the underlying true needs.
• Proactively communicate and collaborate with external and internal
customers to analyze information needs and functional requirements and
deliver the following artifacts as needed.
• Utilize your experience in using enterprise-wide requirements
definition and management systems and methodologies required.
• Strong analytical and product management skills required, including
a thorough understanding of how to interpret customer business needs
and translate them into application and operational requirements.
• Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to
interact professionally with a diverse group, government client,
managers, programmers and testers.
• Develop requirements specifications according to standard templates,
using natural language.
• Collaborate with developers and subject matter experts to establish
the technical vision and analyze tradeoffs between usability and
performance needs.

Preferred skills: Previous health care environment experience.


Interested contact
