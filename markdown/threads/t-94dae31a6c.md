```

I have been looking for a job for a while now, and yesterday I was
lucky enough to talk to a recruiter who told me about the web sites that
the recruiters use. The four he mentioned are:

www.dice.com
www.occ.com
www.headhunter.net
www.careermosaic.com

These are all sites that the headhunters use, and they seem to work
well. I would say that if you post your resume at these sites you will
have many many calls from recruiters. If your resume has COBOL in it,
especially mainframe, your phone will probably ring off the hook. Mine
started at 7 AM the day after I posted my res there. This seems to work
much better than searching the newsgroups.

JR
```
