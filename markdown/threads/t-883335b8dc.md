I am mostly familiar with VMS.  There, you can compile with MAP
option, specifying /map=(alpha,declared) which will give two
dictionaries of data elements, the first in alphabetic order and the
second in declared order.  Add /CROSS for a cross-reference, i.e.
$ cob/copy/list/cross/map=(declared, alpha) myprog

The "copy" option expands copylib inclusions.
