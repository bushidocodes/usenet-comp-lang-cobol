If you're the actual client or end user looking for consultants, you can
post to news://alt.computer.consultants.ads.norecruiters and
http://www.realcontracts.html and I wish I could remember more. These 2,
especially the newsgroup, are for actual clients only, not recruiters or
brokers or consulting firms in the usual sense. They allow small consulting
firms like ourselves that are more like co-ops or groups of independent
consultants practicing together and especially doing in-house projects, but
the usual broker/recruiter type postings will produce only hostility and
will alienate your best candidate prospects.

On the other extreme are DICE etc. which are either for brokers only or are
of little use to end clients because they're so flooded by brokers that the
real clients are hard to find. This is why many of us never even look at
news://alt.computer.consultants.ads and headhunter.net and computerjobs
etc. any more, and especially DICE which is notorious for brokers lying by
setting the "Independent Contractors OK" flag to make it through our
filters when they really want to raid us for their own payroll.

In between are Reqs@rmpcp.com and http://www.scguild.com and I forget what
else, actual clients are preferred but brokers post there too. Also
http://www.icca.org for searching only, no posting capability yet.

Be sure never to post ads to news:alt.computer.consultants - it's only for
business discussions, not free technical support or for recruiting or other
ads.

If you need any contractors or consultants anywhere in the USA, please
check http://rmpcp.com/members.html for a partial list of our people,
including 2 available mainframe Cobol programmers right here in Dallas.
