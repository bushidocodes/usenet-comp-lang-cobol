You need to know the internal structure of each record in order to
decode any packed decimal numbers that may be present.  There may be
numeric fields coded in BCD, or in some semblance of IBM
packed-decimal (similar to BCD but includes a sign nybble in the
low-order byte), or in binary (either big-endian or little-endian, in
various lengths, probably 1, 2, or 4 bytes).  If the file has multiple
record types, you may not be able to count on finding structured
numeric data at the same displacement in every record.
