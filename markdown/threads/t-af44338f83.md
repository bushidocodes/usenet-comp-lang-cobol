```
This recruitment is for a permanent, full-time, Analyst/Programmer IV, who
will work in the Section of Maternal Child and Family Health, Division of
Public Health. This position serves in the Special Supplemental Nutrition
Program for Women, Infants and Children (WIC) in the Juneau Office of the
Nutrition Services Unit. Moving expenses are negotiable.

This position works with other analysts, programmers, clerical staff and
nutritionists to operate and maintain the WIC Information System. This
system is presently a complex desk-top computer DOS application with
components on over twenty processors at clinics across the state. The
system is being converted to client-server software currently under
development. The system primarily provides client case management,
financial accounting, office automation, and management reporting at state
and local levels.

The Division is looking for an individual with programming and systems
analysis experience in a highly visible production environment.
Organizational and communication skills are required along with a high
level of technical proficiency to meet daily demands of users, software and
hardware. Interactive programming and COBOL advanced professional level
capabilities are needed for this position. Technical experience with any
of the following may also be valuable: Relia relative and indexed files,
MPB COBOL, MS SQL, Windows NT, Carbon Copy or Powerbuilder.

Salary: $3,857/mo.

For more information about the job and information on applying:

http://www.state.ak.us/local/akpages/ADMIN/dop/rb/21-98.htm
```
