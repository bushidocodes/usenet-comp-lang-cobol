Daniel,
  It was several products ago that IBM "dropped" that document.  (My memory
is that the original AD/Cycle SAA COBOL/370 had one - but none since then
did.)  When this manual was "first dropped,"  I (semi-officially) complained
to the IBM COBOL DOX people - but was basically told that the thing had
gotten "too big" and that with people using online documentation (and help)
it was no longer (thought to be) needed.

I still disagree with the decision - and suggest that you contact:

 A) IBM COBOL DOX people (via the address in any manual)
 B) You IBM "sales" channel

requesting that this manual be "resurrected".

P.S.  The other manual that I REALLY miss is the "cross-manual index" that
was available in very early releases of VS COBOL II - but not since.
