The most popular COBOL book is the best selling "Teach Yourself COBOL in 21
Days, by Mo Budlong. It is used as the basis for courses in Colleges, private
training establishments, government agencies and corporations across the world.

The book teaches the basics of COBOL. If you study the book and practise the
lessons using an editor or compiler, you will get a good solid grounding in the
fundementals of the language.

For more advanced learning, there is also the "COBOL Cook Book." of
Professional programmers tips and tricks, and "COBOL, Dates and the Year 2000"
which gives a thorough and detailed technical view of the subject.

Depending upon where you plan to use your COBOL skills you can than study up on
the tools and special skills for that environment. I understand the Murach
books are very good.

Best wishes,
Helen Budlong
