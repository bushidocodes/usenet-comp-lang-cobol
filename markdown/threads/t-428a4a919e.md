The TCB, JSCB, TIOT, UCB and JFCB control block haven't changed much in 30
years, and very little since the early 1990s, so that's NOT the problem. 
I haven't seen the code you're trying to get working, but if you pull the
address of the JFCB from the TIOT and get garbage instead of the expected
dsname, it's probably because your job runs with SWA=ABOVE and you need a
SWAREQ macro to convert the JFCB "TTR" to a 31-bit address.
