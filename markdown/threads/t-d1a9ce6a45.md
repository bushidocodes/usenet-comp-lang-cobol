Depends upon your answers to the following :-

(a) Are you evaluating N/E against the other PC Compilers - if so then
you should go to their respective sites and make your own evaluation of
what each has to offer and determine the best fit to your needs

(b) If you have concluded that N/E is the product for you - what is your
existing background, are you comfortable with Windowing techniques etc.
How or what do you want to use N/E for :-

- OO/GUI - Frankly OO is a snip - and I'll give it a 2 - providing you
read a couple of background books - go to objectz.com

 - GUI - 'particularly' if you are not into Windows already - it hits 9
or 10, (lack of examples - have to keep referring back to the C text
books for examples) - if you persist with a combination of OO/GUI you
can do a lot more than the other approaches - but it's that dreaded 9/10
factor.
