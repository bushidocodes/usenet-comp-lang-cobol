PIC X(4) COMP-5 is equivalent to PIC S9(9) COMP-5
with no truncation.

PIC X(?) is used to allocate in terms of bytes rather than digits.

PIC X(4) COMP-X is another strange combination. It is equivalent
to PIC 9(9) COMP-5 with no truncation.
