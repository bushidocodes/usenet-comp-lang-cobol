Hi John --

If you can get to a bookstore, or a web bookstore (barnesandnoble.com,
amazon.com) you can buy a book "Cobol for Dummies" for $22 to $29 and it
has in it a CD with a couple of Cobol compilers to use and sample.

If you have the book, you can claim to have a Cobol compiler on your
machine.

You will also get a kind of review, but the dummies books aren't really
good at that. There is source code in them.

There is a second "dummies" book which is a pocket guide to Cobol, has
all of the language elements defined and sometimes illustrated and it is
$14.95 or so.
