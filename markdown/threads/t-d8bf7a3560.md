JOB ANNOUNCEMENT

NSP Inc., A leading retail solutions provider located in
Richmond, California, has openings for the following position :

JOB TITLE: Programmer/Analyst
STARTING DATE: Immediately

SUMMARY OF JOB DUTIES:

* Preparation of program or subsystem specifications for other team
members or self use, as well as coding and testing of online and batch
programs.

* Analyzes, designs, prepares and tests programs according to supplied
functional specifications and NSP standards.

* Develops and/or maintains programs which support client operations.

* Participate in the design and execution of system test plans.

* Communications with appropriate NSP personnel regarding scheduling and
operation problems and software problem resolution.

* Analyze and resolve program and system problems.

* Support client systems, interfacing with the client on an as-needed
basis.

* Provide day-to-day training and development of junior level team
members.


MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:

* Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience.

* Minimum 3-5 years experience in developing and maintaining on-line
data processing application systems.

* Technical programming skills including COBOL II, CICS, DB2, TSO/ISPF,
CSP and JCL

* Demonstrate abilities in:
Taking initiative
Working independently
Competency in programming and unit testing skills
Create and interpret program specifications.
Identify and solve project and business related problems
Organizational capabilities
Good interpersonal and communication skills.
