```
-------------------------------

The linker that came with your MS-DOS should work. If you have a recent
DOS that didn't include LINK.EXE, you can use one that is in most
compilers (MASM, C, etc.).

Also, you can buy an earlier DOS for a dollar or two (I bought DOS 4
the other day for $1), and use the linker from it.

All of the linkers that I have tried have worked. However, one of them
would only look on drive C: for the RUN-- modules. All of the others
didn't have that problem. One of them required my LINK command to be

link name,,,cobol650,, instead of link name,,,cobol650

Hope you find one.
```
