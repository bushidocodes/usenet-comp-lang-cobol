Normally news.groups doesn't deal with alt. groups, except that I'd like
to point out that since 'alt.cobol' has now been upgraded to the 'big 7'
group comp.lang.cobol, that administrators of systems may want to now
alias alt.cobol to comp.lang.cobol, then eventually eliminate alt.cobol
since it now has a formal 'big 7' home.

I mentioned this earlier before the newsgroup was created; I wanted to
reemphasize the point because now that a 'big 7' group exists, this
should siphon most of the traffic away from 'alt.cobol'.

Again, those who worked to get a 'big 7' group should take another bow;
you've earned it.
