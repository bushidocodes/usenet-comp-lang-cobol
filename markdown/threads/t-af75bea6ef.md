There are a few.  The one I use is called TinyCOBOL, and is available
at http://tiny-cobol.sourceforge.net/.

Another effort, also hosted by the good folks at SourceForge, is
called OpenCOBOL and can be downloaded from
http://open-cobol.sourceforge.net/.  I have never used openCOBOL, so I
couldn't tell you the differences between the two compilers.

Apparently, there is also a product from the Kompany called KOBOL.  It
can be downloaded from the Komany's website at
http://www.thekompany.com/products/kobol/, and works only with KDE
libraries installed.

Hope this helps.

Regards,
