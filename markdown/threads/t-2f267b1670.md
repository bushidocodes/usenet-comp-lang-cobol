  05  Group1.

    Move all "X" to Group1
    INITIALIZE of GROUP1.

next Standard to "initialize to value" which will "reset" the 1st 10 bytes to
their initial values.)

What's so "old fashioned" with moving SPACES to the group level and
moving ZERO to the Packed-Decimal field ? This is MUCH more efficient
that an INITIALIZE statement, which will generate several more
instructions. The old fashioned method will ALSO initialize the FILLER.
The INITIALIZE verb has its place, but should be used with caution as
it can potentially generate an extrodinary number of instructions in an
IBM Big-Iron (MVS/VSE) environment.
