DOS no longer exists, true. But almost all DOS programs execute properly
under W9x, W2K, NT, and, of course, XP. For W2K, NT, and XP, the OS emulates
DOS, but the result is the same. We still have over 200 customers running
our old DOS inventory system on a variety of platforms (W95, W98, W2K, NT,
and, of course, XP) with little trouble. What problems we do have stem
almost exclusively from WinModems and WinPrinters.

From time to time we have to update one of these old programs: the compiler,
Realia 4, runs swell under XP also.
