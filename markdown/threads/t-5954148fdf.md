 One way is to  redefine your output line 40 times with two sub-fields under
each redefine: sub-field 1 is of length 1, 2, 3 etc. up to 40; the second
being a filler to make up 80 ch.  Then count the letters in your input text
string - in this case "Hello world" - and work out which of the redefines you
should move it to.

Crude but effective.
