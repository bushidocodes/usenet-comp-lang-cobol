1993, Micro Focus will assume all product support for
Microsoft in both U.S. and international markets. ..."

5.0 can trade it in for the current release of Micro Focus COBOL for
only the cost of shipping and, as another option, Micro Focus will
continue to support Microsoft COBOL 5.0. Owners of earlier versions
of Microsoft COBOL can upgrade to the current version of Micro Focus
COBOL for a price of only $179 plus shipping. The letter gives a
Micro Focus 800 upgrade number, and an address at Micro Focus of who
to write to for further questions about this new relationship between
the two companies. The letter is signed by John. S. Bennett, Microsoft
COBOL Product Manager. Marian Sichel, Micro Focus Vice President,
Strategic Relationships name is given at the end of the letter, but this
name was not signed.

Perhaps this clarifies the subject a bit more.
