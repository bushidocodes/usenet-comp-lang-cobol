For what it is worth;

You can always parse through the current windows' on your computer, and
obtain the window handle of your emulator by checking the window title
(presuming there is at least one word in the window title that will identify
the window as a terminal window), then you can have your application send a
print screen command to that particular window and finally you can grab that
print screen from the clipboard. This will however feed you with a graphical
image of the screen, so I don't know if that is suitable. If it is, you
don't have to do anything with the emulator or the Cobol app.

Let me know if this sounds interesting and I can provide you with directions
for how to do a print screen.
