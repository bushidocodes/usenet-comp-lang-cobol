mainframe listservs asking for help with IEBPTPCH. This was clearly
someone who has been struggling with IEBPTPCH for a while. As a
recruiter, you may not understand the implications of this so I will
explain it to you.

IEBPTPCH is the "print" command of MVS. Just as you would "print" a file,
in MVS, we IEBPTPCH a dataset. Some of us will REPRO a dataset but that's
another affectation.

That call for help tells me that somewhere, an MVS shop has been raided,
downsized, to the point that no one left understands IEBPTPCH. An MVS shop
is usually a Fortune 5000 scale organization.

If no one there can get IEBPTPCH going, they're doomed. They may not know
it yet... if they were smart they would pay whatever it takes to bring in
a few dozen MVS gear-heads... WHATEVER IT TAKES.

So, George, it is against this background that you are attempting to
recruit programmers. Use all the resources you have available, if you have
a nice high number, I recommend salaries on the sunny side of $100K/year,
it would serve you to post the number, don't be shy, don't be coy, shout it
