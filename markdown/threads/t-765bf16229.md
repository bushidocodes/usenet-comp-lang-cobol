It's a very hard subject.
In fact, 'call by number' are not actual. You should prefer 'call by name'
wich does the same thing, except for those of 'call by number' which are still
documented.
These call are not all Unix/Dos equivalent. For screen io, they are (but you
have to take care with terminfo). Of course, for drive access and so on, they
are not equivalent.
If you really want a list, i suggest you to use XM debugger, it will be very
precious.
