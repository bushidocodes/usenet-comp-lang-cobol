Blue Lobster Software of Rochester, NY is looking for mainframe veterans
who want to walk away from tremendous consulting rates and help us build
cutting edge data access tools. Extensive experience with some of CICS,

this newsgroup and be revered by your coworkers. Feared if you like,

something... Ideally we would like to find someone to hire full time.

we need. We would probably limit the "learn as much as you care to"
part of the job for a consultant. ;)

If you find the CICS Java Gateway or Web Bridge interesting, check out
our web site (www.bluelobster.com) or reply to me at jcotter at
bluelobster dot com.

Jim
