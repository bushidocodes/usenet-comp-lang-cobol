Depending on how/why your session was 'kicked' you might try to get back 
on the system with:
	LOGON userid RECONNECT
_IF_ your session does not think it is still taking to your old terminal 
ID, this will often get you back exactly where you were.  But, if the 
system still believes you are on working with the aborted session, 
forcing [not FORCEing, but CANCEL] your ID off may be the only 
alternative.  Try the RECONNECT.  It works for me most of the time.

Carl
