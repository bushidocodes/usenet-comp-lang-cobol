Given all the recent threads, I thought that I would pass along a portion of a 
note that I received today.

   * * * * * * * * * * ** * *

J4 and WG4 are facing a critical resource shortage, which brings our ability to 
meet current objectives into serious doubt. J4 document 07-0066 by William Klein 
(http://www.cobolstandard.info/j4/files/07-0066.doc) presents some options for 
going forward. I want to use this document to stimulate discussion and 
brainstorming on the future of COBOL and our committees. We also need to 
consider which current features might be inhibitors to full implementations of 
ISO/IEC 1989:2002 and whether we can do anything about them.

If you want to browse the J4 website for other documents of interest, see 
http://www.cobolstandard.info/j4/index.htm.
