You can get invalid data into a comp-3 field (or any other) if the
data was moved or read into a field of a higher level that contains
the comp-3 field, e.g.:

01 garbage pic x(n) value 'QPND...123ACRS...@#...'
01 hi-lev.
   05 cmp-3-fld pic S9(n) comp-3.

move garbage to hi-lev

Check to verify that your data and the data definitions correspond.
You may have the wrong file or the wrong layout.
 
To move the comp-3 field without changing the format, you can redefine
it as pic x(n) then move the redefining field.

You can move the higher level as above: move hi-lev...

For completeness, if your numeric variable is in DISPLAY format you
can use reference modification in the move to avoid conversion. E.g.
move var-d(1:5)...
