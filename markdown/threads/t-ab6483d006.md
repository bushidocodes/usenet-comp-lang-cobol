Depending upon whether you are using Byte or Word
storage mode, the contents of MATH-FIELD will be, either
'0F 00 00'  or  '0F 00 00 00', due to the COMP-5 usage
and the picture clause.

The instruction
MOVE MATH-FIELD (1:1) TO HEX-STORAGE-FIELD
should do what you need.

You could also use
MOVE FUNCTION CHAR (MATH-FIELD + 1)
    TO HEX-STORAGE-FIELD
provided you are not also using an ALPHABET clause.

The first option depends upon the precise definition of
MATH-FIELD; but is fast.

The second option does not depend upon the precise
definition of MATH-FIELD (as long as it is numeric) and
is less likely to break during maintenance; but it may
be slow.

HTH
------------------
Rick Smith
