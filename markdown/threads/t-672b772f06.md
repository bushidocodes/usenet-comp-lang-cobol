Just a quick warning regarding this book.

It appears that there are two (2) different versions of this book
both of which have the same ISBN number.

One ships with a CD-ROM containing a version of AcuCOBOL, and the
other does not.

As a training course, the version without a COBOL compiler is a lot
less useful than the one with the CD-ROM.

I'm about to attempt to return my copy (which does not contain a
CD-ROM), which is a real pain in the ass as I've waited 2 weeks for
delivery to this part of the world.

AcuCOBOL, Inc, could make life a lot easier if they made available for
general distribution the version of their product that is (supposedly)
shipped with this book. I'm still at a loss to understand why both
Acu and MF don't have versions for general release following on from
the licensing arrangement instigated by Fujitsu.
