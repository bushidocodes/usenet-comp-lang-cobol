I'm currenly looking around for a new position right now.....and here
are my thoughts:

Seems to me that the salary ranges would vary depending on where you
live. I would guess that in Vermont, 40K would be nice, but in LA you
would need 70K just to survive.

One thing is for sure, companies are just starting to realize whats
going on. And consulting companies will try to hire for a little as
they can (remember, its a cash bonanza for them too) and give a little
benifits as they can get away with.

BUT .... Cobol programmers have been in the low pay range for a while
now, so any increases are good. I'm not sure it will ever hit that
$100K+ range before 2000. Many companies are opting to replace legacy
systems (good luck for some).


Just my 2 cents.
