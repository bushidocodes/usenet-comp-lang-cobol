Colin,

There have been several releases of Cobol for mutliple platforms over the
years.Cobol can run on PCs, Mainframes, Minis (AS/400), Unix, etc etc..
In the mainframe OS/390 world, Cobol II(aka Cobol 1985) and Cobol I(Cobol
1968)
are frequently used. PC Cobol compilers include Realia and Microfocus.

While Cobol may be outdated compared to some modern languages, it is still
frequently used. It has a lot of life left. Big corporations do not want to
spend the money
to remediate their Cobol mainframe code. The scope of such projects is
staggering.

Check out this web site. It should answer the rest of your questions.
