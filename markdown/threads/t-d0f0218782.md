A progress report:
I (finally) got my copy of Fujitsu Cobol.
I'm chanrged with the maintenance of the ETK Application
Generator. (see: http://www.etk.com). The product is a
million line plus of cobol code.
I converted it all to Fujitsu Cobol in precisely one week.
ETK runs on about 15 different platforms ranging from Pc to
Unix, As/400, VAX, MVD CICS, and more...

The main problem of porting a million lines of code
is COMPILER ERRORS. So far, I've encountered NONE!!!
Last time I ported to MicroFocus, there were dozens of
errors (that is: correct Cobol code, but incorrect
generated object code).
May I use this forum to thank Fijitsu for a job well
done!!!
I'll recommend their compiler (32 bits) any time!!!
