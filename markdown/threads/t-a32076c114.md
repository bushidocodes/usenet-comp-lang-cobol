```
The answer has absolutely nothing to do with 'First Level' being an
01.  It has to do with 'First Level' being a group item in your example
above.  Group items are always Alphanumeric Display.  The class of
a group item also has nothing whatever to do with the items contained
within it.  So you must be very careful when moving group items.

01  Example-1    Pic 9(08).

The above item 'Example-1' is numeric.  Other than a few ways in which
01 level items are treated (e.g. no OCCURS), the level number has
absolutely nothing to do with what the class of an item is, which are
determined by it's Picture, Usage and Group/Elementary attributes.
```
