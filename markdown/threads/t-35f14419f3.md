When I worked in France in the mid 70s there was a sort of French
'version' of COBOL for IBM-370s. The way it worked was that French key
words were used (ALLEZ for GO TO; PLUS for ADD are 2 I remember). This
went through a pre-compiler which translated it to English and it went
through the English compiler.

All dignostics were English though I did hear that someone was trying to
develop a 'post compiler' to translate them to French. Don't know if it
ever happened though.
