Yes those prices are (roughly) comparable to other countries.  What isn't
true is that you are "buying a compiler".  What you are buying is a complete
development environment/kit (which includes a compiler).  Micro Focus (for a
year or so) sold its "Object COBOL" compiler as a separate product (in the
5-6 hundred dollar range) but for whatever reasons (never publicly stated -
but most likely having to do with lack of demand) have gone back to selling
only "complete environments" and fairly high prices (but prices that are
comparable to both the IBM and CA "full environment" prices).

P.S. (In response to another of your posts) as stated earlier in this
newsgroup, Fujitsu now has many (most?) of the cbl_ routines in a compatible
form to the Micro Focus ones.
