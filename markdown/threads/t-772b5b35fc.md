```
As a follow-up to another thread on "which compiler is best,"  I did want to
make it clear that I am *more* than happy to add web-links to the homepage
of any vendor that anyone (vendor or otherwise) can point me to - in the
COBOL FAQ.

Currently I do this for those that I have - but MOSTLY these are UNIX/PC
based compilers - and not the "proprietary" (mainframe and mid-size)
compiler vendors.  If anyone has a COBOL vendor that they would like me to
add a web-site for, please feel free to send it to me by private email - or
via the NG. Similarly my section on both "tools" and "education" are pretty
weak - but I just never seem to get around to updating those (nor do I see
doing this as a very high priority among comp.lang.cobol NG readers).  Let
me know if your "vendor of choice's" homepage is missing from the current
on-line FAQ.

The thing that I (personally) don't want to add to the FAQ is either
feature/price comparisons *or* user (happy or unhappy) reviews.  Again, I
understand the desire for these - but they are outside what I would like to
get in the middle of.  (I do usually use vendor-provided "summaries" of
their products - without comment when describing the various offerings.
Sometimes I "hold my nose" while doing so, but do feel it is the fairest
approach for *my* FAQ to take at this time.)
```
