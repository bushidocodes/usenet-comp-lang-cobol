I'm a smart guy.  I tried to learn CICS programming from several text 
books (years and years ago).  I didn't "Get it".  I finally took a 
couple courses from IBM  at their "Guided Learning Center".  The 
courses are not cheap, but not too expensive.  It might be worth your 
while to see if they are still offered (I did mine in 1989).  

There is a good reason they don't hire people with no  CICS.  It's not
"hard" but it is "special".   

If you are good at taking a task, doing it right now, then forgetting 
you ever did it, you can do CICS. <G>
