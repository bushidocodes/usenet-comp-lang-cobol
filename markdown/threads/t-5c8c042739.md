A 365-day year is 5 hours, 48 minutes, and 46 seconds too short. So we
add an extra day every four years.

That makes the year 11 minutes and 14 seconds too long, off about 1 day
in 128 years. So we skip the extra day every 100 years.

That makes the year 3 minutes and 10 seconds too short, off about 1 day
in 455 years. So we don't skip the extra day every 400 years.

That makes the year 26 seconds too long, off about 1 day in 3323 years.
So we skip the extra day every 4000 years.

That makes the year nominally 4.3 seconds too long, off about 1 day in 20
ky.

It's not necessary to remember this; you can get these figures with a
calculator whenever you need them, as I just did.
