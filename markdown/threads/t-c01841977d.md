```
For all the people who have suggested Fujitsu, I don't think you saw the
original post.  The person want to have an MVS-like work environment (JCL,
etc).  Micro Focus does offer this; Fujitsu does not.  I think that
CA-Realia may support it also.  Interestingly enough, VisualAge COBOL (from
IBM) does not - but does have the MOST identical COBOL to the mainframe
(assuming your mainframe ONLY uses the newest COBOL compilers).

I think if you REALLY want to fully emulate the mainframe environment, your
best bet is to go with MERANT (Micro Focus') Mainframe Express product - but
the other products may well be cheaper - so it will depend on how much it is
worth to you to fully emulate the mainframe environment as opposed to just
the mainframe compiler.
```
