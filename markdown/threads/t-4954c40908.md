Basically, it's Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software, like JD
Edwards One World or SAP. What that means is that it's a
comprehensive, integrated software suite which has modules for HRMS,
Benfits, Payroll, G/L, A/P, A/R, etc., all design to scale to large
companies. Typically, they have a high-capacity RDMS on the back end
(Oracle, SQL-Server), and current versions of PeopleSoft are
web-hosted, requiring only a browser on the client.
