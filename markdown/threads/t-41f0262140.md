clients...) but this is illegal and is treated like "insider dealing" in the
countries I mentioned. (I don't know what the US position is, but I would

burned with the failure of the Dot COMs or with IPOs. I DID lose some cash
in 1987, when I simply couldn't get a "stop loss" sell order executed, due
to being in Holland on my boat and all lines to the LSE busy... But I
