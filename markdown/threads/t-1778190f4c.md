Alan,

Though there are advantages to creating an EXE and one or more DLLs there is
nothing to stop you from greating GNTs and LBRs in Net Express 3.1, as you
did with your 3.4 compiler, if you wish to do so. The development
environment allows you to compile programs to GNT and package the files as
LBRs.

If you decide to switch to EXE + DLLs, you should be able to keep a similar
structure in terms of packaging to what you had before eg if you previously
had A.LBR containing programs A, B, C and D you could now have A.DLL
containing prgrams A, B, C and D. Once A.DLL is loaded all four programs can
be called from anywhere in the application, just as loading A.LBR would have
allowed before.

I hope this helps.
