BASIC FUNCTION :

Analyze business requirements and develop, implement, maintain and

ENVIRONMENT / DIMENSIONS :

Information Services provides the information processing support for
hardware and software services and the human resources required to
provide reliable, efficient and economical services to all Canadian
operations. The area is comprised of the following departments; Group
Systems, Individual Systems, Corporate Systems, Support Services,
Technical Services and Computer Operations. Services provided include
Data Control, Data Entry and Computer Operations, mainframe
application and personal computer support. All functions are directed
towards assisting customer areas by developing or acquiring new
systems and services, maintaining existing systems, services and
networks, and providing internal technical support. Positions in
Information Services are affected by economic fluctuations, insurance
industry trends and government regulations, by advances in data
processing technology and by changes in Corporate data processing
requirements. The incumbent is subject to 24 hour call and may be
required to work hours other than normal working hours.

Indeterminate

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES :

1. Design and implement application or support systems and
enhancements to effectively satisfy business requirements.
2. Evaluate and recommend alternative solutions to contribute to
efficient and cost-effective achievement of business objectives.
3. Interview user areas to collect, verify and organize information
concerning business requirements in order to develop alternative
solutions and quantify economic factors.
4. Investigate and resolve application system or data processing
support problems (eg. Processing bottlenecks, invalid processing
results) to ensure efficient operation and accurate business results.
5. Assist in task management and coordination activities by
directing, monitoring and controlling the work activities of others to
ensure the successful completion of data processing projects.
6. Provide on-the-job training and assistance i.e. regarding
programming techniques or how to use and apply testing tools, to
contribute to the development of technical and business skills of less
experienced personnel.
7. Participate in the planning and development of procedures, prepare
system documentation and provide user training to ensure the
successful operation of data processing systems.
8. Design and implement appropriate application or support system
monitoring, testing and control procedures to ensure that objectives
are accomplished as planned.
9. Maintain or improve technical and business skills by reviewing
current data processing literature, completing additional training and
contributing to projects of increasing scope and complexity.

QUALIFICATIONS / REQUIREMENTS :

- Thorough knowledge of data processing and Head Office systems
including interactive planning, design, programming and testing aids.
- Excellent analytical and problem solving skills and a detailed
understanding of business functions and data processing requirements
of user areas.
- Effective task management, coordination, and communication skills,
and the ability to direct the work of others.
