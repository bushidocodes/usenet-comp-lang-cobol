COBOL is COBOL.  It really doesn't matter what the platform is.  (And before
the purists jump on my case -  You just have to learn and adjust to the
nuances of the platform/operating system involved which isn't all too
difficult.)

I went the other way, I cut my teeth in programming on Wang VS's and then
made the jump to Client-Server COBOL programming.  Wang has (had?) some very
nice features that I miss but I have learned to adjust.

and still have fond memories of the product.

come to grips with.  I found that new Cobol programmers without VS Cobol
experience were immediately productive.
