```
well, this is as far from COBOL as you can get but so was the last
thread on drugs, taxes, et al.
Today, I turned 27.
6 years, 5 months, and 4 days ago, a 20 year old boy with a ponytail and
2 earrings had a dream.
I had just lost a pretty decent albeit highly stressful job.  While
waiting for my last paycheck, I had my life's dream burned into my mind.
I would cash that last check, pack up all I had (a few clothes and my
.44), and head west on my 76 Honda 750 Chopper.
Right before the check arrived, the Chopper (affectionately reffered to
as the "Honda Davidson") suffered an untimely demise when a woman pulled
out in front of me and totalled the bike.
As I sat and watched the sunset tonight, I say a happy-go-lucky kid
riding an old motorcycle westward into that sinking red orb to
destination unknown while I sit here burdened with obligations of a
mature, "productive citizen" such as working 60 hours a week and writing
COBOL code to run a muffler shop on the side.
Not that I'd give up what I have now willingly, but I wonder where he is
now.
If you see that 20 year old ponytailed kid with a dream inspired by
"Easy Rider", tell him to send me a postcard.  I really miss him.
```
