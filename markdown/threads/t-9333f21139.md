PROJECT LEADER

JOB SUMMARY:
o Manage an MIS project from conception through completion
insuring a quality and useful product
o Develop detailed work plans for completing systems projects
o Coordinate the activities of individuals assigned to a project
o Maintain computer programs and operational data as required
o Provide technical support to the stores and Central office
departments to fully satisfy their needs
o Perform system and computer analysis as required
o Code, test and debug computer programs and systems to be as
maintenance-free as possible
o Develop detailed project task estimates
o Report accurate task status and individual activity

REQUIRED SKILLS AND KNOWLEDGE:
o Strong communication skills (both written and verbal)
o Proven project leadership skills in a technical environment
o Good interpersonal skills
o Strong organizational and planning skills
o Ability to relate well with restaurant managers
o Demonstrated ability to apply business understanding to solve
technical problems

DESIRED SKILLS
o Knowledgeable in PC utilities, DOS, C, C++, Btrieve, and
XcelleNet
o Experience with COBOL, IDMS, ADS/O, EZT+, JCL and/or FOCUS
o Knowledge of the retail or restaurant business

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS:
o BS/BA in MIS, Computer Science, Business or Mathematics
o MBA and/or CPA a plus

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE:
o 5 years information systems experience
o Ability to perform systems analysis (both technical and
functional) and critique the analysis of others

TEMP-TO-PERM
