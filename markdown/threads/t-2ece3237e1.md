Hi Tommy,

Net Express support the calling of COM (aka ActiveX) components. It
shouldn't matter what language the component is written in as long as it
exposes an OLE Automation interface.

Check out the OLE Automation and DCOM chapter in the Distributed Computing
Online Book.

There are also some demos (supplied with Net Express) on calling Microsoft
Work/Excel which may be of some interest.

ActiveX DLLs are inprocess servers but ActiveX EXEs are out of process
servers (ie run in a seperate process from the client).

Hope this helps
David.
