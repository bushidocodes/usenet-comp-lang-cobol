John,
I migrated Wang Cobol onto a hp-9000 with acucobol gt ver 4.0
Our apps continued to run in wang extended cobol thru an emulator runtime
from a 3rd party vendor.
As far as native acucobol code running under hp's operating system, i found
it to be a really robust product...  we also combined their other
products; acuodbc and acu4gl to touch our access and oracle databases thru
odbc and embedded sql.  they also have acusql as a new product.
If your question is one of compatibilty between hp cobol and acucobol, you
might want to talk to acucorp personally, but my guess is if the code
was written with ansi-74 or 85 standards in mind, you won't have any
problems.  if you have anything specific to ask about this project, let me
know.
-doug
