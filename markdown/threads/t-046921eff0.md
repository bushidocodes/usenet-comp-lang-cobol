In Mainframe COBOL:
   x'F7' = 7 for PIC X and PIC S9
   x'D7' = -7 for PIC S9
   x'D7' = "P" for PIC X

You can reformat it for display purposes by defining a new variable;
	02  REFORMAT-NUM PIC ----,---,---,--9.999.
Then move it before displaying it;
	MOVE my-value to REFORMAT-NUM
	DISPLAY REFORMAT-NUM

Pete
