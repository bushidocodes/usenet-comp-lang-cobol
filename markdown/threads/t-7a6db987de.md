Although I don't have the title of this thread nor access to all
of the postings the theme tries to tie some significance to the
number of postings to discussion groups. Speaking as a COBOL
type from the mainframe world I would think that just the nature
of languages more common on UNIX or PC-based systems would in
general cause those discussion groups to be more active. I dare
say that the majority of COBOL application developers spend a
great deal of their time at 3270 terminals on non-Internet
accessible computers and would by default generate far less
activity. For one, I don't think you can attach any significance
to the number of postings in this discussion group as to the use,
ease, or capabilities of the particular language. That and $3.25
