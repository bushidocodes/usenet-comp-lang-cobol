I have a DOS application in Realia COBOL that I would like to convert to
Windows and am seeking advice. Frankly I haven't ever done a significant
Windows program, although I've read about it and hope that when I sit
down to do it the pieces will fit.

It looks Realia will let me recompile the app to run under windows with
a DOS-like interface. I would think I should do that first and then
begin the rest of the conversion step-by-step, beginning with the
menus.

I assume that many of the advertised "Visual" products will help me. But
I am unsure as to whether I can continue to use the COBOL program as the
driver and call new Windows modules or must call COBOL modules from one
of the Visual products.

I am sure many of you have been through this. Any advice?
