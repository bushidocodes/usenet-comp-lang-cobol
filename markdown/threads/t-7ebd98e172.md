Why yes. You have, basically 3 options.

The hardest will be to directly communicate with the Windows API, you will
need the documentation at least from the Windows SDK.

The second hardest method would be to use Micro Focus's Dialog System
product.

The easiest way would be to utilize COBOL SP II from Flexus.
http://www.flexus.com.

I have, and continue to have to use all 3. The first for some
communications programming I am doing, the second at my "real" job where
they refused to even seriously consider anything non microfocus, and the
third in my consulting business, where I develop custom business
applications using COBOL and SP2. I am, by far, most productive with SP2,
even though I spend more time working in Dialog System.
