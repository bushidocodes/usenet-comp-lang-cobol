You don't MOVE SPORTTAK TO SPORTKEUZE, so sportkeuze is uninitialized,
probably containing low-value (x'00'). The IF TENNIS, a numeric compare,
will give error 163 then. Either add the move, or (simplier) putt the three
88-levels behind SPORTTAK and remove SPORTKEUZE from working-storage.

There are other errors, see post of SkippyPB ...

I advise to use write AFTER advancing. Somehow, at least in complex outputs,
this is easier to control. ('cause the amount to advance is usually known
for the current line to be printed, not for the future coming line).

Success. The Frog
