You will be able to get the Windows version only.

www.adtools.com has Fujitsu COBOL (now NetCOBOL).
www.microfocus.com has it's own cobol also.

Both supply either a free version or a student version.

In both cases it is normally cheaper to buy one of the several books
available on the market that come with one of the compilers.

Please check http://www.cobolreport.com/faqs/cobolfaq.htm for a list
of possible books.

Also check www.amazon.com (or whatever suits you best) and search for
COBOL.
