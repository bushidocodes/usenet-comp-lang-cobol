No.  This is an empty section followed by another section.  In the procedure
division, sections are groups of paragraphs.  The word section follows their
name.  Paragraphs are names without the word SECTION following them.   CoBOL
words must be 32 characters or less.

I don't use SECTIONs in my PROCEDURE division, but it is popular in Europe.
But they hold paragraphs.  There is no longer any reason to have sections
except to perform several paragraphs with one perform.  (except for a type of
error handling you're not ready for)
