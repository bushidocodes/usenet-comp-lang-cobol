There is a "REEL" option on the COBOL CLOSE statement.
Here is a snip out of our Production COBOL source:

      ****     CLOSE DOCUMENT-OUTPUT-FILE-1, REEL POSITIONING          
      ****     THE "REEL" OPTION CAUSES IT TO NOT CLOSE THE FILE, BUT  
      ****     SKIP TO A NEW REEL WITH A CLEAN END-OF-REEL BREAK.      
               CLOSE DOCUMENT-OUTPUT-FILE-1 REEL                       

Hope this is what you need.
Pete
