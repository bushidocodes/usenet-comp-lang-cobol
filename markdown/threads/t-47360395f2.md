You can do all of those things simply by having your COBOL program
incorporate HP PCL escape sequences in your print lines. I've written
fully HP PCL compatible printer handler source libraries in COBOL for one
of my clients, UBCC, and they would license the source. (I do not know
the price.) The libraries include, CPI, LPI, tray, duplexing, & stroke
weight controls (BOLDING), and uses the ELFRING courier soft font set to
allow printing from 6 & 33CPI. They do not include line drawing
capability. If you are interested you can call Ken Garen 800 762 8222 or
e-mail to 702··.@com··e.com.
