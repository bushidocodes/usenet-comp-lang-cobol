Fair use quote from IBM's COBOL/370 Migration Guide


LABEL RECORDS Clause with TOTALING/TOTALED AREA Options
OS/VS COBOL allows the TOTALING and TOTALED options of the LABEL
RECORDS clause.

COBOL/370 does not support these options. Therefore, you must
remove any occurrences of the TOTALING/TOTALED options from the
LABEL RECORDS clause. Also check the variables associated with
these options.

In COBOL/370, you must add a LABEL declarative, and modify the
PROCEDURE DIVISION of your program to obtain equivalent
function.
