Hi,
Both Word and Excel document their own OLE interfaces:
In Excel95, load VBA_XL.HLP
In Word95, load WRDBASIC.HLP
In Excel97, load VBAXL8.HLP
In Word97, load VBAWRD8.HLP (the old Word95 interface, Word.Basic, is only 
documented in terms of the new interface, Word.Application, although both 
are supported. Look up "Visual Basic Equivalents for WordBasic Commands" 
in the help file. The new interface is much more like the Excel model, 
with sub-objects.)

Steve.
