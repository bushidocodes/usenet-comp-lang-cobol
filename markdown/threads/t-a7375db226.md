```
To ALL,

>From my reading of the FAQ about the year 2000 problem, and my
thoughts about some of the clients where I work, I believe that they
are not going to be that happy about shelling out money for the fix.

The answer I have come up with is to not sell them a year 2000 fix,
but to sell the a year 10000 fix. Think about it. When the year
rolls around from 9999 to 10000, we are going to experience the same
problems. The customers main complaint now is "Why didn't you prepare
better for the year 2000?" So I say to them, that is why we are going
to prepare for the year 10000 NOW, and that will also fix the year
2000 problem.

If you like this idea, please send donations to pdu··.@w··.com.

Thanks,

Paul
```
