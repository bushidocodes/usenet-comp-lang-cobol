joshid@nationwide.com said:

>   I need some help in interpreting SRB and TCB time for a job on MVS.
>How are these related to the elapsed time ?

They have no relationship to the elapsed time.  They represent CPU time
spent executing the instructions in your program (TCB) and CPU time spent
in system routines doing work for your job (SRB).  In most jobs, SRB time
is directly related to I/O activity.
