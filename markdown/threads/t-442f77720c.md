This question seems to be more appropriate for an Autodesk
AutoCAD forum! However, I checked on the possibilities with
Autodesk AutoSketch Version 5 (1997) and found that it may
be possible to export data from drawings in three ways: a CSV
file, the Windows Clipboard, and OLE. Any of these may be
used by a Windows compatible COBOL implementation.

The Google search:

     site:autodesk.com autocad +export +(csv OR ole)

shows 129 hits. This includes one which asks, "How do I export
text from Autocad, preferrably to a CSV or TXT file?"
