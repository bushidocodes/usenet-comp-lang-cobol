It depends on the kind of programming you are doing.
95% of all business applications are high volume record oriented processing.
If you are going to do these kind of applications, COBOL is the best
language in my opinion.
If you are doing fancy gui pc interfaces or complex system device drivers,
COBOL is not suited for that.
Use the best tool suited to solve the problem.
I think COBOL will be around for a very long time and will evolve to where
it will be appropriate for a wide range of applications.
So...in my opinion yes learn COBOL. If you get out into the real world of
business, there are millions of currently executing COBOL programs and
you are bound to run across a few of them.
