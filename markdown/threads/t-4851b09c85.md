Tell us which compiler you are using - then somebody can zero in for
you. "Error 204. Error loading main program..." doesn't look like a
Micro Focus(Merant) error - but just in case, as you've been changing
programs :-

M/F Error 204 - wrong level numbers for redefines :-

01 my-record.
   05 my-numberA	pic 9(08).
   10 my-numberB
      redefines my-numberA.
      15 my-cc		pic 9(02).
      15 my-yy		pic 9(02).
      15 my-mm	        pic 9(02).
      15 my-dd          pic 9(02).

The Level 10 against my-numberB should read "05 my-numberB"; you can
leave the Level 15s 'as is..'.
