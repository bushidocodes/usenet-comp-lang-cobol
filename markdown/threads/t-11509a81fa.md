symbolic debugging info, you even get your original datanames back.  In

decompiled code may look quite convoluted.

if it's been compiled with diagnostics in (which will give you the
identifier names and line numbers).  Depends a lot on the compiler and
machine architecture.

BUT... why do you want to do this?  Would patching the existing compiled
module be more appropriate?  Or commissioning someone to rewrite it from 
scratch - you'd probably end up with something better than the original
this way.
