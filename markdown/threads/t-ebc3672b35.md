here for all to see.
JB


Thank-you very much for your questions and hopefully we can provide the
information you need. Answering your questions I have the following
specifics:
1. OCX (ActiveX) support.
Response: We currently do not have ActiveX or OCX support in
our
PowerCOBOL offering. Our engineers are working on OCX/ActiveX support
we
will be delivering this support to our customers next year.
2. Can a user define their own objects?
Response: Not currently, but we are working on delivering
that capability.
3. Can new attributes and methods be added to existing objects?
Response: Not currently. There are specific actions which
are defined
for each object. We have heard this same request from other users,
but do
not have specific plans for this capability.
4. ODBC functionality.
Response: I think you are asking a ODBC driver question and the
user is
dependent on the support provided in that driver. Please let us
know if
there is another question, about the specifics of our ODBC support
in COBOL.
