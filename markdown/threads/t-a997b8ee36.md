I'd avoid those things like the plague. They're becoming extinct because
it's much easier to create forms and program on a computer, and once you've
done so it's much easier to update the result. In an era where computer time
was very expensive, it made sense to plan things out on paper first... but
that's no longer the case; that era is long since past. The computer is a
much better planning tool than the forms are.
