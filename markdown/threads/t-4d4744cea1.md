Doc, must it be New York? I would *strongly* urge someone with a serious
cancer problem that local doctors view pessimistically to contact M.D.
Anderson Cancer Center in Houston, Texas. They are among the very, very
best at what they do.

See their general URL: http://www.mdanderson.org/
and the thoracic oncology department:
http://www.mdanderson.org:80/centers/thoracic/specialists.htm

These people saved my life when national survival rates for my cancer
(mediastinal germ cell tumor) were in the range of 20%. Their treatment
program for a mediastinal germ cell tumor demonstrates a 90+% survival
rate. Other cancers often have similar survival rates at MD Anderson.

If the person for whom you are doing this search is in need, then I
*strongly* urge them to contact MD Anderson.

Good luck to them and I will say a prayer for this unknown (to me)
person.
