Your instructor is taking you down the corect path. While in mainframe batch
environments the external sort is very common, learning the use of SORT
[USING] [INPUT PROCEDURE IS..] [OUTPUT PROCEDURE IS...] will prepare you for
environments where external sorts are not an option, not to mention give you
general experience with SORT and MERGE applications. (In the real world, the
user always asks for data to be sorted any way EXCEPT the way it is
stored!).
