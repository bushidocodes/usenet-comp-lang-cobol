This is an unmoderated Newsgroup, Herwig. You can post ANYTHING in ANY
Language.

However, as it is echoed around the World, your chances of finding an answer
to your problem (assuming you are posting a problem) is greatly magnified by
using English.

(Simply because there is a much greater chance of finding someone who has
worked with similar software or has encountered your particular situation,
if you post in English. In many cases, this person's first Language may not
be English either...but English (or some form of it...) serves as the
"Lingua Franca" in the Modern World.

Furthermore, as German Students spend 9 years learning English and are
generally pretty fluent in it, does't it make sense to use it <G>?

I believe there ARE COBOL groups in German. Try searching in Google (use a
German phrase or keywords...)
