The IBM site (www.ibm.com) has heaps of on-line manuals (even COBOL) - tip:
when you've found the page you want bookmark it! Note - most of the IBM
manuals have sample programs, worth a quick look.

Also, check out your local bookshop - with the old MB, the COBOL section
seems to have grown (from one book to many). 'COBOL for dummies' has been
mentioned several times in this ng, in the past I have found that Que, Cybex
and Sams publish some of the better language guides.
