You should buy a book called Teach Yourself COBOL in 24 Hours by Thane
Hubbell.  It's very inexpensive ($20.00 US$ or so).

It's a great book, but even better, it includes a CD which contains
the Fujitsu COBOL compiler.

The book can be purchased online at www.amazon.com or you can buy it
at your local book store.

The ISBN number of Thane's book is 0-672-31453-3

Good luck!
