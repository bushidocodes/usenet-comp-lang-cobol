As part of the research for an ongoing project I would like to
ask for your input by completing the following survey. Please
forward a copy to your friends and/or co-workers for the purpose
outlined in the copyright section.

The objective of the survey is to determine
    - what programming tools are currently in use
    - whether their use improves productivity
    - whether there is room for improvement

If you are a programmer by profession I strongly urge you to
please take part. There are a total of 15 questions. It should
only take 10 to 15 minutes of your time.

The information you provide will be used in strict confidence.
No specific individual information relating to any part of the
survey will be released.

The data collection period is between 2 to 3 weeks from the
date of the first post.  Please reply to the original post or
e-mail your response to the following address:

    ac456@freenet.carleton.ca

I will post a summary of the findings once the data have been
tabulated.  I would like to thank you in advance for your
participation.
