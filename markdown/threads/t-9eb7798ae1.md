Hellp Paul,

You wrote:

› Does anyone know of a good web site for info on system error messages like
› S0C7, etc? Thanks.

The pertinent IBM manual is "OS/390 MVS System Codes",
document no. GC28-1780. To access it over the web:
http://ppdbooks.pok.ibm.com:80/cgi-bin/bookmgr/bookmgr.cmd/BOOKS/IEA1H704/CCONTENTS

In that manual Chapter 2, secn 176, deals with System Completion Codes
of 0Cx category.

IBM's Book Manager sites can often be quite S.L.O.W...
For a faster, and less formal, treatment of the subject, you can go to
the "OS/390 Documentation" page at Clemson University, S. Carolina:
http://dcit.clemson.edu/sig/docs/os390/os390.html
and then follow the link to "Common System Codes".
