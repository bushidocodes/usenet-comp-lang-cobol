Here is the scoop on the Linker and 650 COBOL.

MS and IBM DOS, through version 3.3 and possibly version 5.0 CAME with
LINK.EXE. Later versions of DOS and Windows 95 do NOT come with LINK. Why
they EVER had it I don't know! However, if you can find a machine with an
older DOS version, you will find LINK.EXE and be able to link the programs.
I am unaware of any other generally available source for Link.exe.
