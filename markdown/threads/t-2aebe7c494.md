You can get a copy of the COBOL 650 shareware compiler from The Flexus COBOL
Page at http://www.flexus.com

I suggest that you consider Micro Focus' Personal COBOL instead. Although
it is not a free compiler, it is quite low cost. I think you can get it
through one of the software distributors for about $150.00.

It doesn't support Windows development, but it is still an excellent tool
for learning COBOL. Another suggestion is to get one of the books on COBOL
which includes a free compiler. Most of these free compilers are limited in
the number of lines which may be compiled. Access James Fidel's COBOL FAQ
for more information on the COBOL books. A link for the FAQ is also
available from The Flexus COBOL Page. Good Luck!
