As far as I could tell Micro Focus (Fujitsu?) no longer gives away any free
version, the academic version seems to have been cancelled.

I do not know of any free COBOL for PC except the half-baked OpenCOBOL and
various other so-called "free software" projects. None of those are worth
anything. Pete hasn't chimed in yet and that is a bad sign. He would
probably know.

You can bootleg older versions of Micro Focus or Power COBOL off the net. I
don't know how much paying for a license is or how easy it is to buy
something useful for a private individual but if you can make enough to
cover the cost of a license and still have enough left over after your
project that seems like the honorable thing to do.
