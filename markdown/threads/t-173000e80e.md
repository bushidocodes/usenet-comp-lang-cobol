--begin quoted text:

Accounting programs temporarily unavailable (mc) Thu Jan  1 12:13:21 2004

--end quoted text

'Official' leap year determination:
  A year is a leap year if it is evenly divisible by 4
    unless it is also evenly divisible by 100, in which case it isn't
      unless it is also evenly divisible by 400, in which case it is.

Year 2000 was unusual in that it was a century leap year. The problem
with leap year determination is that programmers don't always use the
entire test. Since all leap years after 1900 and before 2100, with no
exceptions, can be found by the simple evenly divisible by 4 test, it
is possible that some programmers shortcut the remainder of the test,
and that code will fail for 2001, which is a non-leap century year.

Like many of us I suppose, I have always enjoyed seeing clever code and
clever algorithms. I don't know the original author of the algorithm
below, but it is the shortest COBOL leap year test I have ever seen,
with one multiply and three compares. Since 'clever' algorithms can be
unclear, I left in some comments.

           03  DW-WORK-CCYYMMDD        PIC  9(08).
           03  DW-WORK-CCYYMMDD-R      REDEFINES DW-WORK-CCYYMMDD.
               05  DW-WORK-CCYY            PIC  9(04).
               05  DW-WORK-CCYY-R          REDEFINES DW-WORK-CCYY.
                   07  DW-WORK-CC              PIC  9(02).
                   07  DW-WORK-YY              PIC  9(02).
               05  DW-WORK-MM              PIC  9(02).
               05  DW-WORK-DD              PIC  9(02).

           03  DW-LEAP-TEST            PIC  9(06).
           03  DW-LEAP-TEST-R          REDEFINES DW-LEAP-TEST.
               05      FILLER              PIC  X(02).
               05  DW-LEAP-LAST-4.
                   07      FILLER          PIC  X(02).
                   07  DW-LEAP-LAST-2      PIC  X(02).

      *    A MULTIPLE OF 4, WHEN MULTIPLIED BY 25, RETURNS A
      *      MULTIPLE OF 100 (I.E. THE LOWER 2 DIGITS = "00"
      *
      *    A MULTIPLE OF 400, WHEN MULTIPLIED BY 25, RETURNS A
      *      MULTIPLE OF 10000 (I.E. THE LOWER 4 DIGITS = "0000"
      *
           MULTIPLY DW-YEAR-CCYY BY 25 GIVING DW-LEAP-TEST.
           IF ( (DW-LEAP-LAST-2 = "00")
                 AND
                (DW-WORK-YY NOT = 00) )
               OR
              (DW-LEAP-LAST-4 = "0000")
               <year is leap year>
           ELSE
               <year not a leap year>
