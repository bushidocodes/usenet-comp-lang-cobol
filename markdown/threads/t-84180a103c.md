Changeman has some merits, but the overhead (for example, even to do a
simple compile, I must go through 3 or 4 panels and hit
[F3][F3][Enter][Enter][Enter][Enter], plus maybe another one depending on

the same box?, Are they using the full implementation?, Will they customize
(cannibalize :)) it?, etc ...
