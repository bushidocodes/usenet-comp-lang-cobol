In today's mail, I got an offer from Micro Focus - they want to sell
me VisOC for $350. Couldn't imagine how I could be more affronted,
since I paid $499 for my copy which has problems they can't (or won't
try to) work out.
Not that any serious programmer would put up with a compiler which
has no hard-copy manual anyway ... help systems have a degree of
allure, mostly to cheap marketers. But they don't solve the problem of
having the documentation between you and your work, when it ought to
be beside your work.
David
