   Do you accept that the use of symbols as opposed to words to represent

Comment:
  I fully support your use of words rather than symbols as a STYLE that best

when I use symbols versus words in MY PREFERRED STYLE.

If all of your posts are simply trying to explain why your style works for
you, that's fine.  If you think that you have reasons that should impact
other peoples preferred style, then you certainly have not succeeded so far
and others have explained why your reasons are not convincing to them.
Unless you have NEW arguments, then you can/should (IMHO) simply accept that
there is a difference in perceived advantages and disadvantages of each
style.
