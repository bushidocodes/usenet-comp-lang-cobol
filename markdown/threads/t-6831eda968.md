Hi All,

I am looking for the COBOL650 (COBOL701) compiler source code.

Does anyone have a copy, or know some one who has ?


For those of you who are not familiar with the COBOL650 compiler for
DOS, and the story behind the compiler, I will expand.

The COBOL650 (COBOL701) compiler was written by Robert Eric Noweck, from
Jesup, Georgia, USA. The binary was, and is,  distributed as shareware. 

Robert Eric Noweck died shortly after he completed version COBOL701. 

It was his wish that after is death, the source code would be placed in
public domain. Supposedly the source was to be placed only on the
AV-SYNC BBS in Atlanta, Georgia. 

The AV-SYNC BBS is no more.

So the question is what happened to the source code ?

Since the  BBS no more, I think it would nice to place the source in
public domain on the internet. I think Robert Eric Noweck would have
approved.



David
