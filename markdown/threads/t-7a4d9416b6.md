I seem to remember a request for someone to look at the
ansi standard to determine what divisions are required
in a COBOL program. Here is the answer from
page IV-29, paragraph 5.3 and 5.3.1 of ANSI X3.22-1985
(American National Standard for information system-
programming language- COBOL)

---- cut ---- cut ---- cut ---- cut ---- cut ---- cut
5.3 STRUCTURE

The following gives the general format and order of presentation
of the entries and statements which constitute a COBOL source program.

5.3.1
identification-division

[environment-division]

[data-division]

[procedure-division]

[end-program-header]
