Amir has mentioned that he is using the Microsoft Compiler V5.0 (rebranded Micro 
Focus compiler).

I can't remember whether that compiler did or did not have "full EBCDIC" support 
(e.g. CHARSET(EBCDIC)).

Does anyone in this group either still have that compiler or remember whether 
this was in that product or not?

Amir's claim is that he is evaluating capabilities of IBM mainframe COBOL.  To 
the extent that he isn't doing "serious" work and not trying to develop on the 
PC for same-results on IBM mainframes, EBCDIC/ASCII run-time behavior shouldn't 
be a "big deal".  However, if he actually DOES develop anything on the PC that 
is dependent on ASCII collation sequence (in general, not just the Special-Names 
items), and expects the same run-time results on an IBM mainframe, this won't 
work without full EBCDIC support.
