To the group,

Well I did a little search of messages posted to this newsgroup over
the past couple of months looking for our esteemed Mr. Webb (much like
he did in Deja News looking for DD) and found some interesting things.

First off, it seems he has been not only a contributor to this NG
answering questions concerning Net Express, but a questioner as well
receiving answers from others in this NG.

Secondly, I found the following (quoted exactly) by Mr. Webb in a
response to a job posting from a recruiter in the Dallas area: 

"This sounds like Frito Lay .... they still are only paying about 50%
of what other people pay ... 40-55k will get you someone with 3 or 4
years experince if that in today's wages ... duh"

It seems he is willing to criticize others for paying low wages, but
at least a rate was quoted and could be judged thusly and yet he is
unwilling to post a reasonable rate or range of rates!  This coupled
with his recent rantings and ravings about and against Mickey and DD
have led me to conclude that Sterling Commerce, Banking Systems
Division would not be an enjoyable place to work at any salary given
Mr. Webb's attitudes.  I think the word should be spread.  Having been
a manager myself, I would never have conducted myself in such a manner
before an audience of potential employees if I hoped to convince
someone to come and work for me.

But don't take my word for it.  Do your own search and draw your own
conclusions.  And Mickey and DD....keep on posting the rates notices.
It's appreciated.

Regards,
