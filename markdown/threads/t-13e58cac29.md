To start with, there are a bundle of printer options to check out:

Start/Settings/Printers/[right click on your printer]
Sharing/tick "Shared as", enter a "Share name"
Sharing/Details (or Properties/Details)
Spool settings/try (un)ticking "Print direct to printer"
Port Settings/try unticking "Spool MS-DOS jobs"
Port Settings/try unticking "Check port state before printing"

As I recall there are some provisos about networking in there
somewhere - perhaps a Win95 guru can help?
