_ 906027 Software Service Discontinuance: Selected IBM eServer zSeries and S/390 
products -- Some replacements available
    at
http://www.ibm.com/isource/cgi-bin/goto?it=usa_annred&on=906-027

Of specific COBOL interest, (check announcement for dates and specifics)
  Enterprise COBOL V3.3 is being withdrawn (replaced by V3.4)

  COBOL for OS/390 & VM, V2.2 *FULL FUNCTION* is being withdrawn (compiler

  ***

Other announced support withdrawal dates include
 - some releases of z/OS
 - some releases of PL/I and HLASM
 - some releases of IMS (and IMS tools)
 - CICS TS V2.2
 - and LOTS more

  ***

Make certain that those in your shop who need to know, do know and being your 
"migration" (upgrade) plans NOW.
