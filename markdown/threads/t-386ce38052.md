```
>I know I could seperate the days, months and years and do some calculations,

Arrange the dates in CCYYMMDD format.  You are right in that you may
have to separate the days, months, etc., depending upon the original
format of the dates.

However, you should not have to do any calculations.  Once you have
put both of the dates in CCYYMMDD format, it becomes a simple matter
to use the LESS THAN comparison to determine if one date is earlier
than the other.
```
