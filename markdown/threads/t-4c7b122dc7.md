BIX has not been associated with Byte directly now for many years, 
and is owned by Delphi Internet Corp.  

It is a very interesting place, and will probably continue plodding along
well into the future. It's pretty cheap too, something like $35/year
gets you a BIX and Delphi account, plus web space and all that jazz. 
Failry good SPAM filtering too. 

BIX is really underpopulated right now; but the folks there are people who
have massive amounts of experience in a lot of areas. (Want to know 
how to build a Solar Power Station? How about how to write a S.F. book,
or a screenplay? Or how to engineer a hunk of SCSI hardware?) 
We lack a bit in COBOL expertise right now; Don runs the COBOL 
conference, but is a very busy man, and there are only a half dozen 
or so Bixen who are active in the COBOL world. 

BIX, unlike Delphi, is a pure text based system as well.
We keep talking about webifying it, but never really seem to get 
around to doing it. : )

If anyone is interested in a Bix/Delphi account, let me know, and 
I'll see if there are any specials running. 

-Paul
