```
(posted and mailed to ils-international)

Someone in the current GUI discussion thought that
   "Nowitt Aol"
        was intended to be read as "Know it all".

It seems to me that given how totally UNRESPONSIVE this person is and how
his/her tone is turning off so many of the other comp.lang.cobol readers, it
may be that we were reading this wrong.  Possibly, this is intended to be
read as,

   "No Wit, A**Hole"

Certainly the tone and approach of the posts would be more typical of a
disgruntled former employer or customer trying to DISCOURAGE anyone from
looking at Gold Mind than from someone who actually wanted those not familiar
with the product/services to look at it seriously.

Obviously, a "disgruntled" person would explain the "anonymous" postings as
well as the silly, conflicting, and counter-productive responses that have
appeared so far.

If someone at Gold Mind wants to track this person down and verify that it is
actually someone trying to sabotage their product, I know that most of the
newsgroup would be happy.  If, on the other hand, this actually is a person
TRYING to interest people in the capabilities of Gold Mind, I strongly,
STRONGLY, suggest that they review the posts so far and take this person to a
"netiquette 101" class.
```
