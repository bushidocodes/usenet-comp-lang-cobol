```
Sorry Jerry, I have to disagree. It CAN be done.

Standard COBOL supports the JUSTIFIED clause, as follows:

01   some-data.
        12 text-field    pic x(10) JUSTIFIED RIGHT.
         [JUSTIFIED can only be specified on an elementary alpha or
alphanumeric item]

Then...

move 'elevenbytes' to text-field

results in...'levenbytes'

move 'twobytes' to text-field

results in 'bbtwobytes'   [where bb are 2 padding spaces inserted by the
COBOL system]

Contrast this with what would happen had JUSTIFIED not been specified...

move 'twobytes' to text-field

results in ...'twobytesbb'  [where bb are 2 padding spaces inserted by the
COBOL system]

Hope this makes it clear,

Pete.
```

```
The program below demonstrates a right justify routine that can
handle input and output fields of any size.  It is also very
efficient; 100,000 out-of-line performed loops on 30 byte fields,
compiled with MF COBOL 3.2 take about 0.11 seconds on my PII450.

       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID.    RJUST.
      *******************************
      *    Judson D. McClendon      *
      *    Sun Valley Systems       *
      *    4522 Shadow Ridge Pkwy   *
      *    Pinson, AL 35126-2192    *
      *       205-680-0460          *
      *******************************
       ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.

       77  INPUT-FIELD                 PIC  X(30).
       77  INPUT-PTR                   PIC S9(07)  COMP.
       77  OUTPUT-FIELD                PIC  X(20).
       77  OUTPUT-PTR                  PIC S9(07)  COMP.

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       TESTER.
           MOVE "ABC" TO INPUT-FIELD.
           DISPLAY ":" INPUT-FIELD ":".
           PERFORM RJUST.
           DISPLAY ":" OUTPUT-FIELD ":".
           DISPLAY " ".
           MOVE "ABCDEFGHIJKLM" TO INPUT-FIELD.
           DISPLAY ":" INPUT-FIELD ":".
           PERFORM RJUST.
           DISPLAY ":" OUTPUT-FIELD ":".
           DISPLAY " ".
           MOVE "ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRST" TO INPUT-FIELD.
           DISPLAY ":" INPUT-FIELD ":".
           PERFORM RJUST.
           DISPLAY ":" OUTPUT-FIELD ":".
           DISPLAY " ".
           MOVE "ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ" TO INPUT-FIELD.
           DISPLAY ":" INPUT-FIELD ":".
           PERFORM RJUST.
           DISPLAY ":" OUTPUT-FIELD ":".
           STOP RUN.

       RJUST.
           MOVE SPACES TO OUTPUT-FIELD.
           IF (INPUT-FIELD NOT = SPACES)
               MOVE FUNCTION LENGTH(INPUT-FIELD) TO INPUT-PTR
               PERFORM UNTIL INPUT-FIELD(INPUT-PTR:1) NOT = SPACE
                   SUBTRACT 1 FROM INPUT-PTR
               END-PERFORM
               COMPUTE OUTPUT-PTR = FUNCTION LENGTH(OUTPUT-FIELD)
                                    - INPUT-PTR + 1
               MOVE 1 TO INPUT-PTR
               PERFORM UNTIL OUTPUT-PTR > 0
                   ADD 1 TO INPUT-PTR OUTPUT-PTR
               END-PERFORM
               MOVE INPUT-FIELD(INPUT-PTR:) TO OUTPUT-FIELD(OUTPUT-PTR:)
           END-IF.

C:\COBOL\>RJUST

:ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRST          :
:ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRST:

:ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ    :
:GHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ:
```
