```
Apologies for posting a non-cobol article to the cobol group. This was
the most appropriate group available here.

My company is downsizing. They want to get the systems area down from
700 to 400 people in 3 years. Rather than laying people off, they're
'transfering' 300 employees to an IBM subsidiary called ISSC. Once
the jobs become obsolete, they'll be moved on to other ISSC clients, just
like any other contractor. Not a bad deal as downsizing goes.

I've heard that ISSC has had similar arrangements with other companies.
Does anyone know what sort of offers they make to the 'transferred' employees?
How are they as an employer?

A rumor is going around that ISSC required outrages hours from AT+T
employee that they brought in. I doubt if its true, given IBM's good
reputation. Does anyone know for sure?

Thanks in advance for any information.
```
