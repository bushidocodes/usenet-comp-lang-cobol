```
When I need a break from COBOL and C# I relax with music and I set a
couple of hours aside for myself, every Sunday.

My neighbours are very understanding and I always finish by 5 pm,
usually around 4:30.

I taught myself to play ukulele and guitar when I was around 11 years
old, and later added ability to read music... (It's really just another
coding language... :-)) I have never had a lesson in my life and you can
see this from the video... :-))

Anyway time is passing and I shall do likewise, probably in the
foreseeable future :-)

The remnants of my family, and some friends who really should know
better, decided that they wanted a performance which could be recorded
for posterity and a memento after I've gone to the big computer room in
the sky...

So, I had a little concert in my garden with about a dozen people and it
was just amazing. The video is still being edited and there will be a
full length MP4 which will be over an hour and will cover a diversity of
musical genres (I did promise them there would be nothing modern and
it's really kind of like a time warp to a street busker in the last
century (which I actually was in Stockholm...))

The weather was perfect, my builder neighbour rigged a tarpaulin for
shade, and we had light refreshments (pizza, savouries, beer and wine)
and a truly relaxed and cosy atmosphere. They all said they loved it and
they were an excellent and truly supportive audience. I had a ball... :-)

Here’s a YouTube link to a small “teaser” but understand this is a raw
draft, in fact, some snippets from the first draft of the recording...
The final cut will have over an hour in it and it will have better
balance...

https://youtu.be/UBKpiiYZVwI

Enjoy!
I used to write COBOL; now I can do anything...
```
