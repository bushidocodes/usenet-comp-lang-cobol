This goes back 5-6 years, but we actually found that the free IBM COBOL II
compiler - with the OPTIMIZE option on - yielded better CPU performance
than the CA-OPTIMIZER. Our staunch supporters of the CA-OPTIMIZER contacted

with their product and that their product provided additional diagnostics).
