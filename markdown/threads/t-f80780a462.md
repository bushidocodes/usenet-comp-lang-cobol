Sounds like a problem with the param's on the capture command.
Double check the name of the Novell print queue.

    CAPTURE L=n Q=x NFF NB AU

        Will start capture where

            L        Lpt port number. Replace n with 1 - 9.
            Q        Novell print queue (replace x with its name).
            NFF    No Form Feed.
            NB      No Banner.
            AU      AUto end capture.

    CAPTURE L=n EC

    Will end capture where.

            L        Lpt port number. Replace n with 1 - 9.
            EC     End Capture. Just as it says....

Hope this helps...

Alister
