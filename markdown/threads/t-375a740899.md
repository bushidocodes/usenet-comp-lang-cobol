Description:
The Public Health Project Technical Team is seeking a senior level
Applications Analyst Programmer Specialist for the maintenance and support
of Public Health Systems. Individual must possess excellent communication
skills (written and oral) to consult with program managers and end-users to
document formal user requirements. Individual will be expected to interact
directly with the end-user to help resolve issues, direct technical
resolutions/alternatives and develop technical solutions to satisfy user
requirements.
The candidate should have experience with technical support of Public Health
or related Healthcare applications and five or more years experience in
using DB2, ISPF, SQL, TSO,MSV, JCL, Easytrieve Plus, Endevor, COBOL and CICS
in an OS/390 mainframe. The candidate should have professional exposure to
multi-tier development of Web applications using J2EE technologies.
Individual will be expected to analyze user requirements, design technical
solutions in an Object Oriented framework. The individual will be expected
to work in a team setting and/or independently leading subprojects that may
require coordination of activities across organizational boundaries. The
position may require on call.


Other Requirements:
Full systems development lifecycle experience in a lead capacity
Impact analysis/Gap analysis
Technical writing/Documentation skills
Lead experience developing technical & non-technical solutions for
supporting business process reengineering
Healthcare/service industry experience a plus
State/federal government experience a plus
Flexible team player
