There are certainly some sites in Germany that still use ADABAS on the
mainframe.

The job site I have found most useful for Europe (obviously, including the
UK) is www.jobserve.com.

If you search there for "ADABAS" you might be lucky. In general the ones
that send you e-mail are not really much cop. By the time they send it the
job is gone and they use software to match on keywords which means they lack
subtlety.

There is really no substitute for searching manually on your own keywords.
Of course there is nothing to stop you using all of these methods; they are
not mutually exclusive.

Good Luck!

Pete.
