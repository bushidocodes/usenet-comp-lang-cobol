```
Barcodes are just black and white rectangles.  A simple code, such as
Code-39 (3 of 9) can be table driven once the required patterns are
known.  I found a public domain C program which did this some time ago
and converted it to Cobol in half a day.  To intermix text printing and
barcodes I delay the barcode printing until the end of the page and then
use positioning and draw rectangle codes to output the bars, this way I
don't lose control of what 'write before 1' is doing.

I can send you the C source and some notes.
```
