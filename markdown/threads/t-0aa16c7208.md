Go to cobolreport.com and check out Bill Klein's COBOL FAQ. He doesn't
give you what you are after but lists all COBOL compiler vendors. Having
their site addresses, then search their on-line help books LRMs (Language
Reference Manuals) which itemize COBOL files in detail. They will list
only the technical background - and I'm afraid you will have to take it
from there.

What you may need to reference (again in Bill Klein's FAQ), are those
COBOL 'sites' that have examples using COBOL files.
