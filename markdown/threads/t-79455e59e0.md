The DCB can be changed to be RMODE(31).  Look in the newer DFP or
SFSMS books.  Basically, the DCB will point to a new control
block, the DCBE (DCB Extended) which will hold the 31 bita
addresses of EODAD and SYNAD routines.  Beyond that, there are no
changes.  PUT and GET work the same and all you do is re-assemble.
