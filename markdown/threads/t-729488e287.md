Everyone seems to be concentrating on source code but ignoring the other
major problem - dates in the database.

In light of this I developed a tool to scan most of the major databases
(mainframe, AS/400, Access, etc.) which I am now making available as an
inexpensive utility.

"Datefind" uses ODBC to connect to the database and scans ALL files finding
dates which are NOT Year 2000 compliant.

A trial copy can be downloaded from http://freespace.virgin.net/y2k.html

I hope this tool saves you as much time as it has saved me.

Chris Young
