Not exactly sure who gave me this but it is worth a groan in this topic:

There was once a COBOL programmer who was getting burned out with all the Y2K conversions taking place.  He was so stressed out that he finally arrainged to have himself put in cryostasis until after Jan 1,2000.

Time passed and he awoke to find himself in a white room with lots of people rushing around.  A voice from behind his head spoke in an awed whisper "He's awake!"  Activity in the room stopped as all turned to look at him.  He was a bit nervous and asked if everything had gone ok - was he in good health and so on.  A nurse assured him he was ok, the doctors declared all his tests showed him to be in excellent health.  He was given a sumpious meal and all his wishes were immediately fulfilled.  

While all this attention pleased him it also made him a bit nervous.  He kept asking what day it was and he was told to never mind that all was well.  Finally about a week after he awakened a very official man walked into the room and sat in the chair near his bed.  He told the programmer that the Galaxy Leader wanted to speak with him.  A screen came down from the ceiling and a man appeared who had a eerie resemblence to Bill Gates, he spoke....

"I have some bad news for you.  You were scheduled to be awakened on 8/1/2000 but there was a glitch in the programming and it is now 8/1/9999.  You have nothing to worry about, all desease and war has been eliminated and the world lives in peace and harmony.  We hope to continue to live this way but to do so we need your help....

We understand you know COBOL......"
