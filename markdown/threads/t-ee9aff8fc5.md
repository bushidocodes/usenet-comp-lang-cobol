Your best bet, (if available in Germany), get a copy of Thane Hubbell's
'Teach Yourself COBOL in 24 Hours" - it comes with a free CD containing
Fujitsu COBOL. Not the latest version, but very adequate to get you
started.

As an alternative, should you qualify as a student, you could get Net
Express (Micro Focus) University Edition for about $US100 - check out
microfocus.com.

Go to cobolreport.com and check out Bill Klein's FAQ (Frequently Asked
Questions) - which answers a lot of other questions about COBOL. If you
want a good set of examples, check out the University of Limerick, Eire
-

http://www.csis.ul.ie/COBOL/examples/

There are other sources for examples it you do Web searches on "COBOL".
You will find Thane's examples and those from the U of Limerick cover
most of the groundwork for you.
