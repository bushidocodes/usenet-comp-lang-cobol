friend heading for - presumably a big metropolitan area ? Going West to
East :-

(1) British Columbia, some large businesses but probably a lot of
'intermediates'; somebody from BC might be able to confirm.
(2) Alberta - forget it - COBOL is no longer taught, and I have seen only
ONE job advertised which asked for COBOL. Because of the oil and gas,
high-tech using the other sexy languages, for graphics associated with
geology, production etc. Imperial Oil in Calgary *did* use COBOL, but they
too are probably more interested in the sexy stuff.
(3) Saskatchewan and Manitoba - basically farming
(4) Ontario - Metropolitan Toronto area - only real possibility - many
large financial institutions and 'intermediates' - perhaps Donald and
Calin can fill in.
(5) Quebec - Jean - is it somewhat like Ontario business wise ?
(6) Atlantic provinces - poor economies tied into lack of availability of
fish, their prime industries.

Note Ontario and Quebec represent the big population base.
