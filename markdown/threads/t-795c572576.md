Why would I want Dialog system ? It's slow, cumbersome and takes
too darn much away from me in the way of control !!! I demo'ed DS for a
month and returned it... I found it to be slow, and by design, it takes too
much control away from me. There are other problems too... For one, I'm a
vendor, which means that when I sell a client part of the revenue will end up
going to MF if I use DS. The other is that without DS I can send my clients
nothing but a diskette full of .EXE's, with DS, well - you know...

program and in the space of about 1/2 hour have a functioning GUI program !

to 'pretty up' an imported BMS map ? I have and I can tell you that large maps
(50-100+) fields will take mucho wall clock time to work on. My deciding
factor in returning DS was when it took 90 seconds to delete one field from a
screenset gen'ed from an imported BMS map...

Besides... I am the Panels2 expert !!!
