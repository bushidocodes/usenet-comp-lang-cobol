Hello,

My shop currently does development in Cobol across different platforms:
DOS on the PC, IBM System/36 and AS/400, and VSAM/CICS on an Amdhal
main-frame.

We are currently looking at major conversion to develop for windows.
The powers-that-be have decided on Watcom for the database (I was not in
that process, so I don't know why), and we are looking at CA-Visual
Cobol. All of our current development for DOS is using CA-Realia cobol,
and CA-Screenio.

We are also considering looking at MicroFocus cobol, or possibly MS
Visual C++ for the GUI and a cobol for the file i/o.

A major concern is wanting to keep as much of the current cobol as
possible.

Has anyone out there gone through this process? I would like to here
from others.
