Will, 

I don't know which COBOL you are using, but it really doesn't matter. 
The whole Windows printing API is a big mess and very difficult for 
the COBOL programmer to work with.  Each brand of COBOL does it 
differently, with their own calls in between you and the API.  What I 
use is another Flexus tool called COBOL FORM PRINT.  You can get an 
evaluation copy from their home page at http://www.flexus.com - not 
only can you use it to see which printer you are using, you can select
different printers, limit the selection of printers, control copies, 
orientation and resolution, and you get a print preview -- if you want
one.  It's a stellar, and IMHO under-touted product.  It does not 
require that you use their SP2 Gui environment (You might want to look
at that too though) and is a fantastic performer.  It does more than 
simple line printing -- you can use bitmaps and a multitude of other 
fancy things in your reports.
