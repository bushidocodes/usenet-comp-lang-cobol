```
Being follicularly challenged (more so as years go by) it isn't very often I
actually need a haircut.

(I don't actually have haircuts any more, I have "search and destroy"
missions...)

Anyway, I decided to take a break from the intensive coding I am doing
currently (especially as I had a major breakthrough around 2:00 am this
morning and got something to work that had baffled me for a couple of days)
and go pamper myself...

It just so happened that on checking the e-mail before going out, there was
a "Friday Funny" which I reproduce here...


"Women on Haircuts"

Woman 1: Oh! That haircut is so cute!
Woman 2: You think so? I wasn't sure when she gave me the mirror. I mean,
you don't think it's too fluffy looking?
Woman 1: Oh God no! No, it's perfect. I'd love to get my hair cut like that,
but I think my face is too wide. I'm  pretty much stuck with this stuff I
think.
Woman 2: Are you serious? I think your face is adorable. And you could
easily get one of those layer cuts - that would look so cute I think. I was
actually going to do that except that I was afraid it would accent my long
neck.
Woman 1: Oh - that's funny! I would love to have your neck! Anything to take
attention away from this two-by-four I have for a shoulder line.
Woman 2: Are you kidding? I know girls that would kill for your shoulders.
Everything drapes so well on you. I mean, look at my arms - see how short
they are? If I had your shoulders I could get clothes to fit me so much
easier. (etc...)

"Men on Haircuts"

Man 1: Haircut?
Man 2: Yeah.

...Needless to say, I walked past the trendy up-market unisex "salon" and
and went to Bob the Kiwi Barber (who still has a red, white, and blue pole
outside his shop), where he cuts hair and provides file storage (always
struck me as a curious blend of occupations, but it works for him.)

Solved three more problems while sitting in his chair...
Pete.
```
