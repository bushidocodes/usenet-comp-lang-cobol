Carolyn, Carolyn, Carolyn--

We are all very busy, professional programmers who visit this
newsgroup briefly, between compiles, to discuss only matters of
great import, such as whether to use SECTIONS VS. PARAGRAPHS. And
you expect us to do your homework for you?

Well, not having anything better to do (or trying to avoid doing
it at least), I did take a peak at your program. The first thing
that struck me was that you read only one record of the order
file. Try putting the read statement in a loop so you read the
entire file.

That's a start. Now go to work and, when you come back to this
newsgroup with another problem, please try to be a little more
specific than saying "it doesn't work" and throwing the listing
in our laps.

from further posts.
