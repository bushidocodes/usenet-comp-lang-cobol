Aaron,

Now that you have been more specific and quoted N/E V 3.1. I don't know, but
would have thought it extremely unlikely that there is any ODBC that is
geared to reading COBOL files. Such a generalized tool would have to
acknowledge the different formats of COBOL files coming with each vendors
'compiler - inevitably an ODBC driver is geared to the format of a specific
DB..

You are already aware that N/E has a pretty detailed on-line book regarding
DB and SQL. It seems to me if you want to bite the bullet on this one, select
your DB of choice, then create Ascil-delmiteds(CSVs) from your COBOL files
and feed them into the DB.
