```
It depends on what you are wanting to read.  In most COBOL shops the
standard is to put some strings in working storage such as:

01  FILLER   PIC X(40)  VALUE '***** WORKING STORAGE BEGINS HERE ****'.

some other fillers like this are nice before tables and other 01 levels. 

If you have these, you can look through your dump for your working
storage, and find out what's actually in your fields.  You have to
switch back and forth between text and hex (or octal), and know how to
translate binary and packed fields.  But the above technique will allow
you to find where to look.
```
