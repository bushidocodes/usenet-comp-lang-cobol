Hi, Sam,
You shouldn't have a problem if you update your resume to indicate you
have kept abreast with computers. Many HR departments scan for key
words, so MVS and COBOL should be there somewhere. The yr2000 issue
is making cobol programmers desirable. Here in upstate NY it is often
difficult to locate COBOL programmers for contract work. In fact, I
suggest you consider doing that for now. It's not uncommon for
contract programmers to be offered 'real jobs' later. For CICS, it's
not that tough for most applications. Suggest you pick up a current
CICS book (Mike Murach Associates offers a good intro and there's one
I wrote for John Wiley 'CICS for COBOL Programmers'. The Murach book
is cheaper. So... I don't see a problem. Good luck, david
