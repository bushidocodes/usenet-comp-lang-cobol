Hi Volker,


It is Report Program Generator. Originally developed to ease development 
on IBM S370 and IBM S360 mainframes. These mainframes use to work with the 
card readers and programers on the deck of punched cards. The purpose was 
to have less punched cards with less coding for printing programs. RPG 
than transferred to S3X, where it was used extensively, with latter 
developments of RPGII and RPG/400 and now RPG/IV or RPG/ILE it is not much 
more a structed language.
