You'll doubtless want to buy at least one introductory book to refresh 
your old know how of COBOL. The COBOL Center has a page that lists 
COBOL books in various categories. The URL of the page:
     http://www.infogoal.com/cbd/cbdbks.htm

For mainframe and AIX programmers, IBM has an online "library" of their 
COBOL manuals at: 
     http://www.software.ibm.com/ad/cobol/library.htm
One cannot recommend these manuals for *learning* COBOL, but they are 
invaluable as works of reference.
