```

Just thought I'd let people know that a new compiler has turned up on of all
things, the ex-Commodore/Escom, now owned by Gateway, Amiga.

NRCOBOL 1.0c is available on Aminet in dev/lang/nrcobol_1c.lha

Early days yet from the looks of it. Supports Most ANSI-85 syntax and Microsoft
2.x syntax (weird I know). GUI interface.

Runs on AmigaOS 2.04 or later though a v1.3 version is available but receiving
no new support/testing. Didn't seem to support retargetable graphics and no OS
copy files.

I'm not connected in any way with the compiler or author btw.

Shaun
```
