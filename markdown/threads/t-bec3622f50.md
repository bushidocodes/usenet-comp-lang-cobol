still be made). Your input will help us make it that way.

I'll notify you when I know the ballot closing date. Your national body will

Any comments to INCITS/J4 (bobkarlin@karlinskorner.com) by the March 10-14, 
2008, J4 meeting in Ontario, CA, will be greatly appreciated.
