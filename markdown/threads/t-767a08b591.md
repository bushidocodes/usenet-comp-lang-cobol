COBOL RUNTIME LIBARY NOT INSTALLED.

› How can I create a EXE-FILE wich is running on every PC?

It's depending on your Cobol-Compiler, many of them have a
'build'-function to
Link all neccesary files together in a EXE-File.

The other way is to copy the RUN.EXE COBRUN.EXE or something like this
to the target-System and start your Pgm with

RUN MYPGM switch

The last way is to manually link the runtime RUNCOBOL.LIB (look to your
directory for the right name) together with the other Files.

Greatings

AS
