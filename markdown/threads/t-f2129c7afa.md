to the group that would be more appropriate.  Thanks in advance
for the help.

In the 1970's Honeywell made a mainframe computer. I think the operating
system was called GECOS, or something like that.
Anyway, the thing I remember most was the COVER PHOTOS for the manuals.
There were photos of collections computer chips and other circa 1970 
computer components that were assembled together in an artistic way 
to create a model of an animal.

that one would remember for a long time.

Am I dreaming? Or have these artistic renderings been lost forever?
Does anyone else remember these COVER PHOTOS?
Do you know where I might get to see some of these (again)?
