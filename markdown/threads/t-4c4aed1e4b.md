Assuming you have the Active-X control registered properly with
Windows:

Right-click on the form-design toolbar.

After several seconds, a list will popup of available Active-X
controls (there may be dozens). Check the ones you want. The
newly-checked controls will now appear on the form-design toolbar.

From there you select the controls you want and spot them on your
form.

Hope this helps.

There are three ways (maybe more!) that I have seen used.

1.  Use the control in conjunction with PowerCOBOL
2.  Use the control natively via INVOKE OLE statements within Standard
COBOL.
3.  Use a third party tool - COBOL Sp2 From Flexus.com that supports
ActiveX controls.
