Interesting point. Even inline performs can be used for this, although the
compiler might complain. You can also use them to provide scope termination
for old statements like ON that were dropped from newer Cobol, if you're
not on the mainframe where ON is now prohibited entirely you can make up
for the lack of an End-On statement by e.g. (using _ for leading blanks)

...
Perform
__On ...
____...
__Else
____..
End-Perform
