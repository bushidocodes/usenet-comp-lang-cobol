There are DFSORT manuals on both the MVS Collection and the OS/390
Collection. Which is best depends on what versions of MVS you will be
using. For versions prior to OS/390 (up to MVS ESA V5) the MVS
Collection is best. For OS/390 flavors of MVS, you want the OS/390
Collection. If you have to support software on both platforms, you may
want both. If your object is just to study MVS and not use a specific
system, then you should be studying the most current, OS/390.
