```
Hi!

Looking for a change in "job scenary" can be very tiring and even more
confusing......if you let it....

It really starts out with someone you can feel comfortable talking too....

Like ...Q: "What is it you really want.....?...no I mean WHAT IS IT YOU
REALLY WANT!"

...to substantially reduce or perhaps even eliminate your daily commute?
(and gas, tolls, trainfare etc )
...flex hrs? (you can't sleep at night ...why not work)
...casual work attire? (trash the $$ tight fitting suits and soon to be
out-of-style dresses)
...more money? (for safety nets & caribbean vacations!)
...or less money! ("who needs it, I want to live longer!")
...less work? (theres nothing wrong with working less than 50 hrs a
week!)
...or more work!? ("hit me, beat me...")
...the opportunity to be creative? (who doesn't?)
...to work alone? ("I like to pick my nose")
...or to work on a team? ("let's start a softball team")
...a company you really enjoy working for? ("..they pay me so much for
doing so little...")
...to be able to acquire new skillsets using the latest tools? (java,
shmava, etc. etc.)
...a piece of the company pie? ("cool")
...to work out of your home office? ("I think I'll water the garden")
...to travel? ("ciao baby")
...the latest software & hardware?..free?..for your personal use? ("I
don't know what it is..but I want it!")

What else....? the list is almost endless.....

If you have strong skillsets then in this job market many if not all of
the above is achievable....

Seriously though, ...what is your strongest skillset?
rgds,
Carl
```
