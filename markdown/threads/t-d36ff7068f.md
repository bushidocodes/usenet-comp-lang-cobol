```
Perhaps newer versions of Cobol have fancy statements for this, but my
old-fashioned (and probably inefficient solution) to this is to redefine
the field as an array. Start with the last character and work your way
backwards looking for the first non-space character. Strip off the two
initials into a second field, then strip off the remaining characters
into a third field. Something like:

01 ws-name pic x(12).
01 ws-name-redef redefines wi-name.
03 wi-name-char pic x occurs 12 times.
```
