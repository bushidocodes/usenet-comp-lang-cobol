I think you're out of luck. CICS/MVS Version/Release 2.1.2 was the
last CICS that supported Macro Level support for HLL's. CICS/ESA
Version/Release 3.1.1 was the last CICS that supported Macro Level
support for Assembler.

You may want to look through IBM Book Manager On-Line -

http://www.s390.ibm.com/bookmgr-cgi/bookmgr.cmd/LIBRARY

or, locate someone that has these manuals in their possession.
