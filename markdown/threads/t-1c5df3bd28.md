Being "involved" with the draft standard does NOT represent a commitment to
implement it.

IBM has *explicitly* avoided any statement on when or IF they will implement
it.

Neither Unisys nor Compaq (aka Tandem, Dec, and future HP) have indicated
publicly a plan to implement it.

Fujitsu HAS talked about all the developers that they have, but they have
NOT stated when/if they will implement the new Standard (in either their
Japanese or US products).

Similarly, NOT being on the committees doesn't mean that vendors won't
implement it.  AcuCorp, CA, PerCOBOL, etc have been equally "quiet" on their
future intentions (regarding providing "conforming" implementations).
