I am the last person in the world to tell someone that they aren't allowed
to post something in this newsgroup. However, I thought that I would point
out that the (in)famous charter does say that we aren't supposed to have "my
language is better than yours" flames in this newsgroup. I think that this
was put there because these discussions are rarely (never?) very productive.

Obviously, this applies to RPG vs COBOL, but I think it also applies to
American English vs British English as well.

Again, this post is NOT intended to tell anyone what they can or cannot
post, it is simply to comment on the futility of some discussions. (Well,
maybe it does "clear the bile" - but I guess that gets back to talking about
body parts and functions.) :-)
