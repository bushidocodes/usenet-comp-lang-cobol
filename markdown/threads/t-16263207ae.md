Noah,
Judson's suggestion is an excellent one. The Teach Yourself COBOL in 21
Days is very good. However, the quiz questions could be a littler better
detailed. Several folks I've talked to got a little confused by the quiz
questions because the author made them a little to short.
I just reviewed the new book COBOL for Dummies. It's very informative
and can help quite a bit. It also includes a CD with several COBOL
compilers on it. That's a big plus over the Teach Yourself in 21 days book.
My only problem with the Dummies book is that it was short on examples.
I suggest getting both of them because it's always good to experience
several styles when learning a new programming language and then adopting
the best from all of them.

Happy Holidays,
