```
Just as nuclear energy can be used to generate electricity or destroy 
cities, so can the power of the Network.

A new web site has emerged which is quite chilling in my opinion.

I would urge anyone reading this to go to this site: http://www.spokeo.com/

... and enter your own name, and the town or state where you live. You may 
be shocked at the results.

I found details on more than one person who posts to this group. (Sorry, I 
wasn't snooping, I haven't saved any of the data, and I'll never go there 
again. I was just curious to see how far this site goes... It was not 
pretty.)

(I did have to smile though when I entered my own name and it showed a map 
of the United States and said it couldn't find me :-) However, I'm not 
feeling smug about this because it is only a matter of time before it 
reaches our little island here if it is allowed to prceed.)

There is no knowing whether the databases they build their presentation on 
are accurate and I'm pretty certain the people who are on their base were 
not consulted or asked if they would like to check the data.

This is a total misuse of technology in my opinion. Computer professionals 
should oppose it vehemently. (If someone had asked me to build such a site, 
I would have refused, but I don't want to pontificate or judge the people 
who DID build it. )

It is all very well to say: "Honest people have nothing to fear from 
information.", but this is a cynical use of data resources, purely for 
making money at the expense of the population.

If you live in the United States or Canada maybe you can write your Senator 
or Congressman, protesting in the strongest terms.

Pete.
```
