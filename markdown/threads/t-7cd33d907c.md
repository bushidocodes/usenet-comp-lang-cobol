that "millennium" comes from "mille annum", while "millenary" comes from

AFAIK, any word derived from "annum" has a double-n.

BTW, "milliner" meaning "maker or seller of women's hats" is derived
from "Milan"/"Milano" (Northern Italian city once know for exporting
women's finery).

dictionary and built my own from scratch. For the first couple weeks I
had to add lots of words to my dictionary, but that's just a single
click, and this way no word that I don't use can sneak in as a
misspelling of a word I do use.
