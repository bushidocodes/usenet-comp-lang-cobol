```
A couple of months ago i posted that this book would soon be
available. well, now amazon.com is showing it. there were some delays
at printer.   

title:  "COBOL for OS/390 Power Programming"	
audience: experienced cobol programmers. the book does NOT teach
syntax or basic commands. the book assumes the reader knows cobol. the
focus of the book is on power techniques to help the reader master the
language environment, not just a few verbs or techniques.  
environment: MVS with IMS or CICS or DB2 or combinations. each
particular environment is addressed
where to find:  www.mvs-training.com    or   www.amazon.com    
featured: all Y2K commands for cobol are included, including MLE.
there is a special section on a Y2K supplier and even info on IBM's
late summer announcement about object-code Y2K remediation.   
thanks for listening...  I don't mean to intrude. 
David
```
