As mentioned in previous posts DISP=MOD will do it for you.

You can also try OPEN EXTEND in your program.

Another option is to use GDGs.
Create a new Generation as each process writes to the file.
When you need the entire file, concatenate all Generations by not
specifying a Generation in the input DD.
