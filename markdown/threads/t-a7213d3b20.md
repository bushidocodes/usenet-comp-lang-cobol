To delete an empty GDG use IDCAMS 

   DELETE MY.GDG  GDG PURGE

If the GDG is not empty, you might try

   DELETE MY.GDG GDG OURGE FORCE

(Which may or may not work due to RACF settings) or

   DELETE MY.GDG.*  NONVSAM PURGE
   DELETE MY.GDG    GDG     PURGE

HTH
