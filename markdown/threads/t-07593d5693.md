W.L. Handler & Associates
SEARCH CONSULTANTS

POSITION TITLE: Applications Development Manager.   Reports to a Director of
Institutional IT, and will manage a staff of ten professionals.

CLIENT OVERVIEW: Our client is a premier financial services company with more
than $65 billion in assets under management and 2,400 employees.  The Company
services more than two million investors with a family of more than 60 mutual
funds which have historically ranked among the best performers in the nation.
Two recent developments, a business partnership with a global financial
services company and a merger one year ago with another mutual fund company,
ranks them among the top few financial services companies in the world.  They
have always been an aggressive pioneer in the utilization of technology for
investment, customer service, customer information and communications.  They
are well known in their area for being very employee oriented, and continue to
lead their industry and maintain a cutting edge attitude with respect to
technology innovation.  The Institutional lines of business account for over
half of the Company�s assets under management, and is the fastest growing
segment of the business, forecasting 50% growth next year and a tenfold
increase in revenue over the next ten years.  This position is located at the
corporate headquarters in a very desirable metropolitan area in the Midwest,
considered one of the most attractive cities to move to in the U.S.

POSITION IMPACT: The Company is implementing a new enterprise-wide
Information Systems architecture, putting in place new development
methodologies, and creating new Web-based applications. The Applications
Development Manager will have significant impact on the user satisfaction,
service offerings, business processes and customer service capabilities of
the Company.  This position will manage a development and implementation team
aligned with a critical business function such as finance, sales and
marketing, customer information or investments analysis. He/she will manage
project delivery, plan and coordinate staffing requirements, scheduling,
review performance, quality, budget, organization, and standards of the
development team efforts. He/she will participate in the department planning
process.  Prepare and implement departmental goals and objectives and assist
in the development of the IT department budget.

PROFESSIONAL QUALIFICATIONS: The successful candidate will have a minimum of
six years of analysis, design, construction, testing, and implementation of
large complex mainframe on-line and batch applications.  Additional
development experience in the Intranet, client server, open systems, and
relational database arena would be a plus. We strongly prefer a minimum of
four years as a manager of highly competent IT personnel, with duties
including: administration of performance appraisals, providing performance
feedback and coaching, and identifying training and development plans for
staff.	The right person will have strong project management skills, and be
able to demonstrate proficiency in providing technical and business guidance
to technical and non-technical staff.  Experience in the financial services
industry is preferred.

PERSONAL CHARACTERISTICS: The successful candidate will have good
interpersonal skills including the ability to communicate at various levels
including staff and middle management.	He/she must be able to communicate
clearly and concisely, both verbally and in written form.  He/she will be
characterized as having good enterprise level project management skills and
be flexible in a continuously changing environment.  The ability to provide
technical and business guidance to technical and non-technical staff and to
generate imaginative or creative solutions in work related situations are
essential.

EDUCATION: A Bachelor�s degree in either computer science, mathematics,
information management or a related field is a requirement.

COMPENSATION: The client offers a very competitive base salary, attractive
bonus, excellent company-paid benefits plan, 401(k) program, and profit
sharing.  A generous relocation package will be provided by the company.
