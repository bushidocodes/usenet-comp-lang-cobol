What you show are the EBCDIC representations of:
+0
+12.35
+123.46
The hexadecimal representations are:
X'F0F0F0F0F0F0C0'
X'F0F0F0F1F2F3C5'
X'F0F0F1F2F3F4C6'
Hex F0 thru F9 display as 0 thru 9;
Hex C0 thru C9 display as {ABCDEFGHI;
Hex D0 thru D9 display as }JKLMNOPQR;
A 'C' in the left half of the low-order byte indicates a positive sign;
A 'D' in the left half of the low-order byte indicates a negative sign;
An 'F' in the left half of the low-order byte indicates unsigned.
