Well, I don't know what the 'standards' are... so, when in doubt, turn to
the Temple of Truth. Code used was:

01 STUFF.
05 WS-ENTRY PIC X(4) OCCURS 100.

(gotta watch out for those 01-level OCCURS, don'cha know)

THE-PERFORM.
PERFORM BUMP-UP THRU BU-EX
VARYING SUB1 FROM 1 BY 1
UNTIL SUB1 > 100.
GOBACK.
BUMP-UP.
IF ( SUB1 > 100)
OR ( WS-ENTRY (SUB1) = HIGH-VALUES)
GO TO A-900-EOL.
BU-EX.
EXIT.
A-900-EOL.
STOP RUN.

IKFCBL00 with LIB,FDUMP,XREF,VBREF,RES,PMAP gives

IF 00053A 48 30 6 008 LH 3,008(0,6)
00053E 49 30 C 02A CH 3,02A(0,12)
000542 58 B0 C 030 L 11,030(0,12)
000546 47 20 B 0AA BC 2,0AA(0,11)
00054A 41 40 6 048 LA 4,048(0,6)
00054E 48 30 6 008 LH 3,008(0,6)
000552 5C 20 C 02C M 2,02C(0,12)
000556 1A 43 AR 4,3
000558 5B 40 C 02C S 4,02C(0,12)
00055C 50 40 D 210 ST 4,210(0,13)
000560 58 E0 D 210 L 14,210(0,13)
000564 95 FF E 000 CLI 000(14),X'FF'
000568 47 70 B 0AE BC 7,0AE(0,11)
00056C D5 02 E 001 E 000 CLC 001(3,14),000(14)
000572 47 70 B 0AE BC 7,0AE(0,11)
000576 GN=05 EQU *
GO 000576 47 F0 B 0B4 BC 15,0B4(0,11)

... now you'll have to take my word for this (cut-n-paste limitations) but
the first LH
is addressing SUB1 (DNM=1-103) and the CH refers to LIT+2; since the entire
LITPOOL is

004C0 (LIT+0) 00010064 00000004

then the 100 (x'64') is pretty clear to see... as is the CLI at 000564 for
x'FF', HIGH-VALUES.

IGYCRCTL with LIB,FDUMP,XREF,VBREF,RES,LIST gives

IF
01C4 D501 9008 A00E CLC 8(2,9),14(10) SUB1
01CA 4720 B0B8 BC 2,184(0,11) GN=4(0001E2)
01CE 4820 9008 LH 2,8(0,9) SUB1
01D2 4C20 A00C MH 2,12(0,10) PGMLIT AT +4
01D6 1A29 AR 2,9
01D8 D503 2044 A018 CLC 68(4,2),24(10) WS-ENTRY()
01DE 4770 B0CC BC 7,204(0,11) GN=3(0001F6)
01E2 GN=4 EQU *
GO

*clearly* showing that SUB1 is addressed by a CLC before WS-ENTRY is.
