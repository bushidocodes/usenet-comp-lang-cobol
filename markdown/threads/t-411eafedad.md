One way is to include then on the first line of the source code. For
turning on the MAIN option, for example, code:

@OPTIONS MAIN

Where the @ begins in column 8.

Otherwise, there is an options button you can press during compile.

For step by step instructions, including a MOVIE of how to do it, get
my book, Sams Teach Yourself COBOL in 24 Hours.
