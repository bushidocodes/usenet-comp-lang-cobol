 you already are a programmer - I'd suggest you just go
 and buy what's known as the K&R book (Kernighan
 and Ritchie) - "The C Programming Language, 2nd Edition"
 (ISBN 0131103709 ) .   This book explains the syntax
 and has info on the standard C library.

 The other top-knotch C book is "C: A Reference Manual"
 by Harbison and Steele  (people often quote Harbison and
 Steele) - ISBN  0133262243

 These are both popular books, available just about everywhere.

of exactly 10 positions to be able to process the same records.
