Depending on where you are going to get your MIS degree their may be some 
internships that are out there.  I've seen very few people in an operational 
environment move onto something more significant within that organization. 
More and more these jobs utilize the "bodies for hire" mentality.  You don't 
get to learn much about anything because you aren't "supporting" anything, 
just watching it most of the time.

I don't know how to gauge the need for interns anymore - there appear to be 
less and less of them where I work...but then I see a lot of cool things 
that are done by interns in other parts of the country.

If you have to do a "thesis" project - if it's optional, do it - then you 
may find some external projects to work on.
