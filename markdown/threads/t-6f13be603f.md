```
The J4 technical committee mailing list has just been notified that a new
draft version of the draft (notice a pattern here, i.e. DRAFT) Standard is
available.  I have seen the PDF version of this which has "hot-spot" or
"hot-link" capabilities so you can actually jump to cross-references and
pages from the index or table of contents. (Nifty and/or useful!)

I do not know how widely available this document (hard or soft-copy) is
being distributed, however, if you are interested (and want to find out for
yourself how/if you can get it), *and* you are in the US, then contact the
J4 chair (Don Schricker) at

   doncobol <at> mediaone.net

If you are outside the US, then check out the WG4 web page for information
on how to contact your own "national body" group.  I am getting errors when
I look for it today, but at least in the past, the WG4 web page was at:

   www.dkuug.dk/jtc1/sc22/wg4

(If this doesn't work for you and you want to follow up on this outside the
US, I suggest that you contact your own national standards group, e.g. BSI,
DNI, ITSJ.)

NOTE WELL:
   When "requesting" this document, make certain that you ask for draft "CD
1.5" dated April of 1999.  Anything other than this is NOT the most current
J4 document.
```
