```
Given several recent (and not so recent) threads about "acquiring" copies of
Micro Focus products OTHER THAN FROM Micro Focus,  I thought that I would
pass along (in this new thread as well as in response to another), the
comment that I received "privately" from someone in the legal department at
Micro Focus.

"Micro Focus takes support and maintenance of users of Micro Focus products
as well as the protection of its Intellectual Property Rights very
seriously. In our efforts to do the preceding, Micro Focus's standard
software license does not allow licenses for Micro Focus products to be
transferred without the parties notifying Micro Focus. Hopefully, this will
allow Micro Focus to continue to provide its users with support and new
products in the future."

    ***

My personal suggestion is that anyone wanting to "sell" an existing LICENSED
(*not* purchased) copy of any Micro Focus product, read their licensing
documentation and/or get appropriate legal advice.
```
