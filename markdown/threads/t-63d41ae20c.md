Just for fun I tried the above program using Fujitsu
Cobol.

I needed to start statements in the correct column.

Comments begin in column 7.
DIVISION and SECTION headers begin in column 8.

The WORKING-STORAGE SECTION line is missing following
DATA DIVISION.

Spaces were needed between the elements on the DISPLAY
statement.

I agree with RWidmer who is right in saying that it is
important to know the Cobol compiler and platform before
help can be provided.
