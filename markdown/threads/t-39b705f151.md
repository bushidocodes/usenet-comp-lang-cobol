I think it's correct to say there have never been trial versions of
Micro Focus compilers. The nearest was VISOC the precursor to Net
Express, where Micro Focus happily distributed Beta versions for people
to try.

I doubt that N/E V.3 is for sale as it has been superseded by N/E V 3.1,
and fairly soon by N/E V 4. The nearest to a 'freebie' is the 'student'
University Edition of N/E V 3 which comes with a price tag of about
$100. However it is a 'tutorial' tool allowing you to build applications
but you can't distribute them commercially.

If you wish to pursue check out the microfocus.com site.
