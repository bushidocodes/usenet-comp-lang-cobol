For the same reason that we all seek board certified doctors and licensed
contractors, your clients want to hire software developers who have met
specific industry standards.

To meet that demand, the American Association for Software Developers has
been formed, and is now accepting applications for membership. The AASD
will provide peer review and credentialing to members, marketing
assistance, news, ID cards, info on continuing education, a free directory
to businesses requiring custom software in your region, and many other
services.

Membership in the AASD will also provide the credibility your clients
will be seeking in the competitive future of custom software development.
If software development is your future, the AASD is your professional
association.

For more information about this organization, please send your name and
mailing address via e-mail to jim··.@ind··t.com.
