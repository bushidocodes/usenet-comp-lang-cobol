> What are the common applications for COBOL?


Core business applications, such as tracking money, inventories, sales,
personnel, etc. For every game or common user application on the PC
there are 10,000 or more COBOL programs quietly running the real
business world. The US government probably has in the tens of millions
of individual COBOL programs. The number of COBOL programs in
businesses, local and state governments is beyond count. During the
Y2K effort, it was estimated that 60%-80% of all currently running
computer code in the world is COBOL. Many large organizations have
had dozens or hundreds of COBOL programmers busy writing code
for decades, much of it still in use.
