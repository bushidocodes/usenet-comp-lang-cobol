ALLOC will allocate a tape dataset -- it's possible that your shop has
prevented this (unusual) action though, perhaps by automating a negative
response to the mount request generated by your TSO session. Try specifying
UNIT(3490) and VOL(nnnn) in the ALLOC, although the catalog entry should
provide this. Perhaps another name for 3490 [UNIT(TAPE) or UNIT(hex drive
address)] will bypass whatever is telling you that the tape is unavailable.

BTW, you know your TSO session will hang until the tape is mounted, and it's
best to ask your operators to get the tape ready before trying to allocate it.
