If you want to take profit of RainCode's technology, we propose you our
One Time Assessment.

The One Time Assessment (shortly, OTA) is a service that provides you
with an extensive and comprehensive report over the current status of
your COBOL source code (PL/1 version available soon). It is a unique
view on the large volumes of source code that have been part of your
company for the last 10 or 20 years.

The One Time Assessment is delivered as a service, and requires no
additional software or licence. The entire process is taken care of for
you. However, if you wish to be able to reproduce the assessment
repeatedly to monitor how your source code real estate evolves over
time, or to integrate new pieces of software as they are developed or
maintained, all you have to do is to purchase a licence of RainCode. You
can then perform the One Time Assessment in batch on your entire source
code basis as often as you wish.

The One Time Assessment provides you with numerous valuable pieces of
information. If gives you insight on how large or complex your software
is, on which part uses which external component (database or TP
monitor), on how do applications compare to each other to direct
maintenance efforts.


Find more details about the OTA in our second newsletter at
http://www.raincode.com/news02.html.
Send us an e-mail if you want to subscribe to the RainCode's newsletter.
