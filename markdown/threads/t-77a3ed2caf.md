SECTION, mainframe COBOL is PC COBOL is Unix COBOL.  You still have tables,

the same language.

I keep hearing about the "50,000 Mainframes" in the world.  I have to
believe there are more... but in any event, there are MANY platforms
where COBOL exists:

Mainframe (of all types)
Midrange - AS/400, RS/6000
Unix
System 36/38 (Don't hear about them much anymore)
PC's.

There have to be a LOT of PC jobs.  There's more PC based COBOL out
there than you might know about.  It seems when people "admit" there
applications are written in COBOL the customer frowns, or expresses
suprise!  I've had it happen more than once.

"How did you get your Visual basic ot be so FAST?" 

"Uh.. it's COBOL not VB."

"COBOL?  You have got to be kidding, this isn't a mainframe!  Won't
this have a Y2K problem?"

Many people just won't admit the product they are selling is from
COBOL source!
