```
Just in case anyone reading this has forgotten (or missed it), IBM has
announced the withdrawal for later this month from marketing of
  IBM COBOL for OS/390 & VM V2R2 (for the OS/390 & z/OS environments - not
VM).

The service withdrawal date is not until 2004.

This means that you will ONLY be able to "purchase" Enterprise COBOL as of
next month.  See:

 http://www-1.ibm.com/servers/eserver/zseries/zos/le/history/cobmvs.html

for the full IBM COBOL Release history for the "z/OS, OS/390�, MVS, and VM
Platforms"

FYI - if you have any programs that still rely on the CMPR2 compiler option
(or the seldom used SOM OO interface) this may be highly significant to you.
```
