No use doing that. You can establish formal correspondence between
a language construct in a programming language and a mathematical
theorem or proof. But then you are where you have been before: Who
says that the thusly discovered mathematical structure has
something to do with the intentions of the programmer?

There will always be a substantial gap between formal systems
and real-world problems. What you need is good programmers and good
salaries for them.

Herwig
