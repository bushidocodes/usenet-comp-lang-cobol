```
Greetings Mr. Woodward,

   It seems that you and several of your colleges are still 
interested in establishing a new news group for the discussion
of Acucorp's Acucobol product. Since you did not want to
respond to all of the post that's were made concerning the initial
RFD, comp.lang.cobol.acucobol, I thought I would post an open
message and you can respond to this or email me if you whish. In
addtion if anyone else has any thoughts on this subject I would like
to hear them.

    Mr. Woodward, I think that the idea of a group for Acucobol is
not an entirely a bad idea and in fact it has some merit. Even though
I did not fully agree with the RFD's rational I think the real problem
was in he name of the news group, comp.lang.cobol.acucobol. It is 
some what vague and it needs to be more descriptive of what the group
is for. So I suggest that the name should be the following:
comp.lang.cobol.acucobol.usage. 

     By adding the term usage it clearly defines that the news group
is for the discussion of the usage of Acucobol. It eliminates the 
possible confusion that this might be another general Cobol discussion
group. IMO, this might reduce the negative responses to the initial
idea and the level of support just might increase.

     This is a potential win/win situation. You get a group to discuss
Acucobol and the group comp.lang.cobol is not affected by the 
establishment of a new news group.

I look forward to hearing any and all thoughts on this subject.
```
