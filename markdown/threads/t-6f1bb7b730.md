I believe the problem here is with the example in the manual. The
example in the manual is still valid in OS/390 COBOL. My recollection
is that old versions of COBOL supported constructs like the following:

             PERFORM CHECK-FOR-MATCH THRU CHECK-FOR-MATCH-EXIT 
                 UNTIL PARM-COUNT = 7 
                 UNTIL SSREJADV-EOF.

In other words, two separate UNTIL clauses were permitted on a single
PERFORM statement.
