You can send Hex codes to the printer by using

  01 PRTR-CODE       PIC X(2)   X"FFFF".

The X in front of the " indicates a hexidecimal value.
THen do something like

MOVE PRTR-CODE TO PRT-REC.
WRITE PRT-REC WITHOUT ADVANCING.
