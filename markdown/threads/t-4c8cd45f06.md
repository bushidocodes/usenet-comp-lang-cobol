allows you to reuse the bullet!".

the tree object to tie it to".

"Before OO cobol we had to code all of bugs by hand; now we inherit
them".
 
Q: How many OO cobol programmers does it take to change a lightbulb? 
A: None. You just send a change bulb message to the socket object! 

Q: How many OO cobol programmers does it take to change a light bulb? 
A: You're still thinking procedurally! A properly designed light bulb
object would inherit a change method from a generic light bulb class!

A surgeon, a civil engineer and a software engineer were chatting at a
bar. The discussion rolled around to whose profession was the oldest.
The surgeon said that his was, since in the book of Genesis, God
created Eve from one of Adam's ribs, and surly that involved surgery.
The civil engineer countered by saying that before God created man, he
created the heavens and the Earth from chaos, surly a feat of civil
engineering. The software engineer just smiled and said
"Where do you think the chaos came from?"
