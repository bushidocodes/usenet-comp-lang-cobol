NOTE: the following is from another non-employee (former employee though)
who is also a stock holder.  These are OPINIONS only - nothing official!!!

A) The "reason" is that with the acquisition of Intersolve, they wanted to
come up with a "new image" that was not tied to either of the old companies

B)  "Micro Focus" really has NOT reflected their true focus for years.
Furthermore, they now want to get away from their previous "COBOL only"
image (which also hasn't really been true for years).

C)  (see separate note for my HUMOROUS views of the new name)  However, the
best explanation of the new name's origin is:

    Merant (accent on first syllable, like merit)
    n. [from Latin root: 'merere' -- 'to deserve', meritum
                                                  -- 'deserved', source of
English 'merit'.

D) In my personal opinion this is a LOUSY idea.  This is from someone who
followed (a decade ago) the change of Xpediter's vendor's name from "ADS" to
"Centura" (about one year before they were bought by Compuware).  The cost
of such a name change along with the lose of "brand name recognition" seems
awful to me.  Having said that, I suspect there is about ZERO chance of
stopping the change at this time.
