I use SP2 with Acucobol. They work well together. The reason I choose
the Flexus product was for the thin client. At the time Acucobol had
no such equivalent.(They are bringing out a thin client product in
beta this month) I like the SP2 screen development environment but we
may switch to native Acucobol screens for a large screen conversion
project we have coming up. The reason we might switch has nothing to
do with SP2 not being adequate. I would actually prefer to use the SP2
product but our conversion project will involve screen and code
conversion from WANG to Acucobol and the tools that do this don't
support SP2. Since Acucobol runs on so many platforms and will now
support thin client architecture we feel confident that switching will
work out fine.
