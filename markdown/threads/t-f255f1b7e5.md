for you, but many people will help if you show what you've done already, what
you've tried, and where you are bogged down.  If you need the algorithm:
for each roll of the dice ("die" is singular, "dice" is plural BTW)
  get a random number between 0 and < 1
  multiply by 6 and add 1
  repeat above two steps and add to above result
  increment appropriate variable, perhaps by using above sum as a subscript
at the end, display the variables incremented in above step and appropriate
labels.
