..
SPECIAL-NAMES.
call-convention 35 is thunked.
..
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01 connID pic 9(9) comp-5.
77 ws-error-code pic 9(9) comp-5.

..

cobol OF98 makesyn "COMP-5" = "COMP" deffile deffiletype"os2" litlink;
