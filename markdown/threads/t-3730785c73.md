```
Can you tell us what compiler you are using? I don't recognize the error
messages. For others in the newsgroup, SOME compilers (I know Micro Focus
does) do have the following extensions:

1) Additional relational operator "<>" which is equivalent to "NOT =" (This
operator was in the JOD, but is NOT planned for the next Standard)

2) Partial expression EVALUATE - when you can put the subject in the
EVALUATE part of the statement and the rest of the relational expression in
the WHEN clause. (This feature IS scheduled for inclusion in the nest
Standard.)

Christopher, you should be aware that these are fairly unusual extensions
today. Unless your compiler explicitly says it supports them, then it
probably does NOT - and you need to recode your programs.
```
