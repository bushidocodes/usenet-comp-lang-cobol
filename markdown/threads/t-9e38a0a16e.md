I do not think you will find a difference in working environment. By
just asking your question here, you have exhibited one of the most
important attitudes you should carry with you through a computer
carreer: keep an open mind! No one language is better than another -
each one has its own function and purpose and it is often the one you
know best, that is the most productive tool for the appropriate job.

Don't be scared to plunge into the industry blindly picking the
environment - if you are enthusiastic about a computer carreer, you
will be happy in most environments and pick up enough knowledge to
make switches to different languages.
