Why is 'SCL-CODE' part of this calculation ?  Why are you dividing by
'BUSINESS'?

If you want the % of students then this is upside down anyway.  What
you may want to do is:

         Make Scl-Code numeric (PIC 9) and the 88 VALUES numeric.

         Create a table Student-Count.

         For each student, when it is read:
           IF GPA > 3
              ADD 1    TO Student-Count(Scl-Code)
              ADD 1    TO Total-Scl

         Then _after_ all reads, after the PERFORM that does the READ,
         for each Scl-Code (PERFORM):
  
         COMPUTE Bus-Mjr = Student-count(SCL-Code) * 100 / Total-Scl

         will give you the % of students in each 'Scl-Code'.
                 

What I don't know is whether the percentage is to be of the total
number of students (as here) or of the students in each group.
