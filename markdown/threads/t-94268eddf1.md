```
Well bejezuz, 'tis near Chris'mas :-

The Iranians had one hell of a conflagration in their oil patch. Really
wanted to call in Red Adair, but as they weren't too friendly with the
Yanks, as a substitute contacted his Irish counterpart Michael O'Dare.

Michael duly took the details, and quoted them a price of $50,000. "When
can you arrive ?". "O'ill be there in four hours", says our Michael.

The Iranians waited nervously at the quickly prepared air strip near the
raging fire, and sure enough in comes a huge green transport plane,
lands and immediately a green fire engine zooms out 'o the back To their
utter astonishment the fire engine heads directly into the centre of the
inferno. Out jump six firefighters in green coveralls and start beating
at the flames with bits of potato hessian sacks. Incredibly in some five
hours they had the fire beat !

Michael duly accepts the cheque for $50,000. When they are safely
emplaned and  en route to the Emerald Isle, looking at the cheque,
Michael clenches his teeth and says, "Holy muther of God. Well me boyos,
the first thing we do when we'se get home is have the bloody brakes
fixed on the fire engine !".
```
