folks got fired.  By the time I got there the only folks left were the
most socially adept ones... they were jovial, pleasant people, full of
goodwill... and every smile was tinged with 'See, I'm a nice person and a
good Team Player... please, *please* don't fire me!'

In terms of technical ability... they weren't worth a bucket of warm spit,
either.
