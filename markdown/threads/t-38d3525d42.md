No, if I understand you correctly, you cannot. Remember that 88
levels are not even fields (variables) - they are mere values against
which you check the field above them (KEYS in your case) and there
"value" clauses do not even take up any space in Working-Storage.

You should be able to use evaluate with any field that is addressable
as such and contains a value.
