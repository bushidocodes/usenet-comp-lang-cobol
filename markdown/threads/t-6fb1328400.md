Pretty big question, Dan (to say the least). IBM's VS COBOL II is their
implementation of the COBOL-85 standard. I found Ann Prince's book, "VS
COBOL II", very helpful when I made the transition in '91-'92. She
concentrates on the language differences and how to use COBOL II's new
features. The book's available in paperback (from Mike Murach, I think).
