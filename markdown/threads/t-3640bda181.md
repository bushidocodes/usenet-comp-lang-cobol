Use for:
Double height, double-width (top of letter) ESC#3
Double height, double-width (bottom of letter) ESC#4
Single height, single-width (Normal) ESC#5
Single height, double-width (Medium) ESC#6
Double height, single-width (top of letter) ESC#:
Double height, single-width (bottom of letter) ESC#;

This escapes sentence are VT100 standard.
