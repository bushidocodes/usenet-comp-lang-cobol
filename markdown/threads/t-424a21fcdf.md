```
No,  COBOL paragraphs do not take "arguments".  However, nested programs do.
Also, COBOL supports OO Objects and methods - which obviously DO also take
arguments.  (This was an extension to the '85 Standard but is included in
the 2002 ISO COBOL Standard).
```
