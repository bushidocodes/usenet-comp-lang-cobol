*	Today's largest and most powerful global corporations run their
businesses on mainframes.
*	There is no PC or midrange technology available or upcoming in the

praiseworthy response times.
*	At its May 2006 analyst conference in New York, IBM estimated that
the number of transactions run on mainframes would double by 2009.
*	Statistically, an IBM mainframe goes down only once every 50 years
or so.
