In order to get full and complete listings from Fujitsu COBOL (not
PowerCOBOL) set all of the following compiler options.  Neglecting any
may result in listings with no copybooks or listings that are
incomplete:

COPY
PRINT
SOURCE

You will proably also need:

LIB and LIBEXT with appropriate settings for your installation.
