Tina, there is a name for this activity. It is called 'spamming', and
you will make no friends in these newsgroups by spamming them. Spamming
is a hated and despised practice on the Internet. This is why many of
us camouflage our email ID's, to defend ourselves from spammers. In
fact, if lots of people complain about it to the spammer's ISP (and they
will), a reputable ISP will terminate their service.

The way to do it is to set up a nice web site, and notify all the search
engines you can think of about your site. That way, the people who are
interested can come and look, and the rest of us won't be irritated by
unwanted junk e-mail. This is how the WWW is designed to work.
