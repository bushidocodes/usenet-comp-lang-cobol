DCI's Year 2000 Issues & Answers Conference in Orlando this month features
a workshop "COBOL Refresher Course for the Year 2000". Presented by The Ken
Orr Institute's David Higgins, this half-day workshop places a special
emphasis on those portions of the language most likely to be used during a
Y2K conversion project. Mr. Higgins reviews both the Data Division
(remember the Data Division?) and the Procedure Division, and covers
important differences between COBOL 74 and COBOL II (COBOL 85). The course
is intended for programmers, programmer analysts, and test analysts
involved in Y2K projects involving COBOL source code.
