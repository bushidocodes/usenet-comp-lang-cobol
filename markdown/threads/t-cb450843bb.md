JUST does NOT right-justify the data within a field. It right-justifies a
field when moved to a larger field.
02 F1 PIC X(10) JUST.
02 F2 PIC X(05).

MOVE "AB" TO F2           F2="ABbbb"
MOVE F2 TO F1               F1="bbbbbABbb"

what we want is F1="bbbbbbbbAB". This cannot be done
with just a JUST.
