Hi,

I've been working with Realia II for about 4 years, and must say it
really works well. Be sure to get at least version 2.0. As for the DB2
part, you really need XDB Maintain, also the latest version.

Several experiences with several installations generally had very good
results. A few even are using up to 100 licences in Spain (Coritel,
EDS).

Harmen Hulsman
