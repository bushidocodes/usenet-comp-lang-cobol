In case your dealing with comma-delimited records which contain
alpha-numeric fields enclosed in double quotes, then you shoud change your
unstring statement slightly:

UNSTRING record-in-question DELIMITED BY ',' OR '",' INTO
VAR-1, VAR-2, VAR-3,
ON OVERFLOW PERFORM not-yet-done
END-UNSTRING

An example of this would be:
Soc#,Name,Address,City,State,Zip
123456789,"Doe, John","22 Any Street, Apt 2B","Anyville","CA",12345
