Acucorp provides with their COBOL runtime, their vision (ISAM) utility
program, vutil.  It has a lot functions, and the -info function will tell
you the record count.

i.e.

vutil -info <yourfilenamehere>

will tell you several basic statistics about your ISAM.

(You may have to hunt for the exact utility program name; sometimes the
utility is named vutil32, but you get the idea.)
