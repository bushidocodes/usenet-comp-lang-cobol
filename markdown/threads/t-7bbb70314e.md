```
The separators ", " and "; " may be used ANYWHERE a separator space may be
used - and it means just as much and as little as a space.

Therefore, although I doubt that any "real programmer" would code it, the
following is perfectly valid.

Procedure, Division.
 Para-1. ,
    Move ; this , to ; that, (1: , 2)
     . , , ; , ; ;
 Para-2 ; .
```
