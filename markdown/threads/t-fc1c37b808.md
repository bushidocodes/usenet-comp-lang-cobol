I suggest you are running into a rounding problem. Make sure you
have defined all of your variables with enough precision, because
the compiler determines the number of intermediat digits to carry
based on the sizes of the argumments.
Your .5 is getting rounded down to zero (in sufficient precision)
and any thing raised to the zero-th power is 1.
