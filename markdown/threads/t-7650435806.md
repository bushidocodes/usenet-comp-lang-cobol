```
This code snippet computes Easter in any year between 1900 and 2099. The
algorithm was developed by Blaise Pascal. 


     05  gregorian-date.
         10  absolute                        pic  9(02).
         10  yymmdd.
             15  yy                         pic  9(02).
             15  month-da                   pic  x(04).
     05  filler redefines gregorian-date.
         10  year                           pic  9(04).
         10  month                          pic  9(02).
         10  da                             pic  9(02).
     05  absolute-date                      pic  9(06).
     05  work-year                          pic  9(03).
     05  day-of-week-work                   pic  9(01).
     05  quotient                           pic  9(06).
     05  temp-date                          pic  9(04)v9(02).
     05  filler redefines temp-date.
         10   filler                             pic  9(04).
         10   leap-year-value             pic  9(02).
     05  pascal-year                        pic  9(04).
     05  pascal-sub                         pic  9(02).

     05  days-to-month-values value
          '000031059090120151181212243273304334365'.
         10   days-to-month occurs 13 indexed x-month pic  9(03).


     05  pascal-values value
              '04140403032304110331041804080328041604050325'.
         10   pascal-date   occurs 11       pic  x(04).

 compute-easter.
     compute work-year = year - 1900
     divide 19 into work-year giving quotient
         remainder pascal-year
     compute pascal-sub = pascal-year + 1
     if  pascal-year greater than 10
         subtract 11 from pascal-sub
     end-if
     move pascal-date (pascal-sub) to month-da
     if  pascal-year greater than 10
         subtract 1 from da
     end-if
     perform compute-absolute-date
     compute easter = absolute-date - day-of-week-work + 7.

 compute-absolute-date.
     compute work-year = year - 1900
     compute absolute-date =
         (work-year * 365.25) + days-to-month (month) + da
     divide 4 into work-year giving temp-date
     if  leap-year-value equal to zero and month less than 3
         subtract 1 from absolute-date
     end-if
     divide 7 into absolute-date
         giving quotient remainder day-of-week-work.
```

037730 is the correct 'absolute' date for Easter in 2003. To convert that to a
Gregorian date, you need this additional code.  I added comments showing how it
arrives at the correct 20030420.


     05  gregorian-date.
         10  century                         pic  9(02).
         10  yymmdd.
             15  yy                         pic  9(02).
             15  month-da                   pic  x(04).
     05  gregorian-date-rd redefines gregorian-date.
         10  year                           pic  9(04).
         10  month                          pic  9(02).
         10  da                             pic  9(02).
     05  absolute-date                      pic  9(06).
     05  work-year                          pic  9(03).
     05  day-of-week-work                   pic  9(01).
     05  quotient                           pic  9(06).
     05  temp-date                          pic  9(04)v9(02).
     05  temp-date-rd redefines temp-date.
         10   temp-days                     pic  9(04).
         10   leap-year-flag                pic  9(02).
     05  pascal-year                        pic  9(04).
     05  pascal-sub                         pic  9(02).

     05  days-to-month-values value
              '000031059090120151181212243273304334365'.
         10   days-to-month occurs 13 indexed x-month pic  9(03).

     05  pascal-values value
              '04140403032304110331041804080328041604050325'.
        10   pascal-date   occurs 11       pic  x(04).

 compute-easter.
     compute work-year = year - 1900
     divide 19 into work-year giving quotient
         remainder pascal-year
     compute pascal-sub = pascal-year + 1
     if  pascal-year greater than 10
         subtract 11 from pascal-sub
     end-if
     move pascal-date (pascal-sub) to month-da
     if  pascal-year greater than 10
         subtract 1 from da
     end-if
     perform compute-absolute-date
     compute absolute-date = absolute-date - day-of-week-work + 7 *>modified
     perform convert-to-gregorian               *> new
     display 'Easter is on ' gregorian-date    *> new
     goback.

 compute-absolute-date.
     compute work-year = year - 1900
     compute absolute-date =
         (work-year * 365.25) + days-to-month (month) + da
     divide 4 into work-year giving temp-date
     if  leap-year-flag equal to zero and month less than 3

     divide 365.25 into century-date giving work-year *> 103
     compute temp-date = century-date - (work-year * 365.25) *> 109.25
     if  leap-year-flag not equal to zero                    *> 110.25
         add 1 to temp-days
     end-if

     search days-to-month
       when temp-days not greater than days-to-month (x-month)
           continue
     end-search                                             *> 05
     compute year = work-year + 1900

     subtract 1 from month                                  *> 04
     compute da  = temp-days - days-to-month (x-month - 1). *> 20
