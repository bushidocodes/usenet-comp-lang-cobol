JOB ANNOUNCEMENT

NSP Inc., A leading retail solutions provider located in
Richmond, California, has an opening for the following position :

JOB TITLE: Programmer
STARTING DATE: Immediately

SUMMARY OF JOB DUTIES:

* Demonstrate understanding of assigned tasks for the preparation of
program level specifications.

* Demonstrate detailed knowledge of computer language and debugging

* Write and test online and batch programs according to detailed

software facilities.

program and Job Control Language.

* Support client systems, interfacing with the client on an as-needed
basis.

* Successfully complete all assignments in an on-the-job training
environment.

* Bachelors Degree or equivalent work experience.

* Minimum 2 years experience in developing and maintaining data
processing application systems.

* Technical programming skills including COBOL II, CICS, DB2, TSO/ISPF,
and JCL

* Demonstrate abilities in:
Taking initiative
Working independently
Competency in programming and unit testing skills
Create and interpret program specifications.
Organizational capabilities
Good interpersonal and communication skills.
