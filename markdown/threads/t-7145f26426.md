The code that Matteo provides will produce a push-button with a bitmap, 
and the text "Cancel" as a 'tool-tip', which will pop-up (unless you 
configure it not to) after the mouse has been over the button for three 
quarters of a second, and will then disappear again (unless you 
configure it not to!) after four seconds.

The only way I am aware of to get the effect Erlend wants is to include 
the word 'Cancel' in the desired font within the bitmap.
