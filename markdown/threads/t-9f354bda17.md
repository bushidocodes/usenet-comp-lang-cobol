```
1) comp.lang.cobol *is* an "open" (unmoderated) newsgroup, so "of course"
anyone may post whatever they want - with or without any "identification"
they want/think is appropriate.

2) I have always (privately) recommended to "vendor representatives" that
WHEN they are speaking about "their" product, that they identify their
relationship to the product.  (This includes when they either
"recommend/compare" it  or when they are providing technical
input/solutions.)

3) When a vendor representative simply MENTIONS their product provides a
solution to a specific question/problem - or as was recently "done" mentions
that such a product should be "evaluated" along with other solutions, then I
don't (personally) think they "need" to identify their relationship.  On the
other hand, it certainly never "hurts" to do so.

FINALLY,
  I really appreciate all the "vendors" who participate within
comp.lang.cobol - both as it relates to their own products and "general"
COBOL issues.  I hope that none of the current participants "worry" about
their posts and, thereby, limit their participation in the future..
```
