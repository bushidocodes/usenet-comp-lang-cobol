Contact Jerry Garfunkel at Micro Focus Cobol. He gives presentations on 
COBOL and where it will be in the future.  He can send you the slides 
from one of his presentations which point out the raw asset value of 
billions of lines of COBOL code, which translates into how most 
businesses in the world are run, the maintainability of COBOL versus C 
code and how the split now is 80% maintenance, 20% new development for 
COBOL versus the opposite ratio for C language and what a maintenance 
nightmare C will be when those numbers flip.  I can't remember the actual 
statistic but if you took the asset value of COBOL code that was in 
production in American business, it would exceed the value of all of the 
national parks real estate value combined. That is a very big number. 
Micro Focus is located in UK so you should be able to look them up in the 
phone book and have them send you a copy of his presentation slides.  
They are always looking for a marketing opportunity.  Tell them I said to 
contact you and that I am a customer of theirs from the US.  Good luck. 
Don't get caught up in any one language's bigotted advocates.  There will 
be many jobs related to COBOL code in all of our lifetimes and the 
integration between routines written in different languages is be 
enhanced everyday to the point where it really doesn't matter what 
language you write in, as long as you are proficient in one of them

Let me know how they treat you over there at Micro Focus.  BC-out.
