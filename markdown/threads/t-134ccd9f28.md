Let me add my $.02 here.
For many years, people who are technically competent have been using
languages that challege and complement their technical skills. But as
business got involved and less competent people started programming,
COBOL became necessary because of its self-documenting nature and
relative ease of programming and maintenacne.
I see this scenerio playing itself out in the PC platform. New, exciting
and good, wonderful, competent people doing the programming. But, 5-10
years from now, the demend for programming will be 10 (100, 1000??) times
greater and, here comes COBOL, for all the same reasons mainframes have
it. Get used to it.
