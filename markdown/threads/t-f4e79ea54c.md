This is a little piece I wrote when I was first learning COBOL and didn't
appreciate it's specific advantages. It's still makes me laugh, so I
hope it is entertaining (not insulting, I hope!)

COBOL is...

.. a contemptible vat of sun-ripened eel guts.

.. a good for nothing lump of rancid maggot brains.

.. a neurotic crock of old sinus clots.

.. a crochety mass of industrial-strength drain clogs.

.. a sick mound of foul snake innards.

.. a spastic glob of gross nose pickings.

.. a atrocious toilet full of moldy pig bowels.

.. a dreadful bowl of foul turkey puke.

.. a sick tub of infested cigar butts.

.. a work of extraordinary limitation.

.. a method of torture practiced in the dark ages.

COBOL = Can Of Barf OLd

Some day, I'll create CHA: COBOL Haters of America. We'd
lay around cackling madly and releasing COBOL-destroying
viruses.
------------------------------------------------------
A typical COBOL pseudo-program:

ENVIRONMENT DIVISION.
INPUT-OUTPUT SECTION.
LACK-OF-FILE-CONTROL.

SELECT OUT-FILE
ASSIGN TO 'NUL'.

DATA DIVISION.
SLEEPING-STORAGE SECTION.

01 SS-REDUNDANT-1 BEAUTIFUL-PAINTING X.
01 SS-REDUNDANT-2 PORTRAIT X.
01 SS-REDUNDANT-3 WORK OF ART X.
01 SS-REDUNDANT-4 WATERCOLORS X.
01 SS-REDUNDANT-5 GRAFITTI X.

0000-MAINLINE-HEY-LETS-CHAT-WASTE-TIME.

PERFORM 0010-FEATS-OF-EXTRORDINARY-STUPIDITY.

0010-FEATS-OF-EXTRORDINARY-STUPIDITY.

MOVE SS-REDUNDANT-1 TO SS-REDUNDANT-2.
MOVE SS-REDUNDANT-2 TO SS-REDUNDANT-3.
MOVE SS-REDUNDANT-3 TO SS-REDUNDANT-4.
MOVE SS-REDUNDANT-4 TO SS-REDUNDANT-5.

ADD BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-1
TO BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-BLAH-2
GIVING SS-HEADACHE.

WRITE O-FILE FROM SS-REDUNDANT-5 AFTER SCREWING EVERYTHING UP.
