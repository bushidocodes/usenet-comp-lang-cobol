I'm big on playing computer games.  So to get me goin back in 1980 I
purchased some books (that are so old they should all be out of print)
that contained program listings of simple games written in good old
BASIC.  I then took on the challange of translating these programs into
other languages.  The translation process forces creative thinking,
which is a real asset in this business.  In the real world of business
programming, you always know WHAT you need to do, but you must think
creatively about HOW to do it.

I still have some of these BASIC books on my office shelf.  One of them
is:
   MORE BASIC COMPUTER GAMES
   Workman Publishing, New York,  c. 1979
