```
Subject: Experience w/ IBM Global Svcs?

We have received notice that our company's entire IT operation is being
outsourced to IBM Global Services.  This includes operations in 4 North
American locations, and all functional areas -- data center operations,
networking, tech support, help desk, applications maintenance.  Has anyone had
a similar experience, particularly with IGS?

I'm 47 years old,have been with my current employer 14 years and wasn't ever
expecting to be looking for another job.  Relocation is not acceptable at this
point.

Slightly off topic: The last 3 years my job title has been Oracle DBA, and I
have been to several of the Oracle classes, but actual task assignments have
not allowed me to develop much skill in that area and I'm not comfortable
about presenting myself to a prospective employer as an Oracle DBA, though I
would like to persue that line.

Given the situation I find myself in, does anyone have any adivce?
```

```
Ed Stevens wrote:
> We have received notice that our company's entire IT operation is being
> outsourced to IBM Global Services.  

Usually, IGS would offer employment to all employees of your company who
are part of the outsourced areas.  There might be salary adjustments to
compensate for any difference in benefits, and there might be a
guarantee of continued employment with IGS for some period of time if
that was negotiated by your company.  But IGS will need your systems
knowledge, at least for the 'intermediate-term', or they will not be
able to cost-effectively support your systems.  My advice - don't worry,
go with the flow...you will have ample opportunity to make an impression
on IGS mgmt, and to make decisions about your future.

Bob
```
