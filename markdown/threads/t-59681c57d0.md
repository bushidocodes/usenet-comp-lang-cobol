```
Calculations done
Do not divide by zero
the answer is nigh

cobol sweet cobol
My mentor has stomped away
How can I learn thee?

The year two thousand
big money for date routines
COBOL's last hurrah

COBOL programmer
old, bitter, scared, dinosaur
make big money soon

type it on a tube
but it is still just punch cards
put in a pig's dress
```
