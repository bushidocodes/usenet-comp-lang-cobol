This is best handled as an error condition or as a loop.

One way to do this is to just output an error message and abort the program.
Another way to do this is put the accept in a routine that is run over and
over until the proper conditions are met.  

You could, as an alternative, accept up to 10% of gross and reject the amount
over 10% of gross.

If this is for tax purposes then you may have to go back and ask the person to
verify the amount explaining the result. 

For instance you could perform a routine until a certain condition is met. 
Ive seen some programs that save only part of a record based on how many
screens have been completed. 

Since you have to accept the amount before you can dertermine what it is, you
can not decide anything untill after you have the data.  You can always make
the person keep entering data till it is acceptable or they run out of
chances and the program is forced to end.
