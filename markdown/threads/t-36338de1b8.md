## Here's a trick. Right click on [Script][COBOL Script], then "Properties"
There you will find a place to supply the path for your copybook libraries.
The path can be specific ("C:\SOURCE\COPY"), relative (..\COPY), or
by device name (\\SERVER\SERV-C\SOURCE\COPY). When the path
is implicitly supplied, you can use statements of the form:

        01  COPY  MYSTUFF.
