I am unsure what your question really is. Are you trying to
format a date?
The US version is MM/DD/YY or MM/DD/YYYY where MM=month DD=day
YY/YYYY=year (2 or 4 digit).

Now, if you are doing European style dates, then it is DD/MM/YY
or DD/MM/YYYY.

There is yet another date format that is unambiguous --
DDMMMYY/YYYY 
Where DD=day, MMM=three letter abbreviation for the month, and
YY/YYYY is the year (2 or 4 digit format).

Ok, if you are trying to determine how many days there are in a
month, well it depends. I must assume you understand the calendar
on the wall, and so you know that months vary between 28/29
(February non-leap year/leap year), and 30-31 (all the other 11
months).

If you are talking about the number of days in a "man month",
this is generally accepted to be 180 hrs (20 days * 8 hrs).

So, there are any number of answers you can get for the question
that you have asked. You need to be a little more precise.

we do not encourage students to copy programs, you need to learn
yourself how to structure it.

Look over your requirements (the assignment)
Write down how the data needs to process (the flow for each record)
Determine what you know about each record
Determine what you need to find out from each record (which fields
trigger an action)
determine what the action(s) should be (write, change a field, ignore

do the same thing.

Programming is very logical and very sequential at this stage of your
learning
