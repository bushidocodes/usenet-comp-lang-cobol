Teach Yourself Cobol in 21 Days may be a best seller but it does not cover
scope terminators
or the evaluate verb and the coverage of inspect,string and unstring is very
limited.
It cannot be recommended as a book to update from Cobol 74 since it has
hardly
anything about Cobol 85.

The best book for people who know '74 and want '85 is by Jim Inglis "Cobol
85
for Programmers" published by Wiley ISBN 0 471 92156 4. It is more tutorial
than
another book of the same name written by Don Nelson and published by North
Holland ISBN 0 444 01232 x.However if you do not want a tutorial style the
latter
is also an excellent book.Both of them are very well written in spare and
exact
language.

John Sampson
jamsam @ cygnus.uwa.edu.au
