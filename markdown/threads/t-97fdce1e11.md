Situated on the grounds of the University of Limerick, PKS Systems
Integration (Ireland) Limited has spent the last two years establishing its
reputation in the remote delivery of high quality software maintenance
services to our Fortune 500 clients.

A subsidiary of PKS Systems Integration Inc in Phoenix, we are one of the
most exciting companies operating in the dynamic information services
industry.  PKS' software engineering centres around the world currently
employ over fifteen hundred software engineers skilled in mainframe, PC and
client server technologies.  Our ultimate parent company is Level 3
Communications Inc, a global telecommunications and information services
company.  Level 3 is NASDAQ quoted with a market capitalisation of US$12
billion.

Having proven our capabilities, primarily in the Year 2000 arena, we are
now gearing up to deliver to the same high standards in software
development, feature enhancement, general maintenance and reengineering. 
Our success to date has resulted in heavy demand from our clients for our
legacy systems renewal, application migration, re-platforming and
re-engineering services. 

Poised for significant growth, we are keen to recruit the following
personnel:

Software Engineers

Software Engineers (1 to 4 years' experience) and Senior Software Engineers
(5+ years' experience) mainly in the area of IBM mainframe COBOL
application development and maintenance. 

You should have reasonable knowledge of a mix of CICS, batch and DB2
systems, in both program design and development. 

If your main development experience is on IDMS, IMS or non-MVS, suitable
training will be provided.  As a minimum, you must be fluent in COBOL. 

Some of the services in our portfolio are designed to meet the needs of
clients, migrating legacy applications to the client server world (WEB
enabling, re-platforming, etc).  To meet the needs of the business, you
must be willing to retrain in non legacy technologies. 

We have a wide range of in-house and external training resources in such
areas as C/C++, TCP/IP, object technology and visual programming. You must
share our enthusiasm for developing your own career as well as the clients'
applications.

Technical Support Specialists

You will be part of a team that continues to enhance and support our
constantly expanding network infrastructure, providing service excellence
to our end-users. 

Experience is essential in a number of the following: PC, LAN, WAN, TCP/IP,
Windows NT, Unix and telecommunications.  

A degree/diploma in computer/electronic engineering is required as well as
a minimum of two years' experience in a similar role.

As part of our operations and technology group, you will be trained in
leading edge enterprise management software tools to proactively manage our
LAN/WAN network infrastructure from our command centre. 
 
Technical Consultants

This is a very challenging and rewarding role.  Responsible for designing
and implementing enterprise management software solutions at our client
sites in Ireland and the UK, your contribution will be a significant factor
to the growth of our Network Integration Services division.  

You will get the opportunity to work with blue chip multinationals and
explore and develop experience in new platforms, application software and
telecommunication technologies. 

You should have at least two years' experience in network management and
ideally be certified in Novell or NT.

It is essential that you have experience in LAN/WAN Windows NT and TCP/IP. 
Experience in CA-Unicenter TNG, HPOpenview or Microsoft SMS is desirable
but not essential.  

Full training will be provided where necessary. 


If you are willing to travel, some of these positions require periods of up
to six months in the US at one of our offices close to New York.  Health
care, accommodation, transport and living allowances will be provided.

Interested candidates should apply in writing enclosing a detailed CV to

Limerick
