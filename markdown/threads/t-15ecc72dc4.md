Ah, yes, the classic "blinkenlights"...

It's so classic, it even got into the Jargon file
 http://www.fwi.uva.nl/~mes/jargon/ ).

blinkenlights /blink'n-litz/ /n./ Front-panel diagnostic lights on a
computer, esp. a dinosaur. Derives from the last word of the famous
blackletter-Gothic sign in mangled pseudo-German that once graced about half
the computer rooms in the English-speaking world.
