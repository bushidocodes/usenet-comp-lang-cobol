jbgibson wrote:
›
› Does anyone know which group of people created the Cobol language?

Back in the 1950's, Admiral Grace Hopper invented a language she called
B0. She then made an enhanced version which she called Flowmatic. In
1960, Admiral Hopper was advisor to the US Navy committee which designed
a language based on Flowmatic, which they called Cobol. If you look in
a Cobol manual, you will find an acknowledgement to Univac (but not
Grace, alas) for the use of Flowmatic.

Do a web search of "Grace Hopper" and "Flowmatic" for more info.
