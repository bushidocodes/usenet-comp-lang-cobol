The ENTER statement is still part of the '85 Standard - but was obsolete
there - and is removed in the draft 200x Standard.

I checked a few years ago and couldn't find any "current" implementations
supporting it. Historically (I think) it was replaced by the CALL statement
combined with EXEC/END-EXEC statements (the former for "full procedures" in
foreign/non-COBOL languages - and the latter for "escapes" into temporary
foreign syntax).
