enhancements)

3) J4/WG4 is working as hard as they can to MAKE the year 2008 for the NEXT
standard

A) J4 is continuing to lose members (and/or having less attendees)

closer to Micro Focus who INITIALLY committed publicly to doing so)

C) Until there is an "initial" draft of the 2008 Standard out for "public
