You haven't received much help yet, from the posts I can see. None
stated the slightly nonobvious COBOL syntax when using a literal (12 in
this example) within a MULTIPLY statement:

multiply 12 by feet giving inches.

Your COMPUTE statement seems not to have the required space separators:

compute inches = feet * 12.

This is all on the assumption that your DATA DIVISION definitions for
feet and inches are correct.
