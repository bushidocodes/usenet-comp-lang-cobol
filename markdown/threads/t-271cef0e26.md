Salary range: 48,000 to 65,000


At EDS, we're looking for bright, talented individuals with a wide
range of experience, interests, goals and approaches to life. With an
end-to-end service portfolio encompassing four complimentary lines of
businesses, Management Consulting, E.solutions, Business Process
Management and Information Solutions, EDS offers an exciting range of
career possibilities.

Position Overview:
This position is in the Sacramento area with a leading healthcare
client. This position is for an individual acting under broad direction
providing enhancement and production support in mainframe Cobol, LAN
systems, and data warehouse environments. This person will be involved
in the design, development, testing and implementation of system
enhancements and fixes in support of a back-end reporting systems and
data warehouse. This individual will also be responsible for
researching and resolving problems in support of the production
environment. This position will require direct interaction with the
customer to determine requirements and develop solutions. Off shift on
call support is required.

Position Requirements:
* 2 years working experience in a mainframe, Cobol environment.
* Strong team player and able to share ideas and contribute to
achievement of common goals
* Strong problem solving skills
* Demonstrates strong verbal and written communication skills.
* Ability to design, code, test, implement and validate computer
programs.

Skill that are a plus:
* Health care experience
* Ability to work flexible hours
* Ability to manage projects within scope of responsibility
