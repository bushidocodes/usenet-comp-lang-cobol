```
We have had full coverage here of the fires in Victoria and there is a 
general feeling of sympathy and support.

Our PM has offered support to Kevin Rudd and I understand it has been 
accepted and 100 firefighters here are waiting to step in and fill the gaps 
when the volunteers who are working so hard over there have to go back to 
work. It may be a drop in the bucket but at least we are doing what we can. 
People here are shocked and appalled at what has happened.

It was heartbreaking to see interviews with some of the people who endured 
this and lost their homes and their loved ones.

Tragedies like Katrina, the Tsunami, earthquakes in Japan and elsewhere, 
flooding in China, and these terrible fires, not to mention the floods in 
Queensland which have also killed people, remind all of us how fragile we 
are against Nature and also how these adversities transcend national 
differences.

I'd like to express my condolences to anyone in CLC who has been touched by 
these events.

We can only hope this dreadful season in Victoria will be over soon without 
further loss of life.

Pete.
```
