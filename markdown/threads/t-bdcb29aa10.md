Alistair,

There are two major COBOL compilers available on AIX.  The first is,
as you mentioned, IBM COBOL for AIX.  The other option you have is the 
Merant MicroFocus COBOL Workbench.  MF COBOL does allow you to distribute the
run-time with your application freely.  Just double check the licence before 
you do so.  

IBM claims that IBM COBOL is faster than MF COBOL on AIX.  I cannot confirm 
this, but I do know that MF COBOL is faster than IBM VA COBOL on NT, and 
other PC platforms.  


Hope this helps,
Faisal Akber
