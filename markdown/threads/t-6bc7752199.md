military sectors are being invited to attend, including a number of Naval

Mayor Susan Golding, California congressman Randy "Duke" Cunningham,
numerous CEOs and CIOs from major software and hardware corporations,
representatives from major educational institutions, and the US Department
of Commerce.  A national publicity campaign will be launched in November to
attract national attention in print, broadcast and online news outlets.

The purpose of this email is to gauge your interest in either attending the
event yourself or sending a representative.  I will have a preliminary plan
prepared by mid-October, and can forward the details at that time.  Please
feel free to contact me by phone at (858) 689-4430.  I will contact you soon
to request your feedback on this important event.

Thank you.

Bill Becorest
Director of Marketing
ACUCORP, Inc.
