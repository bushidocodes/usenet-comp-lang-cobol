As another response pointed out, the field was initialized as blanks.

The obvious impact is that the numeric field starts off with 20
million dollars in it.

Assuming this is not what you want, put the appropriate value in the
field - don't ADD a new value, PLACE (MOVE, INITIALIZE, PRIME) a value
in the field.

This may happen when you creates a new record without initializing the
numeric fields. 2020202 is a typically non-numeric-pattern. Hex20 = Dec32 =
SPACE. This means that you numeric-field is filled with spaces. To avoid
this you can do an INITIALIZE on all your fields when you create your
record - BEFORE you fill them with data.
