Well, gang... I try to be a man o' my word but I am getting concerned.

As any 'regular' here knows... since 15 Jul 1998 I've been involved in
an 'experiment' here; I do not respond to recruitment-ads which do not
contain rates (or a range of rates, associated with the position(s)
offered) and another user (dav··.@my-··s.com) has
volunteered to count the number of advertisements in order to compare
the ads with rates versus the ads without rates... this to determine the
'effectiveness' of my efforts.

This past week I noticed a bit of a... discrepancy in the counts made by
dav··.@my-··s.com and the counts I made by using
DejaNews; I have posted both here and sent email regarding my confusion
and requesting the methodology of the count which was at variance with
mine... and I have received no reply, neither public nor private.

My question, then... how much time should pass before it would be
reasonable for me to consider this experiment to be abandoned by the
only other participant?

DD
