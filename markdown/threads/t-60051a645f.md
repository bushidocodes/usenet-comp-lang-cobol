are not worth a fiddler's fuck.  First of all, you cannot find anything,
then when you do, you cannot read it, then when you manage to get
it to a size that it is readable, you cannot cut and paste the examples.

are going to be online, then why not part of the help?  I want one of
those old-fashioned charts (standard Cobol) with every term and the
arguments in various levels of brackets.  Then every word should be
a hypertext link to the next level of detail, right down to an example
I can paste into my code. *That* would be worth as much as the product.
