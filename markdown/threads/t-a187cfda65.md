Hello,

are you using IBM COBOL on AIX? Let's form a user group.

Currently we are having severe difficulties with IBM's debugger (xldb).
If you are too, maybe we could jointly lobby IBM for relief.

We are also looking into an alternative, which is to add COBOL support to
gdb, the free GNU debugger. Perhaps we could pool resources on this
project.

In any case, it would be helpful to share tips and tricks and our bug
lists.

Drop me a line if you are interested.
