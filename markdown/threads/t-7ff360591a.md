Hi Mike,

When barging into a newsgroup with a job ad v. a technical discussion,
it's considered good form to list the salary range or hourly rate.

You see, this is a virtual collection of digit-heads who are sipping
coffee and chatting about 88-levels and neat ways to set up VSAM
records.

YOU are a guy in a plaid suit who slaps everyone on the shoulder, calls
them 'bud', and asks for their resume because you stand to make
thousands of dollars off them.

The reason to list salary is everyone here knows they can get a job
this afternoon. The Washington Post carries 17+ pages of programmer ads
every week. There are special job listing only newsgroups with
thousands of jobs.

While we cannot request that posters to comp.jobs (or whatever it's
called, follow a format.), the contributors to and readers of this
newsgroup, certainly have every right to ask for salary in postings.

If you supply salary ranges, you bring useful information to the
newsgroup.

Cory Hamasaki
