Here is walkthrough for all my problems (this may help other people too. I
hope...):
Fujitsu Cobol compiler V3.0 wants that section names, statements and so on,
are in their right columns.
Columns 1-6 are for line numbers, column 7 is for special marks like *
(comment mark),
columns 8-11 are for beginning of section and paragraph names, columns 12-72
are for beginning of statements.

That's it! Get it?

Thanks to everybody!

Jake.
