› What is the different between MS Cobol and MF Cobol ????

Prior to MS-Cobol version 4.x the two compilers were completely
different, MS deriving from the pre-1980 MS CP/M product COBOL-80
and MF deriving from its own CP/M CIS-Cobol (derived from an earlier
1500 Cobol in its own turn).

MS Cobol 4.5 is a rebadged MF 2.5, MS 5.0 is MF 3.0. I don't know
if there was an earlier MS 4.x or a later 5.x.

The compilers are the same (or so close as to make no difference).
There are differences in the support programs: PWB instead of
Editor, in 5.0 there is no OS/2 1.x support (ripped out by MS),
and other MS orientated bits.
