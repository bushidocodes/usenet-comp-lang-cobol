I have been a member of DPMA/AITP since around 1981, with a 5 year
hiatus.  I notice that its membership has dropped steadily, while
specialty clubs (Oracle, NT, Powerbuilder, etc) have had increased
membership.

Of course, society as a whole is specializing.  Social clubs have been
largely replaced by specialty clubs.  But I think I am better off
spending one evening per week learning about areas in DP outside of my
specialty than learning more about my specialty.

Is generalism within a technical field such as programming dead?  How
does that effect our productivity?
