Assuming you're speaking of Mainframe COBOL COMP (which is BINARY), are you
asking to convert (for example), a fullword binary to DISPLAY?

Just move the fullword to a 10-byte DISPLAY NUMERIC field. It MUST be 10-bytes
because the high-water mark of a fullword is 2,147,483,647 (X'7FFFFFFF'). I
think it would be wise to use the Compile Option TRUNC(BIN) so that you don't
lose significant high-order digits.

Or, are you asking to convert each 4-Bit binary nibble to its associated
1-Byte DISPLAY counterpart? For example, converting X'7FFFFFFF'(4-Bytes) to
C'7FFFFFFFF'(8-Bytes)?

WOB
