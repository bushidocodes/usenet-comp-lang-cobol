Hopefully, you won't have to deal with COBOL/370 which is 2 release out of date - but instead with either COBOL for MVS & VM (1
release out of date) or COBOL for OS/390 & VM (current product). At the simplest level:

VS COBOL II was just ANSI 85

COBOL/370 added Intrinsic Functions (and a few CALL enhancements, like procedure-pointers)

COBOL for MVS & VM added OO COBOL

COBOL for OS/390 & VM added very little

If you look at the IBM Migration Guide (and there is one for each of the products), it will tell you the differences from all the
earlier products. The latest one can be seen online (although I suggest you get a hardcopy) at

http://ppdbooks.pok.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr/bookmgr.cmd/BOOKS/IGYMG200/CCONTENTS
