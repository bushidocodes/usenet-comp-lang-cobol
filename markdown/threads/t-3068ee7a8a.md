The other day we noticed that a Standard Piano
KeyBoard has 88 Keys - and there is the
Level-Number 88 for conditions. Coincidence?

Level Numbers 66, 77 and 88 into the range
of 97 .. 99, and extend the legal data

data-structure with all 49 Level numbers in
use, I would refuse even to attempt to
understand it.
