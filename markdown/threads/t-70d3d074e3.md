Amway Corporation has created a new position in their Marketing
Department for a Programmer/Analyst to provide computer support with
respect to the Corporation's World Wide Sales Plan Database. This
individual needs to be proficient in programming in COBOL and
Asynchronous Protocol Specification on the IBM ES/9000. He or she
will provide analysis and programming to facilitate database studies,
analyses and queries using a variety of query tools as well as
database development and maintenance activities. The position reports
to the Supervisor, World Wide Sales Plan Administration and is located
in Grand Rapids, MI.

The prototype candidate has a BS in Comp Sci or related high
technology, information systems field. He or she has three or more
years experience programming COBOL and APS on the ES/9000 in a
database environment. A solid foundation in mainframe database query
tools, data warehousing, and mainframe to PC communication systems is
also necessary. Experience with the AS/400 networked to an ES/9000
would be a real plus.

The Wentworth Company is conducting a retained search to fill this
position.
