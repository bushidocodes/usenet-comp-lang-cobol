I would be curious if various people try this with different compilers -
to see if
   ==(a:b)==

does or does not match
  ==(a : b)==

in copy/replacing.  I haven't checked my '85 Standard - BUT the two do
match for TEXT WORDS - but just have different "text separators".
Without looking at the Standard, I *think* (but am not positive) that
these SHOULD match.

Obviously (back to the original comment) something like

  ==a-b==
would NOT match
  ==a - b==

but I really would be interested in how various compilers handle the
spaces around a colon.
