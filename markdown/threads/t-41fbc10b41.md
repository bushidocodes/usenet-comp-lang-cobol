The best revision control tool for UNIX (and for NT too)
is CVS. It is free software availablde from ftp.cyclic.com
and www.cyclic.com. You can buy a maintenance contract from them,
if you need to spend money.

We have millions of lines of Cobol under CVS in our
test source repository. Geographically distributed developement
also works well with CVS.

Features:
- Works on most platforms. Certainly on all UNIXes, NT, Win-95.
- Works in client-server mode via slow lines.
- No need to lock files in repository.

Vadim Maslov
