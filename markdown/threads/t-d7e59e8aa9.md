� Strong technical and functional knowledge of business applications 
including Human Resource (HR), Benefits, and Payroll.
� Bachelor degree from an accredited college or university.

Other skills desired and considered:
� Experience with the support or Lawson business application products 
including writing custom programs using Lawson Cobol.
� Experience in developing web base applications
� JAVAScript, HTML, Lawson Design Studio, SQL
� Knowledge of C#, .Net and Rational UML
� Report Writers such as Crystal or Discoverer
