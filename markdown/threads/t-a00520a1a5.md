Jerry - the most stunning thing about this release IS the
documentation.  Some great care was taken in creating documentation
that was written in English instead of translated to English.  They
have come a long way.  The installer works a whole lot better as well.

Those of you who don't have current maintenance -- you may wish to get
caught up.  V5 is a much bigger change than 4.2 was from 4.0.  There
are some good things here.

One small caveat - the Collection Class library is only available in
the most expensive version - The enterprise edition.  However, the
ISAPI and CGI stuff is available in the standard edition.

All in all I am very happy I have kept up with my maintenance.  It's a
worthwhile release.
