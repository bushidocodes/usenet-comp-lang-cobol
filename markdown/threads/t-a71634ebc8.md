Mooney's what?   I loved the only Mooney I ever flew, but a higher wing
is LESS likely to fold up because it is less likely to hit the ground! 
I can't see it making any difference otherwise.  Hmmm, if the design is
between one single wing vs two separate wings, it could, but it seems
that could favor the high wing.

Passengers like high wings for the shade and view.  But I prefer a low
wing because when I want to look to the left, I dip my wing.  With a low
wing, it gets out of my way, with a high wing, it gets in my way!
