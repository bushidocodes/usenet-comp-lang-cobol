Well, looks like I got it backwards, then... not the first time, won't
be the last. You might try the following statements (suitably modified
for your shop's standards, of course):

MOVE 359 TO A-RECORD-SIZE.
EVALUATE TRUE
WHEN TYPE-1-REC
MOVE SPACES TO SASINV-RECORD(17:343)
WHEN TYPE-2-REC
MOVE SPACES TO SASINV-RECORD(101:259)
END-EVALUATE.
WRITE OUTREC FROM SASINV-RECORD.

(you might want to re-check my math there, I did it off the top of my
head)

DD
