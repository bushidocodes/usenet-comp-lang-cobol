As an intro to OO COBOL on PCs check both Fujitsu and Merant (Micro
Focus) sites. Fujitsu will shortly release a student version and Merant
has the University edition of NetExpress; each of course has a 'full

already familiar with these. Look to Flexus for their SP2 product which
