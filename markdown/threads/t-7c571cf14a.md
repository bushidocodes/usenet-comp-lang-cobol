Hello,

My name is Bill Becorest and I am the director of marketing at Acucorp,
Inc.  I wanted to let everyone know that there is an open invitation to
the 40th Year Anniversary of COBOL Celebration in San Diego on January
21, 2000.  I have included some relevant information below:

Acucorp, Inc., Computerworld Magazine, and the newly formed COBOL
Alliance are hosting this milestone celebration.  Two events are
included on January 21:  Software Technology Summit 2000; and the Grace
Murray Hopper Commemorative Ceremony (alongside the USS Hopper on Navy
Pier).  This is an industry-wide gathering that will include military
officials, government representatives, Fortune 1000 companies,
technologists, CIOs, CEOs, educators, and students.  Alll organizations
and individuals with a vested interested in COBOL are welcome to attend.

The Software Technology Summit will be hosted by CNN's Ann Kellan.  The
Commemorative Ceremony will honor the achievements of Grace Hopper, and
include shipboard tours of the USS Hopper, a multi-mission
AEGIS guided missile destroyer.

There is no cost to attend (aside from personal transportation and
lodging); however RSVPs are required.  To RSVP, call 1-800-262-6585.
You can also RSVP and get more information online at
www.cobol40years.com.  Please feel free to contact me directly for more
information.

Thank you.

Bill Becorest
(858) 689-4430
Director of Marketing
Acucorp, Inc.
