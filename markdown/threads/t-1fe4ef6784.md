One of the reasons for doing away with all periods except the last
one in a paragraph, is that they are small (not highly visible).

I believe that this extends to the period used at the end of the
paragraph as well, especially when using a free-format, variable-
line-length COBOL.

My preference therefore is that the last line of a paragraph is
always:
               CONTINUE.

This helps to guard against accidental deletion of the line.
