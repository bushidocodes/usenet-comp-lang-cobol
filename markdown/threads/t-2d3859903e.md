Go to google.com and as a search enter "SQL" or better "ESQL". However,
your COBOL compiler must have an SQL pre-processor to allow you to insert
E(mbedded)SQL statements into your COBOL programs. Your appropriate COBOL
manuals/on-line help for either Unix or Windows will also need to contain
some reference to the Oracle DB, showing how to 'connect' the COBOL and
Oracle software.
