Attaching new message posts concerning one subject (an online COBOL manual)
to the end of a thread about another subject (the MARK4 computer language)
is usually considered something of a misstep on usenet (unless there is some
other reason I can't see right away; the last post to this thread was back
on the 5th of November, and this new post does not seem to continue the
discussion).

Try posting an original message to this newsgroup.

But before you do, read the COBOL FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions; which can
be located using most search engines; here's one address:
http://pweb.netcom.com/~wmklein/cobolfaq.htm).

Look up FAQ Entry 10.10 for a short answer to your question about COBOL
Unleashed.

For longer answers, go to www.deja.com, do a powersearch on this newsgropu
using the keywords COBOL and Unleashed to get many, many longer answers to
your book question.


Sincerely,
     Chris Osborne
