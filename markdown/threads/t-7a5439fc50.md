```
Gotcha <G>

If you look at your logic when
   PF-PAY-CODE = 1
you will see that you never move anything to
 DL-HOURLY-RATE
not to its redefinition in
 DL-ANNUAL-SALARY

Therefore, it will "keep" whatever was that storage location from the last
print line.

There are several ways that you could "solve" this.

A) Move spaces to your entire Working-Storage area after each write (so it
is cleared out before the next one)

B) Make certain that you move SOMETHING to every byte in your WS area before
doing a print from that record.

C) (probably what you want to do) "fix" the following lines in your logic:

    MOVE PF-ANNUAL-SALARY TO
          DL-PAY-ADJUST-S
    MOVE PF-PAY-ADJUST-S TO
        DL-PAY-ADJUST-S

I'll *bet* that you meant to have that first move go to
  DL-ANNUAL-SALARY
      and not to
 DL-PAY-ADJUST-S

P.S.  This is a "common" error even among "experienced" COBOL programmers.
You copy logic from one place to another and "forget" to make all the
required changes.
```
