```
Thanks to Gary Hasler for helping us solve the problem of opening the
card reader as a 'file' with COBOL II under the LE/VSE runtime.  As I
thought, this was not a programming issue.  It turns out that the
assignment for the virtual card reader that is set at IPL was a 2501,
which is no longer a supported device.  For whatever reason it still
worked under the COBOL II runtime but not the LE/VSE runtime.

My systems programmer IPL'd this morning assigning it as a 3505 (I
think) and everything now works!

Thanks to everyone who tried to help on this matter.
```
