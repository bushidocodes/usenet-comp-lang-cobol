```
Try LINAGE IS 60 LINES.  Then just define how many lines you need for
the page headings and footings.  The report footing, printed at the end
of the report, should be part of the body of the report and has nothing
to do with a page footing.  Normally we do not use the LINAGE clause (in
IBM world) unless you need to explicitly control pagination on special
forms.
```
