  UNSTRING  dataname(n:m)
       delimited by comma
       into ....


.. and the compiler is telling you that "dataname (n:m)" is not allowed, and
you must use something like..

   move dataname(n:m) to ws-dataname
   unstring ws-dataname     <<< no reference modification
     delimited by comma
     into ...
