I think the Gartner Group does something similar to what you are looking
for.  Typically, they charge hundreds of thousands of dollars for it. 
However, you can probably do the same thing they do.  You can try cold
calling several organizations and see if any will help you out.  There
are thousands of organizations which use mainframes - most banks,
utility companies, etc.  Try contacting people in their IS department
(probably too busy to help you) or you can try people in the Public
Relations department whose job it is to be nice to the public.  If you
tried 2 dozen companies, you should get at least a few responses.
