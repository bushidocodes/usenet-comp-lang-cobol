For everyone who knows that the best COBOL code is cranked out by hand
using green text on a black bacground, check out the free ISPF editor

It looks and performs like the real thing (or like SPF/PC for CTC, which
is not an IBM product and costs $199.00). Most of the functionality of

ON/OFF, but it's a beta release, so that may be in the works.
