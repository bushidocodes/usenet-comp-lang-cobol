```
This is not very timely, but I just discovered the following routine
which extracts the numeric portion of a BMS input field and moves the
number, right justified, to a second field:

      *--------------------------------------------------------------*
      *    First step is to extract the numeric part of XCTLIDI      *
      *    (1-15 decimal digits, S-1 contains the byte count WHEN    *
      *    we're done)                                               *
      *--------------------------------------------------------------*
           PERFORM VARYING  S-2 FROM  +1  BY  +1
                     UNTIL  S-2        >   LENGTH OF XCTLIDI
              IF  XCTLIDI (S-2:1)     NUMERIC
                   MOVE  S-2           TO  S-1
               ELSE
                   MOVE  +999          TO  S-2
               END-IF
           END-PERFORM

      *--------------------------------------------------------------*
      *    and right justify it in our work area                     *
      *--------------------------------------------------------------*
           MOVE  LENGTH OF WS-ABSTIME-WORKA    TO  S-2
           INITIALIZE                  WS-ABSTIME-WORKA
           SUBTRACT  S-1               FROM  S-2
           ADD   +1                    TO  S-2

      *--------------------------------------------------------------*
      *    Move just the numeric part of XCTLIDI into the ABSTIME    *
      *    work area, right justified                                *
      *--------------------------------------------------------------*
           MOVE  XCTLIDI (1:S-1)       TO  WS-ABSTIME-WORKA (S-2:S-1)
           MOVE  WS-ABSTIME-WORKA      TO  WS-MONGO-DATE
           PERFORM  G300-DISPLAY-ABSTIME

The fields are defined as:

	05	XCTLIDI			PIC  X(44).
	05	WS-MONGO-DATE		PIC  S9(15).
	05	WS-ABSTIME-WORKA	PIC  X(15).    (not certain)
	05	S-1			PIC  S9999  BINARY.
	05	S-2			PIC  S9999  BINARY.

Hope someone finds this useful,
Bill Lynch
```
