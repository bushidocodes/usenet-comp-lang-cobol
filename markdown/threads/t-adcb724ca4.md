I have been fielding some questions regarding OO.  So I thought I
would put together some mail notes on OO concepts.  Not 'the'
definition of the term or concept more a description of some of the
terms and how they are used in 'normal' programs.

This will highlight the similarities and differences of OO and
procedural programming.

OO really is not a new concept, more it is the bundling of old
concepts in a new way making them work better.

I thought that I run notes on:

Encapsulation
polymorphism
inheritance
design patterns

as a start.  If anyone wants to 'ask Horace and Winnie' please let me
know.

Thanks
