In the next day or so, I am going to post the full (mostly) revised COBOL
FAQ to comp.lang.cobol and alt.cobol. I personally think it is best viewed

"need" to post a pure text version of it in the newsgroups (every few

a newsgroup post - however, I understand not all viewers can read this.

My question is:
Do I just post the text version (and point to the web for the HTML?
