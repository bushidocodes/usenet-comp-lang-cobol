You will be in a sort of limbo John.  While you can claim experience in COBOL
your practical experience in all the other skills is nill and that can be a
show stopper. On the other hand if you've worked with any kind of SQL then you
can relate that to DB2 and maybe can talk your way into that.

A lot of shops want people to have at least a year in all the skills listed. 
You can get away with having just CICS and no DB2 or vice versa but neither
will hurt.

You may be able to find an entry level job that will train you in CICS, DB2 and
JCL but don't expect to go into a mainframe shop any higher than that with out
practical experience.  Also don't expect to do much but grunt maintenance for a
while as you aquire the skills you're lacking.

Eileen
