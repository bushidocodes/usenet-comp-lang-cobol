I don't know anything about FormPrint nor Crystal reports, but the
NetExpress PC_PRINTER_ routines should work.  I used to use
PC_PRINT_FILE to print the whole file which I formated elsewhere, but
have been advised by tech support that PC_PRINTER_OPEN,
PC_PRINTER_WRITE etc are better.  They work.  The only problem I have
had is with special control characters not getting converted under NT.
It may be a problem with my printer drivers, but since it worked under
W95 with PC_PRINT_FILE I think it should work under NT with
PC_PRINTER_WRITE.
