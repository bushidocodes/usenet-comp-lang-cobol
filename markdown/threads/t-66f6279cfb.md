It doesn't matter if you use base 'e' or base 10 or base whatever, as long
as you use matching-base logarithm and exponentiation functions (LOG and EXP
below):

   COMODO = WA-IMP * EXP(LOG (1-TAX/100) * (GIORINI/365))

It's represented as two asterisks.

Compute a square root example:

03 WS-SQRT    PIC S9(08)V07 PACKED-DECIMAL.
03 WS-VALUE PIC S9(15)         PACKED-DECIMAL.

COMPUTE WS-SQRT = (WS-VALUE ** .5)

HTH....

Bill
