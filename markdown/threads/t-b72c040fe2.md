Use PREV-DEPT and LINE-COUNT.
IF CURR-DEPT EQUAL PREV-DEPT
AND LINE-COUNT GREATER THAN 1
MOVE SPACES TO (first few items on the detail line).
Don't forget to MOVE CURR-DEPT TO PREV-DEPT if they are not the same.
And to reset LINE-COUNT to 1 when the new DEPT page is being published.
Of course, this assumes the input is sorted by DEPT.
