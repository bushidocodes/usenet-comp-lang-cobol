accepts a

   $SET

statement are those from Micro Focus.  (Other compilers have other ways of 
specifying directive - e.g.
   @OPTIONS - Fujitsu
   CBL or PROCESS - IBM

    www.microfocus.com

for information on their various compilers available for purchase (and download)

"$SET" statements?
