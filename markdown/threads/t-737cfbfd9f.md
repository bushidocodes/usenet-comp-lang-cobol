In Rancho Cordova EDS supports a COBOL/CICS mainframe system used by 19
California counties for Welfare processing. The work is predominantly
mainframe COBOL and CICS, however Analyst/programmers work on PC
workstations using PC coding, testing, and debugging tools such as
Microfocus COBOL workbench, CA Realia workbench, and REVOLVE.

EDS is looking primarily for potential. Individuals who have been
successful for at least two years in a programming environment, whether
it's been in COBOL or not, should apply if you are looking for a
challeging career.

EDS also has Analyst/programmers working on additional projects for our
customers on alternative platforms such as Sun workstations using UNIX,
Powerbuilder, Openroad, Clipper, MS Access, MAPPER, SYBASE, INGRES, and
ERWIN. People working on these projects have worked with data modeling,
database design, proto-typing, conducting JAD/RAD (Joint Application
Design/Rapid Application Design), and object-oriented programming.

Qualified candidates must possess at least 1 year experience in the
following areas:

*IBM or compatible mainframe in an MVS environment.
*Program design
*COBOL or any business programming
*Communicating technical concepts to non-technical people.
*CICS (preferred, not required)
