I use QWS3270 at work as my terminal emulator. Only $35 and it allows
me to remap the keyboard and do cut-and-paste to any other Windows NT
app. I would recommend this 3270 emulator to anyone, and I have
exchanged e-mail with the author, Jim Rymerson, in Canada.

There is also a freeware version available that works very well, but
does not allow keyboard remapping. Both versions let you change the
font and colors, support screen printing to your LAN printer, and have
typeahead.

QWS3270 does require a TCP/IP stack on the mainframe for direct
connection.
