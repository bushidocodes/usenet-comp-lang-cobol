"MERANT Works With IBM to Provide Customers With High-Performance Development
Platform; MERANT Products Now Available for IBM's AIX 5L On IBM Power and
Intel IA-64 Architectures

August 16, 2000 08:26 AM Eastern Time

ROCKVILLE, Md.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Aug. 16, 2000--MERANT , a leading global
e-business software solutions company, today announced a technology
partnership with IBM, whereby MERANT's Server Express, a product in MERANT's
Egility solutions suite, will be ported to IBM's AIX 5L UNIX operating system
to support both the IBM Power and the Intel IA-64 bit architectures. Under
this agreement, MERANT's Egility solutions will enable customers to utilize
this powerful new development platform for enterprise and e-business
applications.

"MERANT's Server Express is an important development tool for many new and
existing applications," said Tilak Agerwala, vice president of UNIX marketing
and product management for IBM. "Our customers will be delighted with the
increased flexibility as they will have to place their applications on new,
cost-effective server platforms when Server Express becomes available for AIX
5L on both the IBM Power and the Intel IA-64 platforms."

"This partnership, which includes funding from IBM, demonstrates MERANT's
leadership and commitment to supporting key customer platforms in this
rapidly emerging e-business environment," said Tony Hill, senior vice
president and general manager of MERANT. "IBM's AIX 5L Operating System is a
cutting edge environment, and by leveraging our solutions for this platform,
customers will now have a clear business advantage over the competition in
their market spaces."

MERANT's Traditional Development, Legacy Extension and Legacy Integration
solutions are all supported through this agreement. These solutions enable
enterprises to extend their legacy assets to the Web, integrate their newly
developed Web presence with their existing systems, and provide a highly
productive and robust enterprise application development environment for
mission-critical applications including e-business.

AIX 5L on IA-64 incorporates technologies from IBM's AIX, DYNIX/ptx and SCO
UnixWare. AIX 5L is designed to run on servers built around IBM's Power and
Intel's IA-64 and architectures. IBM, and other manufacturers, will offer
customers a choice of servers capable of running AIX 5L, allowing them to
take advantage of the industry's strongest UNIX operating system on both the
Power and IA-64 platform. With MERANT's Server Express on AIX 5L, customers
will be able to build new applications and migrate existing enterprise
applications to the proven POWER architecture or to the emerging IA-64
platform. "
