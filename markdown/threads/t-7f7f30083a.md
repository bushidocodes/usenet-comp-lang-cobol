"Better than me," huh? Well, not at punctuation, anyway.

You can get a student copy of Microfocus Cobol for about $50 here in
the U.S.

Or, you can buy the _COBOL for Dummies_ book; it has several trial
compilers, and one that appears to be long-term (as opposed to a
90-day trial version). The book is not too good, though.

Also, there is a book called _Revolutionay COBOL_ that has a simple
compiler (haven't tried it out yet).

There appears to be at least one shareware compiler at
www.shareware.com, though I haven't downloaded one yet.

Good luck! (And work on those punctuation skills, for heaven's sake!).
