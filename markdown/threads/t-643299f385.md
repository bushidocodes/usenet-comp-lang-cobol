Or to be more explicit, the IBM mainframe compiler VAGUELY supports the '68
(not '74 - much less '85) ANSI Report Writer module with LOTS of "oddities"
(otherwise known as "bugs").  If you look on the web for SPC systems, they
have an "add-on" product that understands all the "peculiarities" of the IBM
OS/VS COBOL (and compatible) RW variations.  I am not positive that they
have a product for Fujitsu, but it is certainly worth looking into.

To the best of my knowledge, Fujitsu's compiler *is* conforming to the
ANSI'85 RW definition - but that will cause you problems (some caught at
compile-time - some not until you look at your output) if you are porting
IBM mainframe RW code.
