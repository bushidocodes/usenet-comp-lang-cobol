1. Firstly it is COBOL (Common Business Oriented Language)

2. At the current time there are three compilers that offer OO COBOL (1)
IBM (sort of !) and on PCs, (2) Fujitsu and (3) Micro Focus

3. The draft COBOL Standard COBOL 200x, has been produced and hopefully
will be known as COBOL 2002 - due for final rubber stamp by December 2002.
This includes updates to Procedural COBOL and Object Oriented COBOL.
(Both Fujitsu and Micro Focus include collections/dictionaries (Lists) in
their current versions - and the setting of the standard for these is
currently under discussion by J4 - the prime COBOL Standards Committee).

4. You can see the draft for COBOL 2002 at :-

        http://www.incits.org/tc_home/j4.htm
