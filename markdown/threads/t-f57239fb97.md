```
You don't tell us your compiler or operating system, but my GUESS is that
you are running on MVS or OS/390 or z/OS.

If so, you can call an IBM-supplied routine

   ILBOWAT0

Passing it a full word binary field with the number of seconds to wait.
HOWEVER, the parameter passed to this routine must be "below the 16M line" -
so either compile your program with DATA(24) - or use a "pass thru" routine
to move the data below the line.
```
