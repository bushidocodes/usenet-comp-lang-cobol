erased from current cursor position to the end of the field.

Haven't found this on a PC keyboard yet, but I'm still looking :)

data entry about twice a week. Due to the format of the data, an
Erase EOF key would really speed things up.

All our processing is done in green-screen I-O, no mouse or windows.

Anyone ever created an Erase EOF key for PC keyboards?

You have at least three options:

1. You may configure ADIS to erase the field before entering data.
2. You may use Ctrl+Z to "Clear to end of field."
3. You may use Ctrl+X to "Clear field."

These options are covered in the "COBOL System Reference"
in the chapter on ADIS.
