If you want an easy way, and space is not an issue, then dump the PDS to 
a temporary IEBUPDTE file.  It will be a QSAM segmented like so:

   ./ADD MEMBER=MEM1
   blah
   blah
   blah
   ./ADD MEMBER=MEM2
   blah
   blah
   blah
   ./ADD MEMBER=MEM3
   etc

You can scan for the './' member separations in the first 2 columns.
