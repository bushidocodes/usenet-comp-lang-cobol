```
To All:



Yesterday we received the final result of the chromosome testing.  They are 
100% certain that our baby has trisomy-18, a fatal chromosome condition. 
This was the result of a G-band test.  The preliminary results we received 
before were from a FISH test and apparently the result was a false negative. 
The cause of the discrepancy is not known but may be related to the small 
number of cells available for testing.  Our baby is expected to pass away 
either before birth or shortly thereafter.



At this time we do not know if Judith will be able to continue staying in 
the hospital or if she and the baby will be monitored weekly on an 
out-patient basis. Our current plan is for Judith to carry the baby until it 
passes or until it is born. The doctors may recommend that labor be induced 
at week 34 (we are now at week 27) if our baby survives that long.



Thanks to you all for you prayers for our baby and also for us.
```
