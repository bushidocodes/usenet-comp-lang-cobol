I had a look at it. It is a new angle on an old idea, but very
professionally done. If I lived in Toronto, I'd probably register, but you
are very wise to seek the experience of people who have done so.

The succes of this will be measured by the number (and quality) of employers
they can attract . If they build relationships over time with major Toronto
players, it could be a very good site to be registered on. If they just do
what most agencies do (protect the interest of the employer rather than
being an 'honest broker' representing both parties), then they are unlikely
to be of much use.

Good Luck!

Pete.
