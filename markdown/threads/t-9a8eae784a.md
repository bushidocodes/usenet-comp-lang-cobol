I think you are getting into a number of legal issues. One way to go with
this is before you go to far seek legal advice. You will likely need proper
contracts drawn up between you and the various shops and between you
and the supplier of the parts catalog. In addition, you will be running into

several tax issues which can get messy.

All of this said, I am not a lawyer/accountant and I think your best bet
is to seek professional advise concerning these issues. It will cost you
money but in the long run it will be money well spent, IMO.

Just my two cents. I needed to do something similar years ago. This 
particular vendor was much too difficult to deal with for resale or 
licensing.

To solve the problem, I simply included the conversion routines in the 
software and made it clear in the documentation where the client could 
purchase the data if they desired and how to run my conversion.

It depends on how easy your vendor is to work with, how much effort you 
want to invest and how inconvenient it is to the client this way.

might just include an upload utility that will load the updated catalog

format compatible with your system.
