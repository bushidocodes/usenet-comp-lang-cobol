```
I'm assuming you want to take a mainframe CICS program and compile it
on the PC. You want the "translator" program that converts the EXEC
CICS statements into standard CALL's. You will need some sort of PC
CICS environment. Anyone can buy CA's Realia-CICS or their new
Workbench II which has that built in. Or you can get Micro Focus
COBOL and get their CICS add on. For BMS macro creation and
modification, you should look at the CICS product from Flexus -
http://www.flexus.com - they even support the 132 column screen size!
```
