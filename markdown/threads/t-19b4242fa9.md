```
Did you actually send the output to a printer or do you just see one "blank
line" when you look at the file?  Is it possible that it is using some
"unprintable" characters that you don't see and that if you actually
directed your output to a printer that it would advance the 3 lines?

I have heard of this as a COMMON problem on IBM mainframes, but don't
remember hearing of it on the PC - but I can imagine there being some "hex"
combinations that don't show up "correctly" when you view them - but would
on a printer.

The other question (sort-of related to above) is whether or not you have
defined your output as "line sequential" or "record sequential".  Again, how
the ADVANCING phrase is handled for those two types of files MAY be
different - and again different in a "text-editor" than an actual printer.
```
