I think you will see it is your nested if statements that is giving
you grief. Try this...

 ADRES-START.
*============
	PERFORM UNTIL ALL-DONE
		MOVE SPACES TO KEY-PRESSED
		DISPLAY MAIN-SCREEN
		ACCEPT MAIN-SCREEN
	        EVALUATE KEY-PRESSED
			WHEN "1"
				CALL "SCHRIJF"
			WHEN "2"
				CALL "LEES"
			WHEN "3"
				CALL "HERSCHRIJF"
			WHEN "4"
				SET ALL-DONE TO TRUE
		END-EVALUATE	
		MOVE SPACES TO KEY-PRESSED	
	END-PERFORM.
