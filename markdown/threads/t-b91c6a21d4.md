Thane:

You are much too kind! Thank you for your wonderful comments, but I
have a confession to make...

Mr. Rodriguez wants to print compressed images such as .JPG and .GIF
images from his Micro Focus COBOL program.

Currently we do support the ability to print 256 color .BMP files
which provide reasonably good quality images, but .JPG images (by
default) are 16 million color images. They're also generally quite a
bit smaller (because of compression) than .BMP image files.

Our COBOL sp2 product has a special dynamic link library which does
allow the display of alternate bitmap formats such as .JPG, .TIF,
PCX, yadda, yadda, yadda (30 formats), but we haven't added that to
FormPrint.

I don't see why we can't add it since sp2 already displays the images.

Perhaps Mr. Rodriguez can initiate an e-mail discussion with me to see
just what we might be able to do to enhance the product. It's
certainly a good enhancement for FormPrint...wait a minute, I just
figured out that Mr. J. Rodriguez is already using sp2! Small world!
Hey Juan...give me a call and we'll talk about it!!

Thane...Thanks again for the kind words!
