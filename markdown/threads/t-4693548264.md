I once asked the same question of Realia. Their response was that the
blinding speed of their ISAM files was a competitive advantage and
they were reluctant to publicize their internal techniques - even so
far as to describe the layout.

You may have to settle for describing the old IBM ISAM techniques,
which are really pretty good for understanding the concept. More
modern methodologies, in my opinion, might get you so bogged down in
minutia as to obscure the basic idea.
