It was the change from IBM COBOL for OS/390 & VM (V2R2) to
                                    IBM Enterprise COBOL for z/OS & OS/390
(or is it OS/390 & z/OS)

where the "switch" from SOM to "Java Interoperability" was introduced.

Enterprise COBOL is now on its second release (still "version 3") - but
either of them include this functionality.  The XML support was enhanced
between releases of Enterprise COBOL.
