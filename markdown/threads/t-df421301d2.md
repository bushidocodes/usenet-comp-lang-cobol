I can't tell you the syntax on your system;  we use copybooks for the WS &
PD that do the same thing.  In general though, those procedures are used
to either find the number of days between two dates or determine what date
occurs a certain number of days before or after a given date.  For
example, to find a date 45 days from today, convert today's date to an
integer, add 45 to it, and convert it back to a date.  To determine the
number of days between two dates, convert both of them to their respective
integer values and subtract.  HTH
