Robert:

Nomex certainly affords standard protection against flames, but for
superior protection, you should consider PBI as the preferred material
for your coveralls.

If you are also concerned about flame resistance as well as puncture
resistance, then Kevlar is what you are seeking as your material of
choice.

And if your priorities do not include puncture resistance, but you
want the highest flame resistance possible, just purchase a proximity
suit.

The only problem is that in your efforts to protect yourself from the
flames, you will succumb to heat stroke by wearing such attire in the
average, late June Las Vegas temperature.

Drink lots of fluids.
