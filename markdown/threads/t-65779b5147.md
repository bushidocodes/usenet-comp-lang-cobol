```
Hi Suman,

You are getting the overlap error because you are painting the X2 of 2nd column over
the X2 of 1st Column !

The CINCRE value 1 will make TELON to start the 2nd Column
1character right to the 1st Column.

To make my point clear let me give one example,

Suppose if you want a segloop like

Column 1 Column 2 Column 3

xxx yyy zzz xxx yyy zzz xxx yyy zzz
xxx yyy zzz xxx yyy zzz xxx yyy zzz
... ... ... ... ... ... ... ... ...

First you should paint only the first line of column 1

Then give the INCRE parameter as

INCRE: 1,1,1

i.e., 1 line increment (rowwise) for all three columns

Then give the CINCRE parameter as

CINCRE: 13, 13

This results in three columns. The first is painted by you.
The second occurs 13 characters to the right of the beginning
of the first column. The third begins 13 characters to the
right of the beginning of the second.


Hope this helps,
Ganesh Kudva.
```
