We are using enscript and Ghostscript to convert a textfile to a PDF:

enscript -p - <xxx.txt |
gs -q -dNOPAUSE -dBATCH -sDEVICE=pdfwrite -sOutputFile=xxx.pdf -f -

You need Ghostscript version 5.x or newer (some AIX versions have
Ghostscript 3.xx, we have build 8.51 on our systems from source)

Lothar
