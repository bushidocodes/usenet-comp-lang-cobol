Hi KK,

IMO, I think there will be a large demand for COBOL programmers
well after the year 2000. I figure that given the rush to get the task
completed it's not likely that all of the code will be tested as well as
it should be resulting in a second panic to fix all of the errors made
by the teams who made these changes.

I imagine the best results will be by teams that know the systems
and can anticipate the impact the changes will have and will test to
make sure every thing will work, assuming their given the time. But just
imagine what will occur when a group is brought in that really does not
know the system?? I hope the have good lawyers and great insurance.

Jeff
