```
I posted an article about our problems few days ago.  I just would
like to mention that the problem is now fixed thru the help of Andy
from Flexus International (makers of SP2).  Tuxedo is not even there
product yet they took time to give us a hand.  There are companies out
there that won't even talk to you if it's not there product.  Thank
you very much Andy and Bob Wolfe for the help.

The problem was the way we coded the C DLL tuxedo routine.  Tuxedo,
Fujitsu Cobol and SP2 are now working together to communicate with the
Unisys Clearpath mainframe (Unisys 2200).  Our next move is to use the
SP2 thin client and last web technology.

Are there other people out there doing the same thing or similar?  If
so, please email me or post to this group.  It would be nice if we
could exchange ideas.

Tony M. Mina
jets@nbnet.nb.ca
```
