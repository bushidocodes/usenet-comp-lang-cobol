Hi Johan,

I am not quite sure what you are asking, but as far as I know,
there are two types of Data General COBOL: their native COBOL
compiler and "ICOBOL", their p-code version. If you are
talking about converting ICOBOL, you will find it very similar
to Acucobol already, so you probably won't need a special tool.
If you are talking about their native version I believe there
are several vendors that may have tools/services that you can
purchase.
