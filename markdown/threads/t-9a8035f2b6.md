Attention: IBM Mainframe and CICS jockeys - please help! I need your
help and guidance into the world of GUI's, client/server, and RDBMS
technology.

To those of you who read this - thank-you for your time! I know how
hard it is to help others at a time when your own schedule is bursting
at the seams. For your time, I can only offer heartfelt gratitude and
the possiblity that your name and organization will be used to give
credit where credit is due - in whatever form that opportunity
takes (city council mtgs, newspaper coverage, technical articles).

Here's the deal... I work for a southeast Virginia municipality. I have
been asked to identify other sites with like characteristics and
find out what they are doing to enhance the productivity of their
application developers. We are in a planning mode and need your valuable
input badly.

------------------------ Summary of our situation ---------------------
General Environment:
* IBM mainframe; applications run in CICS and use VSAM exclusively
* All applications are written in-house using COBOL
* Most of the 30+ COBOL programmers are not PC savvy
* Users want system that allows them to create analytical rpts
without programming
* 50% of user population has dumb terminals, not PC's

Application Development Environment:
* TSO-based ISPF editor
* CICS's "CEDF" transaction for debugging CICS calls
* CICS-based screen painter (no report or code generators)
* TSO-based VSAM file browser

Here's what we'd like to know:
* What are you doing in place of VSAM? Are you using DB2, Oracle?
* Has the switch to SQL been hard for your
development staff?
* Did you hire DBA's or home grow them? Why?
* How are you integrating the mainframe environment with LANs?
* Host as file server?
* Have you replaced character-based apps with GUI's?
* What tools/platforms are you using?
* How do you handle the mixture of PC's and dumb terminals?

It has been suggested that our direction in applications development has
decreased the productivity of our programming staff. As such:
* What products are you using to enhance programmer productivity?
* What products are you using to empower the end-user to do
their own reporting and analysis so users are less of a burden
to the programmers?

We are open to a RDBMS and integration of the PC into apps and app development.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------

Thanks for your advice,

Systems Analyst
City of Newport News
