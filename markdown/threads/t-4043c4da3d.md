Well, I *almost* agree. Had you asked a SPECIFIC question, you might have
gotten a SPECIFIC answer.

But getting a reply to this  vague and general inquiry ("any
information..." ) will generally happen only if your 'subscription' is fully
paid up, and paid in the only currency accepted: your own contributions.

Most people with paid subscriptions are professionals who DONATE their time
and expertise. They (we?) will help you,  but most of us have a congenital
aversion to 'doing the work for you.'  Basic research is your job.

In fairness, there is some enlightened self-interest at work here: by
facilitating your 'instant expertise'  with SAS, we 'cheapen'  the
consulting profession as a whole and thus our own market value.

Perhaps your choice of words was ill-considered; but it sure comes across as
" I have an ISP and a newsgroup reader; therefore I am entitled to the
collective wisdom of others." It don't work that way.
