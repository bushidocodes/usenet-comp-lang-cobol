Hi,
Can you give me a bit more info please? What do you mean by lang and
short fills? What do you classify as a print table?

If I understand your first question correctly, I'd use a two
dimensional array with two (2) indexes on each level viz:

01 ARRAY.
03 LEVEL-1 OCCURS n TIMES INDEXED BY IDX
IDX-MAX.
05 LEVEL-2 OCCURS m TIMES INDEXED BY JDX
JDX-MAX.
05 OCCURS-DATA PIC picture.

Then to load it, although you could use a PERFORM with an AFTER
clause, I'd use:

SET IDX-MAX to n
SET JDX-MAX to m

PERFORM VARYING IDX FROM 1 BY 1
UNTIL IDX > IDX-MAX
OR
PERFORM VARYING JDX FROM 1 BY 1
UNTIL JDX > JDX-MAX
OR

MOVE something TO OCCURS-DATA(IDX, JDX)
END-PERFORM
END-PERFORM

Is this what you had in mind? Let me know if it isn't and I'll try
to give a better answer.

Regards,
Steven
