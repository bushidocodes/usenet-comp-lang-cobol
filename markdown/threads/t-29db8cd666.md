InterTest user and my employer made the switch to Expediter maybe 18
months ago. I think Xpediter's superior in every way. The panels are
ISPF-like and are quite consistent in all environments (TSO & batch &
CICS, at least). InterTest was built around a command line structure and
was a good first effort. Xpeditor's a more modern design.
