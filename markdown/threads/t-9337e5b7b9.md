How about something like this??

.
.
.
OPEN INPUT INPUTFILE.
MOVE ZEROS TO WS-INDEX.
MOVE HIGH-VALUES TO WS-TABLE.
READ-PUT.
ADD +1 TO WS-INDEX.
IF (WS-INDEX IS GREATER THAN +11) THEN GO TO EOF-INPUT.
SET ABC-IDX TO WS-INDEX.
READ INPUTFILE AT END GO TO EOF-INPUT.
MOVE INPUT-RECORD TO WS-TABLE-ENTRY (ABC-IDX).
GO TO READ-PUT.
EOF-INPUT.
CLOSE INPUTFILE.
.
.
.
