Actually, the IBM manual for DFSORT specifies exits E11, E15, E16, E17,
E18, E19, E31, E32, E35, E37, E38, E39 AND E61. The Syncsort/OS manual
adds E14, E21, E25 and E27, while deleting E19. I don't have any older
sort manuals here in the house, but I question whether DFSORT would drop
support for exits from an earlier IBM version, as this would make it
difficult to market the later product. The naming conventions for the sort
exits also place E3x exits in the output phase of the sort, not input.
Input phase exits are numbered E1x. So, just what does an E30 do, anyway?
