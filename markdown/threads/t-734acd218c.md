A move of alphanumeric to numeric-edited is permissible.

The move would be made, AS IF the alphanumeric field
were described as numeric; that is, AS IF the X(6) were
described as 9(6).

The error suggests that SAL-SALARY has characters
other than numbers. Since this is a student problem,
the field most likely has leading spaces.
