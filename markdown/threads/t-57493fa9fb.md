```
I have seen on previous posts that quite a lot of people are interested in
code restructuring and in particular in GOTO removal.

If you want to see how an automated service can restructure your code it is
easy and free. All you have to do is to connect to RainCode Online
(http://www.raincode.com/online).
Once you've logged on, select "COBOL restructuring", then "My submissions",
then "New submission", and "Add file": you can then upload several of your
COBOL programs you want to restructure.



Once your programs are uploaded, click on "add job", and select the
transformations you'd like to see applied on your sources. You can also keep
the default settings.

Click on "commit" to start the renovation.



After a week or so, you will receive an email telling you your job is
finished and your results can be seen online without paying anything.



To give you an idea of what RainCode Online is able to accomplish, we
recorded the following statistics on the first ten applications we
restructured:

   - On average, 85% of the GOTOs were removed.
   - In each program, we managed to re-create between 30 and 85 loops from
GOTO-based equivalent constructs.
   - We typically reduced the size of a program to 60-90% of its original
size, in terms of number of statements.


http://www.raincode.com/online
```
