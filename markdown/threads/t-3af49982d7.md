We are interested in experiencial reports regarding migrating COBOL to
newer languages (e.g. OO COBOL 95, SmallTalk), newer databases (e.g.
distributed object databases), newer interfaces (Windows, Motif), and
newer architectures (client-server). We at Xinotech have developed a
metalanguage-based tool to support automatic program transformation,
and we are interested in hearing what are some of the problems people
have encountered or are currently encountering when trying to modernize
