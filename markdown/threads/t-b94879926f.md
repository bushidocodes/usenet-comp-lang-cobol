I wish I could be more help, I discovered the reason during my reducing
the program to its simplest form while preparing to send it to MF for a
bug fix.  The abend was fairly consistant and while making it smaller
and simpler, the problem went away.  Finally I found one change that
turned on/off the abend/problem and discovered the (stupid) error.
Needles to say, I reported back to MF that it was a user error (Pride
goeth before the Fall).

After words, I experimented with the subscript ranging it from one over
table to huge over table only to discover the three general types of
handling, MF error, nothing and sporatic random strangeness, I think I
recall the break at 22 and 150 but your results may vary (grin).

If your problem is with the table handling, you might try defining the
array VERY long and address down to the 101st row with a VERY large
elementry definition.  Preload the VERY large area (the size of the
array minus the first 100 rows) with nulls or spaces or something
unique and then occasionally compare that addressed VERY large area
with its preloaded value.  A not equal will be proof, while an equal
will not be proof but it might be information.

Good luck,
Bill

Don't forget, GOD has a sense of humor, even if we don't always
understand it.....
