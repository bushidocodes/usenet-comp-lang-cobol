One of the things you might look at is the ISPF Workstation Agent.

It can be configured to make "edit" commands route to a workstation and 
fire up the editor of your choice -- when you save the file it is 
updated back on the mainframe.

It was a really nice integration idea from OS/2 that IBM did absolutely 
nothing with -- but it still exists and is available for Windows and 
Unix.
