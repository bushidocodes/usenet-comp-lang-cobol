I use Xpediter at work and have found that there's a pretty fair number of
help screens behind it (F1 or HELP at the command line).  F1 will list all
of your available commands and HELP <command> gives you the syntax and
example of any particular command.  You can usually get by with about a half
dozen of the commands.  You rarely would need all that are available to you.
I most often use INTercept, SHow INTercepts, breakpoint Before, breakpoint
After, Keep, and Peek.  F6 (resume where you left off), F9 (single step),
F12 (run) and F4 (exit).
