Edgar Green wrote:


› C10-CALCULATE-CELSIUS.
› COMPUTE CELSIUS-LOW-WK = 5 / 9 * (LOW-TEMP-IN - 32).
› COMPUTE CELSIUS-HIGH-WK = 5 / 9 * (HIGH-TEMP-IN - 32).

This compute is dividing 5 by 9 * (temp - 32) giving a very small
fraction that prints as zero. It may work better if you do the
following:
COMPUTE celsius = ((temp-in - 32) * 5) / 9.

Note I always use parens to define the exact order of things for the
compute since I prefer not to remember the hierarchy of what happens
first when they are not used.
