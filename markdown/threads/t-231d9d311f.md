Working long hours has never bothered me.

Posting here does, sometimes.

It is entirely my own fault. It is easy to forget it is just Usenet and 
start taking it seriously, especially when tired or stressed. The 
perspective gets lost  and things which are really not important become so.

You can think too much.

"Thus is the native hue of Resolution sicklied o'er with the pale cast of 
thought. And enterprises of great pitch and moment, with this regard their 
currents turn awry, and lose the name of action."

I guess we get so used to taking work seriously it is too easy to take 
ourselves too seriously.

Nevertheless, I do enjoy the exchanges here and it is also good to see 
people getting helped sometimes.

I also find it is helpful to step out of a real problem occasionally and 
post here instead. I usually get back to work feeling better.
