Price is an issue when competing products have the same functionality.

The reason these products are purchased is that they allegedly increase
productivity. The productivity savings justify the purchase.

If I can get 1 product which supports Batch, and CICS, the learning curve is
reduced by eliminating the need for someone to learn 2 products which may
have different interfaces. This saves money. If 2 products together are
cheaper than a single product, the savings may be offset by the time it
takes someone to become proficient in both products.
