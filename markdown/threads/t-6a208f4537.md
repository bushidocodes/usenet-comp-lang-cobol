We had a Tandem system which some people looked at
porting to Unix, but due to cutbacks, it was decided that the
project was too large.  Lockheed Research in Palo Alto
has been working on various translators such as Jovial to ADA
and ADIOS to IMS/DC (don't ask).
 
They also looked at having a translator for Tandem to Unix.
You can contact them at Lockeed Software Technology Company in
Palo Alto, California.  Although the project was postponed
in house, they would probably be happy to do a port for you.
The input would be Tandem Scobol and the Output would be
Micro Focus Accept/Display.
 
For a few dollars more, they may port it to C.
