PHB (to Bob): "Are you a COBOL programmer?"

Asok: "So, you recommend waiting for a meteor to kill us all."
Bob: "The glaciers are way too slow."

Bob: "Most problems go away if you wait long enough, Asok."

Bob: "It might look like I'm standing motionless, but I'm actively waiting
for our problems to go away."
