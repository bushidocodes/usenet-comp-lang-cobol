### Old-and-New - some coding examples

- **From:** "William M. Klein" <wmklein@ix.netcom.com>
- **Date:** 2003-06-03T22:45:28-05:00
- **Newsgroups:** comp.lang.cobol
- **Message-ID:** `<bbjq0r$86a$1@slb3.atl.mindspring.net>`

```
I thought that I would start a new SERIES of threads showing "minimal" code
examples demonstrating some of the (relatively) small enhancements/features
in the 2002 COBOL Standard.  In these notes, I will show an example of how
something could be coded in an '85/'89/'91 Standard compiler (with all its
"required stuff") and how the same thing could be coded in the new Standard.
(I may also show some "old extension" stuff, such as return-code
processing).

I do *not* claim that my "old" style coding is the best (much less ONLY) way
that this stuff could be coded.  Feel free to post "alternatives" for the
old style as well as comments on the new.

It is my intention to start out with some of the things that address
"functionality" that I recall being raised within CLC in the past.  If
anyone has any specific requests for "demos" - let me know.

P.S.  As I do *not* have a 2002 conforming compiler, I can't guarantee that
I am not making syntax errors in the "new" examples - but I will compile the
"old" ones before posting.  Possibly one of the Micro Focus "ISO2000"
directive users or the Fujitsu/Siemens people will tell me of any errors
that they see/find.
```
