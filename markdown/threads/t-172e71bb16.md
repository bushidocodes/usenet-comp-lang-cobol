As I understand J4/00-0645, the
equivalence of "SS" and "ß" (German
"sharp" s) is now dead.

Very good. Otherwise, the following
would have been equivalent:

       move 17 to Gußstahl
       move 17 to GUSSSTAHL
       move 17 to Gusßtahl

So Gusßtahl == Gußstahl!

Now, as we have a general revision
of orthography in Germany anyway,
we would have taken this!

(BTW "Gußstahl" == "casting steel")

Herwig
