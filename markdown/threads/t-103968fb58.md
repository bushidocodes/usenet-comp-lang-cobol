What you are seeing is the sign character (embedded sign). Nearly
every implementation differs in how they do this. What you are seeing
is probably IBM. The sign is included as a hollerith card overpunch
(like the 11 row signals minus so the letters J through R indicate -1
through -9 and -0 can be lots of things). When I had to implement
something to go along with this method I had to provide for six or
more (I can't remember - it was 20 years ago) different
representations of zero (for plus, minus, and no sign). What you
should do is read the manual for the implementation that created the
number.
