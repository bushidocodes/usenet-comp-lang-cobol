>     FD  ACCOUNT-FILE.

[...]
>     01  ACCOUNT-REC.

You've got two ACCOUNT-RECs ...


[...]
>     READ ACCOUNT-FILE INTO REPORT-REC

... and you aren't using either of them, so your data is uninitialized.

One way to fix this is to delete the 01-level after the FD and replace it
with the entire ACCOUNT-REC from WORKING-STORAGE.  Then READ ACCOUNT-FILE
without the INTO phrase.
