```
No computer shop exists for its own sake;  the "Computer Services" section
exists to DO SOMETHING for the rest of the business.  It is very much in
your interest to know what that SOMETHING is.  If you already know (or at
least have minored in) accounting, bookkeeping, or business you will be
ahead of anybody who will have to be told what a journal or a ledger is
before starting.

Bringing in some samples of your work showing your grasp of COBOL and the
structure of your code (identifiable, maintainable by anyone) would also
help.
```
