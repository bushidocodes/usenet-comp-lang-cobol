done at funerals.   I don't think the Patriot Guard Riders have broadened 
their mission to include this latest development yet, but as both the WBC 
and the PGR are showing up at a soldier's funeral in Nashville this morning, 
some of the PGR may be able to be there in Murfreesboro as well. I'll be
