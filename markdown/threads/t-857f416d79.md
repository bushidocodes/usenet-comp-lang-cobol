I still have 7 or 8 more "ISO COBOL" requirements to created, but the
following requirements (COBOL and LE - so far none for PL/I) are available
in the "Open for discussion" page at:

    http://www.share.org/REQS/reqmain.nsf/Req+by+Status?OpenView&CollapseAll

I have updated a couple of the older requirements and should finish this
initial stage in the next couple of days. So far, there have been LIMITED
user discussions on what is out there. I assume (always dangerous) that this
means that what is there is clear enough - and we are (almost) ready to
vote. I hope to have ALL of the requirements available for voting before Nov
1. Then we can finish voting by the middle of December and "submit" the
priorities to IBM (hopefully for at least initial responses in Long Beach).

Requirement numbers and subjects:
    REQS_APLS_LNGC
        SSLNGC03001 Provide COBOL compiler options in CEEnDUMP
        SSLNGC03002 Allow underscore in COBOL User-Defined Words
        SSLNGC03003 Compiler option to make BLOCK CONTAINS clause SMS
compliant
        SSLNGC03004 COBOL compiler option to generate CEEUOPT run-time
options
        SSLNGC0313578 Implement the new 2002 COBOL standard in a phased
manner
        SSLNGC0313579 IBM support effort to provide Native COBOL XML support
        SSLNGC0313580 Port CEE5DLY functionality to LE for z/OS and for z/VM
        SSLNGC0313582 Assembler DSECTS to COBOL utility
        SSLNGC0313583 Increase Individual Item and Table Compiler Limits
        SSLNGC0313585 2002 ISO COBOL - EXIT and PERFORM Enhancements
        SSLNGC0313586 Enhance CEE3PRM Callable Service
        SSLNGC0313587 New LE Callable Service to get (various) Program Names
        SSLNGC0313588 2002 ISO COBOL - 1-byte and Other "True" Binary
Enhancements
        SSLNGC0313589 2002 ISO COBOL - CALL prototypes, Compile-time Arg
Checking
        SSLNGC0313590 2002 ISO COBOL - COPY & REPLACE Partial Word

        SSLNGC0313591 2002 ISO COBOL - Compiler Directives
