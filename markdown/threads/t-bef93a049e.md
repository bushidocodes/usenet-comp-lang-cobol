The cowboy & his dog chilli

The cowboy and his Chihuahua --- are camping in the desert. he sets
up their tent and both are soon asleep. Some hours later, the
cowboy wakes his faithful friend.

Chilli, lookUp the sky and tell me what do you see? the coboy
asked. "I see millions of stars." chilli replies.
What does that tell you chilli? the cowboy asked.
Chilli ponders for a minute, then said

"astronomically speaking, it tells me there are millions of
galaxies and potentially billions of planets. Astrologically, it
tells me that Saturn is in Leo. Chronologically, it appears to be
approximately quarter past three. Theologically, it's evident the
lord is all-powerful, and we are small and insignificant.
Meteorologically, it seems we will have a beautiful day tomorrow.".

The cowboy is silent for a moment, then says, "Chilli, you stupid.
someone stole our tent.".
