I have a similar problem communicating with my brother-in-law who only
speaks Italian.  What I do is type my email in as plain and simple
English as I can.  I then copy the message into the clipboard (Ctrl-C)
then go to:  http://babelfish.altavista.com/cgi-bin/translate?

Paste the English into their dialogue box and have it translated.  I
then copy and paste the translation into the email and send it off.  I
do the same with my brother-in-laws responses.  It isn't 100%
accurate, but it is close enough.
