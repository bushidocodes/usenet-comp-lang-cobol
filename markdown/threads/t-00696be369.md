1). only Cobol II and up programs can do dynamic CALLs in CICS. Check
this first, we had someone CALLing the Y2K windowing program from an
OS/VS Cobol program, trashed the (test) region 2-3 times in one morning
(until I deleted his program & disabled the PPT);

2). you must add a PPT entry for every program which will be called (if
you forget this, the region doesn't crash, you get a "1029" transaction
abend (LOAD failed));

3). how does your CICS region crash - abend code? message? (ignore the
DFH1800 - that's a general purpose msg, look for the details preceding
it).

Disclaimer - I know nothing about Telon Cobol II - never heard of it
before your post. If that's a factor, you're on your own.

Bill Lynch

PS: 1. This is solvable.
2. We've been doing lots of dynamic calls since 1991-92 - no
problems.
