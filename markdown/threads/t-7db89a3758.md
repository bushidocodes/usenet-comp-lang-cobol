It is possible to use a mouse in MicroFocus Cobol/2 programs.
Cobol/2 V 2.5, and above, have support; V 1.1 does not. I am
not sure when support, for the mouse, was added.

Whether it is available in the MicroFocus Cobol you are using
is a question I cannot answer. If you cannot find any references
in your manuals, support for the mouse is probably not available.

Please, when posting a question, supply the product and version
information so we may more accurately answer your question.
