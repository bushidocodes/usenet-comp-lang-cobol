The path is called PATH= 

On a command line, type 'path' to see your path. It is stored in
\winnt\system32\autoexec.nt. Next run regedit and look at
/HKEY_USERS/(youruser)/Environment/path.

Alternatively, find the shortcut to Fujitsu on your desktop or in
\winnt\profiles\(youruser)\all users\start menu\programs. Right click on the
shortcut and select properties/program/advanced/PIF settings.
