If you are interested in working for yourself as a trainer (or if you already
are), we would be interested in talking to you about teaching for us on a
contract basis.

Run your own business, develop your own clientele as well as teach at our
clients. We can supply course materials in many areas, but we are also
interested in looking at new materials to add to our library.

We have been training in mainframe areas for over 22 years, primarily training
applications programmers in these areas:

TSO
ISPF
CLIST
REXX
JCL
Assembler
COBOL (including most recent products)
PL/I
C
VSAM
DB2
CICS
Language Environment (LE)

We are also expanding our curriculum to include workstation and client/server
technologies (where we are looking for materials most); we would like to
include offerings in

C++
Visual Basic (we have some course materials avaialable)
PowerBuilder (we have some materials in development)
Object technologies
Java
Internet / Intranet
Oracle (we have access to some materials here)

In any event, we are looking for people with technical depth and terrific
presentation skills. Manage your time, run your own business.

Our standard rates are $1,000 per day, plus expenses (sometimes a little more,
sometimes a little less, but usually that number). We can also work royalty
plans if you have materials we can use.

The down side is, the work is erratic (no promises how many days of work we can
arrange for you a year). Our backlog looks promising right now, but in this
business things can change very rapidly.

If you are interested in exploring the possibilities further, let me know.
