Hi Fulvio,
Are the NetCobol programs developed with OO? I mean: are they
"classes" or "programs". If they are "programs", you will have to
terminate one called program before calling it again. This is
sometimes misleading, because in PowerCobol you can INVOKE a DLL
developed with COBOL85 syntax, but what you'r really doing is calling
a contained program in the target DLL. As far as I know, your approach
might be colliding with the no reentrancy directive of the 85
Standard.
