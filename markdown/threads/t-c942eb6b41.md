While white roofs (and roads etc), or any highly reflective finish,
certainly would reduce the heat build up during the hours of sunlight
and thus keep the maximum temperature down, they would also reduce the
emission of heat which occurs 24 hours. This would help prevent the
cooling overnight which is a good thing for housing.

To counter global warming it is not enough to just reflect the heat
elsewhere during the day and hold onto the heat at night (which
satisfies urban planning).
