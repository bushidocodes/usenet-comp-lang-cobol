```
Hello everyone,

I am new to this thread, although I would imagine you will see me here
more often.

As I read through the thread I saw a few questions on basic COBOL
syntax. Some people specified wanting to learn Micro Focus, and some
even mentioned programming COBOL on PC's. I have seen alot of this out
there, so I tuned to the nearest search engine and found very little in
the way of COBOL support.

I have been considering this certain idea for a long time, and I am
wondering what my peers would think. I am thinking about making a web
site which will consist of MF-COBOL and MVS/JCL syntax. A sort of
reference, not meant to teach. I would also like to make part of the
site an area for downloading (or just viewing) COBOL source. Eventually
I would open an area for upload of COBOL source. It would hopefully, in
the long run, become a complete dictionary of COBOL.

Thoughts? Idea? Applause?

--Kevin R. Hoyt
```
