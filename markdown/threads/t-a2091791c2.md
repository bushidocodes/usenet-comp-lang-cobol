VALUE IS EXTERNAL (and VALUE IS REFERENCE) are
not standard COBOL, they, apparently, are DEC COBOL
extensions. Therefore the "COBOL standards" say nothing
about what is permissible with these extensions.

Having said that, I do not know enough about DEC COBOL
to answer the following question regarding 'quadwords'.
