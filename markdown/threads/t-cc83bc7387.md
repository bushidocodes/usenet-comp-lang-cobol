If the same key ("dongle") works in both places, then it sounds like you
"copied" the software from one computer to another.  As I recall the
European contracts ("dongles" were never as common-place in the US), this
was a 'contractual no-no".  Therefore, you may have some trouble getting
help with this.  You may want to check out your licensing agreement before
pursuing this farther (or contact MF - if the license says that you can do
this - i.e. multiple machines but not simultaneous use).
