Wondering about object-oriented COBOL?

So was I. I spent the last week or so poring over IBM manuals and
trying things. Then I wrote some Web pages to summarize what I had
learned:

http://home.swbell.net/mck9/cobol/ooc/ooc.html

Don't regard these pages as authoritative, for I am no expert. In
fact I am eager to have my blunders pointed out. But if you'd like
a quick overview, take a look. In particular, I have made a fairly
detailed comparison of OO COBOL to C++.

[I know a lot of you are skeptical about OO. I'm not trying to
promote OO, just trying to provide some information. So far I
haven't found much on the Web about OO COBOL, so perhaps my
scribblings will help fill the void.]
