I know a little bit about Palm development, and even less about COBOL
compilers, so take this with a 1/2 of salt...

PalmPilot software development kits exist for many operating systems that
allow you to access conduit functions (an ultimately the Palm's data.)
Given that you can call DLL exports from your COBOL environment, it seems
certain that it's possible to write a PalmPilot-aware COBOL program.
However, if it's practical is a question for those suffering the task.

If you're looking to do this, O'Reilly makes an excellent book on the topic:
http://www.oreilly.com/catalog/palmprog/

Other links of interest:
http://astro.uchicago.edu/home/web/valdes/pilot/resources.html
