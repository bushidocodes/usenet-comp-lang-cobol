Anything is permissible depending on standards in use at your location.
Some of those that I have run into include the following:

.COB (Program Source)
.CBL (Program Source)
.PCO (Recompilation Required Source Code)
.FD   (File Descriptions)
.SEL (Select Statements)
.WS  (Working Storage Copy books)
.CPY (Copybooks)
