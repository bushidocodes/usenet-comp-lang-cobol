```
S9:                  Signed numeric, 1 digit (+1 = C1)
S9(9)V9(2):    Signed numeric, 11 digits, 2 decimal places (-123456789.12 =
F1F2F3F4F5F6F7F8F9F1D2)
S9(5)V9(6):    Signed numeric, 11 digits, 6 decimal places (+12345.123456 =
F1F2F3F4F5F1F2F3F4F5C6)
The decimal places are implied, not explicit in the above.  The COBOL
manuals at www.mvshelp.com can give you more information about data types.
If the above were packed decimal (COMP-3) fields, the storage
representations would be very different than the above.
```
