Yes, These are predefined types in "javatypes.cpy".  eg:
  01 jlong                            is typedef pic s9(18) comp-5.
  01 jint                             is typedef pic s9(9) comp-5.

"Typedef's" are available in Merant/Micro Focus COBOL and in the next
COBOL standard too.
