Howdy, all ...

I just noticed a query and reply about contracting rates in Arizona.
It occurred to me that rates are indeed beginning to rise due to
increased demand, but not at the same rate everywhere.

It might be useful for all of us to post our regional rates
periodically, so that we don't have to rely on employment
agencies to tell us what they hope they can get us for ...

I'm in central NJ - senior rates here (COBOL/IMS) are running
in the mid $40's/hour, on a W2 contracting basis.
