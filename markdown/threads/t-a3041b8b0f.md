Yes we did.  We went from Micro Focus Workbench to Fujitsu Cobol
Enterprise with Cobol SP2 for our GUI interface.  The move was easy
because we are not using OO Cobol.  We are using Cobol SP2 for our GUI
interface.  Should we have used OO Cobol, the move would have been
hard.  Yes, we are satisfied with Fujitsu.  The reasons of our move
were price and support.  It is very hard to get support from Micro
Focus (we are under maintenance agreement) .  Even their sales
department they won't return your call.

Tony M. Mina
