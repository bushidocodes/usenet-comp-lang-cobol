```
Good Afternoon,

I believe the following is the final response regarding runtime fees.  The
runtime fees will be waived if the following conditions are met:
 
     1.  Have five or less Net Express development licences.
     2.  Sell an application that costs less than $1000 per authorized user.
     3.  Have total company revenues of less than $1,000,000 for their prior
          fiscal year.
     4.  Have the principal of their company apply to MERANT on company
          notepaper confirming that they meet all three of the above conditions
     5.  Have a current maintenance contract for all Net Express development
          licences.

Thanks,
Bob Hennessey
```
