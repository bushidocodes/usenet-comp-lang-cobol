```
I your shop uses an IBM z series computer and it is looking to upgrade
to version 5.1 and it is using Cross System Product (CSP) or its
Visual Gen successor, migration may be a problem. CSP and possibly
its successor forced an F zone on all signed fields with positive
values leaving the D zone for negative fields. The elimination of
NUMPROC(MIG) means this behavior if still existing can cause problems.

Other program generators may also have this CSP peculiarity.

Clark Morris
```
