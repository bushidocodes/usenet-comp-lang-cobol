› in the steplib (SYSTEMS.OLD.RUNTIME.LOADLIB) of the production jobs.

There are multiple issues to address:
1. The COBOL LE invoked sort was ending with a U4039. I am not sure why,
we need to do dig deeper to find the cause.

2. The U4087/0C4 is a problem in SyncSort that occurs during the clean up
from a prior ABEND. This is fixed by maintenance included in TPF5.
Application of TPF 5 will correct the U4087/0C4 but not the U4039.

3. BSSIC36 and BSSIE36 are SMP functions for SyncSort that are related to
stand alone COBOL exits, *not* COBOL invoked sorts. Neither of these
functions should have any bearing on the problem since your case is an
invoked sort.

Please contact me if you would like further assistance in debugging this
problem.
