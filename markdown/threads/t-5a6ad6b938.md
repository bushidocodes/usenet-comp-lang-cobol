The IBM and Mainframes Jobs and Resumes Board is now not only Fixed but also
has some new features such as Jobs, Resumes, Consultant, Counsulting Searchs:

1. When you get to the board there is an option to be notified via EMAIL of
any  new posts. You may say well "SO WHAT !". Well here are some reasons why
you  might want to do this;

   (a) You are looking for Mainframe Talent ("Employees or Consultants") for
       yourself or a client ("Agent").

       When someone posts a resume or is looking for a Mainframe Postion you
       will receive an EMAIL notice at the same time that the message is
       posted on the board.

   (b) You have posted your resume or are looking for Mainframe work.

       As soon as an employer or agent posts a job offer you will receive
       an EMAIL at that same time.

  (c) If you have already posted a message on the board and someone replies 
on the board to your message you will receive an EMAIL at that same time.

2. Employers, Agents and sometime Mainframe Job seekers are normally charged
   some type of FEE. This creates overhead for both sides.

   If you are the Mainframe Job seeker, you may have been able to get a better
   salary or hourly rate if the employer or agent could have cut their cost to
   locate you.

   Since this board is FREE, that can help this problem.

3. The Mainframe Job Market is and has been growing rapidly, one estimate is
   that 2 out of every 5 Mainframe positions are unfilled.

   If you are looking for a new  job or contract would it not be nice to have
   several options on where or who you could do work for ?

4. Finally with Y2K as well as many NEW systems being used for 
Internet/Intranet purposes, and it is after all the beginning of a new year, 
meaning that many corporations are getting new Budget money to hire both 
employees as well as consultants, it can't hurt.

We do NOT make ANY money providing the board as a service to the IBM and
Mainframe community, in the future the we may use banner ads but at this time
we have not done so.

This is your Board, we will make changes as we get suggestions from you as how
we can make it better.

Intenet IBM and Mainframe Programming Job and Resume Board ("NEW")

http://www.trav-tech.com/ibm

 Again Thank you, and Happy holidays, wishing you all the best for the New
Year

Don Kennedy
