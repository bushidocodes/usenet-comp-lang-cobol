Thanks to everbody who responded. The results of the 1st Unofficial
International Cobol Salary Survey (UICSS) have been tabulated, and
here are the results. (Disclaimer: there has been no massaging of
salaries for cost of living indexes, results were tabulated using hand
held Sharp EL-531G scientific calculator, Papermate Flexgrip Medium
pen, and Xerox 4200 DP 20 lb. paper - on loan from my office)

International Average 1996 Salary: $62,765.90

United States Average 1996 Salary: $58,322.71

European Average 1996 Salary: $74,799.64



The US was divided into 4 quadrants; NE, SE, SW, and NW.

North East Average 1996 Salary: $71,665.76

South East Average 1996 Salary: $63,883.28

South West Average 1996 Salary: $41,457.55

North West Average 1996 Salary: NA (lack of response/lack of
mainframes?)


Thanks again, I guess Corey's lobbying efforts in DC are paying off.

Regards,

Steve "the recruiter" Wray
