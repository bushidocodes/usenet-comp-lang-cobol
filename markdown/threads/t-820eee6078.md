Bob:

Mpeg video files are much more highly compressed than AVI files.  I
guess the best analogy would be a bitmap image stored in a JPG format
versus one stored in a BMP format.

I am not aware of an MPEG to AVI conversion program, but I believe
that JASC should have a product that will do exactly what you need.

I know that they have a product called Illuminatus which can import
and export certain file types (AVI and MPG).

JASC offers many different graphics and multimedia tools for a very
reasonable price.

Check out www.jasc.com

Good luck!
