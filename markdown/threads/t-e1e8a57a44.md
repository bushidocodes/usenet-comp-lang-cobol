```
Focus is a proprietary language supplied by Information Builders Inc of
NYC.

I suspect a call to them would raise at least some manuals which describe
the "language".

One of the opportunities (at least in the past when I was more closely
involved) is that the manuals tend to be "how to" not "what does it do".
Precise determination of what a given FOCUS program would do was sometimes
difficult.

FOCUS shines for one-timers, prototypes, etc. It includes all sorts of
extensions - private databases, accesses to outside databases, screen
painters, etc, etc. Just what can be used to be either very effective or
very stupid. Apps are easy to get up and running, but the same olde stuff
- i.e. who manages the databases etc - will haunt these apps if not tended
to.
```
