The question is not "Should we be able to have fun?", it is "When and
where is it appropriate to do so?"  Asking for a job programming COBOL
business applications is a serious thing.  COBOL programs handle the
most critical functions within businesses.  Businesses often succeed
or fail on the performance of their MIS departments, with millions or
billions of dollars at stake.  If you aren't able to be serious when
asking for a job, when your own interestests are very much at stake,
why would your prospective employer think you would take his business
interests any more seriously, when it is his money at stake? :-)
