```
I know you're all sitting on the edge of your office chairs, waiting to
hear about my evaluation of MFE from Merant.   So, here goes.....

First, I'm not finished with it yet.   I've had it installed for 5 days,
and I'm working my way thru the tutorials (I have to balance this with
my job, you know!)   

Second, I'm both pleased and disappointed with MFE.  I'm pleased because
they have created a total development environment which seems to work
well.  Combine MFE with Revolve (which I think is a great analytical
tool) and I believe you could have one heck of a set of development
tools!   The debugging tool in MFE I find to be very intuitive, and
simpler to use than the Workbench Animator (which I have been using for
over 6 years).

I'm disspointed though because even while working thru the very simple
projects presented in the tutorial, I've encountered MFE abends and page
fault errors.  I suppose my thoughts are what happens to MFE when I try
to use it with some of the more complex programs I've written and plan
to write.

Overall though, I'm still very interested in the capabilities of MFE.  I
still have to get thru the regression and testing tools, but I've been
moderately impressed so far.  If the environment becomes even more
stable with future releases, it will be great.

Dave Bretz
```
