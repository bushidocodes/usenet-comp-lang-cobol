You can get a visual output window back by chosing the entry "Application
Output" of the "View" menu. (It's a long menu, look near the bottom). This
works even when there is no output. When the window is visible, right-clik
on it and choose "float on desktop". When this is disabled you get an
extra option (rightclick again): "Allow docking". When on, you can put the
window fixed in one of the menu bars at the top or bottom of your
netexpress window. Try/play with some of these.
Good luck.
