Buy a copy of SAM'S "Cobol in 21 days". Read thoroughily. Download a
copy of Micro Focus Net Express Personal Edition v5.1 (still available
from their site if you know where to look). Install. Read and
understand the MF examples. Do some coding of your own (a CRUD program
would be a good idea). Read a book like "Systematic Software Testing"
by Craig and Jaskiel. Download the manuals from IBM about their
utilities (IEBCOMPR, IEBGENER, TSO/ISPF, SDSF .... DFSORT). Read and
thoroughily understand. Purchase the Wiley book "z/OS JCL". Read and
fully understand.

In a couple of years' time you might be able to pass the standard
subset of questions that you will be asked at interview.

Good luck.
