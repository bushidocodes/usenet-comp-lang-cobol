```
>Can anyone lend me or tell me where i can get the software Computer Based

I'm not aware of a specific program called CBT.  My understanding is that CBT
is a generic term that stands for any computer based training.  I believe that
some training programs for COBOL are available for purchase, but there's some
very useful stuff available for free.  Try this...

http://www.csis.ul.ie/COBOL/

The lecture notes on that page are Powerpoint slide presentation.  I think they
are a splendid tool for drilling and review.  Another great tool is the Fujitsu
COBOL compiler.  It's free and it comes with extensive documentation.

http://www.adtools.com

Alternatively, you can buy Thane Hubbell's book "Teach Yourself COBOL in 24
Hours"  published by Sams.  It includes the Fujitsu compiler and documentation
with it.

There are a lot of other good ideas in the FAQ maintained by the organizers of
this newsgroup.

RoadRaat
```
