I have recently seen a number of USENET postings and have received some
private email in which the senders/posters have not known the correct
name for various IBM COBOL-related products. The attached information
includes not only the correct name for such products - but also the IBM
announcement numbers, availability dates, and/or drop-dates.

This information was gathered directly from IBM announcements - so it
should be correct. However, as I do not work for IBM, it should be read
at least a little "as is".

Note Well:
All dates and announcement numbers are correct in the United States;
they are probably different in most other countries. Contact your
local IBM team for local information. (Product names and numbers
should be the same everywhere.)

* * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

Announcement - 393-087
Dated - 930504
OS/VS COBOL PRODUCT SUPPORT (LIMITED OFFERING)
Product Number - 5740-CB1, 5740-LM1 (for OS/VS COBOL)

Effective June 30, 1994, IBM will discontinue service through
the IBM Support Center for OS/VS COBOL Compile and Library
(5740-CB1), OS/VS COBOL Library (5740-LM1), and OS/VS COBOL
Interactive Debug (5734-CB4). However, the OS/VS COBOL Product
Support (Limited Offering) will provide continued access to the IBM
Customer Support Center including usage of Electronic Trouble
Reporting (ETR) functions, for a fee. Additionally, Central Service,
via mail, will continue to be available for these programs until
June 30, 1996.
The OS/VS COBOL Product Support (Limited Offering) will
provide support for OS/VS COBOL only when it is used with program
products, program offerings, and operating systems released prior to
June 30, 1994. The OS/VS COBOL Product Support (Limited Offering) is
intended for customers who need continued access to the IBM Support
Center.
...
The Product Support (Limited Offering) is not for usability. Some
customers will not have completed migration from OS/VS COBOL to a
current version and release of either VS COBOL II or COBOL/370 (TM)
by the time of OS/VS COBOL service withdrawal on June 30, 1994. The
Product Support (Limited Offering) for a fee is intended to satisfy
customer needs for defect support, from the Customer Support Center,
as they continue migration activities beyond June 30, 1994 until
June 30, 1996, but not beyond June 30, 1996.

IBM LANGUAGE ENVIRONMENT FOR MVS & VM RELEASE 5
Product Number - 5688-198
Availability - September 21, 1995

PROGRAM CURRENCY: Central Service, including the IBM Support Center,
will be available until discontinued by IBM upon 12 months' written
notice.
Program services previously announced for Language
Environment/370 Release 3 will be available until March 31, 1997.
Program services previously announced for Language Environment
for MVS & VM Release 4 will be available until September 30, 1997.

IBM Language Environment (R) for MVS & VM Release 5 continues
to provide a common set of services in a single run-time environment.
Additional support for emerging application development technologies
such as object-oriented, distributed client/server, and open
standards simultaneously enhances the run-time environment.
IBM Language Environment for MVS & VM Release 5:
o Adds run-time support for IBM COBOL for MVS & VM Release 2. (IBM
COBOL provides object-oriented COBOL language extensions and
client/server enablers.)

Note to Reader - See announcement 296-016 for the fact that Release 5
is the *LAST* release of LE that will be separately orderable for
MVS.
