LINES environment variable (within the RTS at least - and I'm sure the Animator
finds out from the RTS and adjusts itself).

return address from getenv(), so if there is any leading whitespace or
whatever that causes atoi() to return a bad value, we will default to what is
in the terminfo file. 'vi' may just strip whitespace out ... ? That's just a
guess, BTW.
