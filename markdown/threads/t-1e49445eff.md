Carlos,

Have you specified DECIMAL POINT is COMMA under SPECIAL NAMES?

It looks to me like the database knows about the decimal places but the
conversion is failing from COBOL. COBOL figures a comma is just a separator
UNLESS you tell it that this is a decimal.

Make sure your MS local settings on the client and the server reflect the
use of comma as decimal point also.

Pete.
