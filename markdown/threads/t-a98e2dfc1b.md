Try COBOL Unleashed for a more "Advanced" view of Indexed files.  (I
wrote the chapter on Indexed files, directly following Judsons
chapters on file handling).   Look at it in the book store to see if
it has what you require.  However, I think you really want a more
basic understanding - and for that try my book "Sams Teach Yourself
COBOL in 24 Hours".  I have 3 chapters relating to Indexed files.
Check out http://www.barnesandnoble.com and search for my name or the
title of the book and you can see an outline of what the three
chapters cover.  I think this will give you the leg up that you need
to get a good grasp of the subject.  In COBOL Unleashed I assume an
understanding of Indexed files and touch on using them in a multi-user
environment and I also cover VSAM files and their use at a basic
level.
