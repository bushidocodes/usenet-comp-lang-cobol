Microsoft Cobol 2.x always has automatically the .KEY extension on
the key file.

MF Cobol always has automatically the .IDX extension on the key file.
(or is it .idx on Unix?).

Other Cobol may or may not have an 'always automatic extension'
which may or may not be .KEY or .IDX.
