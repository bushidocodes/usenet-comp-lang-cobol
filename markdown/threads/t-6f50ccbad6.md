Pretty much yes in answer to your question. The core of the stock
ordering system went together nice and easily. It has multiple keyword
searches on descriptions, automated export of order histories to Excel,
modem ordering, auto sizing of forms according to screen resolution,
images on cd-rom of stock items etc... almost everything you would
expect,  I did however encounter some problems with parent/child forms
and using 3rd party ActiveX controls such as being unable to retrieve
indexed properties i.e. listing the available DUN connections on the
system but I just used a little VB. to work around. Some of the best
controls i've found for Cobol-WOW come from http://www.mabry.com There a
few other bugs in the system but most of them should be ironed out with
r2.01

Phil
