I have been requested (by a usually reliable source) to make the following
clear to those in this (or any other) forum who are concerned about THEIR
posts being deleted from the Micro Focus' "Ask the Executives Forum".
Please pass this on to anyone else who might be interested.

A) It is true that this is a "moderated forum".

B) It is *not* true that anyone's recent posts have been deleted.

C) *UNFORTUNATELY* the person who is (internally) designated as the
moderator (and therefore able to "promote" posts to general visibility) is
currently out-of-the office. Furthermore, this person didn't (forgot?) to
designate someone to do this in his/her absence.

D) This is a RECOGNIZED "bug" in the new system and Micro Focus intends to
remedy it as soon as possible (with no guaranteed date for delivery)

    ***

Bottom-Line:
  If you tried to post a note to that forum recently (last couple of weeks
or so) and haven't seen it "generally visible" - this does NOT mean that
Micro Focus is censoring you; it simply means that it has yet to be
processed (reviewed) and released.  My understanding (again no guarantee) is
that "review" is intended for "relevance" and "language" and NOT for
content.  However, I do *not* speak for Micro Focus on this issue.
