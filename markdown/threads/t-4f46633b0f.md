Bummer, isn't it?

1. Unregister the new version.
2. Register the old version.
3. Bring up PC project.
4. Delete the control from the project.
5. Delete the OCX from the toolshed
6. Unregister the old OCX
7. Register the new OCX
8. Bring up PC project
9. Add OCX to form tools
10. Add control to form.

God forbid you should ever loose the original OCX. Please tell me you still
have the old OCX available.
