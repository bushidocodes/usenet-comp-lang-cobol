Massimo:

Perhaps you should alter your client/server architecture from a "fat"
client to a thin client.  With a thin client architecture, your client
machines do not necessarily have to open files on the server.  The
files can be opened by the COBOL application running on the server
instead.

It may represent a lot of work to modify the program design, but it
should speed things up a bit.



Bob Wolfe, flexus

Sounds like you are experiencing a bottleneck in your record locking.
It looks tome that your clients are frequently waiting for the other
client to unlock some resource.  This often happens when certain
common records are frequently locked when not necessary, or for long
periods of time.  You may need to examine the paradigm used in the
programs for locking common records to see if it can be improved.
