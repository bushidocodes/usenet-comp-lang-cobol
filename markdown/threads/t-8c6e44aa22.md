Dear COBOL friends,
Since 20 years, some modern language evangelists have been claiming that Legacy Systems are dying and they need to be modernized. However, legacy systems, particularly, COBOL is still the core of almost all financial organizations.

What is so good about legacy systems? Why are still legacy systems running 24/7?

To find answers to such questions, we are conducting a research on how legacy systems are perceived in the industry. Earlier, we have conducted and analyzed >25 interviews with legacy experts from the industry. This survey is the findings of the interview. Please help us to validate the findings through this
link http://servicifi.wordpress.com/legacy-survey/

Also help us to spread this survey to your colleagues.

Thank you for your participation in advance and cooperation.
