Systems Analyst - COBOL/CICS - 51K - Toronto

Will be working in :
COBOL
CICS
CAPS-I-L - prefer ie: Life Insurance Ind.

BASIC FUNCTION :

Analyze, design, implement, maintain and support data processing
programs and systems to contribute to the efficient and effective
achievement of Canadian operation objectives.

ENVIRONMENT / DIMENSIONS :

Information Services provides the information processing support for
hardware and software services and the human resources required to
provide reliable, efficient and economical services to all Canadian
operations. The area is comprised of the following departments; Group
Systems, Individual Systems, Corporate Systems, Support Services,
Technical Services and Computer Operations. Services provided include
Data Control, Data Entry and Computer Operations, mainframe
application and personal computer support. All functions are directed
towards assisting customer areas by developing or acquiring new
systems and services, maintaining existing systems, services and
networks, and providing internal technical support. Positions in
Information Services are affected by economic fluctuations, insurance
industry trends and government regulations, by advances in data
processing technology and by changes in Corporate data processing
requirements. The incumbent is subject to 24 hour call and may be
required to work hours other than normal working hours.

Indeterminate

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES :

1. Research and resolve typical application system or data processing
support problems, developing system designs and procedures, to
contribute to efficient Head Office and field systems operation and
accurate business results.
2. Design and implement application or support systems and
enhancements to effectively satisfy business requirements.
3. Liaise with user areas to collect, verify and organize information
concerning business requirements to facilitate the development of
alternative solutions and to quantify economic factors.
4. Evaluate alternative solutions to support business requirements
and participate in the presentation of recommendations to identify the
most cost-effective means of accomplishing business objectives.
5. Prepare and review program specifications and test plans to ensure
the timely implementation of accurate and efficient application or
support systems to meet business requirements.
6. Communicate or translate complex data processing concepts to less
experienced personnel and assist with programming activities to
contribute to the implementation, maintenance and support of programs.
7. Prepare documentation and participate in providing user training
to ensure the successful usage and operation of application or support
systems and the associated procedures and methods developed.
8. Participate in design and implementation of appropriate
application or support system monitoring, as well as testing/control
procedures, to ensure the successful achievement of objectives.
9. Improve technical skills by contributing to projects of increasing
scope and complexity, completing additional training and reviewing
current data processing literature.

QUALIFICATIONS / REQUIREMENTS :

- Thorough knowledge of data processing and Head Office systems.
- Knowledge of COBOL syntax and structure, office hardware and
software.
- Understanding of insurance principals and the operations of user
departments.
- Analytical and system design skills, programming skills and verbal

communication skills.
