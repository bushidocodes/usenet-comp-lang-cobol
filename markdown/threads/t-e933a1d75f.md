```
First VS COBOL II (commonly mis-called COBOL II) is no longer a particularly
"current" product, There have been 3 or 4 products from IBM since then
(depends on whether you are on MVS or VSE). VS COBOL II doesn't even
support the latest ANSI syntax (it only has the '85 Standard - and not
intrinsic functions - much less the going-to-be-ANSI OO support).

Assuming that you really want to know what is current (and even relatively
new) with IBM MVS COBOL products, I suggest that you start by looking at

http://ppdbooks.pok.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr/bookmgr.cmd/BOOKS/CEEA403/CCONTE
NTS

All the IBM books do assume that you are at least past the very old (late
1970's) product "ANS COBOL V4" and are at least on OS/VS COBOL - but this
may help you anyway.
```
