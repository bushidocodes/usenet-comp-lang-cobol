```
   Do you want this '-1bbbbb' to be stored as a negative 1 or negative 100000?
   It appears that your program is performing as directed. Replacing spaces
with
   zeroes changes the number value such that your initial -1 becomes -100000
   prior to being moved to the destination field.
   I would suggest trying FUNCTION NUMVAL or writing a right justification 
   routine to shift numeric characters and then multiply the result by -1 if a 
   negative sign was found.
   You could also try defining the input field as a numeric edited '--------9'
   and then use a straight move to your zone-decimal numeric field.  I have 
   not had problems doing this although a packed-decimal receiver might create 
   problems.
```
