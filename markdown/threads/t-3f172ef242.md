### Advice for Realia

- **From:** "neil parks" <ua-author-6589435@usenetarchives.gap>
- **Date:** 1995-05-31T20:00:01+00:00
- **Newsgroups:** comp.lang.cobol
- **Message-ID:** `<42227.nparks@slip.net>`

```
Does anyone from CA-Realia read this newsgroup?

I have some marketing advice for you. You ought to bring Realia Cobol 3.x
out of the mothballs. Strip out some of the deadwood such as RED and
REALFED and REALMENU. Just package the compiler, the debugger, REALIF, and
RCOPY into a new product called something like "Realia Lite" or similar,
and price it to compete with Turbo Pascal for DOS, QuickBasic, etc.

I bet you'd sell a whole bunch of them, and people will be amazed to see
what COBOL can really do in a 640K environment on a PC. (Then for anybody
who wants something heavier, you can offer upgrades to Realia 4.x.)
```
