In your invoke Testit you are establishing a handle (object reference)
to the Class TestClass in the FACTORY which is returned to the invoker.
Then its your Object section which does the method work. In a Mickey
Mouse like this - you can see you could have done it in Factory 
with method "Hello-2". Now's the interesting bit - will the result work
in Fuji - let me know.

Don't want to confuse the hell out of you at this stage, but here's
another way you could get at method "hello-2" - and this is the beauty
of inheritance :-
