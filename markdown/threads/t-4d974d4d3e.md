000010 @OPTIONS NOALPHAL
Put the above as the first statement in your code. The ampersand should be
in column 7.  Your problem is that the routines you need to link to are case
specific. whereas the default in Cobol is not case-specific.  The above
option changes that, and is required for most links to C.
