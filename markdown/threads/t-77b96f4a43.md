Hi,
Try a search on half.com and some of the other used-book stores
online.  At least you would have the agony of paying $50 for a text in
case you don't like the language.
If you are near a tech college go check out what they offer, and check
out what is in the text in their bookstore.
!!!  You can still get Fujitzu compilers for free at www.adtools.com.
This is about all you would need to do to get a great look at the
language. 
I was just there for a quick look, and the student section has the
manuals for download too !!!  Please go to,
http://www.adtools.com/student/index.htm
& check the site.  I remember that version 3 was free, but I'm not
sure after that one.  !!!
There is a freeware Cobol compiler in some of the download areas but
the last time I read the documentation it was something that was
started and never got finished( the guy developing the compiler passed
away).
You'd do best to work through Fujitzu.
