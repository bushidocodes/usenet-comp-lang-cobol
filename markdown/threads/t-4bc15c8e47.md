```
For those interested in the revision schedule for the next Standard, the
latest *DRAFT* is available at:

  http://people.ne.mediaone.net/doncobol/99-0187.doc

I am *not* a member of any delegation to the WG4 (ISO) COBOL working group -
but I think that the various country delegations are currently "reviewing"
this schedule.  If you have an opinion about this schedule (especially if
you understand ISO "requirements") - I suggest that you look at this
document

    AND LET YOUR COUNTRY DELEGATION KNOW

what your opinion is (good, bad, or otherwise).

For those in the US, you can email your comments to

     doncobol <at> mediaone.net

For those outside of the US,  I suggest that you check the WG4 home page and
find out how to contact your national delegation.  See
  http://people.ne.mediaone.net/doncobol/99-0187.doc

It is my personal opinion that this schedule (like every schedule before it)
is overly optimistic - but neither do I see any "better" schedule that would
both meet ISO (SC22) rules *and* produce a quality/useful document.   I am
posting this note because I (unlike some others) feel that there is too
little "outside" input to the standardization process.
```
