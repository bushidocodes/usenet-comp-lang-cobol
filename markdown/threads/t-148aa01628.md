### LE z/OS V1R2 - major change regarding COBPACKs (and SOMObjects)

- **From:** "William M. Klein" <wmklein@nospam.ix.netcom.com>
- **Date:** 2001-09-17T17:50:56-05:00

- **Message-ID:** `<9o5utu$7i3$1@slb0.atl.mindspring.net>`

```
Although I doubt that many people are "up to" installing LE z/OS V1R2
immediately into production,  I did want to point out (and hope that you all
notice or pass on to your "responsible" systems *and* application
programmers) the following MAJOR (IMHO) change documented with this release
of LE.

According to:
  http://publibz.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr_OS390/BOOKS/CEEA6110/2.1.3

"The COBOL COBPACKs can no longer be modified. All of the COBOL COBPACK
modification jobs have been removed from SCEESAMP."

FYI - if you look at:
 http://publibz.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr_OS390/BOOKS/ceea5110/E.3?

You will see, however, that the "default" (only!!!) COBPACKs (IGZCPAC  and
IGZCPCO) are now RMODE(any) - which was the "usual" reason that COBPACKs
were modified (in the past).

P.S.  As I pointed out in another post,
 http://publibz.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr_OS390/BOOKS/CEEA6110/2.1.2

states,
"SOMobjects ADE and SOMobjects RTL have been removed from Language
Environment for z/OS V1R2."

P.P.S.  If you haven't seen it yet (although it has been available since
OS/390 V2R10), there is also some discussion of LE "downward compatibility"
functionality.

See:
 http://publibz.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr_OS390/BOOKS/ceea2110/1.2.2?
    and/or
 http://publibz.boulder.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr_OS390/BOOKS/ceea6110/2.3?
```
