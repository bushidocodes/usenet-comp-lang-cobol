Here's the scenario. A good client of ours has asked us to produce a
solution to the following problem:

They have an old (1990) Apricot PC running Xenix. A (presumably bespoke)
database program is used to keep track of their 12,000+ clients. The program
was written in Cobol by a company that is no longer in business. They want
us to migrate the data from this system to a new PC running Win95 and
Access. As we normally only supply hardware and networks and have no
experience of Cobol we are struggling to provide a solution.

the other, and if so, is it a job for a Cobol Guru or can it be achieved by
