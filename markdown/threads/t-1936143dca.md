s04E is a generic "DB2 had a problem" abend code, which could be caused
by any one of a thousand reasons.  

DB2 normally puts out more helpful messages pointing at the exact cause,
either in the job itself and/or in one of the DB2 started tasks
(xxxxIRLM, xxxxMSTR, xxxxDBM1 jobnames) and/or in the syslog.  Armed
with the appropriate Messages and Codes manual and a suitably warped
brain you should be able to work out what went wrong.

Some problems are transitory, so you may already have found that
rerunning the job cures the problem.  As Mark Arnold says, it's not
normally caused by application code, so try palming off responsibility
to someone else!

Happy hunting

Charles Gaskell
