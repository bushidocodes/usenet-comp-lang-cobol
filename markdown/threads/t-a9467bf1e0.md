According to the charter of comp.lang.cobol, it is for the
discussion of all aspects of the use of the programming language
COBOL (eg Object-oriented COBOL).

The charter does not mention job ads; there are too many again!
Some/most advertisers are ignoring the informal guidelines
proposed last year. Here's a revised suggestion:

Mark the Subject line with asterisks and say WHERE the job is
eg Subject: ** USA Ca AS400 Cobol A/P
Keep it brief (20 lines + 4 sig)
NO APPLICATION FORMS or MISSION STATEMENTS
For multiple positions, post 1 ad + pointer to others
Agencies restricted to 1 ad per week

What do you think?
