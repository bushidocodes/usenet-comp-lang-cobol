The always "exciting" partial word expression.  The following works - and is
ANSI conforming.  (However, do remember that once you have a COPY member
like this, you canNOT then use it without the REPLACING phrase and get valid
syntax).

Your copylib member should be:

 01  :Emp-:Record.
        05  :Emp-:Number
        05  :Emp-:Name

Your COPY statement (for Working-Storage) then becomes,

   Copy Whatever
        Replacing ==:Emp-:== by ==WS-==.

but you will also need to have the following in your File Section (if that
is where the original file layout was)

   Copy Whatever
        Replacing ==:Emp-:== by ==Emp-==.


You can use parenthesis "(" and ")" instead of the colons, but there are
some long term maintenance reasons that I usually suggest the colons.

NOTE:  Insert plug for next ANSI/ISO COBOL -

ways of replacing prefixes and suffixes!!!
