We all know that if you:
a) use a credit card
b) have insurance
c) have a mortgage
d) etc. etc. etc.
that you rely on that obscure language we all love called COBOL.

But wait (I need to trade mark this phrase!!)

Did you know that if you:
a) go to the hospital
b) go to the doctor's office
c) go to the dentist's office
d) get a prescription
e) or even take your dog to the vet
you are relying on an even more obscure language!!

ANSI-M (aka MUMPS)

Yes, looks like at this point they hold over 90% of the EMR/EHR
niche. They also have some banking, stocks and other financials,
but they own the medical niche, and not just in the US, but
worldwide.

And yet we still hear that the world runs on Java.....
