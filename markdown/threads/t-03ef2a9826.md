Yes, that most likely is the problem. It's the way DB2 works: the rules
about when you will and will not have to wait are quite complex as they
seem to be related to the proximity of the rows in the system tables in
use by the various programs. What this means is that if you try to
compile a program then if someone else is accessing the system you may
or may not get queued (note - it should not be a hang). Unfortunately
there's no way for MF to predict whether this will happen or take any
kind of avoiding action.
