Check the IBM Global Services Year 2000 Center of Competency Web Site
(WWW.IBM.COM, and follow the Global Services links.) We do have them, and we
are happy to share them with our customers. Of course, the ones available on
the Web are fairly generic. Your local Integration Services office can get you
some information on what it would take to have someone with experience help you
along, either remotely or at your site (for a small fee, of course).

Biggest issue that is often overlooked, is not just testing now, but how to
avoid "breaking" fixed code between now and 1/1/2000. Check out the stuff
titled "Clean Management" for details. We find more folks having problems with
that than the original conversion.
