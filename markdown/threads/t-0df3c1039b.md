Because of Y2K, tables with rows greater than 2000 wrapped and overwrote
earlier entries in the table. (It's a joke!)

>
> Can a list-control display more than 2000 rows?
> If not, what else can I take to display a database with more than 2000
> entries?
> -thanks-

Yes.

Test it yourself:

PERFORM VARYING MYNUM FROM 1 BY 1 UNTIL MYNUM > 5000
       INVOKE CmList1 "AddString" USING MYNUM
     END-PERFORM
