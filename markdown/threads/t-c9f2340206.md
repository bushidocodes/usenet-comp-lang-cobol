Hi Antonio,

WHAT you want to convert is not important. That you are going to
convert with arithmetic, is important.

Make all the fields you are going to use, COMP-3 fields. (Check in
your manual: it might be that they suggest COMP fields for
arithmetic.)

This cuts out a lot of verversion by the machine code and makes your
program more efficient when running a high volume of thru-put.

Further more, make you field sizes large enough so that you do not
loose digits during arithmetic.

Another suggestion: stay away from the COMPUTE statement - it is slow.
Rather use a few individual ADD, SUBTRACT, DIVIDE and MULTIPLY
statements.
