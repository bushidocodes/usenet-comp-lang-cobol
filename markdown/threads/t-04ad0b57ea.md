Read this carefully, neophytes... see all those words? See all that

Now listen closely... skilled, trained Technical Folks are worth money in
a *variety* of ways. Knowing where to find someone can bring another
person a paycheck; this is called a 'referral fee'. Look at these next
few lines:

TIRED OF COLD WINTERS?
BUSINESSES IN WARM PLACES NEED CODERS!
GOOD RATES - EXCELLENT BENEFITS!
SEND RESUMES NOW NOW NOW!

... and tell me where it says 'I have clients who are willing to cut a
Purchase Order for your skills...

... noplace, right? But you bounce your bragsheet off to this weenie-boy
and he calls during working-hours, leaving a message on your machine...
and you call him back, on your own dime... and he quacks and woofs and
mis-spells DB2 and says 'I'll get back to you!'

... and now he is happy, because he has a poetntially valuable bragsheet
to trade... and his boss is happy, because the fellow has a bragsheet
*and* a call-out *and* a return-call *and* a verification of skillset
*and* maybe the nubmers of a few references to bother... reall productive,
that Jones boy is, all *kinds* of stuff going on!

And what do you, the coder, get? You get *zippo*, nada, nichts, rien,
wasted time, wasted dimes... crapola.

PAY ATTENTION... if you see an ad in this group without rates then jes' do
what the ol' Doc does... email the guy saying 'Gee, if you don't post
rates you invite abuse and scorn... and you're ugly, too, ha ha ha!'

*Yes*, I realise that at least I, for one, do not have the social skills
to get my foot into the door that these pimps^H^H^H^H^H headhunters do...
but I also realise that right now there is a shortage of code-humpers,
*so* severe a shortage that we can *demand* something outrageous... like
they do business with us in a reasonable, open and non-antagonistic
manner.


... and thanks for letting me *share* this with you.
