Well.... I have done this an entirely different way.  For example - I
needed to know how OLD someone was.  "Have they reached their 20th
birthday" or not.  This really is related.  The logic I used could
easily be translated to months.

I took the days between their dob and "today".  

Then I converted THAT number to a gregorian date using the function
"integer-to-date".  If someone was 20 - I got a year of 1621.  This
accounts for all the leapdays and such -- FAIRLY CLOSELY, but not
perfectly (because of the 400 year rule).  This could be used to see
how many "months" old data is.
