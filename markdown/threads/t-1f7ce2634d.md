I am part of a small group of self-employed computer trainers that is
looking to expand into new areas.

Traditionally, we have focused on IBM MVS training (Assembler, COBOL,
TSO/ISPF, JCL, CLIST, REXX, DB2, VSAM, PL/I, CICS) and recently we have
added courses in C, C++, GUI, Client/Server, Object-related topics,
Oracle, Visual Basic and PowerBuilder.

We are looking for course developers interested in pooling course
materials and instructors interested in teaching on a contract basis.
There are no guarantees (are there ever in this business?), and we are
just exploring at this point.

If you are interested in exploring this further, drop me an e-mail and
tell me something about your background and experience, or about your
career goals, or simply ask me any questions you may have.
