You have to remember that it is an exponential curve.  Your
first language is the toughest, by far.  The second is not quite
as bad.  By the third or fourth, you can pick up the basics in a few
days, and be fluent in a few months. Most of the regulars in this
NG have been coding for years and years, and most have at
least three or four languages under their belt.

You can be absolutely assured, however, that no one was born
being able to code. ;<)-(yet...maybe in a few million years).

As to your question, it was the sixties, and I find it hard to remember ...
