'So what you want me to do, in essence, is sit in a closet, have my skills

'All right... what kind of rate are they offering for this?'

'What kind of rate are you asking?'

'I asked you first.'

'Well, they're... open.'

'Open?  All right, I'll need to see US$150/hr; adjust your rate to them 
accordingly.'

'No, seriously, what are you asking?'

'That's what I'm asking.  What are they offering?'

'Well... if we go corp-to-corp I think I can get you... US$35/hr, tops.'

'That's a joke, right?'

'No, really... but this is Lincoln, NE, and the cost of living is much 
lower there.'

'So you want me to sit in a closet with a stagnating skillset on the 
plains of Nebraska for two years at US$70K gross?'

'Well, if you want to put it *that* way...'

'No, let me put it *this* way... I hope they're very happy with the person 
they get to fill this slot because it isn't going to be me.')
