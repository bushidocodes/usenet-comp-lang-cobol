```
It sounds like this course prepares you for an IBM mainframe environment.  I
am a little worried by the reference to "OS/MVS/JCL".  This sounds like an
"out-of-date" course.  To find out, ask the vendor/provider:

1) Do you offer JCL targeted at:
   OS/390 (good)
   MVS/ESA (mediocre)
   S/370  (they are out of date)

2) Will you learn the linkage-editor (bad) or binder (good)
   (This is sort-of a trick question, as there isn't much difference, but
does show you how knowledgeable the vendor/provider is.)

3) What IBM compiler will you use in your course work:
   COBOL for OS/390 & VM (great)
   COBOL for MVS & VM (OK)
   VS COBOL II (out of date)
   OS/VS COBOL (do NOT waste your money)
```
