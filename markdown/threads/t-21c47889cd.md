COBOL products with CICS emulators.  Both are QUITE expensive but there are

Relia) also might meet your needs (and is also expensive).

Instead of learning it on your own, call IBM in Riverside.  They have
one of the few remaining "Guided Learning Centers".  For a couple
hundred bucks you can take the CICS course.  (There are several, I
suggest "Basics" and "Application design").  All bets are off if they
closed the learning center.  But IBM should be able to help in some
way.  Then you have a nice certificate of completion too!
