```
PRIMA is seeking to strengthen our PowerCOBOL capability for some work we
plan to undertake in the near future.

PowerCOBOL is a "quickbuild" tool provided by Fujitsu. It runs on Windows
platforms and provides a very quick way to build a GUI interface (Forms)
with COBOL processing for events and processes raised by the user.
PowerCOBOL is Object Oriented, but this is under the covers and not visible
to the PowerCOBOL programmer. For that reason it is quite a good way to get
to grips with OO COBOL, familiarizing yourself with methods, properties, and
events, while the PowerCOBOL system takes care of the Housekeeping.

PowerCOBOL is not a widely held COBOL skill, so we will train you if
necessary, if you can meet the following criteria:

1. Good knowledge of COBOL. (Any flavour on any platform.)
2. Experienced enough to be able to learn a new COBOL flavour quickly, and
with a good knowledge of commercial application development in COBOL. It is
unlikely you will be able to handle this challenge unless you have at least
10 years experience, but if you have less than that and are interested,
make a case to me and I will listen.
3. Able to accept an assignment and deliver it on time, without supervision
and without nagging.
4. Scrupulously honest, with integrity to stand by your work.
5. Your word will be trusted by us, so be prepared to honour it.
6. You will need a PC running Windows (XP or better). You should have a
Skype video capability, SQL server client installed (or prepared to install
a free download), and Oracle Virtual Box VM ware installed (or prepared to
install a free download.)


PRIMA will provide what you need to learn PowerCOBOL and will support you
100% as you gain experience. We won't charge you for this, but you will be
on a lower rate until you establish your competence. (All competent
programmers who do work for us receive the same rate, self included, if I
write code...).You will undertake all work from home and it will be assigned
by and returned back to, me, personally. I will vet your work for quality
and if there are any issues I will tell you, and assist you to resolve them.
Once your work is accepted, you will be paid within 30 days.

Be aware that PowerCOBOL is not a highly marketable skill and we cannot
guarantee continuity of work. You should not consider this your primary
source of income. However, if you are prepared to commit to this, I will
personally make sure that any learning time you invest will not be wasted.

If you are interested in earning some "spare time" cash from home and can
meet the above criteria, please contact me privately by email stating:

1. Name and contact details. (This will not be forwarded to anyone else or
used in any way other than for PRIMA to communicate with you.)
2. How many years COBOL experience. (DON'T send me a CV... I'm not trolling
for them and I'm not in the business of selling resources. PRIMA sells
solutions.)
3. Any existing skills with OO languages. (Don't worry if you have none...)
4. Approximately how many hours per week you can reasonably allocate to
learning and developing with PowerCOBOL.
5. Anything else about yourself you think might help your case.

If your application looks suitable to me, I'll contact you and explain more
about what is planned, what we would expect from you, and what help and
support we will give you. I'll also discuss rates, terms and conditions
then.

I am not looking for an "army"... so the selection criteria will be high. I
want positive, honest people who are prepared to invest some time to make
some cash. It won't be a large number who are selected, but any who are,
will be glad they applied.

This offer is good for 7 days from the date of posting. (Don't respond after
the 20th of November)

Pete.
"I used to write COBOL...now I can do anything."
```
