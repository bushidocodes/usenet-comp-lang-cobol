The general rule for the REDEFINES clause is that the entries redefining the
storage area must immediately follow those that originally defined it. There
can be no intervening entries that define additional storage areas, such as
FSTAT in your example.

If you move FSTAT below the last REDEFINES, you should be all right. However,
may I suggest the following as slightly clearer:

01 LS-CODE-OP PIC 9(02) COMP.

01 LS-PAR.

05 LS-PAR-CLIENT.
10 C-CODE PIC 9(03).
10 NOM PIC X(15).
10 ADRESSE PIC X(20).
10 FONCTION PIC X(10).
10 DATE-ENREG PIC X(10).

05 LS-PAR-COM REDEFINES LS-PAR-CLIENT.
10 COM-CODE PIC 9(10).
10 COM-CLIENT PIC 9(03).
10 FILLER PIC X(45).

01 LS-FSTAT PIC X(02).
