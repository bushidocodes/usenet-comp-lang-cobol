Thirty-five year old company, located in western Pennsylvania, with a
bright future. We have an application written in Micro Focus COBOL
Workbench (v4) that we are looking to migrate to Fujitsu NetCOBOL v9.
We are seeking an energetic individual with experience in this type of
migration project to join our team. Successful completion of the
migration will ensure you have an opportunity to participate on
future, intriguing projects.

Salary: $85,000 to $100,000+ depending on skills and experience

Job Description

Required Qualifications:
- 5 or more years of COBOL experience
- Mastery of the COBOL Language, including the ANSI-85 extensions
- Experience with at least one successful migration project from Micro
Focus COBOL to Fujitsu COBOL
- Thorough understanding of ISAM
- Proficient on a Windows platform
- Self-motivated, able to work and produce with little supervision
- Good analytical, problem solving and communication skills

The following skills are a bonus:
- GUI user interface experience (Dialog System, Flexus, PowerCOBOL,
dotNet)
- Ability to interface with 3rd party DLLs written in MS C
- Any 4GL experience
- Exposure to Crystal Reports or similar product
- Loan processing or financial experience a plus

We are a fast growing company that offers excellent benefits that
include: Health, Dental, Vision Insurance, 401K and Life Insurance.
Send resume in PDF or Word format via email to: cobolwork@gmail.com
