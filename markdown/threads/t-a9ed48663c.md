```
ITUG, the International Tandem Users Group, recently ran a poll on
their website asking about present and future use of COBOL.  I thought
the results would be of general interest on CLC.

http://www.itug.org/pollarchive.cfm?QuestionID=96

The question was:

What is the primary use of COBOL in your shop?

16.0% 	  None of our applications are written in COBOL 
 34.3%     Our COBOL applications run our company, and new
applications are being written in COBOL 
 32.0%    Our COBOL applications substantially run our company, but
new applications are being written in other languages 
 14.4%    Our COBOL programs run only old applications, but we keep
them maintained 
 3.3%     We are converting our COBOL applications to use other
languages 

Total Votes: 181  
Poll Dates: 10/16/2003 - 10/30/2003
```
