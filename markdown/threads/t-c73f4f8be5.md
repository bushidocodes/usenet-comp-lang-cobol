Attention: Registered users of Fujitsu COBOL who have current maintenance
agreements

At Fujitsu COBOL, we are committed to providing great customer service. As
with many fast growing companies we sometimes do not live up to our own
standards.

It has come to our attention that a small percentage of Fujitsu COBOL
Version 4.2 update CDs shipped in March and April may have been lost or
misdirected. If you are a registered user of Fujitsu COBOL Version 4.0 and
have a current maintenance agreement, you are entitled to receive a free
copy of the Version 4.2 update.

If you have not yet received your Version 4.2 update CD, please send a note
to cobol@adtools.com and include your name, serial number and a CURRENT
shipping address (no P.O. boxes please).

We sincerely apologize for any confusion or difficulties that may have
arisen from this situation. Please contact us at cobol@adtools.com if you
have any issues you wish to discuss.

Fujitsu Software Corporation
