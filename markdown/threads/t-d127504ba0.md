Now that might open a few doors.

Here in the UK most of the jobs are put through agencies, and most of 
them and us use a website (http://www.jobserve.com) called Jobserve which 
is the biggest one of its kind over here.  I assume that there must 
surely be an American equivalent.  The joy of this is that a job can 
become available on a Monday and filled before the end of the week, which 
is a lot quicker than can be accomplished by other forms of advertising.  
Using this route, you virtually guarantee that you are in the first wave.
