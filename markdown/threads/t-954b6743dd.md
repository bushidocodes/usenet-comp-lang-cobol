vendor of MicroFocus COBOL, which was spun off last year from Merant.

    http://www.microfocus.com/
    (phone: +1 301/838-5000 or 1-877/772-4450)
    
MicroFocus tells how to get registration help for COBOLexpo:

    http://www.microfocus.com/mfnewsletter/

The other notable independent vendor of which I am aware is:
Liant Corporation (the successor to Ryan-McFarland), which has
both COBOL and PL/I compilers.

    http://www.liant.com/

Of particular interest to you may be the tools Liant sells for
translating PL/I programs to COBOL.

If you have any CICS appliations, you will want to check out  
UniKix:

    http://www.unikix.com/

Most COBOL users should be aware of the existence of the COBOL FAQ
(Frequently Asked Questions) list: 

    http://www.cobolreport.com/faqs/cobolfaq.htm

According to it, two other vendors of UNIX COBOL compilers are Acucorp:
 
    http://www.acucorp.com/

and Fujitsu:

    http://www.adtools.com/products/unix/cobolux.htm
