You are being very foolish to get into a 'line count' contest
with Cobol.  In sed or AWK or Perl things can be done in one
line that would take pages of Cobol, such as a regular
expression search.  These lines, however, tend to look like
hen scratchings and are probably best treated as 'write only'
and are best supported by pages of explanation.

'less lines' is not a feature that Cobol has ever tried to achieve
because it tries to make programs readable like a book.

In a similar way few novels or reference manuals try to
achieve the story in the least number of lines, for much
the same reason.

> Then give it to your PCS and tell him to take a refresher course.

Absolutely, many programmers need to upgrade their skills in
many ways, or even need to realise that they can.
