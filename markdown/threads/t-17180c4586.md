You can always do things like this by attacking the problem one
character at a time. Character-by-character is often not the neatest
or most clever way, but it always works.

UNSTRING is another method.

Beware:

1. You must be aware of name suffixes, viz:

Jr.
III,
M.D.,
MC (or MP)

2. Two (or more) people are involved:

Tom Brown and Mary Smith and daughter Julia

3. Then you have non-businesses

Acme Industries Inc.
General Electric

And you thought it would be easy.

The BEST way to handle this kind of stuff is to have the decision made
