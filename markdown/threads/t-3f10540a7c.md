Locally, resumes have been sold for $10 each and this could be more
now. Corporate phone books bring hefty prices but at the risk of
losing your job.

There are companies which purchase lists of resumes from the sponsors
of job fairs. They pack these on a CD and sell them. It's been 11
months since I last attended a job fair and I still get calls from
around the country stating they got my resume off the CD.

There are also vendors which troll for resumes and send them to
clients without your approval. If you've allowed company A to present
your resume to the client but the troll has also sent your resume
without approval, the client will usually trash your resume since
there is now a conflict of who should get the contract.
