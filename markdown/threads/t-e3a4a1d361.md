Hi, I'm an independent application developer who works in both
environments (Mainframe & Distributed). The role CICS provides is that of
a "transaction monitor" and not merely just for presentation purposes. It
becomes extremely valuable when your application is updating several
different resources and you must have all those resources marked as one
unit of recovery. CICS performs this task by controlling access to the
resources your application has marked as uncommitted work. There is
nothing intrinsic within Unix That performs this for you - You Have to
purchase CICS/Aix or a competing product like tuxedo.
