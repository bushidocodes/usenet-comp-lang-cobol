We Have Available 1994 Versions Of XperCASE LITE

XperCASE is a reengineering/reverse engineering tool, designed to aid in
language conversion. It was reviewed in the April 18, 1994 issue of PC
WEEK, and is available for ftp from SimTel, at its various mirror sites.

It is also used as a CASE Tool, for lower CASE applications, linked to a
compiler.

XperCASE LITE was the student version of XperCASE, available for
university purchase.

As XperCASE V.4.01 is now out, we have a remaining inventory of several
thousand copies of XperCASE LITE V.3.5, in SPX (non-language specific)
and C Versions.

In order to demonstrate XperCASE more widely, we have decided to offer
these copies of Version 3.5 to anyone interested, for a token charge of
$10. US, to cover shipping and handling. COD charges would be extra of
course.

If you are interested, please email back to this address.
