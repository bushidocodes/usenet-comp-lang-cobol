If I have a record like

	05 rname   pic x
	05 rscore1 pic 9
	05 rscore2 pic 9
	05 rscore3 pic 9
	05 rscore4 pic 9

and a table/array

01 table occurs X times.
	05 tname pic x.
	05 scores occurs 4 times.
		10 tscore pic 9.

I can then load the table with;

	perform load varying C from 1 by 1 until C=X.
load
	move rname to tname(C).
	move rscore1 to tscore(C, 1).
	move rscore2 to tscore(C, 2).
	move rscore3 to tscore(C, 3).
	move rscore4 to tscore(C, 4)

My question is can I have instead a simpler record like;

01 rec.
	05 rname   pic x
	05 rscores pic 9(04).

and a simpler load to load the table like with a single move like;

load
	move rname to tname(C).
	move rscores to scores(C).

I tried the above but the compiler (Microfocus personal) complained.
