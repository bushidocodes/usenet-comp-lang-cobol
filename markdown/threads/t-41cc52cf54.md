It might help us if you provide the particulars on your problem, esp.
the platform and compiler you're using. How one uses DISPLAY/ACCEPT
in one envronment, say IBM MVS COBOL/390, is different on another,
like MicroFocus Personal COBOL for Windows. It's one of the few COBOL
syntax that would have different effects on the various platforms.

The newgroup is unique in that it's the only one I know where many
folks from different platforms and compilers can discuss the issues on
a common language and still understand each other. I've had people
from UNIX platforms answered my MVS COBOL questions and I answered PC
COBOL queries. The language is that common enough.

Just my 1.5 cents worth, adjusted for inflation
