```
Warning - this posting relates a thought I had which some might interpret
as making light of a miracle related in the New Testament (Matt.xiv 17-21,
Mark.vi 38-44, Luke.ix 13-17, John.vi 9-13 or thereabouts); those who are 
offended by such things are advised to go, now - perhaps to sin no more? - 
but you'll miss a joke that I think you could tell your 
(pastor/priest/reverend/etc) and raise a smile.

Yesterday, after taking lunch at the 'Don't Speak Our Language?  Screw 
You' shopping-mall in Fall's Church, VA (known to the locals as the Eden 
Center and a hub of the area's Vietnamese commerce) I was driving up the 
highway, just north of Washington, DC and I saw a bus... a recent-model, 
clean, nicely-painted bus emblazoned with the logo of 'Bob and Edna's 
Bible Tours'.

Now... my memory is, admittedly, porous, and I might not be recalling the 
names exactly right... but it was *somebody's* 'Bible Tours'.  Leaving 
aside the incongruity of having a Bible Tour pass so closely to the 
obviously Godless environs of Washington, DC I had a moderately disturbing 
thought which sprang from my own memories of bus-tours from decades past.

Long, weary jostlings over endless miles... and then, to alleviate the 
tedium, somebody would start one of those Songs to Alleviate Bus-Tedium 
that so many folks know... and then, it came to me, one such Song to 
Alleviate Bus-Tedium that might be sung by the group on Bob and Edna's 
Bible Tour...

Five loaves of bread and two fish on the wall,
Five loaves of bread and two fish!
You take some down and pass 'em around,
Five loaves of bread and two fish on the wall!
```
