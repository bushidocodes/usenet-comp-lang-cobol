IBM *does* currently support OO COBOL - but *not* exactly what is in the
draft ANSI/ISO Standard.  (The OO support in Enterprise COBOL, the latest
compiler, is NOT the same as what was in IBM COBOL for OS/390 & VM, the last
compiler).

IBM has made NO public statements concerning implementing any, all, none of
the draft Standard.  My guess is that they will support at least SOME of it,
but that is only a guess.

P.S.  This answer assumes an "OS/390 mainframe" interest.  IBM also provides
OO support on WinTel and AIX - but does not on VM, VSE, OS/400
