Hi all,

Two years ago, as part of my doctoral project, I asked members of this
newsgroup to fill out a survey about learning and knowledge-sharing.
Many people freely gave their time and helpful comments. I promised to
send those who asked a copy of the research report. 

I apologize for the two year wait without any news. For me, that
particular chapter of the dissertation was the most difficult, and I
only finished it recently. 

I've prepared a heavily abridged summary of survey results, with an
introduction to my topic and an explanation of the survey. Even
abridged, it is still 20 pages long, so following a suggestion by one
participant, rather than including it as an attachment, I have posted
it to a friend's web-page so that you can download it if you're
interested. Of course, if you're really interested, drop me a line and
I'll be happy to send you the full dissertation when I finish it, in
about six months time. 

Thanks again to all who participated!


Enrique Murillo-Othon
Mexico City


You can find the summary (218 KB) in Acrobat at:  

 http://www.bisabuelos.com/emo/summary.pdf


You can contact me at   e.m.murillo-othon [at] bradford.ac.uk
