```
(Already covered in comp.lang.cobol - but I am putting this out with a new
"subject" for the purpose of DejaNews)

For those who have (ever) had problems with specifying a literal in the
Select/Assign statement associated with an SD - in Fujitsu COBOL,  there has
been a change made in V4.2.  The message against this is no longer an
"error" message - but is an informational message only.  Therefore, you can
get a "clean compile" with either an implementor-name or a literal in the
Assign clause.

Therefore, if you have a problem with such source code, you have two choices
available to you:

1) Upgrade to V4.2 (or higher?)

2) Change your
      Select SD-Name Assign to "C:wherever/there".
          to something like
      Select SD-Name Assign to Any-ignored-name-you-want.
```
