Hi Mark,

For your information, there is no such command as CONVERSION in
Fujitsu COBOL. That may be a command in another program language like
maybe MicroFocus. You do not need to use CONVERSION. 

Are you accepting data from the screen input? ACCEPT W-FIRST-NUMBER is
all you need and when the program runs, and comes to that point, it
will stop and request information be typed such as a number and then
hit return and that will be entered into the W-FIRST-NUMBER field.

Hope this helps.
