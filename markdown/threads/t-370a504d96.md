Addressed to Bill Klein but others can chip in.

So here you are William, no longer a junior, with a whole daffy of
knowledge from your days with M/F. You pick up Fujitsu and start playing
with it. Surprise, surprise, just like I've had to struggle with
NetExpress you are now asking 'How do I do this in Fuji ?".

It really is bloody dumb of these manufacturers. Just how many hours are
being wasted around the COBOL universe with people re-inventing the
wheel. If you compiler folk are looking in, Acu, Unisys, Liant, Fujitsu,
Merant et al - start giving it some serious thought - provide a more
comprehensive set of examples as to how to use your products, (in the
REAL WORLD), particularly where the module(s) is/are specific to your
compiler.

Want an analogy. You go buy a new vehicle from the salesman at the auto
showroom. He helps you push the car out on to the main road, then with a
smile says, "You're on you own......".

Jimmy, Calgary AB
