As described, it's also inaccurate.

If you use the above description to calculate the number of days
STBT to a date in the 20th Century (i.e. 1901-2000) you will be off
by 15 days, as all the years ending in "00" will be counted as leap
years, when only four of them should be (400, 800, 1200 and 1600).

when the conversion from Julian to Gregorian took place.
