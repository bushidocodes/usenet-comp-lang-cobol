Job Advertisement for Applications Analyst/Sr., Applications Analyst, Applications Analyst
Associate positions in Information Systems Division

The Information Systems Division of the Nebraska Department of Roads (DOR) is looking for five
ApplicationsAnalyst/Sr., ApplicationsAnalysts or ApplicationsAnalyst/Associates. These positions are
available immediately. Applicants must meet the minimum qualifications for these State Classified
positions. The offices are located at 1500 Nebraska Highway 2. The Department of Roads has flexible
working hours, free parking and utilizes state-of-the-art systems development technology. The working
environment is very comfortable.

The applications to be worked on are varied. They include Engineering, Management, Financial, and
Inventory.

These positions may perform differing duties.

One type of position emphasizes developing and maintaining COBOL programsfor business applications.
These programs are for MVS batch and CICS environments. The APS code generator is used for many
new programs. For these positions, knowledge and experience with COBOL and CICS is desired.

A second type of position emphasizes systems analysis and design for business applications.. Individuals
are needed to assist in the analysis and design of computer applications. Currently, these applications are
targeted for MVS-CICS. There may be some transition to Client Server applications in the near future.
Application design uses the Intersolv XL/II Case Tool.

A third type of position develops and maintains engineering applications that run on a variety of platforms
including Windows NT Work Stations and lap top PC s. Languages like C++, C, MDL, and Visual Basic
are used here. The development environment is similar to the target environment.

Some applications are developed for VM/CMS and Office Vision/VM is used for E-Mail.

DB2, DB2/2 and DL/1 are the current data base management systems for business applications.

Much business application systems development and maintenance is performed on an OS/2 PC platform
targeting production on the State s mainframe computer using Micro Focus COBOL.

The Department will provide training in the various development tools that it uses. Any current skill with
the tools is to your advantage..

Completing a questionnaire is required. The questionnaire asks about your experience with various tools
used at DOR. Insure that your resume substantiates the indicated qualifications.

The starting salary for these positions ranges from $2143 to $3435 per month depending on qualifications
and experience.

If you meet the minimum qualifications and have experience and or the aptitude for this type of work, apply
now.
