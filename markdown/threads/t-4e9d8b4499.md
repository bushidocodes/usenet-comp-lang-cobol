This is more of a linking issue than a cobol issue.
You usually have a small "exports library" associated
with a dll that is used when linking.  This library
knows what entry points are in the dll and sets
up the proper calling.

In the absence of this, the convention varies
by compiler, but us usually something along
the lines of knowing or finding the entry address
(via GetProcAddress if in windows) and then doing the
call "indirectly" to that address. Some compilers
supply utilities for this, others don't.
