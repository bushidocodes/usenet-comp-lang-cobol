Hi Philippe

I have been involved with Cobol tools for twelve years and am aware of
Pacbase, although I have never used it.  As you say it is popular in France,
but it is almost unknown outside France.

I believe there are only two Pacbase accounts in the UK, and I am not even
sure whether they still use it.  At the time Pacbase was selling well in
France, the competition was Telon, APS and IEF.  Telon, now owned by CA,
invariably won.

Incidentally, I now work for Casegen Systems Ltd who produce a range of
tools including direct replacements for CA-TELON, including a converter.

What do you think of Pacbase, what do you like about it - I am always
interested in other Cobol tools?

Simon Cordingley
Technical Support Manager
