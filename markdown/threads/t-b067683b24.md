Chubb has an excellent reputation and is a fertile breeding ground for
new programmers. I have hired at least a half dozen of their
graduates over the last year and have been very satisfied with their
technical training.

Keep in mind that they run a number of different programs. I have
found that graduates of the day program are usually better and
prepared to be productive very quickly. The night program graduates
tend to be a little shakier - over 18 months or whatever it is they
lose some of the understanding of the earlier modules because they
don't use them frequently enough. I have had no direct experience
with individual week long modules but I suspect they are comprised of
the same material and same instructors as the long programs and are
probably fine for individual target areas.
