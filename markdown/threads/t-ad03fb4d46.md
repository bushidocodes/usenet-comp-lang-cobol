Over the last several months, I have had many COBOLers ask me for one or
more of the following items that I have provided for free to others.  I came
across your name on the Internet and thought you might be interested.

1.  Free Utility Download: SIZER (No More Counting Lines of COBOL Code)
Check out this free utility download for COBOL and cut the hassle of
calculating the size of COBOL records.  It's called COBOL Record Sizer.
You copy any COBOL record layout to the clipboard and this application will
calculate "the size in bytes" of the (largest) record found.

2.  Free Internet Newsletter aboutlegacycoding.com
Is a new Internet newsletter for IT professionals who work with legacy code.
The content focuses on management and integration of enterprise data written
in COBOL, Fortran, C,  ADA, PL1, RPG and other languages.  It's a newsletter
that is designed to provide the facts and spare you the corporate fluff.

3. Government IV&V Definition Statement:  Many companies are requiring  IV&V
(Independent Verification and Validation) evaluation of the COBOL code.  We
have been asked by many of the IS Directors to provide them an IV&V
definition.

If you are interested in either, send me a reply and I will provide the
web-site.
 If you aren't interested or don't work with legacy code, no need to reply,
I won't e-mail you again.  Have a great day.

Sincerely,

Barry Ewell
bje@progeni.com
