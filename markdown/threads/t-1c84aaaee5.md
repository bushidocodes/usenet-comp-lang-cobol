Looking for a few good people.....

If you're watching the ads, (newsprint, newsgroups, etc.), you know that everyone is searching for Year 2000 programmers.... but not necessarily.

And yes, while we do have Y2K maintenance positions, our clients also need developers for new software systems and analysts for new and existing systems. Not only do we have mainframe openings, we're also looking for PowerBuilder, VB and Access developers.

What makes this company different from all the rest ?

And in our twenty year history, we have remained relatively small in number (under 200) ! Of course, our size tells you that we are NOT a national organization, and in fact, we're currently staffing in the New York, New Jersey, Southern Connecticut and Eastern Pennsylvania.

Help me to help you !
