```
I have a file full of COMP and non-COMP data. I just want to convert it to
all ascii for readability. I tried taking the original record layout as
input-rec, and stripped out the COMPs to create an output-rec. Then I read
with the input-rec and wrote with the output-rec from the input-rec. No
luck. Then I read with the input-rec, moved it to the output-rec, and wrote
the output-rec. Still no luck. In both cases, I got identical copies of the
original file. So, instead of
JOHN SMITH&^%%!*
I want
JOHN SMITH5551212090999
Needless to say, COBOL is not my native language...
Thanks for any help.
```

```
You are on the right track.  But instead of moving the input record to
the output record, move each field of the input record to the
corresponding field in the output record.  That will allow Cobol to do
the data conversion.  Write your output record after all of the moves
are done.
```
