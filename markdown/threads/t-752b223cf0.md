```
As a result of the visibility of my concerns and those of another J4 member,
the latest draft of the COBOL Standard is NOW "freely available".  The PDF
version (zipped) may now be downloaded from a link on:

 http://www.ncits.org/tc_home/j4.htm

PLEASE NOTE WELL:
  This version IS intended for J4 "internal use" - in that it is NOT subject
to any "public review" (or public review period).  The FCD input period is
FINISHED (both in the US and internationally) and J4 is just "finishing" up
corrections that resulted from it.  The output of the February 2002 J4
meeting (and an "editorial review session") is expected to be the "input"
document to the next review period - which will be an FDIS (final draft
international standard).  That document will go out to an "up or down - not
for technical change" review by the countries involved in the ISO SC22
standards process.

I am UNABLE to tell you at this time, what may or may not happen with the
copyright vs "freely available" issue when (come NEXT December) this becomes
an approved Standard (as I expect it will be).

If you do download this draft and have comments/issues regarding the
"copyright statement" - then I recommend that you still contact the ISO
person/group listed within that document.
```

```
"10646".  The ANSI numbers relate to X3.23 (with some additional stuff) -
but the ISO stuff all refers to "1989"
```
