Just wanted to indicate the availability of a new ISPF course: Advanced ISPF (2
days).

Originally I had a 1.5 day course for ISPF V4 differences, but management
didn't seem interested in paying for this (attitude: they can pick it up on
their own; might be true, but a class is often a faster way to see all the
options and learn what works best for you). So I took the 1.5 day class and
boosted it up to include topics that people who learned ISPF a long time ago
might not know even though they've been around quite a while, along with a
short introduction to edit macros and the ISRDDN facility.

The class is intended for use by programmers, not end users or dialog
developers.

Most the folks I've met on newsgroups don't really need this class, in my
observation, because they tend to work at keeping current; but you might know
of people in your organization who could use such training, and perhaps you
could point them to the web site below, which contains a course description and
outline.
