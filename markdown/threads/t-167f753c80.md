```
Hi all;

I have a problem. I need to design a record layout about a score
sheet. The score sheet has upto 4 stages, each stage has upto 8 series
and each series has upto 10 scores. Each score is out of 10

I need this info about the score sheets, so I can calculate the max
score possible for each stage. So that when the user enters the score
from each stage, I can validate if the score is too high.

An example;

A stage with 1 series and 5 scores has a possible max score of 50

The most complicated score sheet will have 4 stages, between 2 to 8
series and from 2 to 6 scores.

Has anyone any ideas? Thanks.
```
