COBOL II supports the ANSI/ISO '85 standard. However, in 1989 the standard
was amended to include intrinsic functions. COBOL II does not support
these. To get COBOL '85 plus support for the '89 amendment you need to
have "COBOL for MVS and VM" (formerly called COBOL/370).
