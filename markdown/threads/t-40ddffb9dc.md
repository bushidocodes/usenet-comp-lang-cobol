This is the text of the Press Release that the X3 Secretariat is issuing:

** ASC X3 Announces the Informal Call for Comment for COBOL **


March 3, 1995 -- The first Working Draft for the revision of Programming
Language COBOL (ISO/IEC 1989:1985 and ANSI X3.23:1985) will soon be
available for an informal comment period.

This revision is being processed simultaneously as an International
Standard by ISO/IEC JTC 1 SC22/WG4 and as an American National Standard
by Technical Committee X3J4. The call for comments is being issued to
better prepare the draft that will be formally submitted for
registration in late 1995.

Comments are requested to be submitted not later than June 27, 1995.
Comments received after the deadline will be addressed and considered
for future balloting and/or revision. Commentors will be notified when
the next revision is issued.

This document is subject to change without notice prior to its approval.
No implementor should claim conformance to the working draft.
