scores of 5, 4, 3, 2, 1.  The odds of hitting the 5-hole is 1/15;  2/15 for
the 4-hole; 3/15 for the 3-hole; 4/15 for the 2-hole; and 5/15 for the
1-hole.  A score of 13-15 wins a Bitty Bear; and a score of 16-20 wins a Big
Bear.  If Bitty Bears cost $4 and Big Bears cost $8, for every dollar the
carney spends, what will be the take?
