I have not studied your sample in detail, but a couple of facts that I think
you may be missing (and which may explain your problems).

1) A group item is ALWAYS considered alphanumeric, not numeric

2) You normally do arithmetic on numeric items and then move them to
numeric-edited items (for printing).

3) When you ACCEPT from DATE, the result comes back in YYMMDD format.

4) I think there are some other problems with your program too - but I think
you will need to "play with it" to find them all. (We all do when we are
first starting out)
