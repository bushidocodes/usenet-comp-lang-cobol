David - I don't know where you got the MIS-information the CORRESPONDING
option. All CORR phrases are "fully" supported in the draft Standard.  They
are *not* marked as either "archaic" or "obsolete" in this Standard - so
they are expected to remain in all "future" COBOL Standards.

I think that your "confusion" *might* have to do with the fact that they
were removed from the JOD at one time.  I can't remember for certain - but I
believe they were added back- even there.

CORRESPONDING is a "basic" (and to be kept) feature of COBOL.
