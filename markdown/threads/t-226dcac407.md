```
In response to another thread, I thought I would start a list of "common
prefixes" that we might consider using in the subject lines of CLC notes (to
help filter and "group" topics)

Please post your suggestions for changes, additions, whatever. If there is
some "general consensus," I will add it to the FAQ.  OBVIOUSLY, people
(especially newcomers) don't HAVE TO use these, but if those of us who are
"regulars" do, it may help.

Admin:  - things like this that talk about the NG itself

OT: - Off-topic (this is fairly common across newsgroups)

OO: Object Orientation (Does "GUI" need to be separated out?)

MVS: (well I certainly do NOT recommend "OS/390:" <G> - but would take IBM)
           (CICS may or may not need its own)

Acu: Acucobol and Acucorp products

Fuj: Fujitsu

MF:  Micro Focus (should be MER for MERANT, but I don't think most users
think that yet)
                               (on the other hand "MF" may get confused with
"mainframe")

RM: RM
```
