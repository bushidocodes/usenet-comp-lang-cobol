For information on the bit patterns used in most COBOL data types, download
the free text and graphics tutorial COBDATA.ZIP from
http://www.flexus.com/softwaredownload.html.

As far as "why" a designer will choose COMP v COMP-3, COMP-5, etc, it
usually is a function of the specific target hardware on which the product
will run.  However, numeric fields (i.e., those on which you wish to do
arithmetic) should always be COMP or COMP-whatever for best perfromance.

MCM
