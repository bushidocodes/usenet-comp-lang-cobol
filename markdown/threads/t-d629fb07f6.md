I may be mistaken, but I think that the "bad coding practice" was that
END-OF-DATA-FLAG was in the record description (under the FD) rather than in
Working-Storage.  There have been previous discussions (in C.L.C.) about

OPEN, or after the last READ or CLOSE).

In this case, your COBOL compiler does not support the

(Other PC COBOL compiler may do.
In VAX and Mainframe COBOL, organization is always LINE.)
