```
I am researching object-oriented COBOL issues for an article and am
interested in the opinions of COBOL programmers. Here are some
questions I have for you:

1. With object-oriented and visual programming technologies
now being introduced to COBOL, how do you feel about the
future of COBOL? About the future of the COBOL shops you
program for?

2. How do you feel about making a shift from a procedural
to an object-oriented approach to programming?

3. How do you see your COBOL shop moving into an object-
oriented programming environment? In small pilots?
In larger groups?

4. Have you heard much about object-oriented COBOL?

These are just starter questions -- feel free to write
your thoughts on other issues relating to object-
oriented COBOL as well.

Thanks in advance.
```
