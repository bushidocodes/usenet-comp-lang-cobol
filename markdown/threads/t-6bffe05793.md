There are different ODBC drivers for the different COBOL dialects.

MicroFocus have a products called CORRELATE. (http://www.mfltd.co.uk) .
Liant have a product called RELATIVITY (Recently renamed to RELATIONAL
DATABRIDGE) (http://www.liant.com)
AcuCobol have a products called AcuODBC for their structures
(http://www.acucobol.com).
Transoft make an ODBC driver that will access MF, ACU and C-ISAM formats
(http://www.transoft.com).

That's all I can think of right now.
