Bruce Eckels "Thinking In Java" is the best I've ever seen for a learn 
and understand.

From an application point of view -- that is, if you are not coding an 
XML parser or an MQ work-alike manager -- then neither of those two 
technologies require more than a page to describe.  Anyone that would 
sell you more than 15 minutes of training is wasting your time and your 
money.

IBM's Application Programmers Guide, free on their public book server 
should answer all of your MQ questions.

A brief look at www.xml.org should explain what xml is and the very few 
rules related to it.
