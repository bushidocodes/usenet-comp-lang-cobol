I wont swear to it - but probably for all M/F compilers it is the same -
"RTS error 114 ("Attempt to access item beyond bounds of memory")

More often than not it is associated with Linkage Section parameters
whether using CALLs or INVOKES - If appropriate, carefully check your
sending and returning parameters. Use your Debugger (Animator ?) to check
the *actual* values being sent or returned.

If you are still stuck go to the Micro Focus Answer Exchange and post your
query under the appropriate product.
