*IGNORING FOR THE MOMENT* when/if any of us will ever see a "full
implementation" of the recently approved COBOL 2002/2003 Standard, I did
want to point this group to two J4 papers (one of which I think I have
mentioned previously).

at:
  http://www.cobolportal.com/j4/files/s16.doc

Is the full list of (currently under consideration - with "rankings")

  "Candidate features for a future revision"

At:
  http://www.cobolportal.com/j4/files/03-0144.doc

is a paper from a SINGLE individual commenting on enhancements under
consideration.

    ***

As I have no way of knowing whether there will or will not be a "relatively
quick" (e.g  less than 10  years) revision of the recently approved
Standard, I can't suggest strongly enough that those of you who DO believe
in the COBOL Standardization process provide your PERSONAL input on those
features currently under consideration NOW - while the list is still being
developed.  If you create a paper "comparable" to that listed above (from
Artur Reimann) and send it to the J4 Chair, it will be looked at by the
Standards committee to give them "outside" input to validate or change their
priorities.

P.S.  Just as Artur did, please DO indicate that your personal opinions are
just that - your opinions and not those of any "group" - unless such a group
authorizes you to do so.

to target, by their general consensus,  what is important.
