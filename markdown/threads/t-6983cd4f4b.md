I did this. Here is how and it worked for me.

- Make a small group with just a couple of entries.
- Do a export of the group
- Use the MF file converter and convert to a line sequential file.
- Now You are ready for edit.
- I use a SPF-PC editor.
I aranged the names right and did a move / overlay.
Be certain to keep the characters in other columns than the names.
- Convert the file back to a index file.
- Do import.
- There You are.
