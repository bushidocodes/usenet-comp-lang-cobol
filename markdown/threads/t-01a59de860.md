I believe that placing the linkage section after the screen section
is not correct sequencing of the data division. Screen section
items may refer to linkage section items.

The syntax summary shows:

data division.
file section.
working-storage section.
local-storage section.
linkage section.
communication section.
report section.
screen section.

I believe this, with the exception of 'communication section.' is
the order of presentation specified for the 2002 standard.

What you suggest may work but could fail when the program
is changed or the compiler replaced.

I probably wouldn't have mentioned this were it not for your
reminding me to think about 'the next programmer'. <g>
