Not shorthand, e.g., Pitman (Pittman?), it's mostly a bunch of
abbreviations based on Latin words/phrases. For example, BID = twice a
day, TID = three times a day, QID = four times a day, Q4H = every 4
hours, Q6H = every 6 hours. HS = bedtime, PRN = at the nurse's
discretion, PO = orally, etc. BTW, Q6H is not usually the same as QID.
QID might be be 10am, 2pm, 6pm & 10pm. Q6H would literally be every 6
hours. You never worked on a formulary.
