In the event any of you are looking to buy the Micro Focus Personal
COBOL compiler:

I just bought the Win31 version, and everywhere I see in the online
docs, object this, object that. Not the COBOL I had in mind. Plus
the compiler won't generate exe files.

Not until I got Mo Budlong's book did I get the scoop on standard
procedural COBOL. Even with this book, note that it was written for
Personal COBOL for DOS. MF still has it in their catalog, but I guess
you've got to ask for it. Dont know why Micro Focus assumes everyone
is using Windoze. (hello Micro Focus, keep the DOS version!).

Now if you know of a good way to learn MVS. let me know .. I found out
about CBT and PC based MVS simulators, but dont know how good they
are. Can anyone share their experiences?

Cheers.
