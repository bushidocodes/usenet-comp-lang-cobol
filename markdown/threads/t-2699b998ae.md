This is how the 1400 series did subroutine linkage in Autocoder. They
had A & B registers. After a branch was taken the B register pointed to
the next sequential instruction after the branch, so the first
instruction in the subroutine was:

	SBR   EXIT+3               save B register
	.
	.
EXIT  	B     *

No thoughts of being reentrant, of course. I gues it's true, "Everything
old is new again".
