Hi Kelly,

I had a conversation with our z/OS systems development group, and the 
resulting advice is, perhaps obvious and predictable, that we would need 
access to the MFA SYSLOGS from z/OS to have a better chance of diagnosing 
this. Failing this, we recommend ensuring that your level of MFA installed 
is the latest level including all published fixpacks.

From the exception code our system guys came up with some educated guesses 
about what may be wrong:
1. Could it be that the level of z/OS is old (or are you indeed using a 
recent/current level, in which case we rule this out)?
2. Security setup....MFA uses cross memory services (XMS) for our Endevor 
and ChangeMan support, to deliver transactions and return responses to our 
Agents running as spawned task in another address space.Security errors 
could cause abends which invalidate XMS setup, and lead to invalid 
cross-memory operations.

Sorry but we cannot be more precise based on the information given.

Best regards
Brian Crane, Micro Focus.
