I am using VSView7 in one of my projects. The VSPrinter control
must be added to the form first.

Here are some lines out of my subroutine that displays the contents
of a file:

         INVOKE vsprinter1 "Clear"
         INVOKE vsprinter1 "StartDoc"
					
         move "0.25in" to "MarginLeft" OF VSPRINTER1

         move ifr-data(1:ws-rec-siz - 11) to "Paragraph" OF vsprinter1

         INVOKE vsprinter1 "NewPage"
         move space to "NavBarText" OF vsprinter1

         INVOKE vsprinter1 "EndDoc"


Most of the VARIANT arguments can be handled as strings. Numbers should
be COMP-5.

A couple of things learned the hard way (8-): The NavBarText property
must be written to force the page count displayed with the NavBar to
update. The NewPage method does not interact with it. Two, the Paragraph
property is the preferred way to write a single line of text.
