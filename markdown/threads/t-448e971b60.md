```
> Does anyone know where I can find a profiler for Cobol on the VAX/VMS
> platform/OS ?

First, get the system manager's tuning manual and the guide to VMS
performance management and make sure your cluster is up to snuff.

Then, review your program's I/O strategy.  The compiler must translate
your CoBOL I/O statements into RMS, and a poor I/O strategy will outweigh
the most careful code.  Start with the overview in chapter 20 of the
CoBOL user manual, and then go on to chapter 3 of the file applications
guide.

Only after you have tuned your system and your I/O is it worth profiling
your code with the VMS Performance Coverage Analyzer (PCA).  PCA doesn't
come free with VMS, but it might already be on your system.  If not, you
can get it fairly quickly.
```
