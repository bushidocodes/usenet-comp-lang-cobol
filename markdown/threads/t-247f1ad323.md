If you want to learn COBOL, you might try:

The Revolutionary Guide to COBOL (diskette with a small COBOL compiler)
or
COBOL for Dummies (CD with FJ cobol compiler)
or
Macmillan's COBOL Starter Kit which has Acucobol on a CD and includes an
electronic version of Teach Yourself COBOL in 21 DAYS (in this case you
may want to get the book,too. I can find things quickly with the
electronic version of the book, but the paper book is easier for me to
read.)
