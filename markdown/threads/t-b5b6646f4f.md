Hi Bruce,

I don't think there has ever been a committment from the OC team to provide 
OO COBOL.

It's a question of focus and priorities. And these, in turn, are pretty much 
dictated by demand from the user community.

COBOLlers generally are not interested in object oriented programming.

OC is a pretty huge undertaking and they have done a very good job of it, 
but actually implementing OO would be a major effort.

I asked Roger about 18 months ago if there would be OO support and, although 
he didn't rule it out long term, it was not something on their horizion at 
that time.

If it is ever implemented I would be glad to use it as part of the Migration 
Tools.

Perhaps you could ask them directly by posting your question to their user 
forum?

Pete.
