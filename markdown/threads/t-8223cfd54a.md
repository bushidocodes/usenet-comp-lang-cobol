```
I have previously told this group about the status of the Collection Classes 
DTR.  In particular, I have indicated that Micro Focus (and I) oppose further 
processing of this document.

The Draft DTR can be viewed at:
   http://www.cobolstandard.info/j4/files/07-0048.pdf

There is currently an international ballot being processed.  I can't tell each 
of you when your national standard's body will be submitting their ballot, but 
in the US, the J4-tag (US domiciled members of J4) are currently conducting a 
letter ballot on what the US position should be. The responses is scheduled to 
be "resolved" at the upcoming J4 meeting in Garden Grove (on April 19-20 - for 
this discussion).

I have already submitted the Micro Focus ballot and it can be viewed at:
   http://home.comcast.net/~wmklein/DOX/07_0053.pdf

Although this is being handled as a J4 Letter Ballot, I believe (but can't 
guarantee) that if anyone within the US wishes to contribute to the discussion 
of this DTR, that they may send their comments to:
      INCITS - at dspittle@itic.org  and incits@itic.org
with a CC to
    J4 - via the Chair at dSchricker2(at)cox.net

Reference the ongoing ballot on:
     "JTC 1 N 8548 (J4/07-0048), ISO/IEC DTR 24717:2007(E), Information 
Technology - Programming languages, their
environments and system software interfaces - Collection Classes"

If you wish to contribute to another national standards body, then you can check 
comp.lang.cobol archives for information that I have previous provided or send 
me a private email and I can help you directly find where to send your comments.
```
