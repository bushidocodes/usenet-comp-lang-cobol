There used to be a Microsoft Cobol. In fact MS were among
the first with a microbased Cobol coming out with a CP/M
version in the late 70s (79?). It was converted to MS-DOS
in the early 80s.

By the mid 80s their product was well out of date and they
licenced the MicroFocus product as MS Cobol 4.x. They
updated and MS Cobol 5.0 was a repackaged 3.0. It was
rumoured that they did release 5.1 (MF 3.1) but MF
moved into the US market and decided that MS need not
distribute a Cobol any more.

MS Cobol may still be located in surplus stores, but
it is a few versions behind MF. Still it may be
considerably cheaper (was around $US 200 or less
in surplus).

Check out Fujitsu at www.adtools.com.
