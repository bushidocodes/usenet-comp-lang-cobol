Alain,

What do you mean by 'export my application' ? To which 'system' are you
trying to export ? Objects (EXEs) are designed to run with the Net
Express  runtime and supporting ADIS files to handle the mouse, (that's
assuming you are working in a DOS/text window), plus support for COBOL
files. (See the on-line help 'Distributing Applications' - BUT REMEMBER
- University Edition is a TUTORIAL - and isn't designed with the intent
of 'allowing' you to export).

You can of course feed your Net Express source into another compiler,
(Fujistsu, AcuCIBOL, RM/COBOL etc.) and re-compile, but the Micro Focus
Mouse routines wont work, and you will need the support files for the
appropriate compiler.

Jimmy
