I'm not entirely sure what you mean by a "CICS compiler". However, if
you take a look at CICS/2 which runs on the OS/2 platform and use VA
COBOL, you should find this to be a good solution. I developed a small
application using OS/2, DB2/2, CICS/2 and VA COBOL earlier this year.

The development took only six weeks from start to finish and the thing
now runs live on a mainframe. It was undertaken as an experiment with
offline development and proved to be very successful. The only fly in
the ointment was the PC wallahs who refused to support OS/2 in favour of
their beloved unscaleable NT, and managed to get their mouths heard
before ours.

I would assume that MF COBOL will also provide you with this capability
but I have no knowledge in that arena.
