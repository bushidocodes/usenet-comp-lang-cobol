Hello Mary,

Always good to hear of student interest in COBOL. There is a tremendous
worldwide shortage of programmers; especially COBOL programmers. It is the
predominate computer language of business around the world.

Many of the COBOL compiler manufacturer's offer versions of their products.
Check out http://www.acucorp.com/ and learn about ACUCOBOL-GT. Also, there is
the "COBOL Starter Kit" (ISBN 0-672-31204-2) that includes a version of the
ACUCOBOL compiler on CD, along with a digital version of "Learn COBOL In 21
Days."

Good luck to you, and may your COBOL instructor be interesting and capable.
