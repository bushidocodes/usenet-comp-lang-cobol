It will be tough to get anyone to port their Cobol compiler to Linux
because Linux users have been spoiled by excellent/free software. I
have actually spent money on just one Linux app (30 bucks). No
commercial firm can operate on such a small market with such a small
unit price.

We have to wait for some effort like cobcy to be developed. There is
something around called gnu cobol but IMHO it is trivial and so
non-standard that I consider it irrelevant.

So wait a while, or write your own compiler.
