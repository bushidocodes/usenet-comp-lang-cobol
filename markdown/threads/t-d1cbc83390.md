Hi Jaya,

It sounds like you want to know the amount of DA space required to house your
dataset. This would depend on how the recs were blocked. For example, if the
file were blocked 80X1 the IRGs (inter record gaps, in tape lingo) occupy more
DA space than the data. I don't recall how much DA space is occupied by an IRG
(they probably will differ by divice type, anyway.) If you 1/2 track block the
data that's not a big factor, however, the diff between the size of the 2 blocks
and the capacity of the track may be.

The thing to remember is that there can be a significant difference between the
# of tracks required and the # of Kbytes in the file.

HTH, Jack.
