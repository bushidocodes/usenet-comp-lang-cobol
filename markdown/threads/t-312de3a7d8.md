Try:

 05    Z-ST-PIC9-REDEFINED      REDEFINES Z-ST-PIC9   PIC 9(10).

Then move Z-ST-PIC9-REDEFINED TO Z-STATPRAEMIE-9.

That way, you're moving a numeric field to your packed field.

Monika,

    05      Z-S.                       
         10     Z-S-1-7           PIC X(7).
         10     Z-KOMMASTELLE     PIC X.   
         10     Z-S1              PIC XX.  
         10     Z-S2              PIC X.  
    05    z-s-rdf redefines z-s.
         10    z-s-1-7-rdf     pic 9(7).
         10    z-dec-pt        pic x.
         10    z-s1-rdf          pic 9(2).
         10    z-sign            pic x.
   05      Z-STATPRAEMIE-9             PIC S9(7)V9(2) COMP-3.

   1)  test numeric, resp. z-s2 "+" or "-", as you did. If valid:
   2)  compute z-statpraemie-9 = z-s-1-7-rdf + 0.01 * z-s1-rdf
         if z-sign = "-" multiply -1 by z-statpraemie-9 end-if

Regards,
Lothar
