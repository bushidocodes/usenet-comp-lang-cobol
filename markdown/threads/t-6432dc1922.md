There are four positions available in the Sacramento, CA area. The
positions are for at least one year with salaries of 45-75k per year +
travel expenses (based on experience). The project is called SACSS
(Statewide Automated Child Support System) and is a federally mandated
state effort to integrate the disparate child support systems possesed
and maintained by the various counties of California. The projects
prime is Lockheed but these positions are for a data conversion effort
which is being led by two subcontractors: The Asgard Group, Inc. and
PRWT Services, Inc. The Asgard Group is providing project management
and is responsible for field teams that operate in the various
counties. Each field team consists of typically three members. The
positions are:

Team Leader: Oversees the team and facilitates contact and
interaction with the county admin. Must have strong interpersonal
skills, technical background, and political "astuteness".

Systems Analyst: Prepares data conversion maps for county data to
SACSS. Assists programmer when needed. Must also have strong
interpersonal skills as well as extensive technical background. Data
conversion experience a must , VMS and Cobol experience big plusses.

Programmer: Performs all needed coding for the conversion task. Data
conversion, VMS experience a plus, Cobol needed.

This project is high profile and politcally sensitive so we are
looking for people who can handle themselves in a politically charged
environment. These positions could very easily lead to full time
employment or continuing contract work as other states begin their
respective efforts.

ch··.@amp··i.edu

Thank you for your time.

Vice President, The Asgard Group, Inc.
