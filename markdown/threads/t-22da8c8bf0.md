Walter,

The following is written as an individual and should not be taken as
representing Micro Focus in any way.

A name change is being considered and this was made public in the
Intersolv acquisition announcements.  As far as I am aware no decision
on the new name has been taken at this time.

I believe one of the reasons for the Company/group name change is
that "Micro Focus" is closely linked with COBOL.  The new combined
group does much more these days and the name needs to reflect
this.  The new Micro Focus has revenues of approx. 400 million
dollars and over 2000 people World-wide.  We have product and
service offerings in a number of solution areas.

Who knows, may be Micro Focus will stay around as a brand name
for the COBOL products?

Hope this helps.

Paddy Coleman

Back when I started at Micro Focus 12 years ago there was still other
Micro Focus products around. A reasonable word processor driven by
function keys and semi-wysiwyg springs to mind. I must add that that
