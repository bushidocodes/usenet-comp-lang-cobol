```
It depends on what compiler you are using. Almost all DOS, OS/2,
Windows, and UNIX compilers support COBOL-C and C-COBOL calls.
However, some of them require special "syntax" to indicate what
language is being called and other compilers support this
transparently.

P.S. It may also depend on what C-compiler you are using too.
```
