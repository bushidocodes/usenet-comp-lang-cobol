Jeremy,

It is best to think of each CICS conversation as one unit of work.
There is nothing special you need to do with regard to DB/2 cursors in a
CICS program. Declare, open, fetch, and close them just as you would do
in a batch program. One difference is committing database changes. In
CICS you need to do a CICS SYNCPOINT (as opposed to an SQL COMMIT in
batch).

I have been working for nine years with the COBOL-CICS-DB/2 combo and
they work together beautifully.

HTH,
Doug McKibbin
