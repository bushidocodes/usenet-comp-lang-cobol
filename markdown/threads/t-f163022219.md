I think MF is too busy to answer questions, so I shall be better off
seeking help from here.

I just upgrade my MF COBOL 3.1 to MF object cobol v4.0.26 and a
flexus SPII. ( I quit dialog system v2.5.64 which I think is too
difficult to use.) I use two identical PC's
(same CYRIX 150+, 64MB, and win95 etc.), one with MF COBOL and SPII
to develop application, the other to test the end-product to be shipped.
I use LIBRARY, BUILD to wrap up my applications and store them in
indepentdent directories on both machines.

On the developing machine my application runs well but on the second

(Memory access violation has been detected by your OS).

I cannot figure out which files are missing or requiring tune-up.
