On July 19, 1999 I posted a request for volunteers to read and comment on a
document I will be making available (free)  on COBOL datatypes and
converting that data for use in BASIC or C/C++ programs; and the
ASCII/EBCDIC ramifications of these conversions/data movements.

I received a double-digit number of replies, and just wanted to say that I
sincerely appreciate and thank all who volunteered.

I have selected a group of three who, collectively, possess the exact mix of
skills I sought, and the paper is now in their hands.

Thanks also to Bob Wolfe of Flexus, who had earlier graciously agreed to
supply web space.

Give me two or three weeks to get feedback and clean it up, and when
questions come up about converting datatypes, there will be, in essence, a
"FAQ" for those who need it.
