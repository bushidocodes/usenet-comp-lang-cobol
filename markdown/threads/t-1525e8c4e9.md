Overview
Wonomi Technologies is a small IT Professional services firm that has clients throughout the Chicagoland area. We are currently looking to add a Unisys mainframe Linc developer to our team. The candidate shall have at least twelve years of experience as a Unisys mainframe developer of which at least three years shall be in developing new applications. The candidate should be familiar (conceptually only) with an environment that has a virtualized environment, multiple data centers, Storage Area Networks, Cisco network devices and a fairly robust process driven environment. The candidate shall be responsible for ensuring that the requirements gathered are well understood, the design meets the requirements, the code written is tested (unit, integration, systems), support security testing and provide level 3 support. The candidate will also have to maintain an existing code base.
A self starter with the confidence to lead efforts in assessing applications, troubleshooting issues, developing solutions/fixes and working in teams requiring significant communication and co-ordination will be able to thrive in this role. A willingness to mentor junior client employees to help them grow into more senior roles with responsibilities of development, team leadership and architectural design is expected.

Relevant Experience (Yrs):
* 11+ years as a hands-on COBOL development against a DMS II database, MCP emulator and ClearPath Operating System.
* 3+ years Linc development in a Enterprise Application Environment (EAE) within a SaaS and/or in-house Windows Server 2008 and 2012 environment
* Familiarity with change control processes and systems
* Experience with documentation, static code analysis and code profiling
* Experience with on demand reporting using WFL, CANDE, DMSINQUIRY, ERGO.
* Analysis and resolution of production issues relating to the application or the database in both batch and online areas
* Knowledge and experience with supporting infrastructure issue resolution VMware, RTU and LTO-3 tape from an application development, deployment and support perspective only

Technical/Functional Skills:
* Write application software, data analysis, data access, data structures, data manipulation, databases, design, programming, testing and implementation, technical and user documentation, software conversions.
* Problem Investigation, Change management, Performance tuning, and system monitoring
* Perform code reviews, peer to peer feedback and design collaboration
* Maintain existing applications by managing the proactive detection and resolution of problems (e.g., an error in a system that may cause one or more incidents), establishing the root cause and preventing the recurrence of service disruptions
* Investigate and diagnose the problem (root cause analysis) including coordination with other internal staff and teams along with 3rd party vendors where required
* Design and implement work-around and/or long-term solution. Initiate necessary change requests
* Coordinate with internal staff and other teams including 3rd party's on requests for change to develop long-term solutions for assigned problems.
* Provide feedback regarding testing, training, procedures, documentation, etc.
* Evaluate problem management performance based on a set of defined metrics (e.g., resolution time, number of incidents before a problem is diagnosed, number of problems per service)
* Manage, monitor, review, and report on server traffic and performance to identify and resolve failures or potential issues in order to meet business demands and maintain Service and Operating Level Agreements
* This person will also be a knowledgeable resource for the other support areas and groups who need to deploy solutions or resolve problems within the enterprise.
* This person will handle support calls and interact with multiple help desks, and their management, within and outside the organization.
* Work hours are day shift however support after hours, when needed (for level 3 support), is required.

Generic Managerial Skills:
* Communicates effectively with technical and business personnel.
* Works as part of a team and works towards meeting the goal as a team
* Demonstrates collaboration with team members, and solid leadership skills when leading others.
* Co-ordinates with multiple teams/stakeholders during upgrade planning, execution and post upgrade support.
