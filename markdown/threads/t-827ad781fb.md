Updates for Micro Focus DOS, Windows and OS/2 products are now available
for anonymous FTP from ftp.mfltd.co.uk:/pub/updates as well as from the
Micro Focus BBS and Compuserve.

Updates for UNIX products, as well as AAI and the CICS v2.5 Production
Option are not available because they are provided as full products.

There is also a link to the updates from the News and Announcements
section of the WWW server (http://www.mfltd.co.uk/).

I will post to this group as updates are added.
