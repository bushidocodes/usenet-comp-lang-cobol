once again, a FS problem.

error # 9/125 (all locks cancelled due to exceeding time limit).

FS is running with following settings:
-s slowenien
-d slowenien.ref
-cm ccinampu
-cm ccitcp
-pf /etc/fscob.pwd
-t 0

CCI: 000114 10072900 0010 07 The CCINAMPU is out of resources, try
