I am a graduate student in the MBA program at California Polytechnical
University in Pomona. For my Master's thesis, I am studying the viable of
the COBOL programming language. Many articles have being written
condemning the continued use of COBOL and yet a large number of the
industry professionals still seem to champion the language. This study
is an attempt to determine if COBOL is still meeting the needs of the
industry, and, if so, how is it now being used.

Your participation in this newgroup indicates that you have an interest in
the future of the computer industry. If you are interested in this
subject, I would greatly appreciate it if you could take a few minutes to
answer the attached survey. This survey contains 20 questions and I am
also grateful for any additional comments you may want to add on this
subject.

In return for your participation in this study, I will provide access to
the results once they become available (summer, 1996). Please include
your addess at the end of the survey or I will return the results or the
location of the results to your E-Mail address.

Any information provided will be held in strict confidence and I will be
using aggregate information only to compose this paper.

The first draft of my paper is due on April 1, 1996, so please try to have
the survey to me no later than March 15, 1996. Thank you in advance for
