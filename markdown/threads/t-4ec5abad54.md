> Are there any professional associations or organizations specifically
> for Cobol programmers?  Thanks.

I don't know.   Certainly CoBOL isn't as complicated as library based
languages, and thus there's less learning needed by experienced
programmers.

But I find it interesting that the local Java User's Group is packed,
while the local AITP (formerly DPMA) chapter is hurting.  We will do
things to help our specialty but remain ignorant about areas outside of
our specialty.

The trouble is, our specialty is no longer (if it was ever) isolated.  To
provide the best product to our customers, we need to be familiar with a
wide variety of technologies, just to have a good guess about what's the
best solution!!
