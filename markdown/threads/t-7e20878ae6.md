Hi Paul,

First, some general comments. 

Have you declared the generation group index? If not, you'll have to
do that first via IDCAMS. See the IBM Access Method Svcs Manual.

When you read the concatenation you'll be reading the most recently
created data first. If you need the data in date order, you'll have to
sort by create date. If your data doesn't have one, you've got a
problem to solve.

See your msg below for further comments specific to your JCL.

Regards, Jack.
