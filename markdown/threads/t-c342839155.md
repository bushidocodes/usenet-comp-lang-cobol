We (ANSI - X3J4 and ISO - WG4) passed and had approved an
"amendment" to the '85 Standard in the '90's - but I doubt that
is what you want to know about.

The current schedule for the next full revision of the COBOL
Standard has it going out for its first "official" public review
in the Summer of 1996 (next summer). Assuming that review is not
controversial and we get all the other work done that we need to
do, then the schedule will have an approved Standard by the end
of 1997.

the document when it is "out for review", send a note to
