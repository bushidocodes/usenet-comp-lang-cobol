PR-ANNUAL is a numeric edited field. Numeric edited items are
of class alphanumeric and comparisons follow the rules as for other
alphanumeric items. For Bessie the Cow, the comparison was
equivalent to:

    IF "+37,107.00" IS GREATER THAN "29000     " ...

Since "+" is less than "2" (it occurs earlier in the collating sequence),
the condition will evaluate to false and the MOVE "*" instruction will
not be executed.
