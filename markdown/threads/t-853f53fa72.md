```
05 nbr pic 9(5)v99
05 filler redefines nbr.
    10 nbrdigit occurs 5 times pic 9(5).
    10 filler pic xx.
05 sub  pic 9.
05 ptr  pic 99

05 output-area pic x(80).

move 1 to ptr
perform varying sub from 1 by 1 until sub > 5
  evaluate nbrdigit (sub)
     when 0
        continue
     when 1
        move 'one' to output-area (ptr:)
        add 4 to ptr
     when 2
        ....
  end-evaluate
end-perform

There's more to it than that. But that's all you get
to help you get started. You have to consider place
value and where to put "hundred" or "dollars" ...,
capitalization at the beginning. etc.  Show us what
you have done so far.
```
