When assiging your output to "Printer" or any print device, Micro 
Focus defaults to Line Sequential.  Line sequential files lose the 
X"1b" characters, or insert extra characters depending on your runtime
settings (+N I think might fix your problem).  An alternative is to 
expand the print records by two bytes, inserting X"0d0a" in these 
bytes and explicitly stating Organization is RECORD sequential in your
select statement.
