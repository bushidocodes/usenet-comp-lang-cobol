I doubt if the installation is less than 1.4 megabytes, but the best
place to get a free compiler is to buy Thane Hubbell's book, "Teach
Yourself COBOL In 24 Hours!"  Perhaps you can simply uninstyall some
of the other software you have on your machine....or are you working
directly from a 3.5" floppy disk drive....Yikes!

It not only includes a great book, but also incudes a 16 bit version
of the Fujitsu COBOL compiler.  The ISBN number of Thane's book is:

0-672-31453-3

You can buy the book on-line at http://www.barnesandnoble.com

Here's another alternative.  Access
www.flexus.com/softwaredownload.html and you can get the COB650 COBOL
compiler.  It only works in MS-DOS, but I'm guessing that the total
installed size is probably less than a megabyte.
