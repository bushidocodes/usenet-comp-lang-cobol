```
I don't care much for the hypertext documents I've had to deal with.

What the heck is wrong with plain text, either ASCII or EBCDIC,
and no hyper-links cluttering it?

Too often hypertext has been used as an excuse for not providing 
any decent LOGICAL structure to a document, and to avoid
providing INDEXing   The stupid links tend to go to useless places,
and that is supposed to substitute for the hard work of 	THINKING
THE DOCUMENT THROUGH WHEN WRITING IT.

The standard SHOULD be available, preferably on-line.  But not
slapped together in the fad-du-jure mode of presentation!
```
