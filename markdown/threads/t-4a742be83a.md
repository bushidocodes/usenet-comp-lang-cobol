000-MAINLINE.
    OPEN INPUT    STUDENT-FILE, COURSE-FILE

                MOVE HOLD-COURSE TO T-COURSE (SUB)

        SUBTRACT 1 FROM SUB.

0100-PROCESS-DATA.
        SEARCH ALL TABLE-ITEM
                    AT END PERFORM 0500-ERROR-REPORT

                    PERFORM 9999-READ-STUDENT-FILE.

        MOVE TODAYS-DAY TO H2-DAY.
        MOVE TODAYS-YEAR TO H2-YEAR.
