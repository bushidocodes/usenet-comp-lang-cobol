You don't say what COBOL compiler you're using, or what platform
you're running on. In RM/COBOL, you use:

DISPLAY stuff CONTROL "FCOLOR=color,BCOLOR=color"

where color is one of BLACK, BLUE, GREEN, CYAN, RED, MAGENTA, BROWN
or WHITE. You can use this in conjunction with HIGH or LOW, and/or
REVERSE and ERASE will reset the entire screen. See your user's
guide for more details.
