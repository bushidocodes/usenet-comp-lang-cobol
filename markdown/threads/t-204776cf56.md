Your Problem is caused by a table Overflow. I think you should check the 
IF-Statement at the beginning of 200-MAIN-PROCESS. This Statement 
increments (via 300-LINE-BREAK) the index S-SUB, without checking the 
maximum value of 6 that S-SUB may have as a valid Index for the 
SUMMARY-TABLE. Thus, depending on your specific Input File, there will be 
an Overflow of SUMMARY-TABLE, which destroys the DETAIL-TABLE.
