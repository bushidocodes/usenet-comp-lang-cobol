Bob, there are lots of AS400's (or "iSeries" as IBM now labels them) still
out there. I understand that COBOL is used here, but RPG is the language
that has been used to develop most of the applications on this machine.

Most of the current action I have seen with COBOL is in the migration
space -- moving existing applications from larger systems (such as MVS
mainframes) to "distributed computing" platforms such as Windows & UNIX
(including Linux).

You can find a representative list of such projects at:

http://www.microfocus.com/migration/hardware.asp

You could consider combining your existing skills with additional
"distributed computing" skills and seek opportunities in this space.

Gazaloo.
