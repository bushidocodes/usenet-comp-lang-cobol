MicroFocus COBOL.  They only support the Workbench product (which has really

saying they have no plans to change the COBOL support prior to the 2nd Half of
1999!

NT.  It is easier to administer than Tuxedo and it supports COBOL with
NetExpress including debugging of server and client applications.  Wow! What a
concept!
