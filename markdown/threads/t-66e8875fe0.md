Hi Anh Le and other new MF users.

Accept is described in language reference, and I think you know that accept
has 3 formats. Read carefully and enjoy.

Your installation could have online help, Then you can search for Accept,
and see the same description on screen. Older versions have HyHelp, and that
is online help in character mode.

If your installation is correct and one of the expenxive ones, try to write
the word 'Accept' in the editor. Position the cursor on the 'A' and press
Alt+1. Some of you might get a syntax diagram directly on the screen.

You could be the type who just like to see and example, and take what's
needed. In that case, go to www.merant.com and find the FTP-site. They have
many samples there.

Happy learning
