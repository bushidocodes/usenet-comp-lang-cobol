public-spirited efforts should be worthy of public notice so that he/she/it
can reap the benefits of public acclaim in the COBOL world that he/she/it so
richly deserves. C'est la guerre!

This stuff stinks up my mailbox, wastes my time and accomplishes nothing.
After you've looked over the 'evidence', all those in favor of Jensen875
continuing on in this self-appointed role of c.l.c. traffic cop please raise
