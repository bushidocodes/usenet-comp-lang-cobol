```
Normally we use our software and hardware (badge reader see www.leggo.it)
with RS232 and RS485 but in some firm there are many problem to connect with
RSxxx and we must connect with LAN.

We connect the badge reader with ABLELINK from Oltremare (www.oltremare.net)
with RS232 and the converter with LAN.

Today we can connect the PLC in many manners like this example:

1) Badge Reader -> RS232 -> PC

2) Badge Reader(1) -> Badge Reader (2) -> up to Badge Reader(32) -> RS485->
Converter 485/232 -> PC

3) Badge Reader -> Converter Ethernet -> LAN -> PC
```
