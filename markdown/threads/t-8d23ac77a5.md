```
Just in case anyone interested in such has missed it (as I did - when I made
a mistake in a post to IBM-MAIN),

On June 6, 2000, IBM announced the removal from marketing - effective Sept 6,
2000 - of:

  IBM COBOL for MVS & VM

See the announcement at:
     http://www.ibmlink.ibm.com/usalets&parms=H_900-124

For those who don't speak "IBM-ese" as a native language <G>, removal from
marketing is NOT the same thing as dropping from support.  However, it is
usually the first step on the path to such a statement.

For those who can't keep track of the "real" names of IBM's mainframe COBOLs
the product that will "remain" after this product is dropped is

 "IBM COBOL for OS/390 & VM" (product number 5648-A25)
                                                  or   (product number
5648-MLE with MLE)
```
