Yes. Deleted records are 0010. Note that 0101 thru 1000 are
also user records or pointers to user records; but I've never
seen them in a file and do not know under what conditions they
are generated. (Possibly for record lengths > 4K)

> QUESTION 2 - There is some data tagged on the end of each record... and
> often it is just blank... but down the list of records I start seeing...
NNI
> or MOS or LBE or MAN or NDR or ORA or REG.
> Anybody have any idea what this may mean??? Maybe that is telling me the
> status of the record? I don't know.

As was mentioned previously, physical data records are a multiple
of 4 bytes in length. 439 (x"01B7") + 2 = 441, 444 is the next multiple.
The three characters are fillers and should be ignored.
