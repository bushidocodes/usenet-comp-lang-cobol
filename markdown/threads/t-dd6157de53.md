Brad,

I am not a VB expert but I seem to remember one of my people
telling me that the DLLs VB creates are not "standard" DLL i.e.
they cannot be CALLed from COBOL/C and so on.

They are in fact OLE servers and have to be accessed via OLE
(or COM as I believe it is now called).  This will require you to
use OLE/COM domain support in Object COBOL in Net Express.
