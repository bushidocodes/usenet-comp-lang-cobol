Maryland. Calverton is just outside the beltway from Washington, DC.

They utilize a CASII , VSAM based mainframe system. They do their own JCL and

programming responsibilities there are also the day to day issues- getting

implementation of new benefits.

VSAM. Additional experience in at least one of the following: health

CICS/BMS, File Aid, or Natural/ADABASE. Salary range: 45 to 54k

There is dial up access, which is available for evening access when on call or
in case of emergency. There are also flexible working hours and/or work week.
The health insurance is 100% paid. There are 3 weeks vacation, 13 days sick
leave (accrued on a weekly basis), 11 holidays and 1 personal day.

If you are interested or know someone who may interested in this opportunity,
please do not hesitate to send an email. Thank you.
