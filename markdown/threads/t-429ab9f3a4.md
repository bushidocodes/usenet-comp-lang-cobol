Financial Analyst and there are tons of jobs. I worked for Keane for a while and
I could pick my spot. Keane had 100 open positions in Greensboro NC and about
half were COBOL. COBOL is in high demand for two reasons:

come home to roost.
2. Many companies are moving from client/server back to the mainframe.

In short, you can go anywhere for almost any amount of money if you have 5 years
of COBOL.
