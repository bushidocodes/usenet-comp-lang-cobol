Totally unrelated to you development platform, I had the black
square problem with and off-the-shelf product using an ATI rage
video card.  An upgrade of that product made it go away--sorry
this information (even if it's related) is not of much use.

As for accessing the A drive.  I would search the registry for
A:\.  Windows caches information about where it can locate
libraries (though this is for ActiveX DLLs) and it may have
gotten fixated on your removable at some point.
