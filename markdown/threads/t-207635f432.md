```
1. Convert the hours and minutes to minutes only.
2. Adjust the stop time if crossing midnight.
3. Calcuate the difference in minutes by subtracting the
    start time minutes from the stop time minutes.
4. Convert the difference in minutes to hours and hundredths.
5. Multiply this difference by the hourly rate.

as in:

    Compute start-minutes = (start-time-hour * 60) + start-time-minute
    Compute stop-minutes = (stop-time-hour * 60) + stop-time-minute
    If stop-minutes < start-minutes
        Add 1440 to stop-minutes
    End-if
    Subtract start-minutes from stop-minutes
        giving minutes-difference
    Divide minutes-difference by 60
        giving hours-hundredths-difference
    Multiply hours-hundredths-difference by hourly-rate
        giving period-earnings
------------------
Rick Smith
```
