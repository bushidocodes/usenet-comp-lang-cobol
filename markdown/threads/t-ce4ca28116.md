There is no native support for binary numbers is CL programs on an AS/400; just
packed decimal numbers and character fields.

I believe you could declare this a two byte CHAR and initialize it to x'0002'. 
Not sure of the exact syntax of the top of my head.  (You could ask in
comp.sys.ibm.as400.misc).
