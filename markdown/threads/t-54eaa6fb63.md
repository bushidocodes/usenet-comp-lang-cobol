```
All,

I've been able to get MicroFocus to the point of investigating whether they
should port NetExpress to Windows NT running on the Alpha platform.

IMO, this is an excellent combination, in that it allows people coming from a
VMS or UNIX environment running on Alphas the ability to re-engineer their
legacy application's interface to a Web-based GUI with little effort while
staying on the same hardware platform.  This makes an excellent investment
protection story for your customers while raising customer satisfaction!

The fact that it's also the fastest NT platform around is a very nice side
benefit too!

If you are interested in seeing this package ported to Alpha NT, please
contact the following Robert Collins at Micro Focus to let him know your
needs:

 nospam-rdc@microfocus.com-nospam

(be sure to remove the nospam- from the above when replying.)

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me regarding this issue.

Regards and thanks for your consideration,
Aaron
```
