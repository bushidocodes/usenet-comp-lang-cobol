A netadmin friend ran across a similar sounding problem with a borland 
app. He suggested setting two registry keys on the NT server. One 
disables files cached after the close, the other disables oportunistic 
locks. The MS Knowledgebase articles were Q102967, Q1260626 and some 
related articles.

These made the problem much less noticable but (unfortunately) not 
completely gone. On the bright side, at least files are not kept open on 
the server now after a user has closed the application.
