```
When you click the start button, and got to Programs, do you see Fujitsu
Cobol 4.2 listed?

If you do, you will have to remove it.  Click the start button, go to
settings and then control panel.   In the control panel click on Add/Remove
programs.  Go down the list of programs until you come to the Fujitsu
Cobols.  If 4.2 is the same as 4.0, there should be 4 or 5 entries that you
have to remove, one by one.   You have to click each name in order to delete
it.

You should do the above even if you don't find Fujitsu listed under
Programs.
```
