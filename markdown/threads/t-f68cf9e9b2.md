```
You have to understand why the utilization of consultants was
so popular to begin with.

It's because female managers and other minority managers would
exploit consultants on a temporary basis to get those managers
over the peak hurdles of the projects in question. The impetus
behind calling on a consultant came from the pressures to meet the
prevailing standards which had been instituted by the previous
managerial class, the White males.

However, over time, female managers and other minority managers
BROUGHT DOWN STANDARDS such that the peak hurdles of their
projects are no long so pronounced and those peaks are getting
fewer and farther between each other. Now that declining standards
have caused the impetus to utilize consultants to diminish, so, too,
has gone the economic demand for them. With lower demand, you see
lower overall compensation for consultants, the most capable of the
lot who should be earning the MOST, but alas, they are not!!

I can tell you for a fact that I used to easily find more than
a year's work at a time under female executives. Then it seemed
the engagements were being made for just two months, then for
just two weeks, then for just two days, and, if you can believe it,
for JUST TWO HOURS!!!

Is this any way to run a career? You bet it's not!

I know what some of you may be thinking (that is, the non-brain-dead
among you). You're thinking that this poster is contradicting himself
because he, on a prior occasion, pointed out that clients are willing
to pay $3,000 per day for a consultant (who, by the way, gets just
10 percent of the gross billing.) $3,000 per day sure is indicative
of strong demand for consultants, isn't it?

Actually, there is no contradiction. What you have to bear in mind
is that you have this Y2K anomaly colliding with the downward wage
equalization mechanisms of equal employment opportunity. Y2K is
causing an aberrant surge in demand in an overall downward trend.

However, once Y2K comes and goes, you are going to see the main
premise of this thread kick in once again with a vengeance, and
it's going to be back-on-your-head time in the hell known as
"equality."
```
