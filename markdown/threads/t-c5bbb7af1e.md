Of all the qualities of a computer language that people prefer, the one
that makes most sence to invest in for the long haul is something you
can read, and has or developes a common meaning. Otherwise, down the
line, not only do you have to either do it over, but you may have to
learn something which has no known meaning. Perhaps for those who lived
during the time this was in use, but for those that follow, a clear
statement of the problem solution is the best spent effort. It was a
surprise that enough English is spoken around the world for COBOL to
catch on so much. It is more a surprise to me to have such well thought 
out ideas about the result of the actions of a COMMITTEE! Still, if
Dilbert is right, many who read and write and speak do not yet
understand "the language". Bosses who permit some of the things you

short of .....!

publish for all to view the specs of all the dll's in Windows for ever.

The GUI is great for creating an industry of specialist who create
