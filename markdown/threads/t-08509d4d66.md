Everyone has different needs and such and can live on different
amounts of salary but to try and live something other than a really
frugal life style in Hawaii on 55K for a year is very hard.  Consider:
If you live in Ohio, the cost of living in Hawaii is 44.5% higher --
thus if you made 55K in Ohio, you would have to make almost 80K to
maintain your life style.

However, if you live in New York city, the cost of living in Honolulu
is 26.2 % lower.  And if you're only making 55K and living and working
in NYC, you're probably not doing so well so you should jump on this.

And as a final comparison, if you lived and worked in San Francisco
(and again on 55K you're probably not doing so well), the cost of
living in Honolulu is 21.8% lower, so go for it.

What I'm trying to point out to all of you potential applicants, is
don't be swayed by the thought of sunny beaches, warm days and bikini
clad tourists.  You still got to eat and drink and pay rent etc. etc.
