	While "number-of-days" is *much* better that "number-of-seconds" (;-)
	I still prefer using the YY & MM digits to hold "number-of-months"
	since some date.  Since *many* business applications have "monthly"
	processing cycles, having to decode "number-of-days" to determine 
	if this account processes on the 15th or not is *much* more computing
	than I want my computer to do on every record.  

	      Remember, the B in COBOL stands for "business"
