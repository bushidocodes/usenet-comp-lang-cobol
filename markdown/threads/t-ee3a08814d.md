The current FAQ for the comp.compilers news group includes the
following text:
Where can I get a Cobol grammar in yacc? Nowhere for free, as far as I
can tell. This question is asked every few months and there has never,
ever, been any positive response. Perhaps some of the interested people
could get together and write one. The commercial PCYACC from Abraxas
(see below) comes with a bunch of sample grammars including one for
Cobol-85. Also see the Cobol FAQ posted monthly to
comp.lang.misc and comp.lang.cobol.
O'Reilly's 1990 book on Lex and Yacc lists Abraxas' address as 7033 SW
Macadam Avenue, Portland OR 97219. Phone 1-503-244-5253, but I didn't
check this out on the net.
