From the IBM System/36 COBOL manual:

... is designed according to the following industry standards as
understood and interpreted by IBM, as of April 1983:

- The ANS COBOL, X3.23-1974 standard.

- The December 1975 Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPSPUB
21-1) low-intermediate level.

And there are a few IBM extensions in the compiler.

And just to make it perfectly clear the System/36 pre-dates IBM even
thinking about supporting ANSI'85.  It also isn't really clear what you mean
by "COBOL74".  There have been some vendors who have actually called their
products that  (Unisys - I think is one).  In which case, the fact that the
System/36 compiler met the ANSI'74 Standard does NOT mean that it is "equal
to" the Unisys implementation.
