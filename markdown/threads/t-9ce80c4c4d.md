  Don.Schricker <at> microfocus.com

current Standards process ... but if you want to, they do take AND PROCESS every 
input document that they receive.)

definition; its solutions for internationalization; common exception handling; 
portable arithmetic, etc) available on your platform and/or from your "vendor of 
choicer"
