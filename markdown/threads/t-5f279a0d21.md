Microsoft Cobol does indeed have a Windows product. I use it in my
mainframe development. As to your question, I don't know if it's worth it
or not. I have the complete workbench, and CICS emulator, which cost just

the animator, and it has saved me hours in debugging time, so for me it's
worth it.
