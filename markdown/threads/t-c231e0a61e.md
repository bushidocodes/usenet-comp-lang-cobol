```
The Cobol VS compiler still runs correctly in the year 2000+.
However the date on the compiler listing and in the resulting load module
still has a date of 19xx.
This date is hard coded in a one of the compiler module.  In our version of
the compiler go look at module IKFCBL02 csect IKF021.
At offset '50'x  there is a character constant 1966,
just patch the 19 to 20,
ie

 //A EXEC PGM=AMASPZAP
 //SYSPRINT DD SYSOUT=W
 //SYSLIB DD DSN=SYS1.COBLINK,DISP=OLD
NAME IKFCBL02 IKF021
VER 0050 F1F9
REP 0050 F2F0

Just don't patch it untill the year 2000 comes !!
```
