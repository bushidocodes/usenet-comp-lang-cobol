```
The Client Server specialist group of the British Computer Society has just launched a mail list for people interested in client server and distributed systems.

This will enable you to:

- ask questions on specific client/server issues

- share insights with other members of the group

- receive information on pitfalls and areas to avoid when implementing client/server

- disseminate interesting (although not necessarily true) fragments of gossip and rumour.

All you need to join the CSG mail list, is an Email address accessible through Internet.

If you send a mail to c.··.@isa··o.uk asking to join the mailing list, you will be added as a subscriber. This will take up to one working week. There is no charge for this service and you do not need to be a member of the CSG (although we hope you are!).

Your message will then be sent to all list subscribers(and if you do not already subscribe you will be added to the list)

I believe that this service will be of significant benefit to all CSG members and indeed to all developers interested in hearing about the latest happenings in the world of distributed systems.

I look forward to hearing from you.



Nick Evans
Chairman, CSG

```
