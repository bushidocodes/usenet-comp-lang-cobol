 http://www.ibmlink.ibm.com/usalets&parms=H_902-010

"Effective December 31, 2002, IBM withdraws from marketing COBOL for OS/390�
and VM (OS/390 Features Only) V2 (5648-A25), licensed under the ICA.
    ...

Announcement 201-343 , dated November 27, 2001."

NOTE well:
  This is not (YET) an announcement of withdrawal of service, on the other
hand, it is a pretty clear "clue" of what will be happening sooner than
later.

NOTE 2:
  For the "vast" number of you running COBOL under VM, this product is not
yet being withdrawn from that environment (which probably has a LOT to do
with the fact that Enterprise COBOL doesn't run there - yet).
