    The "normal" X"E5"  does seem to redirect on Win98.  Pick a subtle sound
(I use WCCLICK.WAV).  One possible reason for "no sound" is the nature of
the xe5 beep.  On an OLD pc, it produced a beep somewhat like the dos beep.
But as pc's got faster, the sound slide into higher and higher pitches,
becoming a click.  By now, even the click is gone, unless dogs and bats
still hear something.

    I was forced to create a routine that turned ansi.sys support on,
displayed a ^G, and then turned ansi.sys support back off.  This gets me a
real beep on non-win98 pc's.  Pity it doesn't work elsewhere.  I do not know
how to turn redirection off.
