```
I have been happily and successfully developing PC Based systems with
MBP Visual Cobol for over 10 years. Last year MBP was "absorbed" by
Micro Focus.

I have not heard one word from Micro Focus since the take-over.
- No word of "WELCOME" from Micro Focus of a "Good Bye" from MBP
- No indication on the future plans for MBP Visual Cobol
- No offer (incentives) to upgrade(?) to MF Cobol in any version
- No indication that the product will be dropped in "n" years (months)
- No indication that SUPPORT will continue or dissappear

I know for a fact, that at one time, MBP new my name and address
because I have gone through SEVERAL revisions of their compiler.

Is my new found "INVISIBILITY" because I am in Canada, and not the USA
or England???

Have other MBP developers been getting information from MF and I was
simply LOST in the transition???

What are other MBP developers doing???
- Adopting a "wait and see" attitude as I have done up to now???
- Converting to other compilers..... if so which ones provide the
easiest conversion. Do any other vendors have a screen management
system similar to SMSED???
- Converting away from COBOL (perish the thought... no spam please!)

I have been very Very VERY happy with MBP in the past and am
frustrated with the lack of information from Micro Focus. What are
similar developers thinking and doing???

Won't it be "refreshing" to talk about something other than
"paragraphs", "sections" and "goto"s for awhile!!!

Best regards and thanks for your responses
Bob Wilkins
```
