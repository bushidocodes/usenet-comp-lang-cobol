As one of the "winning" NO votes, I want to restate that I really DO welcome
all AcuCOBOL *and* Acucorp discussions in comp.lang.cobol.

My suggestions would be:

1) Put AcuCOBOL or Acucorp (depending on whether you are talking about COBOL
or add-on products) in the Subject line (possibly first)

2) Those in comp.lang.cobol who do NOT want to view Acucorp issues, should
add this word to their NG "filter file".

Should (in 6 months or so), there be "too many" Acucorp posts - then, I
would support a revised definition of a new newsgroup.
