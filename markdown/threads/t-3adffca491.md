Anyone,

I am investigating Java to Cobol communication using Corba.  I am a
novice in the Corba world and admit I have limited knowledge on the
subject but it is my understanding that you can use Corba to communicate
between disparate languages (i.e. Java to Cobol, Smalltalk to Java,
etc).  I am having a problem finding information of any kind that will
help me understand if/how this would work, examples of how to implement,
etc.  What I have found is a section in the Corba 2.2 spec "Mapping OMG

isn't something a novice can grasp very easily.  I have some basic
questions that may or may not help me:

available from Sun?  Freebies or at a nominal cost?
- Is the Section from the Corba spec I mentioned above something that
someone would use to create an idl to cobol parser?
- Is Corba only an option with OO Cobol languages?
- Are there any other websites or newsgroups that may provide additional
information?

Any information at all would be helpful, Thanks!
