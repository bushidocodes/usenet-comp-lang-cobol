I am aware of only one decompiler from Rasmussen Software, but I think
that it only works with code compiled with an older NCR COBOL
compiler.

I believe Rasmussen has a listing on the flexus tools page.

http://www.flexus.com/tools.html

Please be aware of the fact that even if you can successfully
decompile the code, it may be unmaintainable.  I cannot believe that
any decompiler would have the ability to decompile variable names to
their original text.  The variables will probably be garbled numeric
values which would make the task of maintenance something short of a
nightmare.

This reminds me of the cartoon I once saw of the grieving widow,
dressed in black and standing in front of the freshly dug grave.

A man in a suit behind her was leaning in her direction and asking
her..."Are you *sure* he didn't tell you where he kept the source
code?"



Bob Wolfe, flexus
Check out The Flexus COBOL Page at http://www.flexus.com
