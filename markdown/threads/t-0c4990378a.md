A while back there was a thread on generating text and a program named
RACTOR or RACTER? In the "Practice of Programming" by Brian Kernigan and
Rob Pike, chapter 3 discusses a "markov chain algorithm". Sounds
intimidating but the program reads text and generates new text based on the
frequency of appearance of phrases of a fixed length. They give the program
in C, C++, AWK, and PERL.

In the same book page 111 they discuss error handling: "Detect errors at a
low level, handle them at a high level. ... In most cases the caller should
determine how to handle an error, not he callee.  ...
