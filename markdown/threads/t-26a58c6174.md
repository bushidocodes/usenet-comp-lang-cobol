```
Further to messages I have posted in the past, I am now informed by a
senior IBM person that the current thinking is to provide only a
subset of the forthcoming ANSI/ISO COBOL 2002 standard on all
platforms including i, p and zSeries, the subset being those features
that users really want. IBM personnel are now working on the NATIONAL
support feature for example.

It seems apparent that interested programmers will have to raise their
heads out of the trenches and persuade their managers/directors to
lobby IBM very seriously to (possibly) get what you want.  COBOL i
Series users (AS/400) will know only too well that the intrinsic
functions were never fully implemented; this serves as an indication
of the task ahead. Good luck.
```
