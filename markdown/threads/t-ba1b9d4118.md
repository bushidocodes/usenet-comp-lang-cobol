Ever since the 1985 standard, lower-case characters and their corresponding
upper-case characters have been considered equivalent in COBOL words (that
includes reserved words as well as user identifiers).   Thus, if "most
people" who program in COBOL feel they are required to write in upper-case
characters exclusively, either they or the compilers they use are, dare I
say it, embroiled in trailing-edge technology.
