Don't know if you are a student looking for a compiler, but if you are,
the CA-Relia compiler in a scaled down version comes with the Prentice
Hall book (reasonably priced)
"COBOL: From Micro to Mainframe" Grauer/Vilar and is compatible with both
win 3.x & Win 95,
although it is essentially a DOS based compiler.
Unfortunately, the 2nd edition of this book is out of print, (and even
Prentice Hall has no more copies, I know, have been trying to track one
down for a friend for about a month now) But my understanding is that the
3rd edition is due out sometime in March, 1998.

If you want the professional version (which has no limit on file size or
program size) it can be had from
Computer Associates International, Inc. but was listed at $995 back in 1994
when the book was last published and prolly costs more now.

Good luck.
