PCTRAN is still widely in use on the Unisys S/80 models.
A description can be found in UP-8842 REv.4 at page F-4.

In case of problems, check the patch-level of your machine to see if it
is adequate. ( Contact your Unisys-rep)

respect to the batch-mode. 
Revert to an older version of PEP/STEP or again contact your Unisys-rep.
