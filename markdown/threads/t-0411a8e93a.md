Best solution is to use a field in the WS-Section defined like
01 DUMMY    PIC S9(9) COMP-5, then move the field control value using:

MOVE "TEXT" OF CMTEXT1 TO DUMMY
EVALUATE DUMMY
    WHEN  something
           do something
    WHEN  other
           do  other
END-EVALUATE.

This is because, as it has been explained by Jerry and  William, the
CMTEXT1 control treats its values as a text and using the solution above
you can resolve your problem...
