The point of a comp.lang group is to discuss the
language. I don't see how jobs postings are of
any relevance at all. Okay, one or two now and
then are no problem, just as people asking for
help with homework is okay occasionally. But
when the group is flooded with advertisements -
and comp.lang.cobol is flooded - it kills the
group.

Many people - like me - get all news articles
whatever the header says. This costs money and
time.

COBOL is an interesting language, by definition,
otherwise it would be dead long ago. It needs a
good active newsgroup. I have started sending a
'don't do this' reply to all people who post job
offers on this group.

Do the same, and maybe comp.lang.cobol will not
die under the weeds.
