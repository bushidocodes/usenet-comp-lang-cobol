>Is it possible to change the paper-tray (hopper) so that I can switch
>between the 2 hoppers. I want a copy of  a  form to be printed on a blank
>paper in hopper 2 and the original form on pre-printed paper in hopper 1.


Define two printer files and a ws switch to indicate whether you are on an
odd or even page.  Your header routine would toggle the switch and your
print routine would check it to know which printer to use.  If the output of
your even printer comes out in reverse order with your hardware setup, your
"even" print routine might have to release your print lines to a sort file
to correct the problem.

HTH
