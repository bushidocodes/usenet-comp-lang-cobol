The following (not "official" as far as I know) statement appears in the recent 
"Cheryl's List #94 - 29 October 2004"

"For example, we have been warned that ...  the ISAM access method ... will no 
longer be supported as of z/OS 1.7."

Should this be true, then this MIGHT impact some OS/VS COBOL customers who have 
remained on OS/VS COBOL (rather than migrating to VS COBOL II or later *because* 
of ISAM support).   Personally, I don't know of any sites that haven't converted 
to VSAM by now, but who knows what actually exists in some shop somewhere. 
(Obviously, if the OS doesn't support ISAM, then it won't matter if you use the 
LE or OS/VS COBOL run-time library - RES or NORES)
