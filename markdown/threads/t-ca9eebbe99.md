### IBM's "DB2 integrated coprocessor"

- **From:** "William M. Klein" <wmklein@ix.netcom.com>
- **Date:** 2003-03-21T16:34:59-06:00

- **Message-ID:** `<b5g42l$8r9$1@slb9.atl.mindspring.net>`

```
If you are currently - or plan in the near future - to use the "DB2
integrated coprocessor" which became available (via PTF) with IBM COBOL for
OS/390 & VM V2R2 and was "built-in" to Enterprise COBOL, I suggest that you
look at the following APARs.  I believe these are the "numbers" for COBOL
for OS/390 & VM - but they should show appropriate "sysroutes" for
Enterprise COBOL (either release)

PQ69319
PQ71089
PQ69652 (PE)
PQ71622 (for PQ69652)
PQ71463 (canceled as PQ71089 works without a Cobol fix)

    ***
Thanks to Roland Schiradin (customer)  and Tom Ross (IBM) for continuing to
pursue getting this feature "fully functional".

Personally, I am *not* aware of comparable APARs for the integrated CICS
support, but others may know if these need to be looked at as well.
```
