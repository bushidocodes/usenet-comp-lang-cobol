In CA-Realia COBOL 4.2 for the IBM PC, COMP-4 and COMP-5 are both binary data
items in 16-bit and 32-bit sizes, but COMP-4 is big-endian (for compatibility
with IBM Mainframe COBOL) and COMP-5 is little-endian (for maximum efficiency
on an X86 microprocessor). They both require picture clauses.

But COMP-4 might be different on a some other compiler or hardware platform.
I hope that helps!
