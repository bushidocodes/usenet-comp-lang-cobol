I posted several response yesterday but they never got there. If I
can recall (it gets harder the older you get) I responded that GO is
not being deleted from the language and the THRU option of PERFORM is
not being deleted either.

I also said that there are rules in the standard on the formation of
user-defined words. Basically, they don't begin with a number or X,
Y, or Z (except for ZERO, etc.). The do not contain only one
alphabetic character. They do not start with 1 or two characters
followed by a hyphen except for I-O, I-O-CONTROL, B-xxx or DB-xxx (the
DB- one will not be in the next standard). They do not contain two or
more contiguous hyphens.

I think that was about it.
