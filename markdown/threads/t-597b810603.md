```
The results were not what I expected.  However I only received 5
responses directly and 2 on the newsgroup.

PCs everyone said they used internal.  No real surprise here as there is
not a decent external SORT tool available.

VMS (1 response)  used a mixture of internal and external sorts.

MVS all said that they basically did both with a internals being common.

I must admit that the use of the internal sort in MVS was a little
surprising.  I have worked at a few shops and they all had a standard of
no internal sorts.  They considered it easier to debug with an external
sort and it gives the process a divide and conquer approach.

Thanks to all for their 2 cents.
Ken
```
