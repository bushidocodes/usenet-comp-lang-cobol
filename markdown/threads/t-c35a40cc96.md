We have experienced considerable difficulty using MF Visual Object
COBOL because of the lack of reference manuals. The help system is
fairly comprehensive and mostly correct, but I like to have reference
material on the desk beside me as I work, not on the screen beween me
and my work. The subject material and examples are too complex to be
memorized from a help screen, and too cumbersome to keep printing help
screens to the printer.
There have also been a few compatibility glitches in which a program
developed under MF 3.2.24 compiles cleanly under VisOC, but won't run.
MF has been pleasant, but hasn't found us an answer.
David Webber
