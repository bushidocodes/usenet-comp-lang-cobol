Offhand, I can't see what's wrong.  Nightly JCL problems solving was a
couple of years back though . . .

However, there might be another problem.  If there are "other" overrides in
the runlib JCL for a specific step, and those overrides do not occur "ddname
order", then all those that are out of order "will not take".  However, to
hear this problem, it sounds like you are doing only a single override for
this step, so this isn't likely to be the problem.

Instead using an override of:

..//qzlqsort.sortin dd unit=cart,disp=(old,keep),
           dsn=myuid.override.dsn

try:

..//qzlqsort.sortin dd dsn=myuid.override.dsn


I don't think you need to override parms that aren't changed, and since the
first two remain the same, there isn't a need to override them.

I wish I had a mainframe (or emulator) handy to try this out on some test
JCL, I'm sure I could get it working in a couple of minutes.


My other suggestion is this: the DD before UNIT=CART appears to begin in
column 11 (unless it's my text editor screwing things up).  If you move that
over one to column 12, that may help, too.
