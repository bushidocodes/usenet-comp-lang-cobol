From everyone's favorite busy-body (not the VSE expert).

The usual DL/I with VS COBOL II things to check are:

1) What are your compiler options for RENT and DATA? How about LE "AMODEALL"
options?

2) Are you overriding any of the IBM "produced" linkage editor options?

3) Do you have the correct "SYSLIB" concatenation for the LE-enabled DL/I
module in your link-edit step.  (These are MVS terms, but I think that VSE
has comparable steps - just called different things.)

All of this is assuming that you are getting the "usual" S0C4 type problem
at run-time.  If you are getting some other problem with your VS COBOL II -
DL/I stuff, tell us what the symptoms are.

P.S. Have you (or "your people") looked at

http://ppdbooks.pok.ibm.com/cgi-bin/bookmgr/bookmgr.cmd/BOOKS/FL2PGE00/24.2

(The "24.2 Using the Interface between LE/VSE and DL/I" section in the
"LE/VSE V1R4 Programming Guide" Document Number: SC33-6684-00)

I don't know for certain that V1R4 is the latest version of LE/VSE - but
this should get your started.
