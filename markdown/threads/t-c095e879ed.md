I have been in the process of developing a 1-shot conversion program to
convert Wang-VS COBOL to ANSI / MF COBOL for the last year (on and
off). I have been using LEX and YACC in addition to C and COBOL for the
different modules. It is still not complete, but the specifications are
all there. It is not an easy nut to crack given all of the very nice
Wang Extensions to the dialect. There are companies that offer products
that will do the conversion, but the really lack in the area of screen
(Workstation) I/O. Part of my process includes a callable subroutine
which maps the screen I/O of WSIO to the PC.
Let me know if this interests you.
