The X-OPEN screen section standard doesn't allow the dynamic change of
color.  This particular standard is the one used in the Realia and
Fujitsu compilers.  However, Merant and most likely AcuCorp's
offerings DO allow this dynamic access.  Simply assign the color to a
variable and populate it accordingly.
