I used DIgital's Business Oriented Language for about a year on a PDP 11/70
under the RSTS/E operating system back in the late 70s.  Very easy-to-use
language.  Kind of a cross between COBOL and BASIC.  Was limited to 6
character data names and paragraph names at the time.  Had programmed in
COBOL up until then.  Although it could be compiled and linked, I think it
was also considered an interpretive language.
