Not sure of your question. I'm assuming you're using 'sysin=' as a JCL term.
JCL is used to connect _files_ (not records) to your O/S. You need separate
JCL statements, i.e. 'sysin1=abc' and 'sysin2=xyz' (using your example above)
or:

sysin1 dd dsn=abc,disp=shr,......etc.
sysin2 dd dsn=xyz,disp=new,......etc.

in the actual jcl.

Once the two files are defined, yes - you need to open them in the program
before reading/writing/whatever to them. Example:

OPEN INVENT-FILE
INVENT-FILE-2.

No comma after the first file.

Hope this helps. If this is not what you're looking for, let me know.
