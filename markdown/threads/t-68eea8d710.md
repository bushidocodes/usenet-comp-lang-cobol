developed for/on a platform where the only other existing language was
Basic and they wanted the Basic programmers to be comfortable trying out
their Cobol. Early versions of Basic used only line numbers and had no
labels (e.g. for GoTo and GoSub) at all. For that matter, APL resolves a
goto label to its step number, if I remember correctly.
